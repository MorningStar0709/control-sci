# Steady-State Condition

As the terminal time $k_{f} \rightarrow \infty$ , the matrix difference Riccati equation (5.3.20) becomes matrix algebraic Riccati equation (5.4.7). Now, let us find the analytical expression for the solution $\overline{P}$ of this ARE. As $(k_{f} - k) \rightarrow \infty$ , $\mathbf{M}^{-(k_{f}-k)} \rightarrow \infty$ , since $M^{-1}$ is stable. This means that in (5.4.35) $\mathbf{T}(k) \rightarrow 0$ . Then the steady-state solution of (5.4.38) gives

$$\overline {{\mathbf {P}}} = \mathbf {W} _ {2 1} \mathbf {W} _ {1 1} ^ {- 1}. \tag {5.4.39}$$

Let us note that the previous steady-state solution (5.4.39) requires the unstable eigenvalues (5.4.24) and eigenvectors (5.4.25). Thus, we have the analytical solution (5.4.39) of the ARE (5.4.7).
