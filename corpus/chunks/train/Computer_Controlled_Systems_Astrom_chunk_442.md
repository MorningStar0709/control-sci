# Example 7.6 Predictive first-order-hold sampling of an integrator

An integrator has the transfer function $G(s) = 1/s$ . The zero-order-hold sampling of the double integrator is

$$\frac {h ^ {2}}{2} \frac {z + 1}{(z - 1) ^ {2}}$$

It then follows from Eq. (7.20) that

$$H (z) = \frac {h}{2} \frac {z + 1}{z - 1}$$

This is the same result obtained in Example 7.4.
