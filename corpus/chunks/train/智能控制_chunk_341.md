# 2. 分段线性型

分段线性型函数表达式为

$$
f \left(\mathrm{net} _ {i}\right) = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0 & \mathrm{net} _ {i} \leqslant \mathrm{net} _ {i 0} \\ k \mathrm{net} _ {i} & \mathrm{net} _ {i 0} <   \mathrm{net} _ {i} <   \mathrm{net} _ {i 1} \\ f _ {\max} & \mathrm{net} _ {i} \geqslant \mathrm{net} _ {i 1} \end{array} \right. \tag {7.5}
$$
