Lemma 3.1 (Matrix Inversion Lemma) Let F be a $( n \times n )$ dimensional nonsingular matrix, R a $( m \times m )$ dimensional nonsingular matrix and H a $( n \times m )$ dimensional matrix of maximum rank, then the following identity holds:

$$(F ^ {- 1} + H R ^ {- 1} H ^ {T}) ^ {- 1} = F - F H (R + H ^ {T} F H) ^ {- 1} H ^ {T} F \tag {3.44}$$

Proof By direct multiplication one finds that:

$$[ F - F H (R + H ^ {T} F H) ^ {- 1} H ^ {T} F ] [ F ^ {- 1} + H R ^ {- 1} H ^ {T} ] = I$$

For the case of (3.37), one chooses $\begin{array} { r } { H = \phi ( t ) , R = 1 } \end{array}$ and one obtains from (3.37) and (3.44):

$$F (t + 1) = F (t) - \frac {F (t) \phi (t) \phi^ {T} (t) F (t)}{1 + \phi^ {T} (t) F (t) \phi (t)} \tag {3.45}$$
