3) Attack 3: is also repeated in the presence of the PSS and again proved to be the strongest attack. The grid experienced the rapid frequency oscillation depicted in Fig. 10. The maximum frequency reached 1.58 Hz. This causes the generators to trip instantaneously leading to a blackout. Fig. 11 demonstrates a sample Attack 3 load and Fig. 12 represents the aggregate of the 4 attack loads. The average frequency deviation among the 10 generators was 1.06 Hz.

This case study demonstrates that even in the presence of the PSS the attack impact is not eliminated when it is crafted properly. To this end, adding a mitigation mechanism that can eliminate LA attack impacts becomes necessary.
