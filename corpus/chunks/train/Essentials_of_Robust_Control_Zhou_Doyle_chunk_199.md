$$P (s, \alpha , \beta) \in \left\{P _ {0} + W \Delta \mid \| \Delta \| \leq 1 \right\}$$

with $P _ { 0 } : = P ( s , 0 , 0 )$ and

$$W (s) = P (s, 1, 1) - P (s, 0, 0) = \frac {1 0 (0 . 2 s ^ {2} + 0 . 7 s + 0 . 2)}{(s ^ {2} + 0 . 5 s + 1) (s ^ {2} + 2 s + 3) (s ^ {2} + 3 s + 6)}$$

Another way to bound the frequency response is to treat α and $\beta$ as norm bounded uncertainties; that is,

$$P (s, \alpha , \beta) \in \{P _ {0} + W _ {1} \Delta_ {1} + W _ {2} \Delta_ {2} | \| \Delta_ {i} \| _ {\infty} \leq 1 \}$$

with $P _ { 0 } = P ( s , 0 , 0 )$ and

$$W _ {1} = \frac {1 0 (0 . 2 s ^ {2} + 0 . 3 s)}{(s ^ {2} + 0 . 5 s + 1) (s ^ {2} + 2 s + 3) (s ^ {2} + 3 s + 6)},W _ {2} = \frac {1 0 (0 . 4 s + 0 . 2)}{(s ^ {2} + 0 . 5 s + 1) (s ^ {2} + 2 s + 3) (s ^ {2} + 3 s + 6)}$$

It is in fact easy to show that

$$\left\{P _ {0} + W _ {1} \Delta_ {1} + W _ {2} \Delta_ {2} \mid \| \Delta_ {i} \| _ {\infty} \leq 1 \right\} = \left\{P _ {0} + W \Delta \mid \| \Delta \| _ {\infty} \leq 1 \right\}$$

with $| W | = | W _ { 1 } | + | W _ { 2 } |$ . The frequency response $P _ { 0 } + W \Delta$ is shown in Figure 8.4. This bounding is clearly more conservative.
