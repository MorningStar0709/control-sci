The figures demonstrate that the RL agent effectively learns a control policy for AGC that ensures system stability and, in some aspects, outperforms the PI controller. Specifically, the RL agent’s control leads to smaller dispatch changes and more balanced load sharing in Area 1, lower tie-line power deviation, and generally more damped system behavior, as shown in Fig. 5.
