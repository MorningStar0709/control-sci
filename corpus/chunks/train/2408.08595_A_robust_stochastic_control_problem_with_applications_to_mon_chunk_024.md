$$\mathbb {E} [ (X _ {T} ^ {u} - \gamma) ^ {2} ]= Y _ {0} ^ {- 1} (h _ {0} x - \gamma) ^ {2} + \mathbb {E} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {T} Y _ {t} ^ {- 1} \left\{\alpha_ {t} ^ {u} + \left(\phi_ {t} - \frac {Z _ {t}}{Y _ {t}}\right) (h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {u} - \gamma) \right\} ^ {\prime} \times \left\{\alpha_ {t} ^ {u} + \left(\phi_ {t} - \frac {Z _ {t}}{Y _ {t}}\right) (h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {u} - \gamma) \right\} d t \right]. \tag {4.8}$$

Therefore, by (3.18), we get the optimal feedback strategy

$$h _ {t} D _ {t} ^ {\prime} \hat {u} _ {t} ^ {\gamma (K)} + X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u} ^ {\gamma (K)}} L _ {t} + h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u} ^ {\gamma (K)}} C _ {t} = - \left(\phi_ {t} - \frac {Z _ {t}}{Y _ {t}}\right) \left(h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u} ^ {\gamma (K)}} - \gamma (K)\right), \tag {4.9}$$

and

$$J (K, \gamma) = Y _ {0} ^ {- 1} (h _ {0} x - \gamma) ^ {2}.$$

Then, combined with the fact $Y _ { 0 } > 1$ obtained by Lemma 3.2, we get

$$F (K) = \sup _ {\gamma \in \mathbb {R}} \left(Y _ {0} ^ {- 1} (h _ {0} x - \gamma) ^ {2} - (K - \gamma) ^ {2}\right) = \frac {Y _ {0} ^ {- 1} (K - h _ {0} x) ^ {2}}{1 - Y _ {0} ^ {- 1}},$$

and

$$\hat {\gamma} (K) = \frac {Y _ {0} ^ {- 1} h _ {0} x - K}{Y _ {0} ^ {- 1} - 1}.$$

Therefore, by (4.3), it holds

$$\hat {K} = h _ {0} x + \frac {1 - Y _ {0} ^ {- 1}}{\theta Y _ {0} ^ {- 1}}, \sup _ {u \in \Pi} \left[ \mathbb {E} [ X _ {T} ] - \frac {\theta}{2} \mathrm{Var} (X _ {T}) \right] = h _ {0} x + \frac {1}{2 \theta} (Y _ {0} - 1),$$

and

$$\hat {\gamma} (\hat {K}) = h _ {0} x + \frac {Y _ {0}}{\theta}. \tag {4.10}$$

Substituting (4.10) into (4.9) and using (3.21) yield

$$\hat {u} _ {t} = (h _ {t} D _ {t} ^ {\prime}) ^ {- 1} \left[ - (\phi_ {t} - \frac {Z _ {t}}{Y _ {t}}) \left(h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}} - h _ {0} x - \frac {Y _ {0}}{\theta}\right) - X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}} L _ {t} - h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}} C _ {t} \right]= (h _ {t} D _ {t} ^ {\prime}) ^ {- 1} \left[ (\phi_ {t} Y _ {t} - Z _ {t}) \left(\frac {\theta h _ {0} x + Y _ {0} - \theta h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}}}{\theta Y _ {t}}\right) - X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}} L _ {t} - h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}} C _ {t} \right]= (h _ {t} D _ {t} ^ {\prime}) ^ {- 1} \left[ \frac {\Lambda^ {\hat {\eta}}}{\theta} (\phi_ {t} Y _ {t} - Z _ {t}) - X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}} L _ {t} - h _ {t} X _ {t} ^ {\hat {u}} C _ {t} \right],$$

which is exactly the optimal strategy of the robust stochastic control problem. Moreover, from the Theorem 3.3, we obtain ${ \hat { u } } \in \Pi .$ This completes the proof.
