# Example 2.12 Double integrator

Consider the double integrator in Example 2.2 when $h = 1$ . From (2.26)

$$
H (q) = \left( \begin{array}{l l} 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c c} q - 1 & - 1 \\ 0 & q - 1 \end{array} \right) ^ {- 1} \binom {0. 5} {1} = \frac {0 . 5 (q + 1)}{(q - 1) ^ {2}} = \frac {0 . 5 (q ^ {- 1} + q ^ {- 2})}{1 - 2 q ^ {- 1} + q ^ {- 2}}
$$
