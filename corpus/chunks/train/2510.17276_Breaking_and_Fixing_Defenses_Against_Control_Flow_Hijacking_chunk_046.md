# C.1 PARSEABILITY

As shown in Table 7, in general, the percentage of generated graphs that parse using Lark is correlated with model capability. With a maximum of three retries, the most capable model in our tests, o4-mini, generates parseable graphs 100% of the time with an average of 1.1 retries. Weaker models like 4o-mini, however, seem to struggle and likely need more retries or in-context examples.
