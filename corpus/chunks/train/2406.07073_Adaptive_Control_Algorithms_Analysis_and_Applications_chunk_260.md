# 5.8 Model Order Selection

In the absence of a clear a priori information upon the plant model structure, two approaches can be used to select the appropriate orders for the values of $d , n _ { A } , n _ { B }$ characterizing the input-output plant model

(a) a practical approach based on trial and error,   
(b) estimation of d, $n _ { A } , n _ { B }$ directly from data.

Even in the case of using an estimation of the orders directly from data it is useful to see to what extend the two approaches are coherent. We will discuss the two approaches next.
