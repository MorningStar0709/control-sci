# 5.4 Simulations under Blue Noise

In this section, simulations were carried out under blue noise. The natural equivalent of blue noise is similar to the hissing sound made by fountains. Blue noise was produced with a sampling time of 0.1 s using the Colored Noise Generator block in Simulink. Fig. 9 shows controller simulations under blue noise.
