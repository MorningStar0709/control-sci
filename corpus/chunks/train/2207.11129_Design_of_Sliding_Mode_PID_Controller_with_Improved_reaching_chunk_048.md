# 2.4.4.3 Power Rate Reaching Law

$$\dot {s} = - k. | s | ^ {\alpha}. \operatorname{sgn} (s), k > 0, 1 > \alpha > 0 \tag {2.24}$$

This reaching law increases the reaching speed when the state is far away from the switching manifold. However, it reduces the rate when the state is near the manifold, resulting in a fast and low chattering reaching mode.
