# 2.4 Conclusion

This chapter presented novel control protocols for the cooperative manipulation of a single object by N robotics agents without employing force sensing. Firstly, we focused on rigid grasps, by introducing two adaptive decentralized control schemes that used quaternion-feedback and prescribed performance control, respectively. Next, we incorporated collision avoidance by using nonlinear MPC, in a centralized and a communication-based decentralized scheme. Secondly, we considered the case of rolling contacts. We developed novel adaptive centralized and decentralized control schemes that compensate for the object’s and the agents’ dynamic uncertainties and guarantee avoidance of contact loss at the contact points.
