# 10.3 FEEDBACK CONTROLLERS

We stated earlier that the controller block in Figs. 10.2 and 10.3 consists of the control logic or “rules” required to turn feedback information into a control signal command that is sent to the plant. In this section, we briefly discuss and demonstrate the attributes of the following “standard” types of controllers for feedback systems:

1. On – off (or relay) controllers   
2. Proportional (P) controllers   
3. Proportional-integral (PI) controllers   
4. Proportional-derivative (PD) controllers   
5. Proportional-integral-derivative (PID) controllers
