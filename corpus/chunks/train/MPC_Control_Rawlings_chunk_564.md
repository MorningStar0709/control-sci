# Exercise 4.6: Max or sum?

Given $\gamma _ { 1 } , \gamma _ { 2 } \in \mathcal { K }$ , show there exists $a , b > 0$ such that

$$a \left(\gamma_ {1} (x) + \gamma_ {2} (y)\right) \leq \max \left(\gamma_ {1} (x), \gamma_ {2} (y)\right) \leq b \left(\gamma_ {1} (x) + \gamma_ {2} (y)\right)$$

for all $x \in \mathbb { R } ^ { n } , y \in \mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ . Therefore it is equivalent if ISS or OSS is defined in terms of inequalities using the max or the sum.
