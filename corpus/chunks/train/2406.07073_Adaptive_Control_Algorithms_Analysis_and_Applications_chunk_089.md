# Regressor Form

Taking into account the form of (2.37), the j step ahead predictor can be expressed also in a regressor form as:

$$P (q ^ {- 1}) \hat {y} (t + j) = \theta^ {T} \phi (t) \tag {2.48}$$

where:

$$\theta^ {T} = \left[ f _ {0} ^ {j}, \dots , f _ {n _ {F}} ^ {j}, g _ {0}, \dots , g _ {n _ {B} + j - 2} \right] \tag {2.49}\phi^ {T} (t) = [ y (t), \dots , y (t - n _ {F}), u (t + j - d - 1), \dots , u (t - n _ {B} - d + 1) ] \tag {2.50}G _ {j} \left(q ^ {- 1}\right) = E _ {j} B ^ {*} = g _ {0} + g _ {1} q ^ {- 1} + \dots + g _ {j + n _ {B} - 2} q ^ {- j - n _ {B} + 2} \tag {2.51}$$
