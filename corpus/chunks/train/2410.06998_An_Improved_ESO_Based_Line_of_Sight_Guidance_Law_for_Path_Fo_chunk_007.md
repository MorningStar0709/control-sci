# 2.1. Coordinate System

For the conventional torpedo-type AUV and the disktype AUH, six differnent motion components in the North-East coordinate system are defined as $\{ n \} = [ x , y , z , \phi , \theta ,$ ?? ]. The body frame is denoted by $\{ b \} \ = \ \left\lceil u , v , w _ { z } , p , q , r \right\rceil$ . In practical operations, the pitch angle and roll angle of the AUH can be continuously maintained to zero approximately. Hence, $p = { \dot { p } } = { \dot { q } } = q = 0$ , and the kinematics of the AUH in four-degrees-of-freedom can be simplified to (Fossen, 2011; Li et al., 2023)
