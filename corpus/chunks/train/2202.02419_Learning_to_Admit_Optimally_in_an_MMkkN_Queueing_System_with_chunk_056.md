Finite-time Performance Analysis In this subsection, we demonstrate that the regret bounds established in Theorem 3 and Corollary 1 can be extended to the multi-server queueing system with a finite buffer. To this end, we first prove an analog of Lemma 5 for the buffered setting. Using the upper and lower bounds derived in (60), we replicate the proof of Lemma 5 (presented in Section B.2) to obtain the following lemma.

Lemma 10. Consider a multi-server queueing system with finite buffer and service rate $\mu$ following policy $\pi _ { \mathrm { A l g 1 } }$ . For $\mu \in ( \mu ^ { * } , + \infty )$ , there exists a problem-dependent constant $c _ { 5 }$ such that

$$\mathbb {P} \left(Y _ {n} ^ {M} \leq - \tilde {\delta} _ {1} n\right) \leq \exp \left(- c _ {5} n\right),$$

$$\mathbb {P} \left(Y _ {n} ^ {M} \geq - \tilde {\delta} _ {2} n\right) \leq \exp \left(- c _ {6} n\right).$$
