# 11.5 Practical Aspects

The previous sections show how the LQ- and the LQG-control problems can be solved. There are several practical problems when applying LQ-control. One occurs in choosing the design parameters—that is, the weightings in the loss function—which is discussed in Sec. 11.2, and the sampling period. Another problem is the difficulty of obtaining good models for the process and the disturbances. Still another problem is making the numerical computations necessary to get the resulting controller.
