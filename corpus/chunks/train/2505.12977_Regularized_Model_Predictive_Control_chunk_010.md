$$
\begin{array}{l} \bar {U} _ {k} ^ {*} = \underset {\bar {U} _ {k}} {\arg \min} \left\{\| \bar {U} _ {k} \| _ {H _ {1}} ^ {2} + \| \mathcal {B} _ {2} \bar {U} _ {k} - \mathcal {A} _ {2} x _ {k | k} \| _ {H _ {2}} ^ {2} \right\} \\ = \underset {\bar {U} _ {k}} {\arg \min} \left\{\left(\mathcal {B} \bar {U} _ {k} - \mathcal {A} x _ {k | k}\right) ^ {\top} \mathcal {H} \left(\mathcal {B} \bar {U} _ {k} - \mathcal {A} x _ {k | k}\right) \right\}. \tag {17} \\ \end{array}
$$

By applying Lemma 2, we obtain the optimal solution as (12). Moreover, substituting this solution into (17) yields

$$
\begin{array}{l} J ^ {*} = \left(\mathscr {B} K _ {k} x _ {k | k} - \mathscr {A} x _ {k | k}\right) ^ {\top} \mathscr {H} \left(\mathscr {B} K _ {k} x _ {k | k} - \mathscr {A} x _ {k | k}\right) \\ = \left(\bar {K} _ {k} x _ {k | k}\right) ^ {\top} \mathcal {H} \left(\bar {K} _ {k} x _ {k | k}\right) \tag {18} \\ = x _ {k | k} ^ {\mathsf {T}} \left(K _ {k} ^ {\mathsf {T}} H _ {1} K _ {k} + Q + \mu \mathscr {R} ^ {\mathsf {T}} \mathscr {R}\right) x _ {k | k}, \\ \end{array}
$$

where $\bar { K } _ { k } = \mathrm { c o l } \{ K _ { k } , I , \mathcal { R } \}$ . The proof follows for

$$P _ {k + l - 1} = K _ {k} ^ {\mathsf {T}} H _ {1} K _ {k} + Q + \mu \mathcal {R} ^ {\mathsf {T}} \mathcal {R}. \tag {19}$$

Remark 1: The formulation of Re-MPC is inspired by the structural characteristics of classical quadratic-cost designs, combined with the penalty function approach (see [21]). This approach enables the extension of recursive Riccati equationbased control to MPC. As $\mu \to \infty$ , equation (17) converges to the constrained problem (16) (see Lemma 2). Within this penalized framework, the last term in (19) vanishes as $\mathcal { R }  0$ , improving controller accuracy and yielding an exact solution to the constrained problem that satisfies the system in (1). This leads to the following recursive Riccati equation

$$P _ {k + l - 1} = K _ {X _ {k}} ^ {\top} \bar {Q} _ {k + l} K _ {X _ {k}} + K _ {U _ {k}} ^ {\top} \bar {R} K _ {U _ {k}} + Q. \tag {20}$$
