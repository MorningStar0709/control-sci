Remark 3.3. The assumption that a minimizer $u _ { f , g } \in \mathcal { U }$ exists for every $( f , g ) \in { \mathcal { D } }$ is used to verify the convexlike condition on $\Psi [ u , ( f , g ) ]$ required by Sion’s minimax theorem (Step (ii) of the proof below).
