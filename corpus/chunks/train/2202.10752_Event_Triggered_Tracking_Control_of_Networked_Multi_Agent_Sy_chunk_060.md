# APPENDIX A EXPLICIT EXPRESSION IN (17a)

The functions in (17a) are presented below in detail.

$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {\eta} = f _ {\mathrm{p}} (x _ {\mathrm{p}}, \hat {u} _ {\mathrm{c}} + \hat {u} _ {\mathrm{f}}, w _ {\mathrm{p}}) - f _ {\mathrm{p}} (x _ {\mathrm{r}}, \hat {u} _ {\mathrm{f}}, w _ {\mathrm{r}}) \\ = f _ {\mathrm{p}} \left(\eta + x _ {\mathrm{r}}, g _ {\mathrm{c}} \left(x _ {\mathrm{c}}\right) + e _ {\mathrm{c}} + u _ {\mathrm{f}} + e _ {\mathrm{f}}, w _ {\mathrm{p}}\right) \\ - f _ {\mathrm{p}} (x _ {\mathrm{r}}, u _ {\mathrm{f}} + e _ {\mathrm{f}}, w _ {\mathrm{r}}) \\ =: F _ {\eta} (\delta , \eta , x _ {\mathrm{r}}, x _ {\mathrm{c}}, e _ {\mathrm{a}}, e _ {\mathrm{r}}, e _ {\mathrm{f}}, w), \\ \end{array}
$$

where $\delta$ is a variable related to uf and/or it differential.
