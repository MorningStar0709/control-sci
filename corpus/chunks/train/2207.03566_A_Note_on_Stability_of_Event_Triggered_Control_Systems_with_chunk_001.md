# Abstract

This note studies stability of event-triggered control systems with the event-triggered control algorithm proposed in [1]. We construct a novel Halanay-type inequality, which is used to show that sufficient conditions of the main results in [1] ensure stability of the event-triggered control systems that was missing in [1]. It is also shown that a positive parameter in the proposed event-triggering condition in [1] can be freely selected to exclude Zeno behavior from the event-triggered control system. An illustrative example is investigated to demonstrate the theoretical results of this study with numerical simulations.
