Figure 1.16 Illustrates performance of controllers that are tuned to the disturbance characteristics. Output error when (a) $\omega = \omega_{e} = 0.1$ ; (b) $\omega = 0.05$ , $\omega_{e} = 0.1$ ; (c) $\omega = \omega_{e} = 0.05$ .

There are many other practical problems of a similar type in which there are significant variations in the disturbance characteristics. Having a controller that can adapt to changing disturbance patterns is particularly important when there is limited control authority or dead time in the process dynamics.
