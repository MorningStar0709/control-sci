# B. Proposed NN Local Tuning Laws

The adaptive tuning laws are derived from a Lyapunov function that captures both control design at each agent and the communication graph topology.

The NN parameter adaptive tuning laws are:

$$\dot {\hat {\theta}} _ {i} = - F _ {i} \left[ \phi_ {i} r _ {i} p _ {i} (d _ {i} + b _ {i} ^ {0}) + \kappa_ {i} \hat {\theta} _ {i} \right], \tag {40}\dot {\hat {\theta}} _ {0} = F _ {i 0} \left[ \phi_ {0} r _ {i} p _ {i} (d _ {i} + b _ {i} ^ {0}) - \kappa_ {0} \hat {\theta} _ {0} \right], \tag {41}\dot {\hat {\theta}} _ {i w} = - F _ {i w} \left[ \phi_ {i w} r _ {i} p _ {i} (d _ {i} + b _ {i} ^ {0}) + \kappa_ {i w} \hat {\theta} _ {i w} \right]. \tag {42}$$

and in the global context, the parameter estimate dynamics are:

$$\dot {\hat {\theta}} = - F \left[ \phi r ^ {\top} P (D + B) + \kappa \hat {\theta} \right], \tag {43}\dot {\hat {\theta}} _ {0} = F _ {0} \left[ \phi_ {0} r ^ {\top} P (D + B) - \kappa_ {0} \hat {\theta} _ {0} \right], \tag {44}\dot {\hat {\theta}} _ {w} = - F _ {w} \left[ \phi_ {w} r ^ {\top} P (D + B) + \kappa_ {w} \hat {\theta} _ {w} \right]. \tag {45}$$

Here, $F _ { i } , \quad F _ { i 0 } .$ , and $F _ { i w }$ are positive definite matrices structured as F = $\mathrm { d i a g } ( F _ { 1 } , F _ { 2 } , . . . , F _ { N } ) , F _ { 0 }$ = diag $( F _ { 0 } , F _ { 0 } , . . . , F _ { 0 } )$ , and $\textit { F } _ { w } \ = \ \mathrm { d i a g } ( F _ { 1 w } , F _ { 2 w } , . . . , F _ { N w } )$ , respectively. The matrix P is defined as in (31), and $\kappa , \kappa _ { 0 }$ , and $\kappa _ { w }$ are positive scalar tuning gains.
