$$
\begin{array}{l} V (x) \geqslant x ^ {\mathrm{T}} P x \geqslant \lambda_ {m i n} (P) \| x \| _ {2} ^ {2} \\ V (x) \leqslant x ^ {\mathrm{T}} P x + \frac {1}{2} \| x \| _ {2} ^ {4} \leqslant \lambda_ {m a x} (P) \| x \| _ {2} ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2} \| x \| _ {2} ^ {4} \\ \end{array}
$$

看出 $\alpha_{1}$ 和 $\alpha_{2}$ 可取为

$$\alpha_ {1} (r) = \lambda_ {m i n} (P) r ^ {2} \quad \text {和} \quad \alpha_ {2} (r) = \lambda_ {m a x} (P) r ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2} r ^ {4}$$

$$b = \alpha_ {1} ^ {- 1} (\alpha_ {2} (\mu)) = \sqrt {\frac {\alpha_ {2} (\mu)}{\lambda_ {m i n} (P)}} = \sqrt {\frac {\lambda_ {m a x} (P) \mu^ {2} + \mu^ {4} / 2}{\lambda_ {m i n} (P)}} \tag {$\triangle$}$$
