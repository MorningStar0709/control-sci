# Integral Action

In the special case in which the disturbance is a constant, that is, $A_{d} = q - 1$ , we have to add an additional closed-loop pole. Hence

$$X = q + x _ {0}$$

and Eq. (3.40) becomes

$$(q - 1) R ^ {\prime} = (q + x _ {0}) R ^ {0} + y _ {0} B$$

Putting $q = 1$ gives one equation to solve for $y_0$ . Hence

$$y _ {0} = - \frac {(1 + x _ {0}) R ^ {9} (1)}{B (1)} \tag {3.41}$$

Inserting $X$ and $Y = y_0$ into Eqs. (3.39) gives the new controller.
