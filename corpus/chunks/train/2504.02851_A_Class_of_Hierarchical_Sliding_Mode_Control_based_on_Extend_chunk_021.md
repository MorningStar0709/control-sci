$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {V} _ {c} = S _ {c} \dot {S} _ {c} = S _ {c} (\alpha \dot {s} + \ddot {s}) \\ = S _ {c} \left[ \alpha c _ {1} (\dot {\hat {x}} - \dot {x} _ {r}) + \alpha c _ {2} (\dot {\hat {y}} - \dot {y} _ {r}) + \alpha c _ {3} (\dot {\hat {z}} - \dot {z} _ {r}) \right. \\ \left. + c _ {1} \left(\ddot {\hat {x}} - \ddot {x} _ {r}\right) + c _ {2} \left(\ddot {\hat {y}} - \ddot {y} _ {r}\right) + c _ {3} \left(\ddot {\hat {z}} - \ddot {z} _ {r}\right) \right] \\ = S _ {c} \left[ \alpha c _ {1} (\dot {\hat {x}} - \dot {x} _ {r}) + \alpha c _ {2} (\dot {\hat {y}} - \dot {y} _ {r}) + \alpha c _ {3} (\dot {\hat {z}} - \dot {z} _ {r}) \right. \\ + c _ {1} (f _ {\hat {x}} + b _ {\hat {x}} u - \ddot {x} _ {r}) + c _ {2} (f _ {\hat {y}} + b _ {\hat {y}} u - \ddot {y} _ {r}) \\ \left. + c _ {3} \left(f _ {\hat {z}} + b _ {\hat {z}} u - \ddot {z} _ {r}\right) \right]. \tag {82} \\ \end{array}
$$

Therefore, the equivalent control law and the switch control law are proposed as:

$$
\begin{array}{l} u _ {e q} = - \frac {c _ {1} f _ {\hat {x}} + c _ {2} f _ {\hat {y}} + c _ {3} f _ {\hat {z}} - c _ {1} \ddot {x} _ {r} - c _ {2} \ddot {y} _ {r} - c _ {3} \ddot {z} _ {r}}{c _ {1} b _ {\hat {x}} + c _ {2} b _ {\hat {y}} + c _ {3} b _ {\hat {z}}} \\ - \frac {\alpha c _ {1} (\dot {\hat {x}} - \dot {x} _ {r}) + \alpha c _ {2} (\dot {\hat {y}} - \dot {y} _ {r}) + \alpha c _ {3} (\dot {\hat {z}} - \dot {z} _ {r})}{c _ {1} b _ {\hat {x}} + c _ {2} b _ {\hat {y}} + c _ {3} b _ {\hat {z}}} \tag {83} \\ \end{array}
u _ {s w} = - \frac {K _ {c} S _ {c} + \eta \operatorname{sat} \left(S _ {c}\right)}{c _ {1} b _ {\hat {x}} + c _ {2} b _ {\hat {y}} + c _ {3} b _ {\hat {z}}} \tag {84}$$

with $K _ { c }$ is a positive number, and $\mathrm { s a t } ( S _ { c } )$ is the saturation sign function. Substituting the control law (80) into the (82), the derivative becomes:

$$\dot {V} _ {c} = - K _ {c} S _ {c} ^ {2} - \eta S _ {c} \mathrm{sat} (S _ {c}). \tag {85}$$

Hence, the sliding surface is stable. To check the stability of all the element sliding surfaces s and s˙, the proof is provided in [40].
