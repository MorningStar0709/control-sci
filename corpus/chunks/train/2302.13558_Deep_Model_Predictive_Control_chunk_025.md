At $t = p _ { \mathrm { m a x } } - 1$ , the buffer becomes full. So new data can be added after the removal of some old data by using some suitable experience selection method [44]. The available approaches are based on retaining the most informative data based on some criterion [52] and ensuring sufficient diversity [53]. Our present approach is compatible with any existing method of experience selection. However, different methods may result in different performance for different problems and their choice may also depend on the availability of resources. We keep the method of experience selection open for the choice of users.
