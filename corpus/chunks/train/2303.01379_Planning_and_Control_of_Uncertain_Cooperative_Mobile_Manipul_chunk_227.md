# 3.3.3 Interaction Forces based on the D&B Rigidity Matrix and Internal-Force based Optimal Cooperative Manipulation

We provide here the main results of this section. Firstly, we give a closed form expression for the interaction and internal forces of the coupled system object-robots. Next, we connect these forces with the D&B rigidity matrix introduced before. After that, we use these results to provide a novel relation between the arising interaction and internal forces and we give conditions on the agent force distribution for cooperative manipulation free from internal forces.
