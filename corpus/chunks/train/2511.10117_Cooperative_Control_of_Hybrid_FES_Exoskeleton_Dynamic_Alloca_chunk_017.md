Remark 7 (Hybrid System Saturation and Anti-Windup). High-level control should account for the cumulative attainable sets of the two actuators $( \mathbb { A } _ { F } \cup \mathbb { A } _ { E } )$ . Otherwise, it may lead to control saturation, necessitating the implementation of an anti-windup mechanism [28], [29]. □
