Interesting future directions include: 1) investigating the closed-loop performance of the DDPC schemes with various regularizers, 2) characterizing the relationship between the performance of different DDPC variants and the degree of system nonlinearity, and 3) developing online trajectory library updated algorithms to ensure control performance for nonlinear systems, while preserving real-time efficiency.
