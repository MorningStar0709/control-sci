# 6 Numerical examples

We demonstrate the efficacy of our method on three systems. We first find local conic bounds for a onedimensional nonlinear system. Here, we must compare to bounds found through ad-hoc ingenuity because we know of no existing, similar technique to systematically seek generic conic bounds of nonlinear systems. Next, we determine the local $\mathcal { L } _ { 2 }$ gain of a two-dimensional pendulum. Finally, we compare our results to that of [25] for a three-dimensional polynomial system.
