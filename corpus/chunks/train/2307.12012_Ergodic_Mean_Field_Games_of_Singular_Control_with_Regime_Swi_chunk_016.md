and we define the value function

$$v (x, i; \theta) := \inf _ {\tau \in \mathcal {T}} \sup _ {\sigma \in \mathcal {T}} \widehat {J} (x, i; \tau , \sigma , \theta) = \sup _ {\sigma \in \mathcal {T}} \inf _ {\tau \in \mathcal {T}} \widehat {J} (x, i; \tau , \sigma , \theta). \tag {3.6}$$

$$\mathcal {C} ^ {\theta} := \{(x, i) \in \mathcal {O}: k _ {2} < v (x, i; \theta) < k _ {1} \},$$
