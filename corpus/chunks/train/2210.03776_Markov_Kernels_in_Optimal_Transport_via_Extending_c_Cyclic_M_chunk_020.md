# 4.4. A Note on Geodesics

An interesting relationship between optimal transport and stochastic processes that deserves to be further explored is the relationship between the family of transition kernels generated by a geodesic in Wasserstein space and the Chapman-Kolmogorov equation for stochastic processes. Because path-lines of geodesics may cross in Wasserstein spaces when $p \geq 2$ , the kernels associated will form an inhomogenous stochastic process.
