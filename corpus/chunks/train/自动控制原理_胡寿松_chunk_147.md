# (2) 临界阻尼单位斜坡响应

由于 $\zeta=1$ ，故单位斜坡响应可表示为

$$c (t) = t - \frac {2}{\omega_ {n}} + \frac {2}{\omega_ {n}} \Big (1 + \frac {1}{2} \omega_ {n} t \Big) \mathrm{e} ^ {- \omega_ {n} t}, \qquad t \geqslant 0 \tag {3-33}$$

由上式可见,稳态误差

$$e _ {s} (\infty) = \frac {2}{\omega_ {n}} \tag {3-34}$$

而误差响应

$$e (t) = \frac {2}{\omega_ {n}} \Big [ 1 - \Big (1 + \frac {1}{2} \omega_ {n} t \Big) \mathrm{e} ^ {- \omega_ {n} t} \Big ], \qquad t \geqslant 0 \tag {3-35}$$

若取 $5\%$ 误差带，则利用数值解法可以求出误差响应的调节时间近似为

$$t _ {s} = \frac {4 . 1}{\omega_ {n}} \tag {3-36}$$
