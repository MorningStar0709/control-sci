where $K _ { p }$ is a design proportionality constant. The desired pure pursuit flight path angle is computed from the relation

$$\varpi = \sin^ {- 1} [ (h _ {2} - h _ {1}) / R ]. \tag {P.2}$$

The desired flight path angle rate of change for the pure pursuit case is determined from the relation

$$\frac {d \gamma_ {1}}{d t} = K _ {\gamma} (\varpi - \gamma), \tag {P.3}$$

where $K _ { \gamma }$ is a design constant.
