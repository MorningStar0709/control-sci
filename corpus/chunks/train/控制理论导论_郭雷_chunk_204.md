$$[ \boldsymbol {e} _ {i}, \boldsymbol {e} _ {i} ] - 0, \quad [ \boldsymbol {e} _ {i}, \boldsymbol {e} _ {j} ] = - [ \boldsymbol {e} _ {j}, \boldsymbol {e} _ {i} ],$$

显见

$$
\mathrm{ad} _ {e _ {1}} = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l} 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & - 2 \end{array} \right], \quad \mathrm{ad} _ {e _ {2}} = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l} 0 & 0 & 1 \\ - 2 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array} \right], \quad \mathrm{ad} _ {e _ {3}} = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l} 0 & - 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 2 & 0 & 0 \end{array} \right],
$$

即如果 $\alpha = \alpha_{1}e_{1} + \alpha_{2}e_{2} + \alpha_{3}e_{3}\in \mathrm{sl}(2,\mathbb{R})$ 简记成 $\pmb {\alpha} = [\alpha_1\alpha_2\alpha_3]^{\mathrm{T}}$ 记 $\beta =$ $\operatorname {ad}_{e_i}(\alpha),i = 1,2,3,$ 则可用上述矩阵形式得到 $[\beta_1\beta_2\beta_3]^{\mathrm{T}} = \operatorname {ad}_{e_i}[\alpha_1\alpha_2\alpha_3]^{\mathrm{T}}.$

定义 3.2.11 Lie 代数 G 的 Killing 形式定义为

$$K (X, Y) = \operatorname{tr} (\mathrm{ad} _ {X} \mathrm{ad} _ {Y}), \quad X, Y \in \mathcal {G}. \tag {3.2.13}$$

定理 3.2.2 $^{[16]}$ Lie 代数 G 是可解的，当且仅当

$$K (X, Y) \equiv 0, \quad \forall X, Y \in \mathcal {G},$$

设 G 为有穷维的，记 $\{e_{1},\cdots,e_{n}\}$ 为 G 的一组基。对于这组基 Killing 矩阵定义为

$$M _ {k} = (K (e _ {i}, e _ {j})).$$

容易检验，Killing 矩阵的秩与基底选择无关，并且有如下定理.

定理 3.2.3 $^{[16]}$ 有穷维 Lie 代数 G 是半单的，当且仅当其 Killing 矩阵非奇异.
