# II. PRELIMINARIES AND PROBLEM FORMULATION

This section introduces the 3-DOF kinematics and dynamics of the Saab Seaeye Falcon AUV and formulates the augmented error model used by the controller. Owing to its thruster configuration, the vehicle is underactuated in roll and pitch; thus we restrict attention to horizontal-plane motion and trajectory tracking in the Earth-fixed frame, see Fig 1.
