# Example 5.3

Consider the minimization of a functional [33]

$$
\begin{array}{l} J = \left[ x _ {1} ^ {2} (k _ {f}) + 2 x _ {2} ^ {2} (k _ {f}) \right] \\ + \sum_ {k = k _ {0}} ^ {k _ {f} - 1} \left[ 0. 5 x _ {1} ^ {2} (k) + 0. 5 x _ {2} ^ {2} (k) + 0. 5 u ^ {2} (k) \right] \tag {5.3.30} \\ \end{array}
$$

for the second order system

$$
\begin{array}{l} x _ {1} (k + 1) = 0. 8 x _ {1} (k) + x _ {2} (k) + u (k) \\ x _ {2} (k + 1) = 0. 6 x _ {2} (k) + 0. 5 u (k) \tag {5.3.31} \\ \end{array}
$$

subject to the initial conditions

$$x _ {1} (k _ {0} = 0) = 5, \quad x _ {2} (k _ {0} = 0) = 3; \quad k _ {f} = 1 0, \text { and } \mathbf {x} (k _ {f}) \text { is free. } \tag {5.3.32}$$
