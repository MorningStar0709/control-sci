# 4 Results and Discussion

In this section, the results of the proposed method SINDy-TD3 will be presented after applying it to the bi-rotor case study. In the beginning, the results of the SINDy algorithm will be presented to see how it captures the system dynamics accurately in order to be used as a surrogate environment to train the TD3 agent. After that, the results of training the agent will be discussed, highlighting the bi-rotor’s performance in three different scenarios. The first one will be stabilizing the bi-rotor into different azimuth and pitch angles, the second and third one will be trajectory tracking with two different trajectories, sine wave trajectory and a square wave trajectory. Finally, all results will be compared to a bench mark of using TD3 only without using SINDy to highlight the importance of combining SINDy to deep reinforcement learning.
