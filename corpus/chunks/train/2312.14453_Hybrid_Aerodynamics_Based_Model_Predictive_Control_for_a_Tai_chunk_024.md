An HMPC with a reduced training dataset is introduced to evaluate the generalization performance of the proposed HMPC. The reduced training dataset only covers translational velocity up to 2m/s. The original dataset contains around 150k sampling points while the reduced one only contains 100k. The tracking RMSE is shown in Table IV by HMPC\*. The results show that HMPC performance degrades with the reduced training dataset but still outperforms NMPC, which indicates that the proposed approach is capable of generalize the dynamics beyond the data it was trained on. The tracked trajectory of HMPC\* is not plotted in previous figures since it is very similar to that of HMPC.
