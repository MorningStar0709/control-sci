# 5.1 Simulations under White Gaussian Noise

In this section, reference tracking simulations were performed under band-limited white Gaussian noise. White Gaussian noise was given to the system with 0.01 power and 0.1 sampling time. Comparative robustness analysis was performed by obtaining rise time, overshoot and settling time data of PID, Lyapunov-based and backstepping controllers. Fig. 6 shows reference tracking simulations under white Gaussian noise.
