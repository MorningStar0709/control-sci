$$\bar {\boldsymbol {x}} (z, t) = \boldsymbol {x} (z, t) - \int_ {0} ^ {z} \boldsymbol {K} (z, \zeta) \boldsymbol {x} (\zeta , t) \mathrm{d} \zeta , \tag {9}$$

with the kernel ${ \pmb K } ( z , \zeta ) \in \mathbb { R } ^ { 4 \times 4 }$ defined on the triangular domain $\mathcal { D } = \{ ( z , \zeta ) \in [ 0 , 1 ] ^ { 2 } | \zeta \le z \}$ , is applied, which maps the system (5) into the form

$$\partial_ {t} \bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {-} (z, t) = \boldsymbol {\Lambda} (z) \partial_ {z} \bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {-} (z, t) + \boldsymbol {A} _ {0} ^ {-} (z) \bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {-} (0, t) \tag {10a}\partial_ {t} \bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {+} (z, t) = - \boldsymbol {\Lambda} (z) \partial_ {z} \bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {+} (z, t) + \boldsymbol {A} _ {0} ^ {+} (z) \bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {+} (0, t) \tag {10b}\bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {+} (0, t) = - \bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {-} (0, t) \tag {10c}\bar {\boldsymbol {x}} ^ {-} (1, t) = \bar {\boldsymbol {u}} (t). \tag {10d}$$

In contrast to (5a) and (5b), the transport equations in (10a) and (10b) involve only local coupling through strictly lower triangular matrices

$$
\boldsymbol {A} _ {0} ^ {\mp} (z) = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} 0 & 0 \\ a _ {0} ^ {\mp} (z) & 0 \end{array} \right]. \tag {11}
$$

The new input $\bar { \mathbf { u } } ( t ) \in \mathbb { R } ^ { 2 }$ is introduced via the state feedback

$$\boldsymbol {u} (t) = \boldsymbol {B} ^ {- 1} \left(\bar {\boldsymbol {u}} (t) - \boldsymbol {x} ^ {+} (1, t) \right. \tag {12}\left. + \int_ {0} ^ {1} \boldsymbol {K} ^ {- - } (1, \zeta) \boldsymbol {x} ^ {-} (\zeta , t) + \boldsymbol {K} ^ {- +} (1, \zeta) \boldsymbol {x} ^ {+} (\zeta , t) \mathrm{d} \zeta\right)$$

to further simplify the system representation.

The kernel $\pmb { K } ( z , \zeta )$ is the solution of the kernel equations

$$\tilde {\boldsymbol {\Lambda}} (z) \partial_ {z} \boldsymbol {K} (z, \zeta) + \partial_ {\zeta} (\boldsymbol {K} (z, \zeta) \tilde {\boldsymbol {\Lambda}} (z)) = \boldsymbol {K} (z, \zeta) \boldsymbol {A} (z) \tag {13a}\left(\boldsymbol {K} ^ {- - } (z, 0) + \boldsymbol {K} ^ {- +} (z, 0)\right) \boldsymbol {\Lambda} (0) = \boldsymbol {A} _ {0} ^ {-} (z) \tag {13b}- \left(\boldsymbol {K} ^ {+ -} (z, 0) + \boldsymbol {K} ^ {+ +} (z, 0)\right) \boldsymbol {\Lambda} (0) = \boldsymbol {A} _ {0} ^ {+} (z) \tag {13c}\boldsymbol {K} (z, z) \tilde {\boldsymbol {\Lambda}} (z) - \tilde {\boldsymbol {\Lambda}} (z) \boldsymbol {K} (z, z) = \boldsymbol {A} (z) \tag {13d}$$
