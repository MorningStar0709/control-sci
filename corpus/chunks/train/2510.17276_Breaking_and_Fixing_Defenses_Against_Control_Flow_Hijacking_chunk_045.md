# C ABLATION: QUALITY OF GENERATED GRAPHS PER MODEL

We evaluate the stability of our graph generation process by generating 5 graphs per CFH-Hard task using each of the following models: o4-mini, GPT-4o, and GPT-4o-mini. Since determining equivalence between context-free grammars (CFGs) is undecidable, we employ proxy metrics based on the agent-graph composition and structure.
