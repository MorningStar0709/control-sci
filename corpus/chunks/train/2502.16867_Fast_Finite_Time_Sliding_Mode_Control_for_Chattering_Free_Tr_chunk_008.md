The mass matrix of the mechanical arm $M ( \theta )$ , consists of all terms in the class of equations (3) that are multiples of $\ddot { \theta }$ The gravitational term, $G ( \theta )$ , includes all the terms in equation (3) in which $^ { g , }$ the earth’s gravitational constant, appears.
