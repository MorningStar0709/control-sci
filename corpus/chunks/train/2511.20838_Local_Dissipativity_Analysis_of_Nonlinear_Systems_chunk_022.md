# 4.1 Storage function conditions

Theorems 12 and 13 give conditions for a CPA function or a combined CPA and quadratic function, respectively, adhere to the QSR dissipativity condition (5) across a region of the state space. Theorem 12 considers the case where the system dynamics make all input affine terms disappear at the origin, while Theorem 13 admits nonzero control affine terms at the origin. This distinction is important, as all error bounds in the optimization problem must be zero at the origin to prevent infeasibility. While this always occurs when the input term disappears, a modified quadratic and CPA storage function (which is quadratic at the origin) is needed for the nonzero case.
