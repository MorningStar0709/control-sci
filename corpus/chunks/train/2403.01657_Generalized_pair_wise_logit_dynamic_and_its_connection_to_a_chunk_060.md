# Proof of Proposition 3

We can follow Proof of Proposition 2.1 of Lahkar et al. (2023) with an adaptation to our case, especially the nonlinear term (15) not considered in this literature. We use Lemma 1 several times in the sequel.

Fix $A \in B$ and $t > 0$ . We have

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm{d} \mu (t , A)}{\mathrm{d} t} - \frac {\mathrm{d} \mu_ {N} (t , A)}{\mathrm{d} t} = I (\mu , A) - I _ {N} (\mu_ {N}, A) \tag {97} \\ = (I (\mu , A) - I _ {N} (\mu , A)) + (I _ {N} (\mu , A) - I _ {N} (\mu_ {N}, A)) \\ \end{array}
$$

We evaluate the last two terms of (97). For the first term in the right-hand side of (97), we have

$$I (\mu , A) - I _ {N} (\mu , A) = \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} a (z, x, \mu) \mu (t, \mathrm{d} z)\right) \mathrm{d} x - \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} a (x, y, \mu) \mathrm{d} y\right) \mu (t, \mathrm{d} x)\left. - \left\{\int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} a _ {N} (z, x, \mu) \mu (t, \mathrm{dz})\right) \mathrm{d} x - \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} a _ {N} (x, y, \mu) \mathrm{dy}\right) \mu (t, \mathrm{dx}) \right\} \right. \tag {98}= \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} (a (z, x, \mu) - a _ {N} (z, x, \mu)) \mu (t, d z)\right) d x\left. - \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} (a (x, y, \mu) - a _ {N} (x, y, \mu)) d y\right) \mu (t, d x) \right.$$

Then, we obtain

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} (a (z, x, \mu) - a _ {N} (z, x, \mu)) \mu (t, \mathrm{d} z)\right) \mathrm{d} x \leq \int_ {A} \int_ {\Omega} | a (z, x, \mu) - a _ {N} (z, x, \mu) | \mu (t, \mathrm{d} z) \mathrm{d} x \\ \leq \sup _ {x \in \Omega} \int_ {\Omega} | a (z, x, \mu) - a _ {N} (z, x, \mu) | \mu (t, \mathrm{d} z). \tag {99} \\ \leq \sup _ {x, z \in \Omega} | a (z, x, \mu) - a _ {N} (z, x, \mu) | \\ \end{array}
$$

We used $\int _ { A } \mathrm { d } x \leq 1$ . For any $x , z \in \Omega$ , we have

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| a (z, x, \mu) - a _ {N} (z, x, \mu) \right| \leq L _ {a} \left| \frac {U _ {N} (z , \mu) - U _ {N} (x , \mu)}{\eta} - \frac {U (z , \mu) - U (x , \mu)}{\eta} \right| \\ = \frac {L _ {a}}{\eta} \left| U _ {N} (z, \mu) - U _ {N} (x, \mu) - U (z, \mu) + U (x, \mu) \right|. \tag {100} \\ \leq \frac {L _ {a}}{\eta} \left(\left| U _ {N} (z, \mu) - U (z, \mu) \right| + \left| U _ {N} (x, \mu) - U (x, \mu) \right|\right) \\ \end{array}
$$
