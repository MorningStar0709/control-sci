# 15.2 Notes and References

The main results presented in this chapter are based on the work of Goddard and Glover [1993, 1994]. Other controller reduction methods include the stability-oriented controller reduction criterion proposed by Enns [1984a, 1984b]; the weighted and unweighted coprime factor controller reduction methods studied by Liu and Anderson [1986, 1990]; Liu, Anderson, and $\mathrm { L y }$ [1990]; Anderson and Liu [1989]; and Anderson [1993]; the normalized $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ controller reduction studied by Mustafa and Glover [1991]; the normalized coprime factor method studied by McFarlane and Glover [1990] in the $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ loop-shaping setup; and the controller reduction in the ν-gap metric setup studied by Vinnicombe [1993b]. Lenz, Khargonekar, and Doyle [1987] have also proposed another $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ controller reduction method with guaranteed performance for a class of $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ problems.
