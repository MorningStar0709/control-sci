# 2.1 Basic Concepts

(a) Increment of a Function: In order to consider optimal values of a function, we need the definition of an increment [47, 46, 79].

DEFINITION 2.1 The increment of the function $f$ , denoted by $\Delta f$ , is defined as

$$\Delta f \triangleq f (t + \Delta t) - f (t). \tag {2.1.4}$$

It is easy to see from the definition that $\Delta f$ depends on both the independent variable t and the increment of the independent variable $\Delta t$ , and hence strictly speaking, we need to write the increment of a function as $\Delta f(t, \Delta t)$ .
