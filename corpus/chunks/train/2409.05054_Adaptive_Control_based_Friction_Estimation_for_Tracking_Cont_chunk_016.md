# IV. EXPERIMENTAL EVALUATION

In this section, we present experiment results for excitation generation, parameter estimation and evaluation. First, we conduct experiments to generate excitations for friction estimation. Then, we estimate friction with our proposed adaptive controller and the generated excitation. Subsequently, we conduct experiments to evaluate the estimated friction model with different nominal controllers, including PD, PID, and ADRC [6], with random Fourier trajectories. For PD and PID controllers, we use an extended state observer [6] to estimate the velocity.
