$$\left[ \frac {\partial}{\partial \alpha} P (\alpha) \Big | _ {\alpha = 0} \right] _ {(s, s ^ {\prime})} = \sum_ {a \in \mathcal {A}} \left[ \frac {\partial}{\partial \alpha} \pi_ {\theta_ {\alpha}} (a \mid s) \Big | _ {\alpha = 0} \right]. P (s ^ {\prime} | s, a).$$

For any vector x $\in \mathbb { R } ^ { S }$ ,

$$\left\| \frac {\partial}{\partial \alpha} P (\alpha) \Big | _ {\alpha = 0} x \right\| _ {\infty} = \max _ {s \in \mathcal {S}} \left| \sum_ {s ^ {\prime} \in \mathcal {S}} \sum_ {a \in \mathcal {A}} \left[ \frac {\partial}{\partial \alpha} \pi_ {\theta_ {\alpha}} (a \mid s) \Big | _ {\alpha = 0} \right]. \mathsf {P} (s ^ {\prime} | s, a). x (s ^ {\prime}) \right|
