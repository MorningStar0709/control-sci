Statement of the Problem</td></tr><tr><td colspan="2">Given the plant as $\dot{\mathbf{x}}(t) = \mathbf{f}(\mathbf{x}(t), \mathbf{u}(t), t)$ ,the performance index as $J = S(\mathbf{x}(t_f), t_f) + \int_{t_0}^{t_f} V(\mathbf{x}(t), \mathbf{u}(t), t) dt$ ,and the boundary conditions as $\mathbf{x}(t_0) = \mathbf{x}_0; \quad \mathbf{x}(t_f)$  is freefind the optimal control.</td></tr><tr><td colspan="2">B. Solution of the Problem</td></tr><tr><td>Step 1</td><td>Form the Pontryagin  $\mathcal{H}$  function $\mathcal{H}(\mathbf{x}(t), \mathbf{u}(t), J_\mathbf{x}^*, t) = V(\mathbf{x}(t), \mathbf{u}(t), t) + J_\mathbf{x}^{*'} \mathbf{f}(\mathbf{x}(t), \mathbf{u}(t), t).$ </td></tr><tr><td>Step 2</td><td>Minimize  $\mathcal{H}$  w.r.t.  $\mathbf{u}(t)$  as $\left( \frac{\partial \mathcal{H}}{\partial \mathbf{u}} \right)_* = 0$  and obtain  $\mathbf{u}^*(t) = \mathbf{h}(\mathbf{x}^*(t), J_\mathbf{x}^*, t).$ </td></tr><tr><td>Step 3</td><td>Using the result of Step 2, find the optimal  $\mathcal{H}^*$  function $\mathcal{H}^*(\mathbf{x}^*(t), \mathbf{h}(\mathbf{x}^*(t), J_\mathbf{x}^*, t), J_\mathbf{x}^*, t) = \mathcal{H}^*(\mathbf{x}^*(t), J_\mathbf{x}^*, t)$ and obtain the HJB equation.</td></tr><tr><td>Step 4</td><td>Solve the HJB equation $J_t^* + \mathcal{H}(\mathbf{x}^*(t), J_\mathbf{x}^*, t) = 0$ .with boundary condition  $J^*(\mathbf{x}^*(t_f), t_f) = S(\mathbf{x}(t_f), t_f).$ </td></tr><tr><td>Step 5</td><td>Use the solution  $J^*$ , from Step 4 to evaluate  $J_\mathbf{x}^*$  and substitute into the expression for  $\mathbf{u}^*(t)$  of Step 2, to obtain the optimal control.</td></tr></table>

HJB procedure using a simple first-order system.
