# 1.3 Paper outline

The rest of this paper is organized as follows. Section 2 formulates the mixed $\mathcal { H } _ { 2 } / \mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ policy optimization. Section 3 examines the geometry of the feasible set and the structural properties of the cost function. Section 4 establishes our main global optimality result, along with characterizations of optimality conditions and the existence and uniqueness of stationary points. Section 5 presents the ECL framework underlying our analysis and develops a tailored ECL for mixed control. Section 6 reports numerical results, and Section 7 concludes the paper. Technical proofs are provided in the appendices.

We use $I _ { n }$ and $0 _ { m \times n }$ for the $n \times n$ identity and m × n zero matrices, respectively, omitting their subscripts when clear. For a subset S of a topological space, int(S) denote its interior and cl(S) its closure.
