The rendezvous condition is achieved when the radial distance between the chaser and target satellite reduces to 1 km which occurs at a mission duration of 29 hr 24 min as illustrated in Fig. 3. From 10, 000 km to 1 km, we have performed impulse maneuver using sliding mode controller. At this point the chaser satellite transitions into the docking phase, which is executed in two distinct phases to ensure safety and precision while avoiding any potential impact at the conclusion of the maneuver.
