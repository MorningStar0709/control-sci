# Example 5.11

Plot the Bode Asymptotic Magnitude Plot for the following single-pole transfer function:

$$G _ {2} (s) = \frac {2 0 0 0}{(s + 2 0 0 . 0)}$$

As above we can decompose this into

$$\mathrm{dB} (| G _ {2} (s) |) = d B (2 0 0 0) - d B (| (s + 2 0 0) |)$$

Thus the Bode plot of Figure 5.4 directly applies as long as we add dB(2000) = 66dB and we have

1. The intersection of the two asymptotes is at $\omega = 2 0 0 .$   
2. The low frequency (horizontal) asymptote is at

$$\left| G _ {2} (j \omega) \right| = 6 6 d B + (- 4 6 d B) = 2 0 d B$$

Where 66dB comes from the factor of 2000, and −46dB comes from 20 log(1/200). Drawing the asymptotes and drawing the smooth curve through 20dB − 3dB at ω = log(200).
