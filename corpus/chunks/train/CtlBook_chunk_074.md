# Example 2.11

We have derived the following transfer function:

$$G (s) = \left(\frac {B}{M _ {1} M _ {2}}\right) \frac {s + K / M}{s ^ {4} + \frac {M _ {1} + M _ {2}}{M _ {1} M _ {2}} B s ^ {3} + (1 / M _ {1} + 1 / M _ {2}) 2 B K s ^ {2} + \frac {2 B K}{M _ {1} M _ {2}} s + \frac {3 K ^ {2}}{M _ {1} M _ {2}}}$$

Check for errors using dimensional analysis: We ignore the normalizing term $\frac { B } { M _ { 1 } M _ { 2 } }$ because it is outside the additions. It could be right or wrong, but dimensional analysis won't help us with this particular term in the same simple way.
