# C. Integration with MPC

By leveraging the deep learning-based predictor, we integrate it into MPC to cope with the implicit dynamics. The goal of MPC in this work is to find a suitable control signal at through online optimization. In this work, given prediction and control horizons T , the optimization problem of MPC can be formulated as follows:

$$\min _ {\mathbf {a} (t)} C \left(\hat {s} ^ {t + 1: t + T}, \mathbf {a} ^ {t + 1: t + T}\right),$$

subject to

$$\dot {\hat {s}} ^ {t + 1} = f (s ^ {t}, \mathbf {a} ^ {t}), \tag {13}s _ {\min} \leq s ^ {t} \leq s _ {\max},\hat {\mathbf {a}} _ {\min} \leq \mathbf {a} ^ {t} \leq \hat {\mathbf {a}} _ {\max}.$$

For the optimization, we leverage Sequential Quadratic Programming (SQP) to solve the nonlinear optimization problem.

First, we need to define the Lagrangian function of nonlinear programming:

$$
\begin{array}{l} L \left(s ^ {t}, \mathbf {a} ^ {t}, \mu , \nu , \xi\right) = C \left(s ^ {t + 1: t + T}, \mathbf {a} ^ {t + 1: t + T}\right) \\ + \sum_ {\tau = t + 1} ^ {t + T} \lambda_ {\tau} \left(f \left(s ^ {\tau}, \mathbf {a} ^ {\tau}\right) - \hat {s} ^ {\tau}\right) \\ + \sum_ {\tau = t + 1} ^ {t + T} \mu_ {\tau} \left(s _ {\min} - s ^ {\tau}\right) + v _ {\tau} \left(s ^ {\tau} - s _ {\max}\right) \\ + \sum_ {\tau = t + 1} ^ {t + T} \xi_ {\tau} \left(\mathbf {a} _ {\min} - \mathbf {a} ^ {\tau}\right) + \xi_ {\tau} \left(\mathbf {a} ^ {\tau} - \mathbf {a} _ {\max}\right). \tag {14} \\ \end{array}
$$

where $\lambda _ { \tau }$ are the Lagrange multipliers for the equality constraints given by the system dynamics, $\mu _ { \tau }$ and $\nu _ { \tau }$ are the multipliers for the state constraints, and $\xi _ { \tau }$ are the multipliers for the control action constraints. The Karush-Kuhn-Tucker (KKT) optimality conditions of (13) are:

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left[ \begin{array}{c} \nabla_ {s} \mathcal {L} (s ^ {*}, \mathbf {a} ^ {*}, \mu^ {*}, v ^ {*}, \xi^ {*}, \zeta^ {*}) \\ \nabla_ {\mathbf {a}} \mathcal {L} (s ^ {*}, \mathbf {a} ^ {*}, \mu^ {*}, v ^ {*}, \xi^ {*}, \zeta^ {*}) \end{array} \right] \\ = \left[ \begin{array}{l} J _ {1} (s ^ {*}) ^ {T} + J _ {f} (s ^ {*}) ^ {T} \lambda^ {*} - \mu^ {*} + v ^ {*} \\ J _ {2} (\mathbf {a} ^ {*}) ^ {T} + J _ {f} (\mathbf {a} ^ {*}) ^ {T} \lambda^ {*} - \xi^ {*} + \zeta^ {*} \end{array} \right], \tag {15} \\ = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
