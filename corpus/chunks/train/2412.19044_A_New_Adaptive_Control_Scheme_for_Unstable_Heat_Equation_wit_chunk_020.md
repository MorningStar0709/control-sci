$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} z _ {N L} (\cdot , t) = z _ {N} (\cdot , t) - z _ {L} (\cdot , t) = \gamma \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {N} [ \tilde {w} _ {N, j} (t) - \tilde {w} _ {L, j} (t) ] \phi_ {j}, \\ \xi_ {N L} (t) = \xi_ {N} (t) - \xi_ {L} (t), \end{array} \right.
$$

with the understanding that $\tilde { w } _ { L , j } ( t ) \equiv 0$ when $j > L$ . Then $z _ { N L } ( \cdot , t ) , \check { w } _ { N L } ( \cdot , t )$ and $\xi _ { N L } ( t )$ satisfy

$$
