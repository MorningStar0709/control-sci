# 4.2 Proposed Combined Adaptive Control

The ideal values of the system matrices are obtained by performing transformations with the matrix $\mathbf { Y } _ { m } .$ Let us define a few terms utilizing these transformations that will be used in proposing new adaptation laws.

$$\mathbf {E} _ {1} = \mathbf {A} _ {r} - \mathbf {Y} _ {m} \left[ \mathbf {I} _ {n \times n} \mathbf {0} _ {n \times (1 + p)} \right] ^ {T}- \mathbf {Y} _ {m} \left[ \mathbf {0} _ {1 \times n} 1 \mathbf {0} _ {1 \times p} \right] ^ {T} \hat {\mathbf {k}} _ {x} ^ {T},\mathbf {E} _ {2} = \mathbf {b} _ {r} - \mathbf {Y} _ {m} \left[ \mathbf {0} _ {1 \times n} 1 \mathbf {0} _ {1 \times p} \right] ^ {T} \hat {k} _ {r}, \tag {27}
\mathbf {E} _ {3} = \mathbf {Y} _ {m} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {0} _ {p \times (n + 1)} & \mathbf {I} _ {p \times p} \end{array} \right] ^ {T}

- \mathbf {Y} _ {m} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {0} _ {1 \times n} & 1 \end{array} \mathbf {0} _ {1 \times p} \right] ^ {T} \hat {\boldsymbol {\theta}} ^ {T}.
$$
