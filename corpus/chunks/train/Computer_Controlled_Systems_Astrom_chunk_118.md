# Example 2.10 Diagonal form

Consider the motor in Example 2.3 with h = 1. Using the transformation

$$
T = \left( \begin{array}{c c} 1 & 1 \\ 1. 4 1 4 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)
$$

gives

$$
\tilde {\Phi} = \left( \begin{array}{c c} 1. 0 0 0 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0. 3 6 7 9 \end{array} \right) \quad \tilde {\Gamma} = \binom {1} {0. 8 9 4 0} \quad \tilde {C} = \left( \begin{array}{l l} 1 & - 0. 7 0 7 1 \end{array} \right)
$$
