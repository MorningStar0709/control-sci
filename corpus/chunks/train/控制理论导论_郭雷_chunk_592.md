# 8.1.1 考虑定常线性系统

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \dot {x} = A x + B u, \quad x \in \mathbb {R} ^ {n}, u \in \mathbb {R} ^ {m} \\ y = C x, \quad y \in \mathbb {R} ^ {p}. \end{array} \right. \tag {8.1.26}
$$

(i) 检验其能控性 Lie 代数为

$$\mathcal {F} = \operatorname{span} \{A x, B, A B, \dots , A ^ {n - 1} B \}, \tag {8.1.27}$$

而其强能控 Lie 代数为

$$\mathcal {F} _ {0} = \operatorname{span} \{B, A B, \dots , A ^ {n - 1} B \}; \tag {8.1.28}$$

(ii) 证明对线性系统，能控性，弱能控性，能接近性及强能接近性均等价.
