# B. Solving the trade execution problem in the case of non-vanishing targets

In Section 4.2 we provide a solution to the LQ stochastic control problem of Section 4.1 in the case of zero targets $\xi = 0$ and $\zeta = 0$ . In this appendix, we consider general terminal targets $\xi$ and general running targets ζ satisfying (15) and (18). We develop a rigorous solution theory which requires a transformation of the control problem removing the cross-terms (products between the control u and the state $\mathcal { H } ^ { u } )$ and relies on results from Kohlmann $\&$ Tang [41] combined with results from Sun et al. [52]. As an alternative, we outline in the next remark a solution approach that uses results from [52] and further arguments to be developed.
