# Exercise 3.3: Equivalent constraints

Assuming that S is a polytope and, therefore, defined by linear inequalities, show that the constraint $x \in \{ z \} \oplus S$ (on z for given x) may be expressed as $B z \leq b + B x .$ , i.e., z must lie in a polytope. If S is symmetric $( x \in S$ implies $- x \in S )$ , show that $x \in \{ z \} \oplus S$ is equivalent to $z \in \{ x \}$ ⊕ S .
