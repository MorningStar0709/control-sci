# III. EVENT-TRIGGERED CONTROL

In this section, we propose an ETS for the discrete-time system in Section III-A. as well as a novel looped-functional for discrete-time sampled-data system in Section III-B. The stability of the event-triggered system is analyzed in Sections III-C. Based on this, Section III-D derives a data-driven analysis and co-design method of the controller and the ETS without any explicit knowledge of the system matrices.
