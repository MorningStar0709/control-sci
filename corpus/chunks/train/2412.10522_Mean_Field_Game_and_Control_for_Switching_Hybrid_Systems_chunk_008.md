# 2.5 PDE system for MFC

Similarly, the optimality condition for MFC in Definition 2 can also be expressed as a system of PDEs. A key distinction in MFC is that when the control α changes, the density m also varies within the optimization formulation of the representative agent in (10). Hence, the value function of the centralized optimizer explicitly depends upon the population distribution.
