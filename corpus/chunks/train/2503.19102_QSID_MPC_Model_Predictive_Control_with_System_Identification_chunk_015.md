# A. DC Motor with Load

A DC Motor with control torque on the load is considered for this example. The dynamics of the system are as follows:

$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {x} _ {1} = x _ {2}. \\ \dot {x} _ {2} = \frac {K x _ {3} - c x _ {2} + u _ {2}}{J}, \tag {12} \\ \dot {x} _ {3} = \frac {u _ {1} - r x _ {3} - K _ {e} x _ {2}}{L}. \\ \end{array}
$$

We use the following values of the motor parameters: $J = 0 . 0 1$ , $c = 0 . 1 , K = 0 . 0 1 , K _ { e } = 0 . 0 1 , r = 1 , L = 0 . 5$ .. Substituting . . . .these parameters and time-discretizing with a discretization interval of 1 second yields:

$$
A = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 1. 0 0 0 & 0. 0 9 9 & 0. 0 4 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0. 0 1 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 0. 1 3 5 \end{array} \right], \quad B = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} 0. 0 4 8 & 8. 9 9 6 \\ 0. 0 8 3 & 9. 9 9 1 \\ 0. 8 6 4 & - 0. 0 8 3 \end{array} \right].
$$
