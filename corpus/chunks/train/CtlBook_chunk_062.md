By applying Equation 2.1 to each mass,

$$(M _ {1}) \quad M _ {1} \ddot {x} _ {1} + B (\dot {x} _ {1} - \dot {x} _ {2}) + K x _ {1} = f (t)(M _ {2}) \quad M _ {2} \ddot {x} _ {2} + B (\dot {x} _ {2} - \dot {x} _ {1}) = 0$$

Note that each EOM for mass i always begins with $M _ { i } { \ddot { x } } _ { i }$ and that in the B and K terms of the $i ^ { t h }$ EOM, $x _ { i }$ is always taken positive.
