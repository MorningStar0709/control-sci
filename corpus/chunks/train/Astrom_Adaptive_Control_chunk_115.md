# THEOREM 2.9 PE of filtered signals

Let the signal $u$ be persistently exciting of order $n$ . Assume that $A(q)$ is a polynomial of degree $m < n$ . The signal $v$ defined by

$$v (t) = A (q) u (t)$$

is then persistently exciting of order $\ell$ with $n - m \leq \ell \leq n$ . Assuming that $A$ is stable, the signal $w$ defined by

$$w (t) = \frac {1}{A (q)} u (t)$$

is persistently exciting of order n.
