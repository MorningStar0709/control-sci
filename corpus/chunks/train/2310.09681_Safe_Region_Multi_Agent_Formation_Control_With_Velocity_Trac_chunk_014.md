# 3.1.3. Nominal: Region formation control with velocity tracking

For velocity tracking we want the formation to track $v _ { d }$ once the formation enters the target region, i.e. after time $t _ { f } .$ This velocity signal and its derivative will be governed based on the objective at hand, like surveillance, rescue, or attack. Motivated by [45], we formulate the control law such that if the velocity signal is constant it only needs to be available to the stealthy leaders. On the other hand, if the velocity signal is time-varying then its derivative should be made available to all the agents. This gives us
