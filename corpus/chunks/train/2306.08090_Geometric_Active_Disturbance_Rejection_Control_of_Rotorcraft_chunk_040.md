# VII. NUMERICAL SIMULATIONS

In this section, we present several sets of numerical simulations, arranged in two subsections to validate the proposed ESO and ADRC. In subsection VII-A, we compare the proposed FFTS-ESO with the existing ESO [11] and DO [12] designs, on their disturbance estimation performance in different flight scenarios. Subsection VII-B describes the numerical simulation of the ADRC proposed in Section VI in different flight scenarios with the controlled dynamics numerically simulated by a geometric integrator[33].
