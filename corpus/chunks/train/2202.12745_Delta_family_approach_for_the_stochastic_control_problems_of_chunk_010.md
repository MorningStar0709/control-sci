$$V (T, x) = U (x),$$

Let $\pi ^ { * }$ be the solution of

$$\sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \left(\int_ {\mathbb {R} _ {+}} V (t + h, y) g _ {k} (y) d y\right) \frac {\partial \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi} g _ {k} (x)}{\partial \pi} = 0. \tag {2.11}$$

$$V (t, x) \approx \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \left(\int_ {\mathbb {R} _ {+}} V (t + h, y) g _ {k} (y) d y\right) \left(h \cdot \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi^ {*}} g _ {k} (x) + g _ {k} (x)\right). \tag {2.12}$$
