The reduced performance under stochastic turbulence (10.26% RMS reduction) compared to discrete gust (23.15% peak reduction) reflects the broadband frequency content of the Von Kármán spectrum: the controller effectively attenuates energy within its bandwidth but cannot reject high-frequency content that excites modes beyond the 8-mode ROM [Da Ronch et al., 2013b].
