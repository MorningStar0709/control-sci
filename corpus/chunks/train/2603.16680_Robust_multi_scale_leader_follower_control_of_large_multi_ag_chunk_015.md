# E. Coupled stability analysis

The analysis in Secs. III-A and III-D treats leaders and followers independently. We now account for the coupling: the followers’ Lyapunov derivative depends on the leaders’ tracking error $e ^ { \bar { L } }$ through the interaction kernel. Specifically, the $\mathcal { L } ^ { 2 } ( \Omega )$ norms of the density errors are given by

$$V _ {t} ^ {F} \leq \left[ D (1 - \alpha) \| g _ {1} \| _ {\infty} - 2 D - \alpha k _ {p} ^ {F} \right] V ^ {F}- \int_ {\Omega} e ^ {F} \left[ \bar {\rho} ^ {F} (f * e ^ {L}) \right] _ {x} \mathrm{d} x - \int_ {\Omega} e ^ {F} \left[ \rho^ {F} (f * e ^ {L}) \right] _ {x} \mathrm{d} x, \tag {33a}V _ {t} ^ {L} \leq - k _ {p} ^ {L} V ^ {L}. \tag {33b}$$

The two additional terms in (33a) capture the effect of the leaders’ transient on the followers. We bound them as

$$\left| \int_ {\Omega} e ^ {F} \left[ \bar {\rho} ^ {F} (f * e ^ {L}) \right] _ {x} d x \right| \leq J \sqrt {V ^ {F}} \sqrt {V ^ {L}}, \tag {34a}\left| \int_ {\Omega} e ^ {F} \left[ e ^ {F} (f * e ^ {L}) \right] _ {x} \mathrm{d} x \right| \leq S V ^ {F} \sqrt {V ^ {L}}, \tag {34b}$$

where

$$J = 2 \| \bar {\rho} _ {x} ^ {F} \| _ {2} \| f \| _ {2} + 2 \| \bar {\rho} ^ {F} \| _ {2} \| f _ {x} \| _ {2}, \tag {35a}S = \sqrt {2} \| f \| _ {2}. \tag {35b}$$

To recover such bounds we proceed similarly to (17) bounding convolutions with Young’s inequality [19].

Theorem 2: Consider the leader–follower system (4) with the followers’ control law (8)–(12) and the leaders’ control law (31), under the hypotheses of Theorem 1. If $k _ { p } ^ { L }$ is chosen sufficiently large, then the followers’ density error $e ^ { F }$ globally asymptotically converges to zero in $\mathscr { L } ^ { 2 } ( \Omega )$ .

Proof: Substituting the bounds (34) into (33a) yields

$$
\begin{array}{l} V _ {t} ^ {F} \leq \left[ D (1 - \alpha) \| g _ {1} \| _ {\infty} - 2 D - \alpha k _ {p} ^ {F} \right] V ^ {F} \\ + J \sqrt {V ^ {F}} \sqrt {V ^ {L}} + S V ^ {F} \sqrt {V ^ {L}}. \tag {36} \\ \end{array}
$$

Letting $\begin{array} { r } { \varepsilon = 1 / k _ { p } ^ { L } , } \end{array}$ , the coupled system becomes

$$V _ {t} ^ {F} = \left[ D (1 - \alpha) \| g _ {1} \| _ {\infty} - 2 D - \alpha k _ {p} ^ {F} \right] V ^ {F}+ J \sqrt {V ^ {F}} \sqrt {V ^ {L}} + S V ^ {F} \sqrt {V ^ {L}}, \tag {37a}\varepsilon V _ {t} ^ {L} = - V ^ {L}, \tag {37b}$$
