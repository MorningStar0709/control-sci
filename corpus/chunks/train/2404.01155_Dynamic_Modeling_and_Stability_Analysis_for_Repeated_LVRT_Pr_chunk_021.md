# B. Sufficient Stability Criterion of WT-GSC Switched System

In this subsection, by combining the WT-GSC switched system model (14) and the stability analysis method from Theorem 1, a sufficient stability criterion for the repeated LVRT switching process of the wind turbine is obtained.

When the wind turbine works under normal operation, $v _ { g } \geq$ vLVRT is satisfied, that is

$$- 1. 5 R i _ {d} + 1. 5 \omega L i _ {q} + v _ {\mathrm{LVRT}} - v _ {G} \leq 0. \tag {22}$$

In order to find the connection between the stable switching law (21) and the LVRT criterion (22), (21) is expanded as

$$
\boldsymbol {\xi} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {P} \left(\boldsymbol {b} _ {1} - \boldsymbol {b} _ {2}\right) = \left[ x _ {1} - x _ {1 e} \quad x _ {2} - x _ {2 e} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{l l} p _ {1 1} & p _ {1 2} \\ p _ {2 1} & p _ {2 2} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{l} d _ {1} \\ d _ {2} \end{array} \right] \tag {23}
$$

where $\mathbf { x } = [ x _ { 1 } , \ x _ { 2 } ] ^ { \mathrm { T } } , \ x _ { e } = [ x _ { 1 e } , \ x _ { 2 e } ] ^ { \mathrm { T } }$ , and $[ d _ { 1 } , \ d _ { 2 } ] ^ { \mathrm { T } } \ =$ $b _ { 1 } - b _ { 2 }$ .

Combining (22) and (23), the state variables $x _ { 1 }$ and $x _ { 2 }$ correspond to $i _ { d }$ and $i _ { q } ,$ respectively. Moreover, $_ { P }$ is a symmetric positive definite matrix with $p _ { 1 2 } ~ = ~ p _ { 2 1 }$ . It is obtained by the undetermined coefficient method that

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c c c} d _ {1} & d _ {2} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & d _ {1} & d _ {2} \\ 0 & 1 & - 1 & 0 \\ d _ {1} x _ {1 e} & d _ {2} x _ {1 e} & d _ {1} x _ {2 e} & d _ {2} x _ {2 e} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{l} p _ {1 1} \\ p _ {1 2} \\ p _ {2 1} \\ p _ {2 2} \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c} - 1. 5 R \\ 1. 5 \omega L \\ 0 \\ v _ {G} - v _ {\mathrm{LVRT}} \end{array} \right]. \tag {24}
$$

When the state equations of these two subsystems are different, we have $[ \dot { d } _ { 1 } , \ d _ { 2 } ] ^ { \mathrm { T } } \ \ne \ { \bf 0 }$ , then the rank of the coefficient matrix in (24) is $^ 3$ . Let $p _ { 1 1 } = p ,$ eliminating $p _ { 1 2 } ,$ p21, and p22 yields

$$
\boldsymbol {P} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} p & - \frac {1 . 5 R}{d _ {2}} - \frac {d _ {1} p}{d _ {2}} \\ - \frac {1 . 5 R}{d _ {2}} - \frac {d _ {1} p}{d _ {2}} & \frac {1 . 5 \omega L}{d _ {2}} + \frac {d _ {1} (1 . 5 R + d _ {1} p)}{d _ {2} ^ {2}} \end{array} \right]. \tag {25}
$$
