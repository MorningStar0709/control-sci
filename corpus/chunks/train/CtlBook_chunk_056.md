# 2.3 Constitutive Relations

Constitutive Relations are basic equations that dene our system components by relating one variable to another. For example, a resistor relates voltage to current by Ohm's Law, V = IR or a damper relates force to velocity, F = Bx˙ . Using the analogy between force and voltage, and velocity to current, we can classify variables as through or across types. Specically
