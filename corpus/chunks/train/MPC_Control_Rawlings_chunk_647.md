$$d _ {H} (\Phi (i), \Phi) \leq c d _ {H} (\Phi (0), \Phi) \gamma^ {i}$$

$$\Phi (i) = \tilde {A} \Phi (i - 1) + \tilde {B} \Psi\Phi = \tilde {A} \Phi + \tilde {B} \Psi$$
