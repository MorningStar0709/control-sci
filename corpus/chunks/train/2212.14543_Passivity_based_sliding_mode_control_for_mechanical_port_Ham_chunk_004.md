The remainder of this paper is organized as follows. First, we introduce passivity-based control, kinetic-potential energy shaping method, and sliding mode control in Section 2. A generalized version of KPES and a new PBSMC method is proposed in Section 3. Section 4 shows application of the proposed method to trajectory tracking control problems. Moreover, the robustness of the proposed controller against matched and unmatched disturbances is investigated in Section 5. The effectiveness of the proposed controller is shown through numerical examples in Section 6. Finally, we conclude this paper in Section 7.
