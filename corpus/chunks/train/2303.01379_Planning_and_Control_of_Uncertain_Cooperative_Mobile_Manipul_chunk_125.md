# 2.3 Rolling Contacts

In this section we relax the assumption on the rigid grasping points. In particular, we assume that the robotic agents are connected to the object in terms of rolling contacts. As discussed before, this more natural approach to cooperative manipulation allows for a wider class of objects to be manipulated, and allows for modular manipulation scenarios where robots can be swapped to adjust the grasp online.
