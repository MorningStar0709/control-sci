$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} s} \langle u (s), x ^ {*} \rangle = \frac {\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} s} \langle x (s), T ^ {*} (t - s) x ^ {*} \rangle \\ = \langle x (s), T ^ {*} (t - s) A ^ {*} x ^ {*} \rangle + \left\langle x (s), \frac {\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} s} \left(T ^ {*} (t - s) x ^ {*}\right) \right\rangle \\ = \langle x (s), T ^ {*} (t - s) A ^ {*} x ^ {*} \rangle - \langle x (s), T ^ {*} (t - s) A ^ {*} x ^ {*} \rangle = 0. \\ \end{array}
$$

因此我们有 $\langle u(s), x^* \rangle = 0, 0 < s < t$ . 但是 $\overline{\mathcal{D}(A^*)} = X^*$ , 故我们有

$$u (s) = T (t - s) x (s) = 0, \quad 0 < s < t.$$

这样，从 $u(s)$ 的连续性可知 $u(t) = 0$ 。最后我们得到 $x(t) = 0, \forall t \in [0, t_1]$ 。
