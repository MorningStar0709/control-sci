Thus far, our main objective has been to demonstrate that the solvability of the considered McKean-Vlasov Control Problem, namely Problem MFT, is equivalent to that of both Problem 1 and Problem 2. Moreover, we show that, under Assumption 1, the sum of the optimal controls corresponding to Problem 1 and Problem 2 yields the optimal control for Problem MFT. Problem 1 and Problem 2 can be solved using classical methods. In the next section, we employ a variational method to complete our analysis. Moreover, while the standard dynamic programming method can clearly be applied to Problem 1, in Appendix B we show that it can also be used to address Problem 2.
