# D. Attitude Controller

The attitude controller takes the desired roll and pitch angles $( \phi _ { d }$ and $\theta _ { d } )$ that are generated by the position controller in Equation (39) with the desired yaw angle $\psi _ { d }$ which is set by the designer according to the configuration of entire system to calculate the rolling, pitching, and yawing moments according to the general controller law in Equation (27) as follows:

$$
\begin{array}{l} U _ {2} = J _ {x} \left(\ddot {\phi} _ {d} - \frac {(J _ {y} - J _ {z})}{J _ {x}} \dot {\theta} \dot {\psi} + \frac {k _ {r _ {d r a g _ {\phi}}}}{J _ {x}} \dot {\phi} - \frac {d _ {r _ {1}}}{J _ {x}}\right) \\ + J _ {x} \left((\xi + \eta a | e _ {\phi} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {\phi} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {\phi} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {\phi})\right) \\ U _ {3} = J _ {y} \left(\ddot {\theta} _ {d} - \frac {(J _ {z} - J _ {x})}{J _ {y}} \dot {\phi} \dot {\psi} + \frac {k _ {r _ {d r a g _ {\theta}}}}{J _ {y}} \dot {\theta} - \frac {d _ {r _ {2}}}{J _ {y}}\right) \\ + J _ {y} \left((\xi + \eta a | e _ {\theta} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {\theta} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {\theta} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {\theta})\right) \\ U _ {4} = J _ {z} \left(\ddot {\psi} _ {d} - \frac {(J _ {x} - J _ {y})}{J _ {z}} \dot {\phi} \dot {\theta} + \frac {k _ {r _ {d r a g _ {\psi}}}}{J _ {z}} \dot {\psi} - \frac {d _ {r _ {3}}}{J _ {z}}\right) \\ + J _ {z} \left((\xi + \eta a | e _ {\psi} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {\psi} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {\psi} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {\psi})\right) \tag {40} \\ \end{array}

\begin{array}{l} U _ {4} = J _ {z} \left(\ddot {\psi} _ {d} - \frac {(J _ {x} - J _ {y})}{J _ {z}} \dot {\phi} \dot {\theta} + \frac {k _ {r _ {d r a g _ {\psi}}}}{J _ {z}} \dot {\psi} - \frac {d _ {r _ {3}}}{J _ {z}}\right) \\ + J _ {z} \left((\xi + \eta a | e _ {\psi} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {\psi} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {\psi} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {\psi})\right) \tag {40} \\ \end{array}
$$
