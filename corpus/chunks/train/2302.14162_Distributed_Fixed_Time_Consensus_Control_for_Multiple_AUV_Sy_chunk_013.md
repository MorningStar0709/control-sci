$$u (\tau (t)) = \left[ \bar {J} \left(\eta_ {2}\right) \bar {\Pi} \right] ^ {- 1} \left(- \bar {\Phi} (v, \eta) v + \mathbf {1} _ {N} \otimes \ddot {\eta} ^ {d} + \dot {\alpha} _ {s} + \tau^ {\prime}\right), \tag {27}$$

$$\tau^ {\prime} = - \beta_ {s} \operatorname{sign} \left(s _ {2}\right) - k _ {8} s _ {2} - k _ {9} s _ {2} ^ {\gamma} - k _ {1 0} s _ {2} ^ {\iota}, \tag {28}$$

$$

$$
