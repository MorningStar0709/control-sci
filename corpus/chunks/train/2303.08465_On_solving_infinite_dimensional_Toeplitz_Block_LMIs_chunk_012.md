# IV. MAIN RESULTS

COP is an infinite-dimensional problem in the sense that the dimension of the involved entries and unknowns is infinite. The main objective here is to show how COP can be solved up to an arbitrarily small error. The main results are presented in three steps. The first one defines what is a truncated TBLMI. The second step shows how to combine truncation and banded approximation operations in order to obtain a finite-dimensional problem. The third step proves that the solution of COP can be recovered up to an arbitrary small error by solving a finite optimization problem.
