# Exercise 6.14: A matrix inverse

Compute the four partitioned elements in the two-player feedback gain $\left( I - L \right) ^ { - 1 } \overline { { K } }$

$$\mathbf {u} ^ {\infty} = (I - L) ^ {- 1} \overline {{{K}}} x (0) \quad | \operatorname{eig} (L) | < 1$$

in which

$$
(I - L) ^ {- 1} \overline {{K}} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} I & - L _ {1} \\ - L _ {2} & I \end{array} \right] ^ {- 1} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} K _ {1} & 0 \\ 0 & K _ {2} \end{array} \right]
$$
