$$
\sigma_ {i j} [ t ] = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & \text { if } \left((\sigma_ {i j} [ t ^ {-} ] = 0) \cap \left(\| \boldsymbol {q} _ {i j} \| \geq R _ {c o m} - \varepsilon\right)\right) \\ & \cup \left((\sigma_ {i j} [ t ^ {-} ] = 1) \cap \left(\| \boldsymbol {q} _ {i j} \| \geq R _ {c o m}\right)\right), \\ 1, & \text { if } \left((\sigma_ {i j} [ t ^ {-} ] = 1) \cap \left(\| \boldsymbol {q} _ {i j} \| <   R _ {c o m}\right)\right) \\ & \cup \left((\sigma_ {i j} [ t ^ {-} ] = 0) \cap \left(\| \boldsymbol {q} _ {i j} \| <   R _ {c o m} - \varepsilon\right)\right), \end{array} \right. \tag {7}
$$

This hysteresis mechanism avoids constant switching of the dynamical network with multiple agents for edges close to $R _ { c o m }$ and helps preserve connectivity in reactive control schemes [24].

The final safe interaction controller for each agent i with maximum propulsion $U _ { m a x , i }$ is then defined as

$$\left(\pi_ {s a f e}\right) _ {i} (\boldsymbol {q}) = - \frac {\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {N} \nabla_ {\boldsymbol {q} _ {i}} \psi \left(\left\| \boldsymbol {q} _ {i j} \right\| _ {2}\right)}{\left\| \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {N} \nabla_ {\boldsymbol {q} _ {i}} \psi \left(\left\| \boldsymbol {q} _ {i j} \right\| _ {2}\right) \right\| _ {2}} U _ {\max, i} \tag {8}$$
