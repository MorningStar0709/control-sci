$$\sum_ {k _ {1}, \dots , k _ {n} = 0} ^ {\infty} \left(\int_ {\mathbb {R} _ {+} ^ {n}} V (t + h, \mathbf {y}) \prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} \left(y _ {i}\right) d \mathbf {y}\right) \frac {\partial}{\partial \pi} \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi} \left(\prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} \left(x _ {i}\right)\right) = 0. \tag {2.20}$$

Then inserting $\pi = \pi ^ { * }$ into (2.19) gives that

$$V (t, \mathbf {x}) \approx \sum_ {k _ {1}, \ldots , k _ {n} = 0} ^ {\infty} \left(\int_ {\mathbb {R} _ {+} ^ {n}} V (t + h, \mathbf {y}) \prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} (y _ {i}) d \mathbf {y}\right) \left(h \cdot \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi^ {*}} \left(\prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} (x _ {i})\right) + \prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} (x _ {i})\right).$$

Taking inner product to (2.12) with $\prod _ { j = 1 } ^ { d } g _ { m _ { j } } ^ { ( j ) } ( x _ { j } ) , m _ { j } = 0 , 1 , \ldots ; j = 1 , \ldots , n$ and denoting

$$V _ {\mathbf {m}} (t) := \int_ {\mathbb {R} _ {+} ^ {n}} V (t, \mathbf {x}) \prod_ {j = 1} ^ {d} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (x _ {j}) d \mathbf {x}, \quad m _ {j} = 0, 1, \ldots ; j = 1, \ldots , n,$$

we obtain that for $m _ { j } = 0 , 1 , . . . ; j = 1 , . . . , n$ ,

$$V _ {\mathbf {m}} (t) \approx V _ {\mathbf {m}} (t + h) + h \sum_ {k _ {1}, \dots , k _ {n} = 0} ^ {\infty} V _ {\mathbf {k}} (t + h) \int_ {\mathbb {R} _ {+} ^ {n}} \prod_ {j = 1} ^ {d} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (x _ {j}) \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi^ {*}} \left(\prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} (x _ {i})\right) d \mathbf {x}, \tag {2.21}$$

with $\pi ^ { * }$ being the solution of (2.20) which can be rewritten as

$$\sum_ {k _ {1}, \dots , k _ {n} = 0} ^ {\infty} V _ {\mathbf {k}} (t + h) \frac {\partial}{\partial \pi} \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi} \left(\prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} \left(x _ {i}\right)\right) = 0. \tag {2.22}$$

Using the orthogonality of the basis functions $g _ { j } ( x )$ , we can obtain that

$$V (t, \mathbf {x}) = \sum_ {k _ {1}, \dots , k _ {n} = 0} ^ {\infty} V _ {\mathbf {k}} (t) \prod_ {i = 1} ^ {d} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} \left(x _ {i}\right). \tag {2.23}$$
