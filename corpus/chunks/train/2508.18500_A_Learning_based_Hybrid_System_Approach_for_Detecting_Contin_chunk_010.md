# IV. TIME SERIES LEARNING FOR CONTINGENCY DETECTION WITHIN THE SHS FRAMEWORK

Recent advances in deep learning have demonstrated the effectiveness of attention-based architectures for modeling complex temporal dependencies in sequential data [26]–[30]. Among these, the Transformer has emerged as a powerful and flexible model, originally developed for natural language processing but now widely adopted in time series forecasting, anomaly detection, and classification tasks [28], [29]. Unlike recurrent neural networks (RNNs), which process sequences sequentially, Transformers operate on the entire sequence in parallel through a self-attention mechanism [31]. This allows the model to efficiently capture both short-term fluctuations and long-range dependencies.
