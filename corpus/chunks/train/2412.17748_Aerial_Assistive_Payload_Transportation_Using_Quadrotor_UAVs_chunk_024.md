# C. Position Controller

Using the controller designed in Equation (27), the virtual control inputs can be expressed as follows:

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} u _ {x} = & m _ {t} \ddot {x} _ {d} + k _ {l _ {d r a g _ {x}}} \dot {x} - d _ {l _ {1}} \\ & + m _ {t} \left((\xi + \eta a | e _ {x} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {x} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {x} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {x})\right) \\ u _ {y} = & \ddot {y} _ {d} + k _ {l _ {d r a g _ {y}}} \dot {y} - d _ {l _ {2}} \\ & + m _ {t} \left((\xi + \eta a | e _ {y} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {y} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {y} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {y})\right) \\ u _ {z} = & m _ {t} \ddot {z} _ {d} + m _ {t} g + k _ {l _ {d r a g _ {z}}} \dot {z} - d _ {l _ {3}} \\ & + (\xi + \eta a | e _ {z} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {z} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {z} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {z}) ] \end{array} \right. \tag {37}
$$

According to Equations (18) and (37), the total thrust $U _ { 1 }$ is given as follows:

$$
\begin{array}{l} U _ {1} = \frac {m _ {t}}{c \phi c \theta} \left(\ddot {z} _ {d} + g + \frac {1}{m _ {t}} k _ {l _ {d r a g _ {z}}} \dot {z} - \frac {d _ {l _ {3}}}{m _ {t}}\right) \\ + \frac {m _ {t}}{c \phi c \theta} \left((\xi + \eta a | e _ {z} | ^ {a - 1}) \dot {e} _ {z} + \lambda_ {1} S _ {z} + \lambda_ {2} s g n (S _ {z})\right) \tag {38} \\ \end{array}
$$

and the desired roll and pitch angles $( \phi _ { d }$ and $\theta _ { d } )$ can be calculated by utilizing the tangent function within limits $\left( - { \frac { \pi } { 2 } } , { \frac { \pi } { 2 } } \right)$ of Euler angles, as follows:

$$\theta_ {d} = \arctan \left(\frac {u _ {x} \cos (\psi_ {d}) + u _ {y} \sin (\psi_ {d})}{u _ {z}}\right), \tag {39}\phi_ {d} = \arctan \left(\cos (\theta_ {d}) \left(\frac {u _ {x} \sin (\psi_ {d}) - u _ {y} \cos (\psi_ {d})}{u _ {z}}\right)\right)$$
