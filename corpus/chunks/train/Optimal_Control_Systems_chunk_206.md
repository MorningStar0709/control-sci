# Linear Quadratic Optimal Control Systems II

In the previous chapter, we addressed the linear quadratic regulator system, where the aim was to obtain the optimal control to regulate (or keep) the state around zero. In this chapter, we discuss linear quadratic tracking (LQT) systems, and some related topics in linear quadratic regulator theory. It is suggested that the student reviews the material in Appendices A and B given at the end of the book. This chapter is based on $[6, 89, 3]^{1}$ .
