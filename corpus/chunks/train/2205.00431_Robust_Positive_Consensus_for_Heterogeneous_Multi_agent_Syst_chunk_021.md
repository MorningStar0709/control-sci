# 5 Simulation

In this section, we consider an eight-agent system to illustrate the effectiveness of our preceding designs. Suppose the system matrices are as follows:

$$
A _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} - 2 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & - 3 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & - 1 \end{array} \right], B _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{l} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array} \right], C _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{l} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}, D _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right], i = 1, 2, 7
$$

and

$$
A _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} - 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right], B _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \end{array} \right], C _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}, D _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right], i = 3, 4
$$

and

$$
A _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} 0 & 0 \\ 1 & - 3 \end{array} \right], B _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right], C _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}, D _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right], i = 5, 6, 8
$$

It can be verified by Lemma 1 that these agents are indeed positive. Suppose the communication graph is alternatively switching between $\mathcal { G } _ { 1 }$ and $\mathcal { G } _ { 2 }$ given in Fig. 2 every 10 seconds. Assumption 2 is then fulfilled.
