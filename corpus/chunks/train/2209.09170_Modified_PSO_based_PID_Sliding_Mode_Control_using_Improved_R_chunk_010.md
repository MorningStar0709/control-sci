# III. MODIFIED PARTICLE SWARM OPTIMIZATION

Particle swarm optimization (PSO) is the evolutionary computation technique proposed by Kennedy and Eberhart in 1995. Evolutionary algorithms are generic population based search approach, inspired by biological evolution i.e. reproduction, mutation, recombination and selection. Evolutionary Algorithms (EAs) are more elegant than traditional optimization techniques as they generate a population of potential solution rather than a single point solution. EAs have to proceed through several iterations and after each iterations, solutions having better fitness value are selected over poor solutions and offspring are generated under some rule using these selected solutions.
