$$Y (s) = \boldsymbol {C M} \left(s \boldsymbol {I} - \boldsymbol {A} _ {\mathrm{m}}\right) ^ {- 1} \boldsymbol {B} _ {\mathrm{m}} - \boldsymbol {C} \left(s \boldsymbol {I} - \boldsymbol {A} + \boldsymbol {B K}\right) ^ {- 1} \boldsymbol {M B} _ {\mathrm{m}} \tag {7.243}$$

最后，由于 $CM=C_{m}$ ，则有

$$Y (s) = \boldsymbol {C} _ {\mathrm{m}} \left(s \boldsymbol {I} - \boldsymbol {A} _ {\mathrm{m}}\right) ^ {- 1} \boldsymbol {B} _ {\mathrm{m}} - \boldsymbol {C} \left(s \boldsymbol {I} - \boldsymbol {A} + \boldsymbol {B K}\right) ^ {- 1} \boldsymbol {M B} _ {\mathrm{m}} \tag {7.244}$$

因此，在时域中，有

$$y (t) = y _ {\mathrm{m}} (t) - [ \text {由} K \text {控制的衰减暂态项} ] \tag {7.245}$$

这正是我们开始时提出的。
