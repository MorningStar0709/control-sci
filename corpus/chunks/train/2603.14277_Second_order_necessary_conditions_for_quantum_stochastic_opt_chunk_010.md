# 3 Main result

This section is dedicated to deriving the integral-type second order necessary condition for Problem (QOP) using the variational method.

Let $u ( \cdot ) \in \mathcal { U } ^ { 4 } [ 0 , T ]$ be an admissible control that corresponds to the state $x ( \cdot )$ . Set

$$\delta u (\cdot) = u (\cdot) - \bar {u} (\cdot) \quad \text { and } \quad \delta x (\cdot) = x (\cdot) - \bar {x} (\cdot). \tag {3.1}$$

For any admissible control $\boldsymbol { u } ( \cdot ) \in \mathcal { U } [ t _ { 0 } , T ]$ , let $x _ { 1 } ( \cdot )$ and $x _ { 2 } ( \cdot )$ be the corresponding solution to the following equations:

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} d x _ {1} (t) = \left\{D _ {x} (t) x _ {1} (t) + D _ {u} (t) \delta u (t) \right\} d t + \left\{F _ {x} (t) x _ {1} (t) + F _ {u} (t) \delta u (t) \right\} d W (t) \\ \quad + d W (t) \left\{G _ {x} (t) x _ {1} (t) + G _ {u} (t) \delta u (t) \right\}, \text {   in   } [ t _ {0}, T ], \\ x _ {1} (t _ {0}) = 0, \end{array} \right. \tag {3.2}
$$

and

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} d x _ {2} (t) = \left\{D _ {x} (t) x _ {2} + D _ {x x} (t) \left(x _ {1}, x _ {1}\right) + 2 D _ {x u} (t) \left(x _ {1}, \delta u\right) + D _ {u u} (t) (\delta u, \delta u) \right\} d t \\ \quad + \left\{F _ {x} (t) x _ {2} + F _ {x x} (t) \left(x _ {1}, x _ {1}\right) + 2 F _ {x u} (t) \left(x _ {1}, \delta u\right) + F _ {u u} (t) (\delta u, \delta u) \right\} d W (t) \\ \quad + d W (t) \left\{G _ {x} (t) x _ {2} + G _ {x x} (t) \left(x _ {1}, x _ {1}\right) + 2 G _ {x u} (t) \left(x _ {1}, \delta u\right) + G _ {u u} (t) (\delta u, \delta u) \right\}, \text { in } [ t _ {0}, T ], \\ x _ {2} (t _ {0}) = 0. \end{array} \right. \tag {3.3}
$$

Combining with Itˆo-isometry of Clifford stochastic integral in noncommutative space $L ^ { 2 } ( \mathcal { C } )$ , we have the following auxiliary result.

Lemma 3.1. Let assumptions (H1) and (H3) hold. Then

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \| \delta x (\cdot) \| _ {C _ {\mathbb {A}} ([ t _ {0}, T ]; L ^ {2} (\mathscr {C}))} \leq \mathcal {C} \| \delta u \| _ {L ^ {2} (t _ {0}, T; \mathcal {H})}, \\ \| x _ {1} (\cdot) \| _ {C _ {\mathbb {A}} ([ t _ {0}, T ]; L ^ {2} (\mathscr {C}))} \leq \mathcal {C} \| \delta u \| _ {L ^ {2} (t _ {0}, T; \mathcal {H})}, \\ \| x _ {2} (\cdot) \| _ {C _ {\mathbb {A}} ([ t _ {0}, T ]; L ^ {2} (\mathscr {C}))} \leq \mathcal {C} \| \delta u \| _ {L ^ {4} (t _ {0}, T; \mathcal {H})} ^ {2}, \\ \| \delta x (\cdot) - x _ {1} (\cdot) \| _ {C _ {\mathbb {A}} ([ t _ {0}, T ]; L ^ {2} (\mathscr {C}))} \leq \mathcal {C} \| \delta u \| _ {L ^ {4} (t _ {0}, T; \mathcal {H})} ^ {2}. \end{array} \right. \tag {3.4}
$$
