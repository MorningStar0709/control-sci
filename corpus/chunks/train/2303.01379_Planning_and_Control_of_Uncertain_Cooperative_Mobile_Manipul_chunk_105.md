# 2.2.6 Problem Statement - Constrained Transportation

We deal here with a slightly different problem, that is, the problem of cooperatively transporting an object to a desired constant pose, while complying to certain constraints. Such constraints consist of inter-robot collision avoidance, collision avoidance with obstacles, singularity avoidance, as well as robot velocity and torque saturation constraints.
