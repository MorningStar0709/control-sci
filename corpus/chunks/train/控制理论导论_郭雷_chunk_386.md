$$N _ {\lambda} (A) \stackrel {{\text { def }}} {{=}} \overline {{\operatorname{span}}} \bigcup_ {k = 1} ^ {\infty} \mathcal {N} ((\lambda I - A) ^ {k}).$$

证明 设 $x \in \mathcal{N}(\lambda I - A)$ , 并记 $u(t) = T(t)x$ , 则 $\dot{u}(t) = AT(t)x = \lambda u(t), u(0) = x$ . 由此 $T(t)x = \mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}x, \forall t \geqslant 0$ , 这表明 $\mathcal{N}(\lambda I - A) \subset \mathcal{N}(\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}I - T(t)), \forall t \geqslant 0$ . 今设 $k \in \mathbb{N}$ , 并假定 $\mathcal{N}((\lambda I - A)^k) \subset \mathcal{N}((\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}I - T(t))^k), \forall t \geqslant 0$ . 如果 $x \in \mathcal{N}((\lambda I - A)^{k+1})$ , 则 $(\lambda I - A)^k x \in \mathcal{N}(\lambda I - A) \subset \mathcal{N}(\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t} - T(t))$ , 这意味着 $(\lambda I - A)^k (\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}I - T(t))x = 0$ , 从而根据归纳假设, $(\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}I - T(t))x \in \mathcal{N}((\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}I - T(t))^k)$ . 这样我们证明了 $\mathcal{N}((\lambda I - A)^{k+1}) \subset \mathcal{N}((\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}I - T(t))^{k+1}), \forall t \geqslant 0$ . 于是依照归纳法, $N_{\lambda}(A) \subset N_{\mathrm{e}^{\lambda t}}(T(t)), \forall t \geqslant 0$ . 证毕
