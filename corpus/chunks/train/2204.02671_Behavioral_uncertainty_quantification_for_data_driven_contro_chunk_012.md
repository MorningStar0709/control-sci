Remark 5. We presented Corollary 2 for single-input singleoutput models for clarity of exposition, but the result holds for more general multi-input multi-output systems. Corollary 2 raises the natural open question of how to relate the L-gap to classical uncertainty models [18 – 21] including additive, multiplicative, and coprime factor uncertainties. This is left as an area of future work. △
