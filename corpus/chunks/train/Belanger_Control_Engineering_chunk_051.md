# Example 2.7 (Active Suspension)

Calculate the dc steady state for the suspension system of Example 2.2, with $y_{R}=0$ , $u^{*}=0$ .

Solution From Equation 2.20,

$$
\begin{array}{l} 0 = v _ {1} ^ {*} \\ 0 = v _ {2} ^ {*} \\ 0 = - \frac {K _ {1}}{M} x _ {1} ^ {*} + \frac {K _ {1}}{M} x _ {2} ^ {*} - \frac {D}{M} v _ {1} ^ {*} + \frac {D}{M} v _ {2} ^ {*} + \frac {K _ {1}}{M} x _ {1 0} - g \\ 0 = \frac {K _ {1}}{m} x _ {1} ^ {*} - \frac {K _ {1} + K _ {2}}{m} x _ {2} ^ {*} + \frac {D}{m} v _ {1} ^ {*} - \frac {D}{m} v _ {2} ^ {*} - \frac {K _ {1}}{m} x _ {1 0} + \frac {K _ {2}}{m} x _ {2 0} - g. \\ \end{array}
$$

The solution is

$$
\begin{array}{l} v _ {1} ^ {*} = v _ {2} ^ {*} = 0 \\ x _ {1} ^ {*} = x _ {1 0} + x _ {2 0} - \frac {M g}{K _ {1}} - \frac {m + M}{K _ {2}} g = 1. 1 5 6 \mathrm{m} \\ x _ {2} ^ {*} = x _ {2 0} - \frac {m + M}{K _ {2}} g = 0. 6 3 6 \mathrm{m}. \\ \end{array}
$$
