# 2.4.3 Electrical Circuit analysis is the same thing

A student in Electrical Engineering might feel unfamiliar working on masses, springs, and dampers, but the similarity runs very deep between electrical circuits composed of inductance-capacitance-resistance and mass-spring-damper systems.

In electrical circuits, Kirchov's Voltage Law (KVL) is directly analogous to d'Alembert's principle which we use to derive mechanical equations of motion.
