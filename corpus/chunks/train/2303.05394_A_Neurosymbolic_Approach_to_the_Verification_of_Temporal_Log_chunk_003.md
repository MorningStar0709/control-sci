The rest of this paper is as follows. In Section 2, we present the background, primary concepts with STL semantics and problem definition. In Section 3, we present the steps to characterize STL2NN. In Section 4 we classify the verification problem based on the involved activation functions and propose a verification method for each class. We also introduce a structure for formulation of verification problems and introduce our verification toolbox. Finally, we present several case studies and experimental results for our verification methods in Sections 4.3 and 5.1. We conclude with a discussion on related works in Section 6.
