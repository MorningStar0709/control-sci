Remark 2. However, Lemma 1 in [12] shows that this condition can be relaxed to existence of a quadratic bound on the maximum eigenvalue of $W ( x )$ , that is $\lambda _ { m a x } ( W ( x ) ) \leq$ $\| A x + B \| _ { 2 } ^ { 2 }$ for some matrix A and vector B.
