# Summary

The basic SOAS is simple to implement and can cope with large gain changes in the process. Result 10.1 shows that the SOAS will automatically adjust itself so that the amplitude margin is 2. However, the limit cycle in the SOAS is noticeable and can be disturbing. The introduction of lead network, gain changer, and dither can decrease the amplitude of the oscillation. The EEAS is a similar system in which a high-frequency signal is introduced externally.
