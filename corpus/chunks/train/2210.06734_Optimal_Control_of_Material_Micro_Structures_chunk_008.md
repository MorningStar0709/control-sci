# B. Discretized model for PDE control

The phase-field is discretized into a 2D grid of dimension $N \times N$ . Let $\phi _ { t } ^ { i , j }$ denote the phase field of the cell defined by grid location $( i , j )$ for all $i , j = \{ 1 , . . . , N \}$ , at time t.

From Eq. (1) and (3), we have the A-C Equation of the form

$$\frac {\partial \phi}{\partial t} = - M (4 \phi^ {3} + 2 T \phi + h - \gamma \nabla^ {2} \phi) \tag {4}$$
