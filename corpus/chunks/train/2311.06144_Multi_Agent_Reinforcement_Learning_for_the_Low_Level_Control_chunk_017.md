# D. Integral Terms for Steady-State Error

RL-based quadrotor low-level control often suffers from steady-state errors [19]. To address this and also to improve robustness, an integral term is formulated as

$$\dot {e} _ {I _ {x}} = - \alpha e _ {I _ {x}} + e _ {x}. \tag {22}$$

with $\alpha > 0$ chosen to mitigate the integral windup.
