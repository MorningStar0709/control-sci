$$
\begin{array}{l} \operatorname{rank} \boldsymbol {V} ^ {\prime} = \operatorname{rank} \left[ (\boldsymbol {C P}) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \quad \left(\boldsymbol {P} ^ {- 1} \boldsymbol {A P}\right) ^ {\mathrm{T}} (\boldsymbol {C P}) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \quad \left(\left(\boldsymbol {P} ^ {- 1} \boldsymbol {A P}\right) ^ {2}\right) ^ {\mathrm{T}} (\boldsymbol {C P}) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \right. \\ \dots \quad ((P ^ {- 1} A P) ^ {n - 1}) ^ {T} (C P) ^ {T} ] \\ = \operatorname{rank} \left[ \begin{array}{l l l l l} \boldsymbol {P} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & \boldsymbol {P} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {A} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & \boldsymbol {P} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \left(\boldsymbol {A} ^ {2}\right) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & \dots & \boldsymbol {P} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \left(\boldsymbol {A} ^ {n - 1}\right) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \end{array} \right] \\ = \operatorname{rank} \boldsymbol {P} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \left[ \begin{array}{l l l l l} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & \boldsymbol {A} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & (\boldsymbol {A} ^ {2}) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & \dots & (\boldsymbol {A} ^ {n - 1}) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \boldsymbol {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \end{array} \right] \\ = \operatorname{rank} \left[ \begin{array}{l l l l l} \mathbf {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & \mathbf {A} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \mathbf {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & (\mathbf {A} ^ {2}) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \mathbf {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} & \dots & (\mathbf {A} ^ {n - 1}) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \mathbf {C} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \end{array} \right] = \operatorname{rank} \mathbf {V} \\ \end{array}
$$

可见，变换后与变换前系统的可观测性矩阵的秩相等，故系统的可观测性不变。
