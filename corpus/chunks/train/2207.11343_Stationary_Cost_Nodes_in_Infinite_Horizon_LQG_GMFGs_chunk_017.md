$$m ^ {\alpha} = m, \quad \text { for some } \quad m \neq 0, m \in R. \tag {44}$$

Proposition 7. Assume that assumptions $( \mathrm { A 1 } ) – ( \mathrm { A 2 } ) – ( \mathrm { A 3 } )$ hold. The equilibrium costs admit the following representation, for every $\alpha \in [ 0 , 1 ]$ ,

$$J (u ^ {\alpha}, z) = \pi \left(\nu^ {2} + m ^ {2} + \frac {\sigma^ {2}}{\rho}\right) - 2 m ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \bar {g} (\alpha , \beta) d \beta- m ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \tilde {g} (\alpha , \beta | \alpha) d \beta ,$$

where the introduced finite-rank graphons $\bar { g } ( \cdot , \cdot )$ and $\tilde { g } ( \cdot , \cdot \mid \alpha )$ with $\alpha \in [ 0 , 1 ]$ are defined for all $( \epsilon , \beta ) \in [ 0 , 1 ] \times$ [0, 1] by

$$\bar {g} (\epsilon , \beta) := \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {L} \bar {\lambda} _ {k} f _ {k} (\epsilon) f _ {k} (\beta), \tag {45}\tilde {g} (\epsilon , \beta | \alpha) := \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {L} \tilde {\lambda} _ {k} ^ {\alpha} f _ {k} (\epsilon) f _ {k} (\beta), \tag {46}$$

$$\bar {\lambda} _ {k} = \frac {\lambda_ {k}}{\theta (\lambda_ {k}) + \theta (0)},\tilde {\lambda} _ {k} ^ {\alpha} := \sum_ {\ell = 1} ^ {L} f _ {\ell} (\alpha) \langle 1, f _ {\ell} \rangle \left(\frac {\rho}{\theta (\lambda_ {\ell}) + \theta (\lambda_ {k})} - 2\right)\left(\frac {1}{\rho} \lambda_ {k} \lambda_ {\ell} - \frac {b ^ {2}}{\rho r} \bar {\lambda} _ {k} \bar {\lambda} _ {\ell}\right).$$
