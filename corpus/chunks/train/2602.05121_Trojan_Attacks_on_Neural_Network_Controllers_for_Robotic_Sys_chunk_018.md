# Controller Model

To imitate the geometric controller, we train a fully connected multi-layer perceptron (MLP). The network takes as input the robot state and the desired position:

$$\left(x _ {r}, y _ {r}, \theta_ {r}, x _ {d}, y _ {d}\right), \tag {10}$$

and outputs the corresponding wheel velocities $( \omega _ { l } , \omega _ { r } )$ .
