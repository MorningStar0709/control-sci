$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} - a > \frac {3}{4} | b | + \frac {2}{4} | c | + 3 + \frac {3}{4} | a | + \frac {2}{4} | d - b | + \frac {1}{4} | e - c |, \\ - f - 1 > \frac {1}{4} | b | + \frac {2}{4} | c | + 1 + \frac {1}{4} | a | + \frac {2}{4} | d - b | + \frac {3}{4} | e - c |. \end{array} \right. \tag {8.6.47}
$$

式(8.6.47)的一个特殊的解为 $a = -25, b = 4, c = 0, d = 4, e = 0, f = -10$ . 于是

$$
\psi = \left[ \begin{array}{c} - 2 5 z _ {1} ^ {2} + 4 z _ {1} z _ {2} \\ 4 z _ {1} ^ {2} - 1 0 z _ {2} ^ {2} \end{array} \right].
$$

为镇定线性部分，可选择 $a_1^1 = -1, a_2^1 = -2, a_1^2 = -1$ 。再由式(8.6.31)得到

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} u _ {1} = - \frac {f _ {1} (x , z)}{g _ {1} (x , z)} + \frac {1}{g _ {1} (x , z)} (- x _ {1} - 2 x _ {2} - 2 5 z _ {1} ^ {2} + 4 z _ {1} z _ {2}), \\ u _ {2} = - \frac {f _ {2} (x , z)}{g _ {2} (x , z)} + \frac {1}{g _ {2} (x , z)} (- x _ {3} + 4 z _ {1} ^ {2} - 1 0 z _ {2} ^ {2}). \end{array} \right.
$$
