# 6 Related Work & Conclusions

Related work. Safety verification of NNCS is well studied in the literature. We can classify these works in two categories. One group addresses open loop control systems. The authors in [25] present a verification technique based on Satisfiability Modulo Theories (SMT). They extend simplex to handle ReLU activation functions and propose an efficient verification for ReLU networks called ReLUplex. They also propose in [26] another technique based on SMT called Marabou, which is not restricted on ReLU activation function.
