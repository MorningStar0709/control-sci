# 10.8 IMPLEMENTING CONTROL SYSTEMS

We end this chapter with a brief discussion on some of the practical issues associated with implementing feedback controllers in physical systems. Much of the analysis in the previous sections has involved a barrage of controller transfer functions, which may lead the reader to wonder how “controller design” via root locus or Bode diagrams relates to the actualization of a control scheme in a physical system. This brief section attempts to explain how particular controllers and feedback structures are implemented as a combination of hardware and software.
