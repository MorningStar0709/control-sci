Proposition 1. Suppose that $p = 1$ and let all eigenvalues of Λ be real and such that

$$\min _ {\varsigma \in \sigma (\Lambda)} | \varsigma | \geq \max _ {\varphi \in \sigma (A)} | \varphi |. \tag {53}$$

Then, it holds that $\| \mathcal { G } _ { v } \| _ { \infty } = 1 .$

Under the assumptions of Proposition 1, if a scalar $\delta _ { v } \geq 0$ is known such that $\| v ( \cdot ) \| _ { 2 , [ 0 , T ] } ^ { 2 } \leq \delta _ { v }$ , we obtain

$$\left\| d _ {v} (\cdot) \right\| _ {2, [ 0, T ]} \leq \sqrt {\delta_ {v}}. \tag {54}$$
