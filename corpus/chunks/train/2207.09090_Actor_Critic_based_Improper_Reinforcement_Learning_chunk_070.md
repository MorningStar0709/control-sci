$$\frac {\partial}{\partial \theta} V ^ {\pi_ {\theta}} (\mu) = \frac {1}{1 - \gamma} \sum_ {s \in \mathcal {S}} d _ {\mu} ^ {\pi_ {\theta}} (s) \sum_ {a \in \mathcal {A}} \frac {\partial \pi_ {\theta} (a | s)}{\partial \theta} Q ^ {\pi_ {\theta}} (s, a).$$
