# Resistance R

Recall equation (12.1).

$$V = I R + \frac {\omega}{K _ {v}}$$

When the motor is stalled, $\omega = 0$ .

$$V = I _ {s t a l l} RR = \frac {V}{I _ {\text { stall }}} \tag {12.5}$$

where $I _ { s t a l l }$ is the stall current of the motor and V is the voltage applied to the motor at stall.
