# G. Simulation and Visualization

The trained agent is evaluated using a two-vehicle simulation implemented in Pygame. The lead vehicle follows a sinusoidal acceleration profile:

$$a _ {l} (t) = 0. 8 \sin (0. 2 t) - 0. 5 \sin (0. 0 5 t), \tag {17}$$

while the ego vehicle applies learned control $u _ { t }$ . The simulator visualizes spacing error, velocities, and acceleration in real time for qualitative assessment of safety and smoothness.
