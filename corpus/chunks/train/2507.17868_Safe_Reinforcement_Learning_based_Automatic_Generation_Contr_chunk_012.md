# A. CBF-based safety

An erroneous AGC reference power signal to generator G1 in Area 1 (refer to Fig. 1) is depicted by the blue curve in Fig. 2(a). This erroneous AGC signal, if followed, would lead to a breach of the power system’s safety set by causing an excess of power supply over demand, thereby inducing a frequency deviation in the two areas beyond 0.5 Hz. A potential consequence could be the tripping of generators, resulting in system instability or cascading failure. The CBF preempts the violation of safety by adjusting the AGC signal minimally, as illustrated by the leveling of the blue curve in Fig. 2(a). The adjusted AGC signal is computed per (6).
