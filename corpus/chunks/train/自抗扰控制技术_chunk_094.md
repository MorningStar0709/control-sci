$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} d = h ^ {2} r, a _ {0} = h x _ {2} \\ y = x _ {1} + a _ {0}, z = y + a _ {0} \\ a = y + a _ {0} (| a _ {0} | / d - 1) / 2 \\ \text { if   } | z | \leqslant d \text {   and   } | y | \leqslant d) \\ \text { then } \\ u = - r s a t (z, d) \\ \text { elseif } \\ u = - r s i g n (a) \\ \text { endif } \end{array} \right. \tag {2.7.30}
$$

利用符号函数又可以改写成

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} d = h ^ {2} r, a _ {0} = \hbar x _ {2} \\ y = x _ {1} + a _ {0}, z = y + a _ {0} \\ a = y + a _ {0} (| a _ {0} | / d - 1) / 2 \\ s _ {y} = (\operatorname{sign} (y - d) - \operatorname{sign} (y + d)) / 2 \\ s _ {z} = (\operatorname{sign} (z - d) - \operatorname{sign} (z + d)) / 2 \\ u = - r ((z - \operatorname{sign} (z) - \operatorname{sign} (a)) s _ {y} s _ {z} + \operatorname{sign} (z) + \operatorname{sign} (a)) \end{array} \right. \tag {2.7.31}
$$

式(2.7.31)的运算只含线性运算和符号运算．可以作为公式(2.7.23)的线性近似公式.
