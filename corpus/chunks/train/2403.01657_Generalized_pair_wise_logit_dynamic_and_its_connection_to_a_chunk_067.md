# C.1 Discretization of MFG system

The MFG system to be solved has the HJB equation

$$\frac {\partial \Phi (t , x)}{\partial t} = \int_ {\Omega} \frac {\Phi (t , x) - \Phi (t , y)}{1 + e _ {\kappa} \left(\frac {\Phi (t , x) - \Phi (t , y)}{\eta}\right)} \mathrm{d} y + \int_ {\Omega} F \left(\frac {1}{1 + e _ {\kappa} \left(\frac {\Phi (t , x) - \Phi (t , y)}{\eta}\right)}\right) \mathrm{d} y - U (x, \mu (t, \cdot)) \tag {118}$$

for any $t < T$ and $x \in \Omega$ , subject to the terminal condition $\Phi \big ( T , \cdot \big ) = 0$ , and the FP equation

$$\frac {\mathrm{d} \mu (t , A)}{\mathrm{d} t} = \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} a ^ {*} (z, x, \mu) \mu (t, \mathrm{d} z)\right) \mathrm{d} x - \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} a ^ {*} (x, y, \mu) \mathrm{d} y\right) \mu (t, \mathrm{d} x) \tag {119}$$

for any $t > 0$ and $A \in B$ ， subject to a prescribed initial condition $\mu ( 0 , \cdot ) \in \mathscr { P }$ .Here, we assume F of (35), and hence, $a ^ { * } \left( x , y , \mu \right) = F ^ { \prime \left( - 1 \right) } \left( \Phi \left( t , y \right) - \Phi \left( t , x \right) \right)$ and $G = U$ . The HJB and FP equations need to be solved backward and forward in time, respectively.

The discretization method of the MFG system is based on the finite difference method in Section 2.5, and hence we use the notations employed there. The FP equation (119) is discretized in space as

$$\frac {\mathrm{d} \hat {\mu} (t , \Omega_ {i})}{\mathrm{d} t}
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{N} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {N} a ^ {*} \left(x _ {j - 1 / 2}, x _ {i - 1 / 2}, \hat {\mu}\right) \hat {\mu} (t, \Omega_ {j}) - \left(\frac {1}{N} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {N} a ^ {*} \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, x _ {j - 1 / 2}, \hat {\mu}\right)\right) \mu (t, \Omega_ {i}), i = 1, 2, \dots , N \text {and} t > 0, \\ (= \Lambda (i, \hat {\mu}, \hat {\Phi})) \end{array} \tag {120}
$$

where

$$a ^ {*} \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, x _ {j - 1 / 2}, \hat {\mu}\right) = F ^ {\prime (- 1)} \left(\hat {\Phi} (t, x _ {j - 1 / 2}) - \hat {\Phi} (t, x _ {i - 1 / 2})\right). \tag {121}$$
