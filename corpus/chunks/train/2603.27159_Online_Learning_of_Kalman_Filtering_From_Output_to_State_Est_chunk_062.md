# B.3 Proof of Lemma 3.2

Unrolling (1) for $t \in \{ 0 , \ldots , T - 1 \}$ and recalling the assumption that $x _ { 0 } = 0$ , we get

$$x _ {t} = \sum_ {s = 0} ^ {t - 1} A ^ {t - (s + 1)} w _ {s}. \tag {B.1}$$

Suppose the event $\mathcal { E } _ { w } \cap \mathcal { E } _ { v }$ holds. It follows from (25) and Assumption 3.1 that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \| x _ {t} \| = \left\| \sum_ {s = 0} ^ {t - 1} A ^ {t - (s + 1)} w _ {s} \right\| \\ \leq \sum_ {s = 0} ^ {t - 1} \left\| A ^ {t - (s + 1)} \right\| \| w _ {s} \| \\ \leq \sqrt {5 \operatorname{tr} (W) \log \frac {3 T}{\delta}} \sum_ {s = 0} ^ {t - 1} \kappa_ {A} \gamma_ {A} ^ {t - (s + 1)} \\ \leq \sqrt {5 \operatorname{tr} (W) \log \frac {3 T}{\delta}} \cdot \frac {\kappa_ {A}}{1 - \gamma_ {A}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

In addition, we have

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left\| y _ {t} \right\| = \left\| \left(C \sum_ {s = 0} ^ {t - 1} A ^ {t - (s + 1)} w _ {s}\right) + v _ {t} \right\| \\ \leq \| C \| \sqrt {5 \operatorname{tr} (W) \log \frac {3 T}{\delta}} \cdot \frac {\kappa_ {A}}{1 - \gamma_ {A}} + \sqrt {5 \operatorname{tr} (V) \log \frac {3 T}{\delta}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

Finally, recall from (22) that $Y _ { t - 1 : t - h } = I _ { p } \otimes [ y _ { t - s } ^ { \top } ] _ { s \in [ h ] } ^ { \top }$ , which yields

$$Y _ {t - 1: t - h} ^ {\top} Y _ {t - 1: t - h} = I _ {p} \otimes ([ y _ {t - s} ^ {\top} ] _ {s \in [ h ]} [ y _ {t - s} ^ {\top} ] _ {s \in [ h ]} ^ {\top}).$$

We then obtain that for any $t \in \{ h , \ldots , T - 1 \}$ ,

$$
\begin{array}{l} \| Y _ {t - 1: t - h} \| = \sqrt {\lambda_ {\mathrm{max}} (Y _ {t - 1 : t - h} Y _ {t - 1 : t - h} ^ {\top})} \\ = \sqrt {[ y _ {t - s} ^ {\top} ] _ {s \in [ h ]} [ y _ {t - s} ^ {\top} ] _ {s \in [ h ]} ^ {\top}} \\ = \sqrt {\sum_ {s = 1} ^ {h} \left\| y _ {t - s} \right\| ^ {2}} \\ \leq \sqrt {h} R _ {y}, \\ \end{array}
$$

which completes the proof of the lemma.
