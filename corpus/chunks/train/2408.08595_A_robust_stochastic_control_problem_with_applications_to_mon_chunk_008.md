and obtain the following SDE:

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} d \Delta X _ {t} = A _ {t} \Delta \bar {X} _ {t} d t + \Delta \bar {X} _ {t} C _ {t} ^ {\prime} d W _ {t}, \\ \Delta X _ {0} = 0. \end{array} \right. \tag {2.6}
$$

For any $0 < \varepsilon < T$ , from [1, Lemma 1.6] we have

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \int_ {0} ^ {t} A _ {s} \Delta \bar {X} _ {s} d s \right| ^ {2} \right] \leqslant \mathbb {E} \left[ \left| \int_ {0} ^ {\varepsilon} | A _ {t} | \sup _ {0 \leqslant s \leqslant \varepsilon} | \Delta \bar {X} _ {s} | d t \right| ^ {2} \right] \\ \leq 8 \| A \| _ {\mathrm{BMO} _ {2}} ^ {2} \mathbb {E} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {\varepsilon} \sup _ {0 \leqslant s \leqslant \varepsilon} | \Delta \bar {X} _ {s} | ^ {2} d t \right] \\ \leq 8 \varepsilon \| A \| _ {\mathrm{BMO} _ {2}} ^ {2} \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \Delta \bar {X} _ {t} \right| ^ {2} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$

On the other hand, by BDG-inequality and, we have

$$\mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \int_ {0} ^ {t} \Delta \bar {X} _ {s} C _ {s} d W _ {s} \right| ^ {2} \right] \leqslant 4 \mathbb {E} \left[ \left| \int_ {0} ^ {\varepsilon} | \Delta \bar {X} _ {s} C _ {s} | ^ {2} d s \right| \right] \leq 4 \varepsilon K _ {C} \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \Delta \bar {X} _ {t} \right| ^ {2} \right],$$

where the positive constant $K _ { C }$ is determined by the uniform bound of process C. Therefore, we can deduce that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} | \Delta X _ {t} | ^ {2} \right] \leqslant 2 \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \int_ {0} ^ {t} A _ {s} \Delta \bar {X} _ {s} d s \right| ^ {2} \right] + 2 \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \int_ {0} ^ {t} \Delta \bar {X} _ {s} C _ {s} d W _ {s} \right| ^ {2} \right] \\ \leqslant \big (1 6 \varepsilon \| A \| _ {\mathrm{BMO} _ {2}} ^ {2} + 8 \varepsilon K _ {C} \big) \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \Delta \bar {X} _ {t} \right| ^ {2} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$

Choosing suitable ε such that

$$0 < 1 6 \varepsilon \| A \| _ {\mathrm{BMO} _ {2}} ^ {2} + 8 \varepsilon K _ {C} < 1,$$

we can get that $\Theta \big ( \bar { X } \big ) : = X$ is a contraction map on $[ 0 , \varepsilon ]$ . We can use the same method to verify

$$\mathbb {E} \bigg [ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \Delta X _ {t} \right| ^ {2} \bigg ] < \mathbb {E} \bigg [ \sup _ {0 \leqslant t \leqslant \varepsilon} \left| \Delta \bar {X} _ {t} \right| ^ {2} \bigg ]$$

on $[ \varepsilon , 2 \varepsilon ] , \cdots$ , and finish our proof.
