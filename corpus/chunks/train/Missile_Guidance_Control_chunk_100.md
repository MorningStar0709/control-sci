The remaining force acting along the $X _ { b } \mathrm { - a x i s }$ of the missile is the X-component of weight due to gravity. Referring to Figure 3.10, we note that the missile body and inertial Earth axes will show that the weight component of the missile along the $X _ { b } \mathrm { - a x i s }$ is

$$x _ {m g} = m g \sin \theta , \tag {3.45}$$

where

$$x _ {m g} = \text { weight component along the } X _ {b} \text {-axis},z _ {m g} = \text { weight component along the } Z _ {b} \text {-axis},g = \text { acceleration due to gravity },$$

θ = angle between the body axes and Earth axes (pitch angle), defined as positive for the counterclockwise sense of rotation in the $X - Z$ plane.

$$
