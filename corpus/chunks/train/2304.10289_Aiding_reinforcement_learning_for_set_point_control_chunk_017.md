# 6. RESULTS

In this section, the results for PPO are compared with ResidualPPO, where PPO is augmented with a prior Pcontroller as proposed in Section 4. All empirical results are presented with mean and standard deviation across 10 random seeds. It is again noted that the cascaded tank process can be well controlled e.g. with PID control. Thus, it is stressed that the purpose here is to demonstrate the need for aiding control when RL is used.
