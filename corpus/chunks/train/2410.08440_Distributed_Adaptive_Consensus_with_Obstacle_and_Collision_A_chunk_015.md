# IV. MAIN RESULT

Theorem 1: Under the Assumptions 2.1, 2.2 and 3.1, there exist a control law and NN tuning laws that guarantee synchronization, stability, and collision/obstacle avoidance in the distributed multi-agent with the follower dynamics (1) and leader dynamics (3).

Proof: This theorem will be proved by construction in Section IV-C, i.e., by providing a control law and NN tuning laws.

Before proving Theorem 1, we present a control strategy together with adaptive tuning strategy in Sections IV-A and IV-B, respectively. We then demonstrate that applying the distributed control law (35) along with the NN tuning laws (43), (44), (45) guarantees the following:

1) Synchronization: All follower agents achieve cooperative formation consensus tracking with the leader, such that the tracking errors $\delta ^ { 1 } , \ldots , \delta ^ { n }$ are cooperatively uniformly ultimately bounded (CUUB). This implies that all agents synchronize with the leader while maintaining bounded residual errors.   
2) Stability: The closed-loop system is globally bounded and remains stable for all $t \geq 0$ .   
3) Collision/Obstacle Avoidance: Agents successfully avoid local agents/obstacles while maintaining synchronization with the leader and preserving system stability.
