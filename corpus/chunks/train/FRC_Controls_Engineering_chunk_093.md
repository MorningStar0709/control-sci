# 4.1.3 Chain rule

This is for taking the derivative of nested expressions.

$$
\begin{array}{l} h (x) = f (g (x)) \\ h ^ {\prime} (x) = f ^ {\prime} (g (x)) \cdot g ^ {\prime} (x) \\ \end{array}
$$

For example,

$$
\begin{array}{l} h (x) = (3 x + 2) ^ {5} \\ h ^ {\prime} (x) = 5 (3 x + 2) ^ {4} \cdot (3) \\ h ^ {\prime} (x) = 1 5 \left(3 x + 2\right) ^ {4} \\ \end{array}
$$
