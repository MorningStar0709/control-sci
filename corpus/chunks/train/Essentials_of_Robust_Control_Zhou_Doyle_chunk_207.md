$$\| \Delta \| _ {\infty} \leq \frac {\max _ {j} \left\{\tilde {\alpha} _ {j} \right\} \max _ {i} \left\{\tilde {\beta} _ {i} \right\}}{\sigma_ {1}} \leq \frac {\max _ {j} \left\{\tilde {\alpha} _ {j} \right\} \max _ {i} \left\{\tilde {\beta} _ {i} \right\}}{\gamma} < 1$$

and

$$\Delta (\sigma + j \omega_ {0}) = \frac {1}{\sigma_ {1}} v _ {1} u _ {1} ^ {*}\det \left(I - M (\sigma + j \omega_ {0}) \Delta (\sigma + j \omega_ {0})\right) = 0$$

Hence $s = \sigma + j \omega _ { 0 }$ is a zero for the transfer function det $( I - M ( s ) \Delta ( s ) )$ .
