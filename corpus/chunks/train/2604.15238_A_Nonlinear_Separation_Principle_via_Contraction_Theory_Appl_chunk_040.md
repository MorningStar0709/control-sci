$$
\begin{array}{l} \| \dot {u} _ {\text { ext }} \| _ {\mathcal {U}} = \| \varepsilon K _ {i} C (x ^ {\star} (u ^ {\star}) - x) \| _ {\mathcal {U}} \\ = \| \varepsilon K _ {i} C (x ^ {\star} (u ^ {\star}) - x ^ {\star} (u _ {\text { ext }}) + x ^ {\star} (u _ {\text { ext }}) - x) \| _ {\mathcal {U}} \\ \leq \varepsilon \ell_ {i, \mathcal {U}} (\| x ^ {\star} (u ^ {\star}) - x ^ {\star} (u _ {\mathrm{ext}})) \| _ {\mathcal {X}} + V _ {2}) \\ \end{array}
$$

Since $x ^ { \star } ( u )$ is globally Lipschitz in u with constant $\ell _ { u } / c _ { K }$

$$\| \dot {u} _ {\mathrm{ext}} \| _ {\mathcal {U}} \leq \frac {\varepsilon \ell_ {i , \mathcal {U}} \ell_ {u}}{c _ {K}} V _ {3} + \varepsilon \ell_ {i, \mathcal {U}} V _ {2}$$

Substituting $\| \dot { u } _ { \mathrm { e x t } } \| _ { \mathcal { U } }$ back into (70) yields:

$$D ^ {+} V _ {2} \leq - (c _ {K} - \varepsilon \frac {\ell_ {i , \mathcal {U}} \ell_ {u}}{c _ {K}}) V _ {2} + \ell_ {u} \ell_ {K} V _ {1} + \varepsilon \frac {\ell_ {i , \mathcal {U}} \ell_ {u} ^ {2}}{c _ {K} ^ {2}} V _ {3}, \tag {71}$$

In order to find the Dini derivative of $V _ { 3 }$ , we first rewrite the integral controller (68) as

$$\dot {u} _ {\text { ext }} = \varepsilon K _ {i} C (x ^ {\star} (u ^ {\star}) - C x ^ {\star} (u _ {\text { ext }})) + \varepsilon K _ {i} C (x ^ {\star} (u _ {\text { ext }}) - x).$$

Treating the second term as a disturbance to the contracting reduced order dynamics (64), from the incremental ISS property of contracting systems [7, Theorem 3.16],

$$
\begin{array}{l} D ^ {+} V _ {3} \leq - \varepsilon c _ {r} V _ {3} + \varepsilon \ell_ {i, \mathcal {R}} \| x ^ {\star} (u _ {\mathrm{ext}}) - x \| _ {\mathcal {X}} \\ = - \varepsilon c _ {r} V _ {3} + \varepsilon \ell_ {i, \mathcal {R}} V _ {2}. \tag {72} \\ \end{array}
$$

Upon stacking inequalities (69), (71), (72), we obtain the following inequality,

$$
D ^ {+} \left[ \begin{array}{c} V _ {1} \\ V _ {2} \\ V _ {3} \end{array} \right] \leq \underbrace {\left[ \begin{array}{c c c} - c _ {O} & 0 & 0 \\ \ell_ {u} \ell_ {K} & - (c _ {K} - \varepsilon \frac {\ell_ {i , \mathcal {U}} \ell_ {u}}{c _ {K}}) & \varepsilon \frac {\ell_ {i , \mathcal {U}} \ell_ {u} ^ {2}}{c _ {K} ^ {2}} \\ 0 & \varepsilon \ell_ {i , \mathcal {R}} & - \varepsilon c _ {r} \end{array} \right]} _ {M (\varepsilon)} \left[ \begin{array}{c} V _ {1} \\ V _ {2} \\ V _ {3} \end{array} \right]
$$
