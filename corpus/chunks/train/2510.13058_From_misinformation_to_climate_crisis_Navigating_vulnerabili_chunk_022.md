# Key takeaways

• Misinformation strains crisis response by diverting resources and increasing confusion.   
• Digital platforms amplify misinformation, spreading both facts and falsehoods.   
• Public trust is vital as misinformation undermines science, policy, and emergency response.   
• Delays in climate adaptation and policy reforms result from persistent misinformation.   
• Proactive misinformation management is essential, requiring coordinated efforts across governments, media, and scientific institutions.
