Theorem 1: Given neural network Φ and its reduced-size version Φˆ with input set U, the model reduction precision $\rho ( \Phi , { \hat { \Phi } } , \mathcal { U } )$ can be computed by

$$\rho (\Phi , \hat {\Phi}, \mathcal {U}) = \sup _ {\tilde {\eta} _ {0} \in \mathcal {U}} \left\| \tilde {\Phi} (\tilde {\boldsymbol {\eta}} _ {0}) \right\| \tag {12}$$

where neural network $\tilde { \Phi }$ is an augmented neural network of Φ and $\hat { \Phi }$ defined as follows:

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \tilde {\boldsymbol {\eta}} _ {\ell} = \tilde {\phi} _ {\ell} (\tilde {\mathbf {W}} _ {\ell} \tilde {\boldsymbol {\eta}} _ {\ell - 1} + \tilde {\mathbf {b}} _ {\ell}), \ell = 1, \dots , L + 1 \\ \tilde {\boldsymbol {\eta}} _ {L + 1} = \tilde {\Phi} (\tilde {\boldsymbol {\eta}} _ {0}) \end{array} \right. \tag {13}
$$

in which input $\tilde { \eta } _ { 0 } = \eta _ { 0 } = \hat { \eta } _ { 0 }$ and

$$
\tilde {\mathbf {W}} _ {\ell} = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {W} _ {1} \\ \hat {\mathbf {W}} _ {1} \end{array} \right], & \ell = 1 \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {W} _ {\ell} & \mathbf {0} _ {n _ {\ell} \times \hat {n} _ {\ell - 1}} \\ \mathbf {0} _ {\hat {n} _ {\ell} \times n _ {\ell - 1}} & \hat {\mathbf {W}} _ {\ell} \end{array} \right], & 1 <   \ell \leq \hat {L} - 1 \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {W} _ {\ell} & \mathbf {0} _ {n _ {\ell} \times \hat {n} _ {\hat {L} - 1}} \\ \mathbf {0} _ {\hat {n} _ {\hat {L} - 1} \times n _ {\ell}} & \mathbf {I} _ {\hat {n} _ {\hat {L} - 1}} \end{array} \right], & \hat {L} \leq \ell \leq L - 1 \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {W} _ {L} & \mathbf {0} _ {n _ {L} \times n _ {\hat {L} - 1}} \\ \mathbf {0} _ {n _ {\hat {L}} \times n _ {L - 1}} & \hat {\mathbf {W}} _ {\hat {L}} \end{array} \right], & \ell = L \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {I} _ {n _ {L}} & - \mathbf {I} _ {n _ {\hat {L}}} \end{array} \right], & \ell = L + 1 \end{array} \right. \tag {14}

\tilde {\mathbf {b}} _ {\ell} = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \left[ \begin{array}{c} \mathbf {b} _ {\ell} \\ \hat {\mathbf {b}} _ {\ell} \end{array} \right], & 1 \leq \ell \leq \hat {L} - 1 \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c} \mathbf {b} _ {\ell} \\ \mathbf {0} _ {\hat {n} _ {\hat {L} - 1} \times 1} \end{array} \right], & \hat {L} \leq \ell \leq L - 1 \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c} \mathbf {b} _ {L} \\ \hat {\mathbf {b}} _ {\hat {L}} \end{array} \right], & \ell = L \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c} \mathbf {0} _ {(n _ {L} + n _ {\hat {L}}) \times 1} \end{array} \right], & \ell = L + 1 \end{array} \right. \tag {15}
