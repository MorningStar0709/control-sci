With computer control, it is possible to have many other operating modes. Parameter estimation and control-design algorithms can be included in the controller. An estimation mode, in which a model of the process is estimated, may be introduced. The estimated model may be used in the design algorithm to give an update of the parameters of the controller in a tuning mode. Adaptive control modes, in which the parameters are updated continuously, may also be added. Computer control offers many other interesting possibilities. The performance of the control loop may be displayed instead of set point and error.
