# A. Robust Optimization Reformulation

Begin by rewriting the consistency set as

$$\mathcal {P} _ {1} \doteq \left\{F: \operatorname{Tr} \left(F \Phi_ {i, k} ^ {\pm}\right) \leq d _ {i, k} ^ {\pm}, \right. \tag {11}\forall i = \{1, \dots , T \}, k = \{1, \dots , n \} \}$$

where $\Phi _ { i , k } ^ { \pm }$ is a matrix with $\pm \phi ( x [ i ] )$ in its $k _ { \mathrm { t h } }$ column and zeros elsewhere:

$$\Phi_ {i, k} ^ {\pm} = \left[ \mathbf {0}, \dots , \pm \phi (x [ i ]), \dots , \mathbf {0} \right], \tag {12}\text { and } d _ {i, k} ^ {\pm} = \epsilon \pm (\dot {x} [ i ] - G u [ i ]) _ {k}.$$

For example, for a single measurement $x [ 1 ] , u [ 1 ] \in \mathbb { R } ^ { 2 }$ , we have

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Phi_ {1, 1} ^ {\pm} = \left[ \pm \phi (x [ 1 ]) \quad \mathbf {0} \right], \quad d _ {1, 1} ^ {\pm} = \epsilon \pm (\dot {x} [ 1 ] - G u [ 1 ]) _ {1} \\ \Phi_ {1, 2} ^ {\pm} = \left[ \mathbf {0} \quad \pm \phi (x [ 1 ]) \right], \quad d _ {1, 2} ^ {\pm} = \epsilon \pm (\dot {x} [ 1 ] - G u [ 1 ]) _ {2}. \end{array} \tag {13}
$$

In the sequel the ± superscript in $\Phi _ { i , k } ^ { \pm }$ and $d _ { i , k } ^ { \pm }$ is omitted to keep the notation cleaner.

For actuated systems of the form (6), condition (5) now depends on finding a differential control input $\frac { \partial u ( x ) } { \partial x }$ that satisfies:

$$\dot {M} (x) + \left(F \frac {\partial \phi (x)}{\partial x} + G \frac {\partial u (x)}{\partial x}\right) ^ {\top} M (x) \tag {14}+ M (x) \left(F \frac {\partial \phi (x)}{\partial x} + G \frac {\partial u (x)}{\partial x}\right) \prec - 2 \lambda M (x).$$
