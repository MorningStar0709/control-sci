This paper is organized as follows. Section 2 recalls some basic mathematics mainly involving the Koopman operator and Petersen’s lemma. Sections 3 and 4 discuss direct and indirect data-driven control approach respectively, followed by some discussions in Section 5. Section 6 demonstrates the proposed results via numerical examples with conclusions in Section 7.
