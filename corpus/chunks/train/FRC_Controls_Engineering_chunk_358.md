# 11.6.2 Forward kinematics

Let M be the $4 \times 3$ inverse kinematics matrix above including the $\scriptstyle { \frac { 1 } { \sqrt { 2 } } }$ factor. The forward kinematics are

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{l} v _ {x} \\ v _ {y} \\ \omega \end{array} \right] = \mathbf {M} ^ {+} \left[ \begin{array}{l} v _ {f l} \\ v _ {f r} \\ v _ {r l} \\ v _ {r r} \end{array} \right] \tag {11.13}
$$

where ${ { \bf { M } } ^ { + } }$ is the pseudoinverse of M.
