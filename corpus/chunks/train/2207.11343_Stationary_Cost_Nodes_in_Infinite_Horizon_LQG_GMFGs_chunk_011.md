$$\frac {d z _ {t} ^ {\ell}}{d t} = - \frac {b ^ {2} \pi}{r} z _ {t} ^ {\ell} - \lambda_ {\ell} \frac {b ^ {2}}{r} s _ {t} ^ {\ell}, \quad z _ {0} ^ {\ell} = \lambda_ {\ell} \langle m, f _ {\ell} \rangle ,\frac {d s _ {t} ^ {\ell}}{d t} = z _ {t} ^ {\ell} + \left(\frac {b ^ {2} \pi}{r} + \rho\right) s _ {t} ^ {\ell}, \quad s _ {\infty} ^ {\ell} = 0,$$

from which we seek an explicit solution that is compatible with the infinite horizon condition $z _ { \infty } ^ { \ell } = 0$ , for all $\ell \in \{ 1 , \ldots , L \}$ . From the ODE for sℓ, it admits the representation below:

$$s _ {t} ^ {\ell} = - \int_ {t} ^ {\infty} \exp \left(\left(\frac {b ^ {2} \pi}{r} + \rho\right) (t - s)\right) z _ {s} ^ {\ell} d s, \tag {32}= - \int_ {t} ^ {\infty} \exp \left(\frac {1}{\pi} (t - s)\right) z _ {s} ^ {\ell} d s, \tag {33}$$

which is derived invoking the Riccati equation (10),

$$\left(\frac {b ^ {2} \pi}{r} + \rho\right) = \frac {1}{\pi}, \tag {34}$$

and which satisfies the condition

$$s _ {\infty} ^ {\ell} = 0. \tag {35}$$

By substituting this expression for $s ^ { \ell }$ back into the ODE for $z ^ { \ell }$ , we obtain the representation below

$$\frac {d z _ {t} ^ {\ell}}{d t} = - \frac {b ^ {2} \pi}{r} z _ {t} ^ {\ell} + \lambda_ {\ell} \frac {b ^ {2}}{r} \int_ {t} ^ {\infty} \exp \left(\frac {1}{\pi} (t - s)\right) z _ {s} ^ {\ell} d s.$$

By differentiating the above ODE and making appropriate substitutions, we obtain the second order ODE for $\overset { \cdot } { z }  \overset { \ell }  $ ,

$$\frac {d ^ {2} z _ {t} ^ {\ell}}{d t} - \rho \frac {d z _ {t} ^ {\ell}}{d t} + \frac {b ^ {2}}{r} (\lambda_ {\ell} - 1) z _ {t} ^ {\ell} = 0.$$

This can be solved via a characteristic equation

$$\xi_ {\ell} ^ {2} - \rho \xi_ {\ell} + \frac {b ^ {2}}{r} (\lambda_ {\ell} - 1) = 0,$$

which admits as a negative solution

$$\xi_ {\ell} = \left(\frac {\rho}{2} - \sqrt {\frac {\rho^ {2}}{4} + \frac {b ^ {2}}{r} (1 - \lambda_ {\ell})}\right) = \frac {\rho}{2} - \theta (\lambda_ {\ell}) < 0,$$

under Assumption (A1). We thus obtain

$$z _ {t} ^ {\ell} = \lambda_ {\ell} \langle m, f _ {\ell} \rangle \exp (\xi_ {\ell} t), \forall t \geq 0, \tag {36}$$

where, because $\xi _ { \ell } < 0$ for all $l \in \{ 1 , \ldots , L \}$ , the infinite horizon condition $z _ { \infty } ^ { \ell } = 0$ is satisfied.
