Algorithms 1 and 2 summarize the client–cloud protocol realizing the proposed VEMPC. The protocol separates computation into an offline and an online phase.

1) Offline preprocessing: The offline phase precomputes quantities that depend only on the MPC parameters and the reference sampling distribution. Let $L _ { U } \in \mathbb { R } ^ { N m \times N m }$ denote the Cholesky factor of $\Sigma _ { U }$ in (14b), i.e., $\Sigma _ { U } = L _ { U } L _ { U } ^ { \top }$ .
