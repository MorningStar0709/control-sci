# II. SWITCHED SYSTEM MODEL OF THE WIND TURBINE GRID-SIDE CONVERTER

This section commences by introducing fundamental concepts and the generic mathematical model of switched systems. Then, taking the wind turbine’s external connection impedance and internal control dynamics into account, dynamic equations describing its behaviors under normal operation and LVRT conditions are derived on the basis of the structure and control strategies of the WT-GSC. Finally, by incorporating a switching law that governs the switching between the aforementioned operational states, the WT-GSC switched system model for the repeated LVRT process is established.
