Such is the process of learning that it is never possible for anyone to say exactly how he acquired any given body of knowledge. My own knowledge was acquired from many people from academia, industry, and the government. Specifically, my knowledge in guided weapons and control systems was acquired and nurtured during my many years of association with the Department of the Air Force’s Aeronautical Systems Center, Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio, while participating in the theory, design, operation, and testing (i.e., from concept to fly-out) the air-launched cruise missile (ALCM), SRAM II, Minuteman III, the AIM-9 Sidewinder, and other programs too numerous to list.
