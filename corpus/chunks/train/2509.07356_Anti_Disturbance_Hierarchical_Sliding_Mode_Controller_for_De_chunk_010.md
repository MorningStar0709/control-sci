# 3.2.2 Adaptive Compensation Term

Adaptive control is used to dynamically adjust the control gain, designed as:

$$u _ {a d} (t) = \hat {\Theta} (t) s _ {0} (t) \tag {14}$$

where $\hat { \Theta } ( t )$ is the adaptive gain matrix.

Update Law is given by:

$$\dot {\hat {\Theta}} (t) = - \gamma s _ {0} (t) s _ {0} ^ {T} (t) \tag {15}$$

where $\gamma > 0$ is the learning rate.
