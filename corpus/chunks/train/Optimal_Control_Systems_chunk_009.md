# Background and Audience

This is a second level graduate text book and as such the background material required for using this book is a first course on control systems, state space analysis, or linear systems theory. It is suggested that the student review the material in Appendices A and B given at the end of the book. This book is aimed at graduate students in Electrical, Mechanical, Chemical, and Aerospace Engineering and Applied Mathematics. It can also be used by professional scientists and engineers working in a variety of industries and research organizations.
