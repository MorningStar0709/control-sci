Corollary 5. Consider the nominal CAN (3) under the control protocol (4). Let G be weakly connected and all of its edge weights are positive. Then, the leaders in every CSC reach prescribed-time consensus and the followers converge towards the convex hull spanned by all the leaders’ states within the preset finite time $T _ { 1 }$ .

Remark 4. Note that if every CSC of G has exactly one node, the weak connectivity topology condition in Corollary 5 collapses into the classical spanning forest condition commonly employed in the containment control literature. Moreover, the result of Corollary 5 contains as a special case the standard prescribed-time containment control result derived in Reference [29] for first-order traditional networks under unsigned digraph topology which contains a spanning forest.
