$$\epsilon (x, \theta) \leq \min _ {1 \leq i \leq N _ {\mathrm{data}}} \left\{L _ {p} \| (x, \theta) - (x _ {i}, \theta_ {i}) \| + \epsilon_ {i} \right\} \tag {B.4}$$
