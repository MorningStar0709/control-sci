# II. RELATED WORK

SARL for Quadrotor Control. Prior works have mainly focused on developing quadrotor control strategies for stabilization and trajectory tracking. The pioneering work in [1] introduced deep reinforcement learning for quadrotor lowlevel control. Expanding the scope, Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO)-based algorithms were applied for quadrotor position tracking control in [8], which was further extended and generalized to multi-rotor aerial vehicles in [9]. The study presented in [10] explored sim-to-(multi)-real transfer of low-level control policies, bridging the reality gap. They successfully trained and transferred a single robust control policy from simulation to multiple real quadrotors. Recently, a data-efficient learning strategy was proposed in [11] in the context of equivariant reinforcement learning, leveraging the symmetry of the quadrotor dynamics.
