The following lemma establishes the key properties of the cone $\mathcal { P } _ { Q }$ that we will require to apply Theorem 1. The first part of the result gives a formula for the dual cone, and establishes that a suitable function $\phi \left( \mu \right)$ exists (the function is in fact the same as that from section 3.1). The final part gives a formula that can be used to compute the optimal control law, and it is from this formula that we can deduce that the optimal control inherits additional structural features as claimed above.

Lemma 1. Let A, T, $\mathcal { P } _ { Q }$ and all the block partitioning of matrices be as in Definition 1. Then

$$\mathcal {P} _ {Q} ^ {*} = \left\{T ^ {\top} A (Y) T: Y \succeq 0 \right\}.$$

Furthermore if there exists a Λ such that

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{l l} \Lambda & M _ {1} \\ M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {2} \end{array} \right] \in \mathcal {P} _ {Q} ^ {*}, \tag {12}
$$

then for fixed X

$$
\inf \left\{\operatorname{tr} \left(\left[ \begin{array}{l l} 0 & M _ {1} \\ M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {2} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{l l} 0 & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right]\right): \left[ \begin{array}{l l} X & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right] \in \mathcal {P} _ {Q} \right\} \tag {13}
$$

equals tr $\left( M _ { 1 } M _ { 2 } ^ { + } M _ { 1 } ^ { \top } X \right)$ . A pair $( U _ { 1 } , U _ { 2 } )$ that achieves the infimum above can be computed through the equation

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{l l} X & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c} I \\ - M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] X \left[ \begin{array}{c} I \\ - M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}. \tag {14}
$$

Proof. See Appendix A.

$$

with a similar expression for G. We may write this compactly according to

$$F = F _ {\mathrm{sd}} \otimes \bar {F} \text { and } G = G _ {\mathrm{sd}} \otimes \bar {G}, \tag {15}$$
