# 3 The Ergodic Distribution Dependent BSDE

The aim of this section is to prove, under the two different sets of assumptions $( \mathcal { H } _ { S D E } 1 )$ and $( \mathcal { H } _ { S D E } 2 )$ , a result of existence and uniqueness of the following distribution dependent ergodic BSDE

$$Y _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} = Y _ {T} ^ {[ \theta ]} + \int_ {t} ^ {T} \left(f (X _ {s} ^ {[ \theta ]}, [ X _ {s} ^ {[ \theta ]} ], Z _ {s} ^ {[ \theta ]}) - \lambda\right) \mathrm{d} s - \int_ {0} ^ {T} Z _ {s} ^ {[ \theta ]} \mathrm{d} W _ {s}, \quad \forall 0 \leq t \leq T \tag {EBSDE}$$

where the unknown is the triple $( Y ^ { [ \theta ] } , Z ^ { [ \theta ] } , \lambda )$ and with $X ^ { [ \theta ] }$ the solution to (MV-SDE) with $s = 0$ and $b , f$ satisfying following assumptions.
