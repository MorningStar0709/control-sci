From (54),

$$V _ {n, 0 \rightarrow N} ^ {H, r} (x _ {0} ^ {H, r}) \leq \lim _ {k \rightarrow \infty} V _ {n} ^ {H} (\mathbf {x} _ {0: k - 1} ^ {H, r}) = V _ {n} ^ {H} (\mathbf {x} ^ {H, r}), \tag {56}$$

and from (48b), (56), we conclude

$$V _ {n} ^ {H} (\mathbf {x} ^ {H, r - 1}) \leq V _ {n, 0 \to N} ^ {H, r} (x _ {0} ^ {H, r}) \leq V _ {n} ^ {H} (\mathbf {x} ^ {H, r}),$$

and therefore $V _ { n } ( \mathbf { x } ^ { r - 1 } ) ~ \leq ~ V _ { n } ( \mathbf { x } ^ { r } )$ .
