The following lemmas are a direct consequence of duality and useful for our subsequent analysis.

Lemma 7. The set of Schur stabilizing gains S is regular open, contractible, and unbounded when $m \geq 2$ and the boundary ∂S coincides with the set $\mathsf { \subseteq L } \in \dot { \mathbb R } ^ { n \times m } : \rho ( A - L H ) = 1 \}$ . Proof. Consider the duality described in Remark 3. The proof then follows identical to [6, Lemmas 3.5 and 3.6] by noting that the spectrum of a matrix is identical to the spectrum of its transpose.
