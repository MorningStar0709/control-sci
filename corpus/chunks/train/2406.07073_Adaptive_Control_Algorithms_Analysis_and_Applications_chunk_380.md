# 1. Additive uncertainties

$$\left\| S _ {u p} \left(z ^ {- 1}\right) W _ {a} \left(z ^ {- 1}\right) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq 1 \tag {8.20}$$

or equivalently

$$\left| S _ {u p} \left(e ^ {- j \omega}\right) \right| < \left| W _ {a} \left(e ^ {- j \omega}\right) \right| ^ {- 1}; \quad 0 \leq \omega \leq \pi \tag {8.21}$$
