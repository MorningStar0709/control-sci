# DEFINITION 5.9 Passive system

A system with input u and output y is passive if

$$\langle y \mid u \rangle \geq 0$$

The system is input strictly passive (ISP) if there exists $\varepsilon > 0$ such that

$$\langle y | u \rangle \geq \varepsilon \| u \| ^ {2}$$

and output strictly passive (OSP) if there exists $\varepsilon > 0$ such that

$$\langle y \mid u \rangle \geq \varepsilon \| y \| ^ {2}$$
