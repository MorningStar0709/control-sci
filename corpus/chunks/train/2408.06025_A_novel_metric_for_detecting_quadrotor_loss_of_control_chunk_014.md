# A. Simulation Overview

The identified high-speed aerodynamic models of [28] are deployed in a Python-based non-linear quadrotor simulation environment running at 250 Hz. Control of the quadrotor is facilitated through cascaded PID loops which map desired position and yaw commands to desired accelerations and rates. The inner-most rate and acceleration loop utilize the incremental non-linear dynamic inversion (INDI) controller developed by [30]. Briefly, the goal of the controller is to align the thrust-vector of the quadrotor with the desired acceleration vector in the NED frame.
