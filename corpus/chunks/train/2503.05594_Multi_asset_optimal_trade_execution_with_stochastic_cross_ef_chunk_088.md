$$d \widehat {\mathcal {Y}} (s) = - \hat {g} \big (s, \cdot , \widehat {\mathcal {Y}} (s), \widehat {\mathcal {Z}} ^ {1} (s), \dots , \widehat {\mathcal {Z}} ^ {m} (s) \big) d s + \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {m} \widehat {\mathcal {Z}} ^ {k} (s) d W _ {k} (s), \quad s \in [ 0, T ], \tag {B.11}\widehat {\mathcal {Y}} (T) = \frac {1}{2} I _ {n}$$
