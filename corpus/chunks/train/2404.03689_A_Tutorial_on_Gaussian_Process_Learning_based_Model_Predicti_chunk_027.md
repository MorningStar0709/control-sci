# 3.4 Mean and Uncertainty Approximation with Linear Nominal Model

This section details uncertainty approximation for systems with a linear nominal model in (11a). When the nominal model is linear, it is defined as $f \left( x _ { k } , u _ { k } \right) = A x _ { k } + B u _ { k }$ , with ?? and ?? representing the state transition and control input matrices, respectively. Leveraging equations (13) and (25b), we derive the uncertainty propagation as follows:

$$
\Sigma_ {k + 1} ^ {x} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] \Sigma_ {k} \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}, \tag {26a}

= \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x u} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x d} \\ \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x u ^ {\top}} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {u} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {u d} \\ \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x d ^ {\top}} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {u d ^ {\top}} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {d} + \Sigma^ {w} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}. \tag {26b}
$$

When employing the mean equivalent approximation method, the variance calculation results as:

$$
\Sigma_ {k + 1} ^ {x} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x u} & 0 \\ \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x u ^ {\top}} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {u} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \Sigma^ {d} \left(\mu_ {k} ^ {z}\right) + \Sigma^ {w} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}, \tag {27}
$$

Alternatively, using the Taylor approximation method, the variance is elaborated in (28) as:

$$
\Sigma_ {k + 1} ^ {x} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x u} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x} (\nabla \mu^ {d} (\mu_ {k} ^ {x})) ^ {\top} \\ \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x u ^ {\top}} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {u} & \Sigma_ {k} ^ {u} (\nabla \mu^ {d} (\mu_ {k} ^ {u})) ^ {\top} \\ (\nabla \mu^ {d} (\mu_ {k} ^ {x})) \Sigma_ {k} ^ {x} & (\nabla \mu^ {d} (\mu_ {k} ^ {u})) \Sigma_ {k} ^ {u} & \Sigma^ {d} (\mu_ {k} ^ {z}) + \nabla \mu^ {d} (\mu_ {k} ^ {z}) \Sigma_ {k} ^ {z} (\nabla \mu^ {d} (\mu_ {k} ^ {z})) ^ {\top} + \Sigma^ {w} \end{array} \right]

\left[ \begin{array}{c c c} A & B & B ^ {d} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}. \tag {28}
$$
