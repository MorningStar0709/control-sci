At $t = 0_{-}$ , the state and control are at the equilibrium values. For $w(t) = 0.1u_0(t)$ , $\ell_2(0_+) = \ell_2(0_-) - 0.1$ ; all other state variables remain the same. Figure 7.20 shows simulation results. Note that both $\ell_1$ and $x$ come back to their starting values; that is expected, since the equilibrium values are the same after the step in $y_R$ .
