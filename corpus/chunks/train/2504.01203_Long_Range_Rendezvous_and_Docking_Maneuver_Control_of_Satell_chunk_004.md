# II. RIGID BODY DYNAMICS OF SATELLITES

This section presents the rigid body dynamic model of the target and the chaser satellite orbiting around the Earth. To model the dynamics three reference frames are defined as International Celestial Reference Frame (ICRF) [17] denoted by $E _ { i } .$ , the chaser body-fixed frame denoted by $E _ { c }$ and the target body-fixed frame denoted by $E _ { t }$ .
