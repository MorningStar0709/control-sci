$$\mathbb {E} \big [ \left\langle X - X ^ {\prime}, h _ {1} (X - \tilde {X}) - h _ {1} (X ^ {\prime} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}) \right\rangle \big ] = \mathbb {E} \big [ \left\langle \tilde {X} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}, h _ {1} (\tilde {X} - X) - h _ {1} (\tilde {X} ^ {\prime} - X ^ {\prime}) \right\rangle \big ] = - \mathbb {E} \big [ \left\langle \tilde {X} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}, h _ {1} (X - \tilde {X}) - h _ {1} (X ^ {\prime} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}) \right\rangle \big ]$$

which implies that

$$\mathbb {E} \big [ \left< X - X ^ {\prime}, h _ {1} (X - \tilde {X}) - h _ {1} (X ^ {\prime} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}) \right> \big ] = \frac {1}{2} \left(\mathbb {E} \big [ \left< X - X ^ {\prime}, h _ {1} (X - \tilde {X}) - h _ {1} (X ^ {\prime} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}) \right> \big ] - \mathbb {E} \big [ \left< \tilde {X} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}, h _ {1} (X - \tilde {X}) - h _ {1} (X ^ {\prime} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}) \right> \big ]\right)= \frac {1}{2} \mathbb {E} \left[ \left\langle X - \tilde {X} - \left(X ^ {\prime} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}\right), h _ {1} (X - \tilde {X}) - h _ {1} \left(X ^ {\prime} - \tilde {X} ^ {\prime}\right) \right\rangle \right] \leq \mathbb {E} \left[ | X - \tilde {X} - X ^ {\prime} + \tilde {X} ^ {\prime} | ^ {2} \right] \leq 0$$

by using the 0-dissipativity of $h _ { 1 }$ .
