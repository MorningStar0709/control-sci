$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {V} _ {t} \leq - \gamma_ {t 1} V _ {t 0} - \gamma_ {t 2} V _ {t 0} ^ {\frac {1}{p}} \\ + 2 e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \Big [ \psi_ {t} - \kappa_ {t} e _ {b} - \kappa_ {t} (e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b}) ^ {\frac {1 - p}{p}} e _ {b} \Big ] \\ \leq - \gamma_ {t 1} V _ {t 0} - \gamma_ {t 2} V _ {t 0} ^ {\frac {1}{p}} \\ + 2 e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \psi_ {t} - 2 \kappa_ {t} e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b} - 2 \kappa_ {t} (e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b}) ^ {\frac {1}{p}} \tag {63} \\ \leq - \gamma_ {t 1} V _ {t 0} - \gamma_ {t 2} V _ {t 0} ^ {\frac {1}{p}} \\ - 2 \kappa_ {t} e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b} - 2 \kappa_ {t} (e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b}) ^ {\frac {1}{p}} + \psi_ {t} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \psi_ {t} + e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b} \\ \leq - \left(\gamma_ {t 1} - \frac {1}{k _ {t 3} ^ {2} \lambda_ {\min} \left\{\mathcal {P} _ {t} \right\}}\right) V _ {t 0} - \gamma_ {t 2} V _ {t 0} ^ {\frac {1}{p}} \\ - (2 \kappa_ {t} - 1) e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b} - 2 \kappa_ {t} (e _ {b} ^ {\mathrm{T}} e _ {b}) ^ {\frac {1}{p}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

Therefore, we further obtain:

$$\dot {V} _ {t} < - \Gamma_ {t 1} V _ {t} - \Gamma_ {t 2} V _ {t} ^ {\frac {1}{p}}, \tag {64}$$

where

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Gamma_ {t 1} = \min \left\{k _ {t 3} \frac {\lambda_ {\min} \left\{\mathcal {Q} _ {t} \right\}}{\lambda_ {\max} \left\{\mathcal {P} _ {t} \right\}} - \frac {1}{k _ {t 3} ^ {2} \lambda_ {\min} \left\{\mathcal {P} _ {t} \right\}}, 2 \kappa_ {t} - 1 \right\}, (65) \\ \Gamma_ {t 2} = \min \left\{\frac {\lambda_ {\min} \left\{\mathcal {Q} _ {t} \right\} \lambda_ {\min} \left\{\mathcal {P} _ {t} \right\} ^ {\frac {p - 1}{p}} p}{\lambda_ {\max} \left\{\mathcal {P} _ {t} \right\} (3 p - 2)}, 2 \kappa_ {t} \right\}. (65) \\ \end{array}
$$

Based on (64), we conclude that when the resultant disturbance force is constant, and the ESO gains satisfy the constraints 1-3 in Proposition 1, the error dynamics of the ESO (58) is FFTS. This concludes the proof of Theorem 2.
