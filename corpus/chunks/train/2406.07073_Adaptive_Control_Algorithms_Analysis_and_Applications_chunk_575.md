# Step II: Computation of the Controller Parameters and of the Control Law

We will use the Strategy 1 (see Sect. 12.1) for updating the controller parameters. The controller equation generating u(t) is:

$$\boxed {\hat {S} (t, q ^ {- 1}) u (t) + \hat {R} (t, q ^ {- 1}) y (t) = \hat {\beta} (t) P (q ^ {- 1}) y ^ {*} (t + d + 1)} \tag {12.19}$$

or alternatively:

$$u (t) = - \hat {S} ^ {*} (t, q ^ {- 1}) u (t - 1) - \hat {R} (t, q ^ {- 1}) y (t) + \hat {\beta} (t) P (q ^ {- 1}) y ^ {*} (t + d + 1) \tag {12.20}$$

where:

$$\hat {\beta} (t) = 1 / \hat {B} (t, 1) \tag {12.21}\hat {S} (t, q ^ {- 1}) = 1 + \hat {s} _ {1} (t) q ^ {- 1} + \dots + \hat {s} _ {n _ {S}} (t) q ^ {- n _ {S}} = 1 + q ^ {- 1} \hat {S} ^ {*} (t, q ^ {- 1}) \tag {12.22}
\begin{array}{l} \hat {R} (t, q ^ {- 1}) = \hat {r} _ {0} (t) + \hat {r} _ {1} (t) q ^ {- 1} + \dots + \hat {r} _ {n _ {R}} (t) q ^ {- n _ {R}} \\ = \hat {r} _ {0} (t) + q ^ {- 1} \hat {R} ^ {*} (t, q ^ {- 1}) \tag {12.23} \\ \end{array}
$$

and $\hat { \boldsymbol S } ( t , \boldsymbol q ^ { - 1 } ) , \hat { \boldsymbol R } ( t , \boldsymbol q ^ { - 1 } )$ are solutions of:2

$$\boxed {\hat {A} (t, q ^ {- 1}) \hat {S} (t, q ^ {- 1}) + q ^ {- d - 1} \hat {B} ^ {*} (t, q ^ {- 1}) \hat {R} (t, q ^ {- 1}) = P (q ^ {- 1})} \tag {12.24}$$

$P ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ in (12.19), (12.20) and (12.24) are the desired closed-loop poles and $y ^ { * }$ is the desired tracking trajectory. Equation (12.24) can be reformulated in matrix form:

$$M [ \hat {\theta} (t) ] \hat {x} = p \tag {12.25}$$

where:

$$\hat {x} ^ {T} = [ 1, \hat {s _ {1}}, \dots , \hat {s} _ {n _ {S}}, \hat {r} _ {0}, \dots , \hat {r} _ {n _ {R}} ] \tag {12.26}p ^ {T} = [ 1, p _ {1}, \dots , p _ {n _ {P}}, 0, \dots , 0 ] \tag {12.27}$$

and M is the Sylvester matrix which has the form (see Sect. 7.3 for details):

$$
\underbrace {\left[ \begin{array}{c c c c} 1 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ \hat {a} _ {1} & 1 & \ddots & \vdots \\ \vdots & & \ddots & 0 \\ & & & 1 \\ \vdots & & & \hat {a} _ {1} \\ \hat {a} _ {n _ {\hat {A}}} & & & \\ 0 & \ddots & & \vdots \\ \vdots & \ddots & & \\ 0 & \dots & 0 & \hat {a} _ {n _ {\hat {A}}} \end{array} \right]} _ {n _ {\hat {A}} + n _ {\hat {B}}} \underbrace {\left[ \begin{array}{c c c c} 0 & \dots & \dots & 0 \\ \hat {b} _ {1} ^ {\prime} & 0 & \ddots & \vdots \\ \vdots & & \ddots & \\ \vdots & & & 0 \\ \hat {b} _ {n _ {\hat {B}}} ^ {\prime} & & & \\ 0 & \ddots & & \vdots \\ \vdots & \ddots & & \\ 0 & \dots & 0 & \hat {b} _ {n _ {\hat {B}}} ^ {\prime} \end{array} \right]} _ {n _ {\hat {A}} + n _ {\hat {B}}} \tag {12.28}
$$
