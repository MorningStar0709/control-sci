# Appendix

The ADM1 simplifications of Weinrich and Nelles [15] have been transformed into standard control notation. This section summarizes the model equations and parameters of all successfully investigated models, starting with the simplest model structures. The following notation was applied:

• $x _ { i }$ - states (mass concentrations of the species involved),   
• $y _ { i }$ - measurements   
• u - control variable (feed volume flow)   
• $\xi _ { i }$ - time-variant, uncertain parameters (inlet mass concentration)   
• $\theta _ { i }$ - time-variant parameters   
• $c _ { i }$ - (aggregated) time-invariant parameters   
• $a _ { i j }$ - time-invariant stoichiometric coefficients

States and inlet concentrations are usually given in $\mathrm { g / l . ^ { 3 } }$ The feed volume flow is stated in $1 / \mathrm { d } .$ . Units of states, inputs and measurements are summarized in Table 4. Units of all model parameters can be reviewed in [28].
