There is still only one EOM but it has the additional spring element:

$$J \ddot {\theta} + B (\dot {\theta} - 0) + K (\theta - 0) = \tau (t)$$

or

$$J \ddot {\theta} + B \dot {\theta} + K \theta = \tau (t)$$
