- \left(2 k _ {A D} \lambda_ {\min} \{L _ {A} \} - 1\right) \lambda_ {\max} \{J \} ^ {- 1} \left(\frac {1}{2} \psi_ {A} ^ {\mathrm{T}} J \psi_ {A}\right) \right.- 2 ^ {\frac {1}{p}} k _ {A D} \lambda_ {\min} \{L _ {A} \} \lambda_ {\max} \{J \} ^ {- \frac {1}{p}} \left(\frac {1}{2} \psi_ {A} ^ {\mathrm{T}} J \psi_ {A}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{p}}- 2 \lambda_ {\min} \left\{L _ {A} \right\} \left(\frac {1}{2} k _ {A I} \psi_ {A I} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \psi_ {A I}\right)- 2 ^ {\frac {1}{p}} k _ {A P} ^ {\frac {p - 1}{p}} \lambda_ {\min} \left\{L _ {A} \right\} \left(\frac {1}{2} k _ {A I} \psi_ {A I} ^ {\mathrm{T}} \psi_ {A I}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{p}}, \tag {96}$$

where $\Gamma _ { a 1 } , \Gamma _ { a 2 }$ and $\mathcal { P } _ { a }$ are defined by 76 in the proof of Theorem 3. Simplify (96):

$$\dot {V} _ {A} \leq - \Gamma_ {A 1} V _ {A} - \Gamma_ {A 2} V _ {A} ^ {\frac {1}{p}} \tag {97}$$

where $\Gamma _ { A 1 }$ and $\Gamma _ { A 2 }$ are defined as:

$$\Gamma_ {A 1} = \min \left\{\left(\Gamma_ {a 1} - \frac {1}{2} \lambda_ {\min} \{J ^ {- 2} \} ^ {- 1} \lambda_ {\min} \{\mathcal {P} _ {a} \} ^ {- 1}\right), \right.\left. 2 \Big (k _ {A D} \lambda_ {\min} \{L _ {A} \} - \frac {1}{2} \Big) \lambda_ {\max} \{J \} ^ {- 1}, \kappa_ {A}, 2 \lambda_ {\min} \{L _ {A} \} \right\},\Gamma_ {A 2} = \min \left\{\Gamma_ {a 2}, 2 ^ {\frac {1}{p}} k _ {A D} \lambda_ {\min} \{L _ {A} \} \lambda_ {\max} \{J \} ^ {- \frac {1}{p}}, \right.\left. \kappa_ {A} k _ {A P} ^ {\frac {p - 1}{p}}, 2 ^ {\frac {1}{p}} k _ {A P} ^ {\frac {p - 1}{p}} \lambda_ {\min} \{L _ {A} \} \right\}.$$

Based on (97), we conclude that when the resultant disturbance torque is constant $( \dot { \tau } _ { D } = 0 )$ , and the ESO and ADRC gains satisfy the constraints in Proposition 2 and Proposition 4, the tracking error dynamics (93) is AG-FFTS. Since the discussion on the equilibrium set including SO(3) is covered in the proof of Theorem 3, we omit it here in this proof for brevity. This concludes the proof of Theorem 5.
