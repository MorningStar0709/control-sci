(1) For a fixed mean-field parameter $\theta \in \mathbb { R } _ { + }$ , we solve the ergodic control problem aiming at maximizing (2.8) over $\mathcal { A } _ { e }$ .   
(2) We determine the stationary distribution of the optimally controlled state process, we impose the consistency condition (2) in Definition 2.1, and we prove the existence and uniqueness of the mean-field parameter $\theta ^ { * }$ via a fixed-point argument.   
(3) We finally show that the constructed ergodic MFG equilibrium realizes an $\epsilon _ { N }$ -Nash equilibrium for a suitable ergodic N -player game of singular control with regime-switching.
