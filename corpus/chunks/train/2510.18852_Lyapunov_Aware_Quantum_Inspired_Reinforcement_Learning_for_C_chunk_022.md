# F. Comparison with Classical Controllers

For benchmarking purposes, the LQRL framework was compared against a conventional PID controller and a classical Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) agent trained without Lyapunov constraints. As shown in Table III, the proposed LQRL achieved lower RMSE and improved transient response, but exhibited higher control effort due to the aggressive stability penalty.
