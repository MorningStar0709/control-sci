# 8.3 Limitations of the proposed method:

Through the theoretical analysis (see Theorem 3.1 and Remark 5.3) and the experimental results, we found that the high-frequency noise in the measurements and the bounded stochastic disturbances in the system dynamics almost do not affect the control performance. However, the low-frequency disturbances or errors in the measurements cannot be suppressed effectively by the sliding mode system, resulting in the reduced control accuracy. The potentially effective methods, such as utilizing the signal fusion with multiple sensors, can be used to obtain the relatively accurate measurements.
