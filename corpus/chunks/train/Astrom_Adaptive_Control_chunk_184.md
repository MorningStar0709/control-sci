# Mixed Direct and Indirect Algorithms

Another direct algorithm can be derived in the particular case in which no process zeros are canceled. In this case we have $B^{-} = B$ , and the model Eq. (3.24) becomes

$$A _ {o} A _ {m} y (t) = B \left(R u (t) + S y (t)\right)$$

which can also be written as

$$y (t) = \frac {B}{A _ {o} A _ {m}} (R u (t) + S y (t)) = B ^ {*} \left(R ^ {*} u _ {f} \left(t - d _ {0}\right) + S ^ {*} y _ {f} \left(t - d _ {0}\right)\right) \tag {3.35}$$
