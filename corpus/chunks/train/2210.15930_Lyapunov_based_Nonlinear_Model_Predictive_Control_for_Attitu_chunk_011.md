# 4 Stability analysis

In this section, we analyze the recursive feasibility and closed-loop stability of the LNMPC. First, we consider the following definition and lemma:

Definition 1 If $x \in \mathbb { R } ^ { n }$ , the maximum norm of x is

$$\left\| x \right\| _ {\infty} = \max \left\{\left| x _ {k} \right|: 1 \leq k \leq n \right\}. \tag {17}$$

For $1 \leq p < \infty$ , the p-norm of x is

$$\| x \| _ {p} = \left(\sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} | x _ {k} | ^ {p}\right) ^ {1 / p}. \tag {18}$$

Lemma 1 For any square matrices M, $\ b { N } \in \mathbb { R } ^ { n \times n }$ ,

$$\left\| \boldsymbol {M} \boldsymbol {N} \right\| _ {\infty} \leq \left\| \boldsymbol {M} \right\| _ {\infty} \left\| \boldsymbol {N} \right\| _ {\infty}. \tag {19}$$

Then, we make the following assumptions.

Assumption 1 The desired and real configurations of the $U A V$ are smooth and bounded, i.e.,

$$\left\| \boldsymbol {\xi} _ {1 d} (t) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq \bar {\xi} _ {1 d}, \left\| \dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {1 d} (t) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq \bar {\xi} _ {2 d}, \left\| \ddot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {1 d} (t) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq \bar {\xi} _ {3 d} \tag {20}\left\| \boldsymbol {\xi} _ {1} (t) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq \bar {\xi} _ {1}, \left\| \dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {1} (t) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq \bar {\xi} _ {2}, \left\| \ddot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {1} (t) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq \bar {\xi} _ {3}. \tag {21}$$

Assumption 2 The UAV torques are bounded, i.e.,

$$\tau_ {\phi} \leq \tau_ {m a x}, \tau_ {\theta} \leq \tau_ {m a x}, \tau_ {\psi} \leq \tau_ {m a x}. \tag {22}$$
