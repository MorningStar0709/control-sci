Sometimes specications are expressed in terms of inequalities. For example, if the specication is $T _ { s } \le$ 0.25, then any pole location to the left of σ = −0.25 meets the specication. In drawing inequality performance specs in the s plane, we shade the region which the poles should NOT occupy to meet the spec. Two inequality specications generate a region in the s-plane which the poles must be in to meet the specications (Figure 9.5).
