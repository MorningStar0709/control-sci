Equations of the motion of the quadrotor are presented from Eqs. (17) to (22).

$$\ddot {X} = \frac {U _ {1}}{m} [ c (\varphi) s (\theta) c (\psi) + s (\varphi) s (\psi) ] \tag {17}\ddot {Y} = \frac {U _ {1}}{m} [ s (\theta) s (\psi) c (\varphi) - s (\varphi) c (\psi) ] \tag {18}\ddot {Z} = - g + \frac {U _ {1}}{m} [ c (\varphi) c (\theta) ] \tag {19}\ddot {\varphi} = \frac {1}{I _ {x}} U _ {2} + \left(\frac {I _ {y} - I _ {z}}{I _ {x}}\right) \dot {\psi} \dot {\theta} + w _ {r} \frac {J _ {R}}{I _ {x}} \dot {\theta} \tag {20}\ddot {\theta} = \frac {1}{I _ {y}} U _ {3} + \left(\frac {I _ {z} - I _ {x}}{I _ {y}}\right) \dot {\theta} \dot {\psi} + w _ {r} \frac {J _ {R}}{I _ {y}} \dot {\varphi} \tag {21}\ddot {\psi} = \frac {1}{I _ {z}} U _ {4} + \left(\frac {I _ {x} - I _ {y}}{I _ {z}}\right) \dot {\theta} \dot {\varphi} \tag {22}$$
