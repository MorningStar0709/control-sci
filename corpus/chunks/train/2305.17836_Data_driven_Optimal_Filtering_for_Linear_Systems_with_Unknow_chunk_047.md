$$
\begin{array}{l} X - X ^ {*} - A _ {L} (X - X ^ {*}) A _ {L} ^ {\intercal} \\ = (L ^ {*} R - A _ {L ^ {*}} X ^ {*} H ^ {\intercal}) (L - L ^ {*}) ^ {\intercal} + (L - L ^ {*}) (R L ^ {* \intercal} - H X ^ {*} A _ {L ^ {*}} ^ {\intercal}) \\ + (L - L ^ {*}) R (L - L ^ {*}) ^ {\intercal} + (L - L ^ {*}) H X ^ {*} H ^ {\intercal} (L - L ^ {*}) ^ {\intercal} \\ = (L - L ^ {*}) (R + H X ^ {*} H ^ {\intercal}) (L - L ^ {*}) ^ {\intercal} \\ \end{array}
$$

where the second equality follows because $Y _ { ( L ) } \succ 0$ and thus

$$L ^ {*} R - A _ {L ^ {*}} X ^ {*} H ^ {\intercal} = - Y _ {(L)} ^ {- 1} \nabla J (L ^ {*}) = 0.$$

Recall that

$$J (L) - J \left(L ^ {*}\right) = \operatorname{tr} \left[ \left(X - X ^ {*}\right) H ^ {\intercal} H \right],$$

then by the equality in (20) and cyclic property of trace we obtain

$$J (L) - J \left(L ^ {*}\right) = \operatorname{tr} \left[ Z Y _ {(L)} \right],$$

where

$$Z := (L - L ^ {*}) (R + H X ^ {*} H ^ {\intercal}) (L - L ^ {*}) ^ {\intercal}\succeq \underline {{\lambda}} (R) (L - L ^ {*}) (L - L ^ {*}) ^ {\intercal}.$$

Therefore, for any $L \in S _ { \alpha }$ , we have

$$J (L) - J \left(L ^ {*}\right) \geq \underline {{\lambda}} \left(Y _ {(L)}\right) \operatorname{tr} [ Z ] \geq \underline {{\lambda}} (R) \kappa_ {\alpha} \| L - L ^ {*} \| _ {F} ^ {2},$$

and thus, we complete the proof of first part by the following choice of

$$c _ {3} (\alpha) = \underline {{\lambda}} (R) \kappa_ {\alpha}.$$
