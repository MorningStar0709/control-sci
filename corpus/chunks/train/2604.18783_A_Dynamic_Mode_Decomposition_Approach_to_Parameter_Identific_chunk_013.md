$$(1 2) \Longleftrightarrow \forall \delta \in \mathbb {R} ^ {M}, G _ {\beta} ^ {+} I ^ {\top} \delta = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {M} \sigma_ {i} \left\langle \delta^ {\top} d, v _ {i} ^ {\top} d \right\rangle_ {\tilde {H} _ {d}} w _ {i}\Longleftrightarrow \forall \delta \in \mathbb {R} ^ {M}, G _ {\beta} ^ {+} I ^ {\top} \delta = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {M} \sigma_ {i} \left(w _ {i} v _ {i} ^ {\top} G _ {d}\right) \delta\Longleftarrow G _ {\beta} ^ {+} I ^ {\top} = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {M} \sigma_ {i} \left(w _ {i} v _ {i} ^ {\top}\right) G _ {d}\Longleftarrow G _ {\beta} ^ {+} I ^ {\top} G _ {d} ^ {+} = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {M} \sigma_ {i} w _ {i} v _ {i} ^ {\top} = W \Sigma V ^ {\top},$$
