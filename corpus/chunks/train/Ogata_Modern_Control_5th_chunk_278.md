B–5–1. A thermometer requires 1 min to indicate 98% of the response to a step input. Assuming the thermometer to be a first-order system, find the time constant.

If the thermometer is placed in a bath, the temperature of which is changing linearly at a rate of 10°min, how much error does the thermometer show?

$$G (s) = \frac {1}{s (s + 1)}$$
