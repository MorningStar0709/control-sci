# V. RESULTS AND DISCUSSION

This section presents the performance of the proposed quaternion-based position and attitude controllers for VTOL-UAV. The stability and tracking control capabilities are implemented to verify the proposed QABC and QASMC. Firstly, we simulate the proposed control system to study its performance in handling unknown time-varying parameters (m, J11, J22, $J _ { 4 4 } )$ uncertainties. Subsequently, a comprehensive comparison is provided to confirm the superiority and singularity-free of the proposed quaternion-based controllers compared to a commonly established method (Euler-based approach) in the literature.
