Suppose that we choose an admissible control $u \in \mathcal { U }$ from Lemma 1. In Proposition 2, we prove that for any $\delta > 0$ and $U _ { \delta }$ , one can find an admissible control ¯u such that $\bar { u } ( s ) \in U _ { \delta }$ and $| \bar { u } ( s ) - u ( s ) | < \delta$ for $s \in [ t , T ]$ .
