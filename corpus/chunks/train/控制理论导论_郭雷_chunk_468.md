# 6.7.1 试证明非线性系统

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \dot {x} = f (x) + g (x) u, \\ y = h (x) \end{array} \right.
$$

是 $\gamma$ -耗散的充分必要条件为如下 Hamilton-Jacobi 不等式有半正定解 $V(x)$ :

$$\frac {\partial V}{\partial x} f (x) + \frac {1}{4 \gamma^ {2}} \frac {\partial V}{\partial x} g (x) g ^ {T} (x) \frac {\partial^ {T} V}{\partial x} + h ^ {T} (x) h (x) \leqslant 0, \quad \forall x.$$
