$$- \frac {1}{k} > 1 \quad \text { or } \quad k > - 1, \quad k < 0$$

or, more concisely,

$$- 1 < k < 8.$$

If $(- \frac{1}{k}, 0)$ lies between $(- \frac{1}{8}, 0)$ and the origin, $N = 2$ and the closed-loop system has two RHP poles. This occurs, then, for $k > 8$ .
