In proving Theorem 2, we also show that the class of truncated linear controllers satisfies two natural assumptions of continuity first proposed in Schiffer and Janson [2024], formalized in the following two lemmas. Informally, Lemma 1 says that the cost of the optimal truncated linear controller is Lipschitz continuous in the dynamics. Therefore, using the optimal controller for dynamics θ that are close to the true dynamics $\theta ^ { * }$ does not incur significantly higher cost.
