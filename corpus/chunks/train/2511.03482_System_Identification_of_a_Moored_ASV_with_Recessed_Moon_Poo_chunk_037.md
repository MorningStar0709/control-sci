This paper aims to assess the capability of HDMDc and BHDMDc as data-driven system identification methods for obtaining a reduced-order model of the CK-14e from experimental measurements, predicting the vessel response to waves not only under the same conditions used for learning the model, but also in unseen conditions. For this reason, the data from each experimental run in irregular waves was split into training (the first half) and testing data (the second half). Data from regular wave tests are used completely as testing.
