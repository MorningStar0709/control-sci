$$\dot {V} (t) \leq - \frac {c _ {1}}{5 c _ {2}} V (t) - \frac {c _ {1}}{5 c _ {3}} V ^ {\frac {2 \beta}{\beta + 1}} (t) - \frac {c _ {1}}{5 c _ {4}} V ^ {\frac {2 \alpha}{\alpha + 1}} (t). \tag {47}$$

Case II: $t \in \Pi _ { D } ( t _ { 0 } , t )$ .

Taking the time derivative of V (t) along (7b) yields

$$\dot {V} (t) = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} k _ {i} (\mu_ {1} \varsigma_ {i} (t) + \mu_ {2} \mathrm{sig} ^ {\alpha} (\varsigma_ {i} (t)) + \mu_ {3} \mathrm{sig} ^ {\beta} (\varsigma_ {i} (t))) ^ {T} S \varsigma_ {i} (t)\leq \left| \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} k _ {i} \left(\mu_ {1} \varsigma_ {i} (t) + \mu_ {2} \operatorname{sig} ^ {\alpha} \left(\varsigma_ {i} (t)\right) + \mu_ {3} \operatorname{sig} ^ {\beta} \left(\varsigma_ {i} (t)\right)\right) ^ {T} S \varsigma_ {i} (t) \right|.$$

Then, by the triangle inequality, one further has

$$\dot {V} (t) \leq \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} k _ {i} (\mu_ {1} | \varsigma_ {i} ^ {T} (t) S \varsigma_ {i} (t) | + \mu_ {2} | (\mathbf {s i g} ^ {\alpha} (\varsigma_ {i} (t))) ^ {T} S \varsigma_ {i} (t) |+ \mu_ {3} | (\mathrm{sig} ^ {\beta} (\varsigma_ {i} (t))) ^ {T} S \varsigma_ {i} (t) |). \tag {48}$$

Note that

$$\left| \varsigma_ {i} ^ {T} (t) S \varsigma_ {i} (t) \right| \leq \| S \| \left\| \varsigma_ {i} ^ {2} (t) \right\| _ {1}. \tag {49}$$

In addition, via Lemma 4, one can infer that

$$\left| \left(\operatorname{sig} ^ {\alpha} \left(\varsigma_ {i} (t)\right)\right) ^ {T} S \varsigma_ {i} (t) \right| \leq q ^ {\frac {1 - \alpha}{2}} \| S \| \| \varsigma_ {i} ^ {\alpha + 1} (t) \| _ {1}, \tag {50}\left| \left(\operatorname{sig} ^ {\beta} (\varsigma_ {i} (t))\right) ^ {T} S _ {\varsigma_ {i}} (t) \right| \leq q ^ {\frac {\beta - 1}{2}} \| S \| \| \varsigma_ {i} ^ {\beta + 1} (t) \| _ {1}. \tag {51}$$

Substituting (49)–(51) into (48) leads to

$$\dot {V} (t) \leq \| S \| (\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} k _ {i} (\mu_ {1} \| \varsigma_ {i} ^ {2} (t) \| _ {1} + q ^ {\frac {1 - \alpha}{2}} \mu_ {2} \| \varsigma_ {i} ^ {\alpha + 1} (t) \| _ {1}+ q ^ {\frac {\beta - 1}{2}} \mu_ {3} \| \varsigma_ {i} ^ {\beta + 1} (t) \| _ {1}))\leq c _ {5} V (t), \tag {52}$$
