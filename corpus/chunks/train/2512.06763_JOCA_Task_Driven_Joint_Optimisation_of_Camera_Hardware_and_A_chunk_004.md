• We present the first end-to-end framework that jointly optimises camera hardware, adaptive camera control algorithms, and downstream perception tasks.   
• We introduce DF-Grad, a hybrid optimisation strategy integrating derivative-free and gradient-based methods to train deep learning-based ACC algorithms under nondifferentiable rendering of image effects.   
• We demonstrate that our approach consistently outperforms prior methods in both standard and challenging conditions, with a detailed analysis of the impact of joint learning and DF-Grad.

We believe this work advances the automated design of intelligent imaging systems that emphasise task performance in dynamic environments.
