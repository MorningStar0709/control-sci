Therefore,

$$S _ {i j} = \frac {\frac {- 0. 0 2 2 5 9}{0. 2 4 9 2 2}}{0. 4 / 4} = - 0. 9 0 6 = - 91 \%$$

This is a high degree of sensitivity. A negative value for $S _ { i j }$ means that the performance goes down when the parameter goes up.

The important point of the previous two examples is that performance of the closed loop negative feedback system depends strongly on H but weakly on G (especially as $| G H | > > 1 )$ .
