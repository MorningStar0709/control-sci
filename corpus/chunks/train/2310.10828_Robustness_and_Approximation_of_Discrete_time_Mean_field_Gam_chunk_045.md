$$k _ {1, n} = \frac {K _ {F} K _ {1} \left(1 + \frac {2}{n}\right)}{\rho (1 - \beta)} < k _ {2, n} = \frac {1 - \left(K _ {2} \left(1 + \frac {2}{n}\right) + K _ {1}\right) \left(\left(\frac {2}{n} + 1\right) \frac {K _ {F}}{\rho} + 1\right)}{L _ {1} + \beta \left(\frac {2}{n} + 1\right) \frac {L _ {2} K _ {1}}{1 - \beta K _ {2} (2 / n + 1)}},$$

and

$$k _ {n} = \max \left(\beta , \left(\frac {K _ {F}}{\rho} \left(\frac {2}{n} + 1\right) + 1\right) \left(K _ {2} \left(\frac {2}{n} + 1\right) + K _ {1}\right)\right) < 1$$

as a consequence of Lemma 5. Thus, we can apply Theorem 1 to the quantized games, as the operators $H _ { n }$ , when n is sufficiently large, are well defined by Proposition 1. □
