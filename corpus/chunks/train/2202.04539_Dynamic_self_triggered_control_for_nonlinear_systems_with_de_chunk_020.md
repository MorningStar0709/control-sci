# 6. CONCLUSION

In this paper, we have presented a dynamic STC mechanism for nonlinear NCS with transmission delays. From a technical point of view, the main difference to the delay free case is that more involved hybrid Lyapunov functions are required, leading further to more parameters that influence the behavior of the dynamic STC mechanism. The effectiveness of the approach was demonstrated with a numerical example. By taking transmission delays into account, it becomes possible to apply dynamic STC based on hybrid Lyapunov functions to realistic NCS setups, where delays are not negligible. Future work therefore includes to investigate the interplay between dynamic STC and realistic network setups.
