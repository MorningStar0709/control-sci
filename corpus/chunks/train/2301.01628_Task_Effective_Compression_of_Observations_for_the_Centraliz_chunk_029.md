Algorithm 1. Action Based State Aggregation (ABSA-2)   
1: Initialize replay memory D to capacity 10'000.
2: Initialize state-action value function $Q(\cdot)$ with random weights $\theta$ .
3: Initialize target state-action value function $Q^{t}(\cdot)$ with weights $\theta^{t} = \theta$ .
4: Obtain $\pi^{*}(\cdot)$ and $Q^{*}(\cdot)$ by solving (2) using Q-learning [40]*, where $R >> H(\mathbf{o}_{i}(t)) \forall i \in \mathcal{N}$ .
5: Compute $\pi_{i}^{*}(\mathbf{o}_{i}(t)) = \text{Mode}[\mathbf{m}_{i}^{*}|\mathbf{o}_{i}(t)]$ , for $\forall\mathbf{o}_{i}(t) \in \Omega$ , for $i \in N$ .
6: Solve problem (5) by applying k-median clustering to obtain $P_{i}$ and $\pi_{i}^{c}(\cdot)$ , for $i \in N$ .
7: for each episode k = 1 : 200'000 do
8: Randomly initialize observation $o_{i}(t = 0)$ , for $i \in N$ 9: Randomly initialize the message $c(t = 0)$ 10: for $t = 1 : T'$ do
11: Select $c_{i}(t)$ , at agent i, following $\pi_{i}^{c}(\cdot)$ , for $i \in N$ 12: Obtain the message $\langle c_{1}(t), ..., c_{N}(t)\rangle$ at the CC
13: Follow $\epsilon$ -greedy, at CC, to generate the action $m_{i}(t)$ , for $i \in N$ 14: Obtain reward $r(t) = R(s(t), m(t))$ at the CC
15: Store the transition $\left\{c(t), m(t), r(t), c(t + 1)\right\}$ in D
16: $t \leftarrow t + 1$ 17: end
18: Sample $D' = \left\{c(t'), m(t'), r(t'), c(t' + 1)\right\}_{t' = t_{1}''}^{''}$ from D
19: for each transition $t' = t_{1}' : t_{62}'$ of the mini-batch $D'$ do
20: Compute DQN's average loss $L_{t'}(\theta) = \frac{1}{2}\left(r(t') + \max_{m^{*}} Q^{t}(c(t' + 1), m^{*}, \theta^{t}) - \max_{m^{*}} Q(c(t'), m^{*}, \theta)\right)^{2}$ ,
21: Perform a gradient descent step on $L_{t'}(\theta)$ w.r.t $\theta$ 22: end
23: Update the target network $Q^{t}(\cdot)$ every 1000 steps
24: end
