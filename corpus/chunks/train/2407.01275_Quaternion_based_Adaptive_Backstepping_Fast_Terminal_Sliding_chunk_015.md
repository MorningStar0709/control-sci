# B. Expanded Vehicle Nonlinear Dynamics

By substituting (2) in (3), the expanded state space equations of the UAV translation dynamics can be described by:

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \dot {x} _ {p} & = v _ {1} \\ \dot {v} _ {1} & = - 2 m ^ {- 1} F _ {t h} \left(q _ {0} q _ {2} + q _ {1} q _ {3}\right) \\ \dot {y} _ {p} & = v _ {2} \\ \dot {v} _ {2} & = - 2 m ^ {- 1} F _ {t h} \left(q _ {2} q _ {3} - q _ {0} q _ {1}\right) \\ \dot {z} _ {p} & = v _ {3} \\ \dot {v} _ {3} & = - m ^ {- 1} F _ {t h} \left(q _ {0} ^ {2} - q _ {1} ^ {2} - q _ {2} ^ {2} + q _ {3} ^ {2}\right) + g \end{array} \right. \tag {6}
$$

Expanding (5) results in the following rotational dynamics:

$$\dot {p} _ {v} = I _ {x x} ^ {- 1} \left(\left(I _ {y y} - I _ {z z}\right) q _ {v} r _ {v} + \mathcal {T} _ {1}\right)\dot {q} _ {v} = I _ {y y} ^ {- 1} \left(\left(I _ {z z} - I _ {x x}\right) r _ {v} p _ {v} + \mathcal {T} _ {2}\right) \tag {7}\dot {r} _ {v} = I _ {z z} ^ {- 1} \left(\left(I _ {x x} - I _ {y y}\right) p _ {v} q _ {v} + \mathcal {T} _ {3}\right)$$

Likewise, in the light of (5), the quaternion orientation dynamics are given by:

$$\dot {q} _ {0} = - \frac {1}{2} \left(q _ {1} p _ {v} + q _ {2} q _ {v} + q _ {3} r _ {v}\right)\dot {q} _ {1} = \frac {1}{2} \left(q _ {0} p _ {v} - q _ {3} q _ {v} + q _ {2} r _ {v}\right) \tag {8}\dot {q} _ {2} = \frac {1}{2} \left(q _ {3} p _ {v} + q _ {0} q _ {v} - q _ {1} r _ {v}\right)\dot {q} _ {3} = \frac {1}{2} \left(- q _ {2} p _ {v} + q _ {1} q _ {v} + q _ {0} r _ {v}\right)$$
