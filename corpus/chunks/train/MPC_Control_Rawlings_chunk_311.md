# 2.12 Exercises

Verify that the set

$$\mathcal {X} _ {2} := \{x \in \mathbb {R} ^ {2} \mid \exists \mathbf {u} \in \mathbb {R} ^ {2} \text { such that } (x, \mathbf {u}) \in \mathbb {Z} \}$$

is given by

$$
\mathcal {X} _ {2} = \{x \in \mathbb {R} ^ {2} \mid P x \leq p \} \qquad P = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right] \qquad p = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right]
$$
