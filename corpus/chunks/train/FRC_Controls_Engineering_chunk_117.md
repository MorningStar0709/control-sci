# 5.10 Matrix definiteness

<table><tr><td>Type</td><td>Eigenvalues</td><td>Notation</td></tr><tr><td>Negative definite</td><td>All  $\lambda < 0$ </td><td> $M < 0$ </td></tr><tr><td>Negative semidefinite</td><td>All  $\lambda \leq 0$ </td><td> $M \leq 0$ </td></tr><tr><td>Positive semidefinite</td><td>All  $\lambda \geq 0$ </td><td> $M \geq 0$ </td></tr><tr><td>Positive definite</td><td>All  $\lambda > 0$ </td><td> $M > 0$ </td></tr><tr><td>Indefinite</td><td>Positive and negative</td><td>N/A</td></tr></table>

Table 5.2: Eigenvalue distribution and notation for each type of matrix definiteness. Let M be a matrix.

<table><tr><td>Type</td><td>Definition</td></tr><tr><td>Negative definite</td><td> $\mathbf{x}^{\top}\mathbf{M}\mathbf{x}<0$  for all  $\mathbf{x}$ </td></tr><tr><td>Negative semidefinite</td><td> $\mathbf{x}^{\top}\mathbf{M}\mathbf{x}\leq 0$  for all  $\mathbf{x}$ </td></tr><tr><td>Positive semidefinite</td><td> $\mathbf{x}^{\top}\mathbf{M}\mathbf{x}\geq 0$  for all  $\mathbf{x}$ </td></tr><tr><td>Positive definite</td><td> $\mathbf{x}^{\top}\mathbf{M}\mathbf{x}>0$  for all  $\mathbf{x}$ </td></tr><tr><td>Indefinite</td><td> $\exists\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}\ni\mathbf{x}^{\top}\mathbf{M}\mathbf{x}<0<\mathbf{y}^{\top}\mathbf{M}\mathbf{y}$ </td></tr></table>
