# B.1 RL algorithms

Reinforcement Learning (RL) is a field of machine learning focused on how intelligent agents should make decisions in an environment to maximize cumulative rewards (Sutton & Barto, 2018). In our OPS environment, RL algorithms learn a policy that aims to maximize the output pulse power by controlling time delay, as detailed in section 2.3. For this study, we conducted a thorough evaluation of three RL algorithms: Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), Twin Delayed Deep Deterministic Policy Gradients (TD3), and Soft Actor-Critic (SAC).
