# 8.7 Notes and References

The small gain theorem was first presented by Zames [1966]. The book by Desoer and Vidyasagar [1975] contains an extensive treatment and applications of this theorem in various forms. Robust stability conditions under various uncertainty assumptions are discussed in Doyle, Wall, and Stein [1982].
