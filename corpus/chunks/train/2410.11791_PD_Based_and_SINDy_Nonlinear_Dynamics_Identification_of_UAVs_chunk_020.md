# C. Validation using MPC

The validation of the identified models (PD-based and SINDy) is performed in a real-world experimental setup, where the UAV is required to follow a predefined reference trajectory, defined in Table III:

Trajectory tracking is achieved using MPC, which optimizes the control inputs over a finite prediction horizon to minimize tracking errors and control effort. The cost function for OCP is defined as:

$$\min _ {\tilde {\mathbf {x}} (\cdot) \boldsymbol {\mu} (\cdot)} \frac {1}{2} \tilde {\mathbf {x}} _ {N} ^ {\intercal} \mathbf {Q} \tilde {\mathbf {x}} _ {N} + \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {N - 1} \frac {1}{2} \tilde {\mathbf {x}} _ {k} ^ {\intercal} \mathbf {Q} \tilde {\mathbf {x}} _ {k} + \frac {1}{2} \boldsymbol {\mu} _ {k} ^ {\intercal} \mathbf {R} \boldsymbol {\mu} _ {k} \tag {9a}\text { s.t.: } \quad \boldsymbol {x} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {f} _ {\text { din }} (\boldsymbol {x} _ {k}, \boldsymbol {\mu} _ {k}), \quad \forall k \in [ 0, N - 1 ] \tag {9b}\boldsymbol {x} _ {0} = \left[ \boldsymbol {\eta} _ {0} \boldsymbol {\Omega} _ {0} \right] ^ {\intercal} \tag {9c}\left| \dot {\eta} _ {z _ {d}} \right| \leq 5 \mathrm{m} / \mathrm{s} \tag {9d}\left| \phi_ {d} \right| \leq 0. 6 1 1 \text { rad }, \tag {9e}\left| \theta_ {d} \right| \leq 0. 6 1 1 \mathrm{rad} \tag {9f}| \dot {\psi} _ {d} | \leq \frac {5}{6} \pi \mathrm{rad/s} \tag {9g}$$
