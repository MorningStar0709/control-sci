# 1. 固定端点的情况

这时 $x(t_0) = x_0, x(t_f) = x_f$ ，它们不发生变化，所以 $\delta x(t_0) = \delta x(t_f) = 0$ 。而式(3-2)中第二项可写成

$$\left. \frac {\partial F}{\partial \dot {x}} \delta x \right| _ {t _ {0}} ^ {t _ {\mathrm{f}}} = \left(\frac {\partial F}{\partial \dot {x}}\right) _ {t = t _ {f}} \cdot \delta x \left(t _ {\mathrm{f}}\right) - \left(\frac {\partial F}{\partial \dot {x}}\right) _ {t = t _ {0}} \cdot \delta x \left(t _ {0}\right) \tag {3-4}$$

当 $\delta x(t_0) = \delta x(t_{\mathrm{f}}) = 0$ 时，式(3-4)自然为0。
