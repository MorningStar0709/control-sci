# 4. Main result

This section of the manuscript is devoted to the formulation of the $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ criterion-based LMI conditions which ensures the asymptotic stability of the error dynamic (19).

For the stability analysis of the system (19), the following quadratic Lyapunov function is used:

$$V (e _ {k}) = e _ {k} ^ {\top} P e _ {k}, \text { where } P > 0 \in \mathbf {S} ^ {n} \tag {22}$$

Let us consider

$$\Delta V _ {k} = V (e _ {k + 1}) - V (e _ {k}).$$

Through the utilization of (19) and (22), one can obtain:

$$\Delta V _ {k} = e _ {k} ^ {\top} \left(- P + \mathbb {A} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {A}\right) e _ {k} + e _ {k} ^ {\top} \left(\mathbb {A} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {E}\right) \omega_ {k} + \omega_ {k} ^ {\top} \left(\mathbb {E} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {A}\right) e _ {k} \tag {23}+ \omega_ {k} ^ {\top} \mathbb {E} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {E} \omega_ {k}.$$

According to [14], the $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ criterion (21) is satisfied if the ensuing inequality is true:

$$\mathcal {W} _ {k} \triangleq \Delta V _ {k} + | | e _ {k} | | ^ {2} - \mu | | \omega_ {k} | | ^ {2} \leq 0. \tag {24}$$

From (23), the inequality (24) is modified as:

$$\mathcal {W} _ {k} = e _ {k} ^ {\top} \left(\mathbb {I} - P + \mathbb {A} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {A}\right) e _ {k} + e _ {k} ^ {\top} \left(\mathbb {A} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {E}\right) \omega_ {k} + \omega_ {k} ^ {\top} \left(\mathbb {E} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {A}\right) e _ {k} \tag {25}+ \omega_ {k} ^ {\top} \left(\mathbb {E} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {E} - \mu \mathbb {I}\right) \omega_ {k}.$$

Further, ${ \mathcal { W } } _ { k } \leq 0 { \mathrm { ~ i f ~ } }$

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbb {I} - P + \mathbb {A} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {A} & \mathbb {A} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {E} \\ \mathbb {E} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {A} & \mathbb {E} ^ {\top} P \mathbb {E} - \mu \mathbb {I} \end{array} \right] \leq 0. \tag {26}
$$

Through the deployment of Lemma 4, the inequality (26) is equivalent to

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbb {I} - P & \mathbb {O} \\ \mathbb {O} & - \mu \mathbb {I} \end{array} \right] + \left[ \begin{array}{l} \mathbb {A} ^ {\top} \\ \mathbb {E} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] P \left[ \begin{array}{l l} \mathbb {A} & \mathbb {E} \end{array} \right] \leq 0. \tag {27}
$$
