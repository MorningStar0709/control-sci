# DEFINITION 5.3 Uniform Lyapunov stability

The solution $x(t) = 0$ of Eq. (5.25) is uniformly stable if for $\varepsilon > 0$ there exists a number $\delta(\varepsilon) > 0$ , independent of $t_0$ , such that

$$\| x (t _ {0}) \| < \delta \Rightarrow \| x (t) \| < \varepsilon \quad \forall t \geq t _ {0} \geq 0$$

The solution is uniformly asymptotically stable if it is uniformly stable and there is $c > 0$ , independent of $t_0$ , such that $x(t) \to 0$ as $t \to \infty$ , uniformly in $t_0$ , for all $\| x(t_0) \| < c$ .
