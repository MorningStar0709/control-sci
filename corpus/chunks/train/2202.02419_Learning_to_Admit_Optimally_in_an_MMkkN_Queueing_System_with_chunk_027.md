$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {E} \big [ X _ {i + 1} - X _ {i} \big | X _ {i}, N _ {i}, \alpha_ {i}, A _ {i} \big ] \\ = \mathbb {E} \left[ g \left(T _ {i + 1}, N _ {i} + A _ {i} - N _ {i + 1}, \frac {c}{R}\right) - h \left(T _ {i + 1}, N _ {i + 1}, \frac {c}{R}\right) \mid N _ {i}, A _ {i} \right] \\ = \mathbb {E} \left[ \left(N _ {i} + A _ {i} - N _ {i + 1}\right) g \left(T _ {i + 1}, 1, \frac {c}{R}\right) \mid N _ {i}, A _ {i} \right] - \mathbb {E} \left[ T _ {i + 1} N _ {i + 1} \mid N _ {i}, A _ {i} \right] \\ = \left(N _ {i} + A _ {i}\right) \mathbb {E} \left[ g \left(T _ {i + 1}, 1, \frac {c}{R}\right) \right] - \mathbb {E} \left[ N _ {i + 1} g \left(T _ {i + 1}, 1, \frac {c}{R}\right) \mid N _ {i}, A _ {i} \right] - \left(N _ {i} + A _ {i}\right) \mathbb {E} \left[ T _ {i + 1} \mathbb {1} _ {A} \right], \tag {19} \\ \end{array}
$$

where A is the event that a fixed server from the $N _ { i } + A _ { i }$ busy servers remains busy during interarrival $T _ { i + 1 }$ . The second term of (19) can be simplified as follows

$$\mathbb {E} \left[ N _ {i + 1} g \left(T _ {i + 1}, 1, \frac {c}{R}\right) \mid N _ {i}, A _ {i} \right] = \left(N _ {i} + A _ {i}\right) \mathbb {E} \left[ g \left(T _ {i + 1}, 1, \frac {c}{R}\right) \mathbb {1} _ {A} \right]
\begin{array}{l} = (N _ {i} + A _ {i}) \int_ {t = 0} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {t \exp (- t \frac {c}{R})}{1 - \exp (- t \frac {c}{R})} \lambda \exp (- \lambda t) \exp (- \mu t) d t \\ = \left(N _ {i} + A _ {i}\right) \int_ {t = 0} ^ {+ \infty} t \exp \left(- t \frac {c}{R}\right) \lambda \exp (- \lambda t) \exp (- \mu t) \left(\sum_ {s = 0} ^ {+ \infty} \exp \left(- s t \frac {c}{R}\right)\right) d t \\ = \left(N _ {i} + A _ {i}\right) \sum_ {j = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {\lambda}{\left(\lambda + \mu + (j + 1) \frac {c}{R}\right) ^ {2}}. \tag {20} \\ \end{array}
$$

Furthermore, we derive $\mathbb { E } \left[ g ( T _ { i + 1 } , 1 , c / R ) \right]$ ] using similar calculations as above,

$$\mathbb {E} \left[ g \left(T _ {i + 1}, 1, \frac {c}{R}\right) \right] = \int_ {t = 0} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {t \exp \left(- t \frac {c}{R}\right)}{1 - \exp \left(- t \frac {c}{R}\right)} \lambda \exp (- \lambda t) d t = \sum_ {j = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {\lambda}{(\lambda + (j + 1) \frac {c}{R}) ^ {2}}. \tag {21}$$

Next, we simplify the third term of (19):

$$(N _ {i} + A _ {i}) \mathbb {E} [ T _ {i + 1} \mathbb {1} _ {A} ] = (N _ {i} + A _ {i}) \int_ {t = 0} ^ {+ \infty} \int_ {x = t} ^ {+ \infty} t \mu \exp (- \mu x) \lambda \exp (- \lambda t) d x d t = (N _ {i} + A _ {i}) \frac {\lambda}{(\lambda + \mu) ^ {2}}.$$
