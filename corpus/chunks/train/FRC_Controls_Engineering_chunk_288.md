# 9.4.2 Error covariance matrix evolution

Now, we will use this to compute how the error covariance matrix P evolves.

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbf {x} _ {k + 1} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {A} \mathbf {x} _ {k} + \mathbf {B} \mathbf {u} _ {k} + \mathbf {w} _ {k} - (\mathbf {A} \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k} - \mathbf {B} \mathbf {u} _ {k}) \\ \mathbf {x} _ {k + 1} - \overline {{{{\mathbf {x}}}}} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {A} (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{{{\mathbf {x}}}}} _ {k}) + \mathbf {w} _ {k} \\ \end{array}
E \left[ \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k + 1} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k + 1}\right) \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k + 1} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k + 1}\right) ^ {\top} \right] = E \left[ \left(\mathbf {A} \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}\right) + \mathbf {w} _ {k}\right) \left(\mathbf {A} \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}\right) + \mathbf {w} _ {k}\right) ^ {\top} \right]\mathbf {P} _ {k + 1} = E \left[ \left(\mathbf {A} \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}\right) + \mathbf {w} _ {k}\right) \left(\mathbf {A} \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}\right) + \mathbf {w} _ {k}\right) ^ {\top} \right]\mathbf {P} _ {k + 1} = E \left[ \left(\mathbf {A} \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}\right) \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}\right) ^ {\top} \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} \right] + E \left[ \mathbf {A} \left(\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}\right) \mathbf {w} _ {k} ^ {\top} \right] \right.+ E [ \mathbf {w} _ {k} (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) ^ {\mathsf {T}} \mathbf {A} ^ {\mathsf {T}} ] + E [ \mathbf {w} _ {k} \mathbf {w} _ {k} ^ {\mathsf {T}} ]\mathbf {P} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {A} E [ (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) ^ {\top} ] \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} + \mathbf {A} E [ (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) \mathbf {w} _ {k} ^ {\top} ]+ E [ \mathbf {w} _ {k} (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) ^ {\top} ] \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} + E [ \mathbf {w} _ {k} \mathbf {w} _ {k} ^ {\top} ]\mathbf {P} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {A} \mathbf {P} _ {k} \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} + \mathbf {A} E [ (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) \mathbf {w} _ {k} ^ {\top} ] + E [ \mathbf {w} _ {k} (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) ^ {\top} ] \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} + \mathbf {Q} _ {k}
$$

Since the error and noise are independent, the cross terms are zero.

$$\mathbf {P} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {A} \mathbf {P} _ {k} \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} + \mathbf {A} \underbrace {E [ (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) \mathbf {w} _ {k} ^ {\top} ]} _ {\mathbf {0}} + \underbrace {E [ \mathbf {w} _ {k} (\mathbf {x} _ {k} - \overline {{\mathbf {x}}} _ {k}) ^ {\top} ]} _ {\mathbf {0}} \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} + \mathbf {Q} _ {k}\mathbf {P} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {A P} _ {k} \mathbf {A} ^ {\top} + \mathbf {Q} _ {k}$$
