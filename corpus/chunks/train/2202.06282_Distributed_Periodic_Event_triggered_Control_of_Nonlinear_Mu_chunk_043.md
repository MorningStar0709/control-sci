$$
\begin{array}{l} \max _ {\mathcal {R} \subset \mathcal {R} _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i} - \Gamma_ {m} \mathbf {1} _ {N})} \left| e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}} + Y _ {m} s _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}}, r _ {i}) \right. \\ + \sum_ {l \in \mathcal {R}} Y _ {l} s _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i} - \Gamma_ {m} \mathbf {1} _ {N}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}} + Y _ {m} s _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}}, r _ {i}), r _ {i}) \Big | \\ = \max _ {\mathcal {R} \subset \mathcal {R} _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i} - \Gamma_ {m} \mathbf {1} _ {N})} \left| e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}} + \sum_ {l \in \mathcal {R} \cup \{m \}} Y _ {l} s _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}}, r _ {i}) \right| \\ \leqslant \max _ {\mathcal {R} \subset \mathcal {R} _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i})} \left| e _ {i} ^ {\text { out }} + \sum_ {l \in \mathcal {R}} Y _ {l} s _ {i} (\ell_ {i}, b _ {i}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\text { out }}, r _ {i}) \right|. \tag {A.11} \\ \end{array}
$$

Combining (A.10) and (A.11) with (A.9), we obtain (A.7).

Inequality (A.8). Observe that (A.8) with $\tilde { W } _ { i }$ as in (A.4) is equal to

$$\max \Big \{| e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}} + s _ {i} (Z _ {i} \mathbf {1} _ {N}, \mathbf {0} _ {N}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}}, y _ {i}) |,\lambda_ {i} \max _ {\mathcal {R} \subset \mathcal {R} _ {i} (Z _ {i} \mathbf {1} _ {N}, \mathbf {0} _ {N})} \left| e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}} + \sum_ {l \in \mathcal {R}} Y _ {l} s _ {i} (Z _ {i} \mathbf {1} _ {N}, \mathbf {0} _ {N}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}}, y _ {i}) \right| \Bigg \}\leqslant \lambda_ {i} | e _ {i} ^ {\text { out }} | \tag {A.12}$$

for each $i \in \mathcal N$ as $b _ { i } = \ell _ { i } = { \bf 0 } _ { N }$ and thus $\mathcal { R } _ { i } ( \ell _ { i } , b _ { i } ) = \varnothing$ in (A.4) and (A.5). By using the fact that, according to (A.5),

$$s _ {i} (Z _ {i} \mathbf {1} _ {N}, \mathbf {0} _ {N}, y _ {i}, e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}}, y _ {i}) = - e _ {i} ^ {\mathrm{out}} \tag {A.13}$$

we find that (A.12) is equal to

$$\max \left\{0, \lambda_ {i} \max _ {\mathcal {R} \subset \mathcal {R} _ {i} (Z _ {i} \mathbf {1} _ {N}, \mathbf {0} _ {N})} \left| e _ {i} ^ {\text {out}} - \sum_ {l \in \mathcal {R}} Y _ {l} e _ {i} ^ {\text {out}} \right| \right\} \leqslant \lambda_ {i} | e _ {i} ^ {\text {out}} | \tag {A.14}$$

and thus (A.8) holds. ✷
