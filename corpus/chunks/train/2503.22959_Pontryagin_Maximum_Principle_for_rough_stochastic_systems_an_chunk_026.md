Differences $\Delta Y , \Delta Y ^ { \prime } , \Delta z , \Delta z ^ { \prime }$ are defined similarly. Note that $\Delta Y ^ { \prime } = F \Delta y$ . It follows by Lemma 2.3 that

$$\| \delta \Delta Y ^ {\prime} \| _ {\beta^ {\prime}, m} \lesssim K \| \delta \Delta y \| _ {\beta^ {\prime}, m} \lesssim K \| \delta \Delta y \| _ {\beta , m} \tau^ {\beta - \beta^ {\prime}}, \tag {3.27}$$

where the implicit constant depends only on T . Since $\Delta z = F \Delta y = \Delta Y ^ { \prime }$ this shows that

$$\| \delta \Delta z \| _ {\beta , m} \lesssim K \| \delta \Delta y \| _ {\beta , m}. \tag {3.28}$$

Since $\Delta z ^ { \prime } = ( F \Delta y ) ^ { \prime }$ , it follows again by Lemma 2.3 that

$$\left\| \delta \Delta z ^ {\prime} \right\| _ {\beta^ {\prime}, m} \lesssim K \left(\left\| \delta \Delta y \right\| _ {\beta , m} + \left\| \delta \Delta y ^ {\prime} \right\| _ {\beta^ {\prime}, m}\right) \tag {3.29}$$

and because $\Delta z _ { 0 } ^ { \prime } = 0$ ,

$$\sup _ {r \in [ 0, \tau ]} \| \Delta z _ {r} ^ {\prime} \| _ {m} \lesssim K (\| \delta \Delta y \| _ {\beta , m} + \| \delta \Delta y ^ {\prime} \| _ {\beta^ {\prime}, m}). \tag {3.30}$$
