In certain command guidance applications the target tracking data, as opposed to missile tracking data, is the dominant source of command guidance noise. Also, noise within the radar and on the target signal limits the accuracy to which $d \lambda / d t$ can be measured and significantly affects the miss distance. Specifically, augmented proportional navigation offers a reduction in the interceptor terminal acceleration requirement relative to proportional navigation for the same miss distance. However, the terminal noise level g’s increase when APN is used. For more discussion on the application of APN, the reader is referred to [17], [26], and [35].
