As noted in [7] for the deterministic case, CBFs are prone to the curse of dimensionality in multi-agent settings. This motivates more scalable formulations based on mean-field approximations.

The rest of the paper is organized as follows. In Sec. II, we introduce mean-field control barrier functions for advection-diffusion equations, representing the mean-field approximation of stochastic agents. In Sec. III, we introduce the metric we use to describe our safety sets. Finally, in Secs. IV-A and IV-B, we apply our framework to two characteristic settings, coverage and shepherding. Conclusions in Sec. V close our work.
