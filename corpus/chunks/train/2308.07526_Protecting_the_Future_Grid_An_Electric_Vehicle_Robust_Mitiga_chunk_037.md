# 4.6. CASE STUDY 5: SMALLER LA ATTACKS

The previous case studies aimed at demonstrating the EV-based robust mitigation scheme’s success against large LA attacks (800MW) to showcase the mitigation scheme’s effectiveness. In this case study, however, we examine the impact of smaller attacks and the performance of our proposed EV-based mitigation scheme in such scenarios. As a starting point, we repeat Attack 2 and Attack 3 and cap their attack loads at different magnitudes between 100MW and 800MW. Table 2 demonstrates the impacts of such attacks before and after the addition of our EV-based robust mixed H-2/∞ mitigation scheme.
