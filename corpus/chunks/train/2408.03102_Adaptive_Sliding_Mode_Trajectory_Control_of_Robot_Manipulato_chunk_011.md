$$\dot {V} = \eta^ {T} \left[ Y \tilde {\phi} - K _ {1} \eta - K _ {2} s g n (\eta) + \tau_ {v} + \tau_ {l} \right] \tag {17}- \tilde {\phi} ^ {T} \Lambda^ {- 1} \dot {\hat {\phi}}.$$
