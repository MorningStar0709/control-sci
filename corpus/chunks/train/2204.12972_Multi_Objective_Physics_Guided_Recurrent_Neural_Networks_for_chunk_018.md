(2021); G¨otte and Timmermann (2022).

M _ {F, t} = r \mu \tanh \gamma \dot {\tilde {\varphi}} _ {t} \cdot \left| m \dot {\tilde {\varphi}} _ {t} ^ {2} a + m g \cos (\tilde {\varphi} _ {t}) \right|.$$
