The left side of Figure 8 shows the frequency responses for the nominal regret-optimal controllers along the Pareto curve. The $H _ { \infty }$ and competitive ratio controllers are highlighted in red-dashed and blue solid, respectively. These two controllers lie on the endpoints of the Pareto curve. The other Pareto controllers (black dotted) transition between these two extremes for this example. It is notable that the competitive ratio controller has a larger low frequency gain and smaller high frequency gain than the other controllers.
