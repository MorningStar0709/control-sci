$$H _ {i, t} = c _ {i, t} (\boldsymbol {x} _ {i, t}, \boldsymbol {u} _ {i, t}, \boldsymbol {\theta} _ {i}) + \boldsymbol {f} _ {i} (\boldsymbol {x} _ {i, t}, \boldsymbol {u} _ {i, t}, \boldsymbol {\theta} _ {i}) ^ {\prime} \boldsymbol {\lambda} _ {i, t + 1}. \tag {8}$$
