# Regressor Formulation

Taking into account the form of the polynomial S, u(t) can be expressed as:

$$
\begin{array}{l} u (t) = - S ^ {\star} \left(q ^ {- 1}\right) u (t - 1) - R \left(q ^ {- 1}\right) y (t) + T \left(q ^ {- 1}\right) y ^ {\star} (t + d + 1) \\ = - \theta_ {C} ^ {T} \phi_ {C} ^ {T} (t) \tag {7.76} \\ \end{array}
$$

where:

$$\theta_ {C} ^ {T} = \left[ s _ {1}, \dots , s _ {n _ {S}}, r _ {0}, \dots , r _ {n _ {R}}, t _ {0}, \dots , t _ {n _ {\beta}} \right] \tag {7.77}\phi_ {C} ^ {T} (t) = [ u (t - 1), \dots , u (t - n _ {S}), y (t), \dots , y (t - n _ {R}),\left. - y ^ {\star} (t + d + 1), \dots , - y ^ {\star} (t + d + 1 - n _ {P}) \right] \tag {7.78}$$
