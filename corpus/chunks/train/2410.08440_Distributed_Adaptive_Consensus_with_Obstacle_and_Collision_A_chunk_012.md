# C. Local Error Dynamics

The error dynamics are derived by differentiating the synchronization error from (19):

$$\dot {e} ^ {k} = e ^ {k + 1}, \quad k = 1, \dots , n - 1\dot {e} ^ {k} = - (\nu_ {1} L + \nu_ {2} B) (\dot {\bar {x}} ^ {n} - \dot {\underline {{x}}} _ {0} ^ {n}), \quad k = n \tag {21}$$

which, in the expanded form, is written as:

$$\dot {e} ^ {1} = e ^ {2} = - (\nu_ {1} L + \nu_ {2} B) (\bar {x} ^ {2} - \underline {{\bar {x}}} _ {0} ^ {2})\dot {e} ^ {2} = e ^ {3} = - (\nu_ {1} L + \nu_ {2} B) (\bar {x} ^ {3} - \underline {{\bar {x}}} _ {0} ^ {3})
\begin{array}{c} \bullet \\ \vdots \\ \bullet \end{array}
\dot {e} ^ {n} = - (\nu_ {1} L + \nu_ {2} B) (f (\bar {x}) + u + w - f _ {0} (\bar {x} _ {0}, t)) \tag {22}$$
