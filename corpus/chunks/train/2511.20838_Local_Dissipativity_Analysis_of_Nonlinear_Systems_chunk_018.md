# 3.2 Quadratic Error Bounds

Section 3.1 bounded matrix behavior across a simplex of a triangulation. Here, the focus shifts to bounding an inequality constraint within a closed two-norm ball about the origin. As in previous sections, Taylor’s Theorem bounds the inequality across the region. However, by focusing on a two-norm ball about the origin, Theorem 11 expands concepts used in CPA function synthesis to different function types.
