# 2. Multiplicative uncertainties

$$\left\| S _ {y b} \left(z ^ {- 1}\right) W _ {m} \left(z ^ {- 1}\right) \right\| _ {\infty} \leq 1 \tag {8.22}$$

or equivalently

$$\left| S _ {y b} \left(e ^ {- j \omega}\right) \right| < \left| W _ {m} \left(e ^ {- j \omega}\right) \right| ^ {- 1}; \quad 0 \leq \omega \leq \pi \tag {8.23}$$
