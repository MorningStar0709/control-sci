# 3. Decomposition Approach

We decompose Problem MFT into two auxiliary problems: (1) a standard stochastic optimal control problem, and (2) a standard stochastic optimal control problem with a constrained admissible control set. These auxiliary problems may be solved using classical methods. As a result, classical methods can be also used to address Problem MFT, which highlights the main advantage of the proposed decomposition method.

We begin the analysis of Problem MFT using the proposed decomposition method by defining the two auxiliary stochastic optimal control problems, followed by an examination of their well-posedness, solvability, and optimal control characterization, as well as their relation to those of Problem MFT in the subsequent subsections.
