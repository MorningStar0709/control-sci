We study the following problem with its value function characterized by

$$V (t, x, s, v) = \sup _ {\tau \in [ t, T ], \pi \in \mathcal {A}} \mathbb {E} [ e ^ {- \gamma (\tau - t)} U (X _ {\tau} - L) \mid X _ {t} = x, \mathcal {V} _ {t} = v, S _ {t} = s ],$$

where $L > 0$ is the minimum wealth threshold value and $\gamma$ is utility discount factor. From Guan et al. (2017), the value function V satisfies the following HJB variational inequality

$$\min \Big \{- \frac {\partial V}{\partial t} - \sup _ {\pi \in \mathcal {A}} \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi} [ V ], V - G (x) \Big \}, \tag {3.24}$$

where $G ( x ) = U ( x - L )$ and

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi} [ f (t, x, s, v) ] = - \gamma f + \Big [ r + \Big (\frac {\omega (s , v)}{s} - r \Big) \pi \Big ] x f _ {x} + \frac {1}{2} \frac {\eta^ {2} (v) \Gamma^ {2} (s)}{s ^ {2}} \pi^ {2} x ^ {2} f _ {x x} \\ + \omega (s, v) f _ {s} + \frac {1}{2} \eta^ {2} (v) \Gamma^ {2} (s) f _ {s s} + \beta (v) f _ {v} + \frac {1}{2} \zeta (v) f _ {v v} \\ + \eta^ {2} (v) \Gamma^ {2} (s) \frac {\pi x}{s} f _ {x s} + \zeta (v) \eta (v) \Gamma (s) \rho \Big (\frac {\pi x}{s} f _ {x v} + f _ {s v} \Big), \\ \end{array}
$$

with terminal condition

$$V (T, x, s, v) = G (x), \quad x \in [ 0, \infty).$$

The HJB variational inequality (3.24) can be transformed as a nonlinear complementarity problem

$$- V _ {t} \geq \sup _ {\pi \in \mathcal {A}} \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi} [ V ], \tag {3.25}V \geq G, \tag {3.26}(V _ {t} - \mathcal {L} V) (V - G) = 0. \tag {3.27}$$

Adding the slack function $\psi ( t , x , s , v )$ , we rewrite (3.25) - (3.27) as

$$- V _ {t} = \sup _ {\pi \in \mathcal {A}} \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi} [ V ] + \psi , \tag {3.28}V \geq G, \tag {3.29}\psi \geq 0, \tag {3.30}\psi (V - G) = 0. \tag {3.31}$$
