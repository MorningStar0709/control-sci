# 11.7.2 Forward kinematics

Let M be the $8 \times 3$ inverse kinematics matrix from equation (11.14). The forward kinematics are

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{l} v _ {x} \\ v _ {y} \\ \omega \end{array} \right] = \mathbf {M} ^ {+} \left[ \begin{array}{l} v _ {1 x} \\ v _ {1 y} \\ v _ {2 x} \\ v _ {2 y} \\ v _ {3 x} \\ v _ {3 y} \\ v _ {4 x} \\ v _ {4 y} \end{array} \right] \tag {11.17}
$$

where ${ { \bf { M } } ^ { + } }$ is the pseudoinverse of M.
