# 7.3. Transition dynamics with extended states

Expanding the transition dynamics in (1) from time 0 to $T _ { 0 }$ gives

$$\boldsymbol {y} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} = \mathcal {O} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} \boldsymbol {x} (0) + \mathcal {T} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} \boldsymbol {u} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 2 ]} \tag {21}$$

where $\mathcal { T } _ { [ 0 , T - 1 ] }$ and $\mathcal { O } _ { [ 0 , T - 1 ] }$ are the Toeplitz and observability matrices of order $T _ { 0 }$ represented as

$$
\mathcal {T} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} := \left[ \begin{array}{c c c c c} \boldsymbol {C B} & \mathbb {O} _ {d \times m} & \mathbb {O} _ {d \times m} & \dots & \mathbb {O} _ {d \times m} \\ \boldsymbol {C A B} & \boldsymbol {C B} & \mathbb {O} _ {d \times m} & \dots & \mathbb {O} _ {d \times m} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \boldsymbol {C A ^ {T _ {0} - 2} B} & \boldsymbol {C A ^ {T _ {0} - 3} B} & \boldsymbol {C A ^ {T _ {0} - 4} B} & \dots & \mathbb {O} _ {d \times m} \end{array} \right] \quad \mathcal {O} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} := \left[ \begin{array}{c} \boldsymbol {C} \\ \boldsymbol {C A} \\ \vdots \\ \boldsymbol {C A ^ {T _ {0} - 1}} \end{array} \right].
$$

Since the system (1) is observable, $\mathcal { O } _ { [ 0 , T _ { 0 } - 1 ] }$ is full column rank. Thus,

$$\boldsymbol {x} (0) = \underbrace {\left(\mathcal {O} _ {[ 0 , T _ {0} - 1 ]} ^ {\top} \mathcal {O} _ {[ 0 , T _ {0} - 1 ]}\right) ^ {- 1} \mathcal {O} _ {[ 0 , T _ {0} - 1 ]} ^ {\top}} _ {\mathcal {O} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} ^ {\dagger}} \left(\boldsymbol {y} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} - \mathcal {T} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} \boldsymbol {u} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 2 ]}\right) \tag {22}$$

Then pluging in the expression of ${ \mathbf { } } y ( T _ { 0 } )$ yields

$$
\begin{array}{l} \boldsymbol {y} \left(T _ {0}\right) = \boldsymbol {C A} ^ {T _ {0}} \boldsymbol {x} (0) + \boldsymbol {C A} ^ {T _ {0} - 1} \boldsymbol {B u} (0) + \dots + \boldsymbol {C B u} \left(T _ {0} - 1\right) \\ = \boldsymbol {C} \boldsymbol {A} ^ {T _ {0}} \mathcal {O} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} ^ {\dagger} \left(\boldsymbol {y} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} - \mathcal {T} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} \boldsymbol {u} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 2 ]}\right) + \mathcal {T} _ {[ T _ {0}, T _ {0} ]} \boldsymbol {u} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 2 ]} \tag {23} \\ = \boldsymbol {C} \boldsymbol {A} ^ {T _ {0}} \mathcal {O} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} ^ {\dagger} \boldsymbol {y} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} + \left(\mathcal {T} _ {[ T _ {0}, T _ {0} ]} - \boldsymbol {C} \boldsymbol {A} ^ {T _ {0}} \mathcal {O} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]} ^ {\dagger} \mathcal {T} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 1 ]}\right) \boldsymbol {u} _ {[ 0, T _ {0} - 2 ]} \\ \end{array}
$$
