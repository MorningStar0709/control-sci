3. Linear Mean-field Games. In this section, we introduce N -agent linear mean-field games, which are called linear because both the transition probability and the one-stage cost function are linearly dependent on the mean-field term. This linear dependence on the meanfield term is the main difference between linear mean-field games and classical mean-field games.
