# 7.3 Pole Placement

The pole placement strategy is applicable to plant models of the form of (7.1). We will make the following hypothesis upon the plant model of (7.1):

H1: No restrictions upon the orders of the polynomials $A ( q ^ { - 1 } ) , B ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ and the value of the delay d.

H2: The orders $n _ { A } , n _ { B }$ , the delay d and the coefficients of $A ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ and $B ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ are known.

H3: The zeros of $B ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ can be inside or outside the unit circle.

H4: $A ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ and $B ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ do not have any common factors.

H5: The zeros of $A ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ can be inside or outside the unit circle.
