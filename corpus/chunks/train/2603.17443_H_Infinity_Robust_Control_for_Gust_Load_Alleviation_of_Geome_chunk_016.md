# 4.1 Global Hawk-Like UAV

The UAV has a 17.75-m semi-span wing modelled with 20 beam elements, yielding $n = 5 4 0$ full-order DOF reduced to $m = 8$ ROM modes [Tantaroudas et al., 2014, Tantaroudas and Da Ronch, 2017]. The DSTL (Defence Science and Technology Laboratory) geometry representation is shown in Figure 4. Flight conditions: $U _ { \infty } = 5 9$ m/s, $\rho =$ 0.0789 kg/m3, trim angle of attack $\alpha _ { 0 } = 4 ^ { \circ }$ . The static aeroelastic deformation at the trim state is presented in Figure 5.
