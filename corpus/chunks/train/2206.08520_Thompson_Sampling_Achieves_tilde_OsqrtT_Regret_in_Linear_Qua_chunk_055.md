# Appendix D. Constant Probability of Sampling Optimistic Models, Proof of Theorem 6

In this section, we give the proof of the main technical contribution of this work, showing that TS samples optimistic model parameters with constant probability (Theorem 6). The proof follows the outline provided in Section 5. We first provide the proofs of each lemma in Section 5. In particular Lemma 7 is proven in Appendix D.1, Lemma 8 is studied in Appendix D.2, Lemma 9 in Appendix D.3, and Lemma 10 in D.4. Finally, we combine these results to prove Theorem 6 in Appendix D.5.
