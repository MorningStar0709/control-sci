$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {s} = \ddot {q} + \lambda \bigg (\dot {q} + \frac {\dot {\tilde {b}} _ {n e w}}{2} \bigg) \\ = - k (t) \dot {q} - \frac {1}{2} k (t) \bar {\mathcal {Q}} (t) b + w + \lambda \left(\dot {q} + \frac {\dot {\tilde {b}} _ {n e w}}{2}\right) \\ = \underbrace {\left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 0 _ {m n \times 1} & 0 _ {m n \times 1} & - \frac {1}{2} k (t) \bar {\mathcal {Q}} (t) \end{array} \right]} _ {Z \in \mathbb {R} ^ {m n \times (m n + 2)}} \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 1 & 1 & b \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} \\ - k (t) \dot {q} + w + \lambda \left(\dot {q} + \frac {\dot {\tilde {b}} _ {n e w}}{2}\right). \tag {9} \\ \end{array}
$$

The corresponding dynamics for each agent $s _ { i } \in \mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ , is given by

$$\dot {s} _ {i} = Z _ {i} \theta - k (t) \dot {q} _ {i} + w _ {i} + \lambda \Big (\dot {q} _ {i} + \frac {\dot {\tilde {b}} _ {i} ^ {i}}{2} \Big)$$

where $Z _ { i } ~ \in ~ \mathbb { R } ^ { m \times ( m n + 2 ) }$ is defined similar to $Y _ { i }$ . We now define the second part of the control, w at each agent node as,

$$w _ {i} = k (t) \dot {q} _ {i} - Z _ {i} \hat {\theta} ^ {i} - \lambda (\dot {q} _ {i} + \frac {\dot {\tilde {b}} _ {i} ^ {i}}{2}) - \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} a _ {i j} (t) (s _ {i} - s _ {j}), \tag {10}$$

which can be written in an implementable form as,

$$
\begin{array}{l} w _ {i} = k (t) \dot {q} _ {i} - \lambda \dot {q} _ {i} - \frac {\lambda \dot {\tilde {b}} _ {i} ^ {i}}{2} + k (t) \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} a _ {i j} (t) \hat {b} _ {i} ^ {i} \\ - \sigma \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} a _ {i j} (t) \left(s _ {i} - s _ {j}\right) - \frac {k (t)}{2} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} a _ {i j} (t) \left(\hat {b} _ {i} ^ {i} - \hat {b} _ {j} ^ {i}\right), \sigma > 0. \tag {11} \\ \end{array}
$$

The above yields for the entire network the following,

$$w = k (t) \dot {q} - \lambda (\dot {q} + \frac {\dot {\bar {b}} _ {n e w}}{2}) - \sigma \bar {\mathcal {L}} (t) s - Z _ {n e w} \hat {\theta} \tag {12}$$

where $\hat { \theta } : = [ \hat { \theta } ^ { 1 } , . . , \hat { \theta } ^ { n } ] ^ { \top }$ and $Z _ { n e w } ( \dot { q } , t ) \in \mathbb { R } ^ { m n \times n ( m n + 2 ) }$ is defined as

$$Z _ {n e w} (\dot {q}, t) \triangleq \operatorname{diag} (Z _ {1} (\dot {q} _ {1}, t), Z _ {2} (\dot {q} _ {2}, t),.., Z _ {n} (\dot {q} _ {n}, t)).$$
