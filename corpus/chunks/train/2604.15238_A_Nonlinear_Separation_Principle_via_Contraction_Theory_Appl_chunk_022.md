$$\mathcal {W} (\cdot , \cdot , \text { CONE }) \subseteq \mathcal {W} (\cdot , \cdot , \text { MONE }).$$

$$\mathcal {W} (\cdot , \mathrm{DISC}, \cdot) \subseteq \mathcal {W} (\cdot , \mathrm{CTS}, \cdot).$$
