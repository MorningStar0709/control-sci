3.59 Given the system

$$
\dot {\mathbf {x}} = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l} 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \end{array} \right] \mathbf {x} + \left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ - 1 \end{array} \right] u

y = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l} 1 & -. 5 & - 1 \end{array} \right] \mathbf {x}
$$

a. Show that the system is not controllable, and find the uncontrollable mode. Is the system internally stable?

b. Calculate the transfer function $y / u$ and show that the system is input-output stable.

c. Suppose a disturbance input w has been ignored in the modeling process, whose effect is taken into account by adding

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array} \right] w
$$

to the RHS of the state equation. Show that the transfer function y/w is not input-output stable.
