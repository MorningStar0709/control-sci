# A. Perch on different planes

To verify the feasibility of our method, we carry out several tests of perching indoors. Transparent acrylic boards with a width of 120 cm are placed at different incline angles to represent various cases, as shown in Fig. 1 and 5. Both perching target planes and the quadrotor have pose feedback from the motion capture system. Fig. 5 also displays snapshots of the quadrotor in the perching processes and Fig. 6. depicts the roll command and feedback in the experiment of perching to a $9 0 ^ { \circ }$ wall. The quadrotor usually takes less than 2 seconds to reach the targeted angles. The experiments confirm that the quadrotor can tilt to be parallel to the planes and make contact successfully.
