# Variational Encrypted Model Predictive Control

Jihoon Suh, Yeongjun Jang, Junsoo Kim, and Takashi Tanaka

Abstract— We develop a variational encrypted model predictive control (VEMPC) protocol whose online execution relies only on encrypted polynomial operations. The proposed approach reformulates the MPC problem into a sampling-based estimator, in which the computation of the quadratic cost is naturally handled by tilting the sampling distribution, thus reducing online encrypted computation. The resulting protocol requires no additional communication rounds or intermediate decryption, and scales efficiently through two complementary levels of parallelism. We analyze the effect of encryptioninduced errors on optimality, and simulation results demonstrate the practical applicability of the proposed method.
