# 5.1. Toy Scenario

We start with a toy experiment that demonstrates our ideas in a simple setting. We visualise this problem in fig. 1 (a). We have a 2D agent that tries to reach the green box on the western side of the room. The cost is the distance to the goal zone and the agent can observe its location with some additive Gaussian noise that is significantly higher inside a circle in the center (marked by the grey circle in fig. 1 (a)). The agent can control its velocity subject to additive noise.
