We characterize the state-of-the-art of cyber-physical data exfilration techniques (Section II and introduce a generalized cyber-physical covert channel attack model for data exfiltration that is optimized against state estimation techniques to maximize the transmission rate while maintaining stealthiness (Sections III and IV).   
We evaluate our approach on a variety of applications across two exemplary cyber-physical systems and show the efficacy of such an attack (Section V).   
We discuss future directions of such attacks and enumerate countermeasures (Sections VI and VIII).
