# 4 STL Verification using Reachability

In this section, we show how we can use the proposed STL2NN for verifying that a given STL formula $\varphi$ holds for all initial states in a given set. Based on the structure of the plant model and the kind of activation functions used by the DNN controller, we will look at two different methods. We propose the overall verification approach and a reachability analysis based sound and complete method in this section. In the next section, we provide a sampling-based sound and complete method.
