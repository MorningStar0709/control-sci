C _ {R 2, i} (v _ {i}) = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 0 & I _ {z, i} \omega_ {z. i} & - I _ {y, i} \omega y, i \\ - I _ {z, i} \omega_ {z. i} & 0 & I _ {x, i} \omega x, i \\ I _ {y, i} \omega y, i & - I _ {x, i} \omega x, i & 0 \end{array} \right],

C _ {A 1, i} (v _ {i}) = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 0 & \beta_ {\dot {v} z, i} v _ {z, i} & - \beta_ {\dot {v} y, i} v _ {y, i} \\ - \beta_ {\dot {v} z, i} v _ {z, i} & 0 & \beta_ {\dot {v} x, i} v _ {x, i} \\ \beta_ {\dot {v} y, i} v _ {y, i} & - \beta_ {\dot {v} x, i} v _ {x, i} & 0 \end{array} \right],

C _ {A 2, i} (v _ {i}) = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 0 & - \beta_ {\dot {\omega} z, i} \omega_ {z, i} & \beta_ {\dot {\omega} y, i} v _ {y, i} \\ \beta_ {\dot {\omega} z, i} \omega_ {z, i} & 0 & - \beta_ {\dot {\omega} x, i} \omega_ {x, i} \\ - \beta_ {\dot {\omega} y, i} \omega_ {y, i} & \beta_ {\dot {\omega} x, i} \omega_ {x, i} & 0 \end{array} \right],
$$

and $\beta _ { \dot { \omega } x , i } , \beta _ { \dot { \omega } y , i } , \beta _ { \dot { \omega } z , i }$ are the hydrodynamic parameters.

Remark 1. It is worthwhile noting that the dynamics of AUV is a typical nonlinear system as described in (1), having 6 degree of freedom (DOF), i.e., 3 translational DOF and 3 rotational DOF, and subject to the external disturbances as well as numerous uncertain hydrodynamic related parameters.
