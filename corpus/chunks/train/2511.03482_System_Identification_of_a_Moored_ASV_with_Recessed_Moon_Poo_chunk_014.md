# 2.2.2 Regular waves

In the second part of the experiments, the moored model was subjected to head-sea regular wave trains. The nominal wave height h was fixed to $h = 0 . 1$ M model scale (5 m full scale) Three different wavelengths $\lambda _ { w } = 1 . 5 L _ { m } , 2 L _ { m }$ , and $3 L _ { m }$ were tested. Actual realizations were characterized by (average ± standard deviation) $\hat { h } = 0 . 1 0 5 \pm 0 . 0 1 \mathrm { m } , 0 . 1 0 7 \pm 0 . 0 1 \mathrm { m }$ , and $0 . 1 1 6 \pm 0 . 0 1$ m for the three $\lambda _ { w } ,$ respectively.
