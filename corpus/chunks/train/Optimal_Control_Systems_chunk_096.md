# 2.5 Extrema of Functions with Conditions

Summarizing, the extrema of a function $f(x_{1}, x_{2})$ subject to the condition (or constraint) $g(x_{1}, x_{2}) = 0$ is equivalent to extrema of a single augmented function $\mathcal{L}(x_{1}, x_{2}, \lambda) = f(x_{1}, x_{2}) + \lambda g(x_{1}, x_{2})$ as though $x_{1}, x_{2}$ and $\lambda$ are independent.
