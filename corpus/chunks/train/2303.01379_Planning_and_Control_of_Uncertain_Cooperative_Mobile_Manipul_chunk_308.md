# 4.3 Adaptive Leader-Follower Coordination with Transient Constraints

We study next the problem of leader-follower coordination of a multi-agent system in an obstacle-free workspace. In particular, we consider that a leader agent aims to navigate to a predefined position, subject to collision and connectivity constraints, as well as uncertain 2nd-order dynamics. We use innovatively a useful property of the graph’s incidence matrix (see Appendix E) to obtain the desired results.
