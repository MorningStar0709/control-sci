The minimal value of $\bar { m } ,$ which corresponds to the rank of the matrix-valued sample mean, was 3. Hence, the sizes of $\widetilde { G } _ { A }$ and $\widetilde { G } _ { B }$ became $9 \times 3$ and $9 \times 1 .$ , respectively.
