# 11.1 Linear motion

$$\sum F = m a$$

where $\textstyle \sum F$ is the sum of all forces applied to an object in Newtons, m is the mass of the object in $k g .$ , and a is the net acceleration of the object in s 2 . $\frac { m } { s ^ { 2 } }$

$$x (t) = x _ {0} + v _ {0} t + \frac {1}{2} a t ^ {2}$$

where $x ( t )$ is an object’s position at time t, $x _ { 0 }$ is the initial position, $v _ { 0 }$ is the initial velocity, and a is the acceleration.
