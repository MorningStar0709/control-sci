# Exercise 4.11 (Linear-quadratic problem)

Solve the problem

$$u ^ {*} := \underset {u: [ 0, T ] \rightarrow \mathbb {R}} {\operatorname{argmin}} J (u) := \frac {1}{2} q \cdot x (T) ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {T} u (t) ^ {2} d t \tag {230}$$

subject to (231)

$$\dot {x} (t) = a x (t) + b u (t), \quad x (0) = x _ {0} \tag {232}$$

where $^ { a , }$ b are given nonzero scalars, $x _ { 0 } \in \mathbb { R }$ is an initial state, $T > 0$ is a fixed final time, and $q > 0$ is a given positive scalar.
