# I. INTRODUCTION

RECENT advancements in microcontroller and sensing technologies have led to rapid developments in unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). The UAVs find extensive application in tasks such as environmental monitoring, land surveying, and disaster response [1]–[3]. These applications impose specific requirements on the performance of UAV systems, including factors such as endurance during flight, tracking accuracy, and wind resistance capability.
