a) Material Micro-structure: The material model simulated consists of a 2-dimensional grid of dimensions 10x10, 20X20 and 50X50 i.e., 100, 400 and 2500 fully observable states respectively. Furthermore, the model is solved using an explicit, second order, central-difference based scheme. The control inputs $( T , h )$ are actuated at each grid point separately; thus, the number of control variables is twice the total number of observable states(200, 800, 5000 resp.).
