The paper is organized as follows. In Section II, we provide an overview of the decentralized constrained optimal control for CAVs in any conflict area setting, along with a brief review of CBFs to set the stage for the OCBF approach. We also review the time-driven approach for solving such optimal control problems, motivating the proposed solutions to the problem. In Section III, both methods are separately presented, including the formulation and solution of QPs in both frameworks. In Section V, simulation results compare time-driven, eventtriggered, and self-triggered schemes, in terms of their performance metrics, computational load, and infeasible cases to show how constraint violations can be reduced through the proposed approaches.
