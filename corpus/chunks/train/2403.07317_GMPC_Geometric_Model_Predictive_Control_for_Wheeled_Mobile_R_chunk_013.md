# B. Convex MPC Formulation

Since the exponential map allows us to map the element in Lie algebra to the Lie group. Define $\psi ( t ) ^ { \wedge }$ as an element of the Lie algebra se(2) corresponding to $\Psi ( t ) \in S E ( 2 )$ . By capital exponential map (2), we have

$$\Psi (t) = \mathrm{Exp} (\psi (t)). \tag {11}$$

Taking Taylor expression on (11) at the identity, we have

$$\Psi (t) = \mathrm{Exp} (\psi (t)) = I + \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{i !} (\psi (t) ^ {\wedge}) ^ {i}. \tag {12}$$

The first-order approximation of (11) is as follows

$$\Psi (t) = \mathrm{Exp} (\psi (t)) \approx I + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge}. \tag {13}$$

Taking time-derivative on both sides of (13), we have

$$\dot {\Psi} (t) \approx \frac {d}{d t} (I + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge}) = \dot {\psi} (t) ^ {\wedge}. \tag {14}$$

Substitute (13) and (14) into (9a), we have

$$\dot {\psi} (t) ^ {\wedge} \approx \Psi (t) \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} \Psi (t)\approx (I + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge}) \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} (I + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge}) \tag {15}= \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge} \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} \psi (t) ^ {\wedge}.$$

The nonlinearity in (15) still persists due to the presence of the high-order term $\psi ( t ) \^ { \wedge } \zeta ( t ) ^ { \wedge }$ . A naive solution to address this issue is to drop this term and approximate (15) as follows

$$\dot {\psi} (t) ^ {\wedge} \approx \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} \psi (t) ^ {\wedge}. \tag {16}$$

However, (16) is equivalent to (15) when $\psi ( t ) = 0 .$ . Nevertheless, $\psi ( t ) = 0$ is attainable only when there is no tracking error. To provide a better approximation, we approximate (15) as follows

$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {\psi} (t) ^ {\wedge} = \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge} \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} \psi (t) ^ {\wedge} \\ = \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge} \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} \psi (t) ^ {\wedge} \tag {17} \\ + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge} (\zeta (t) - \zeta_ {d} (t)) ^ {\wedge} \\ \approx \zeta (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} + \psi (t) ^ {\wedge} \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} - \zeta_ {d} (t) ^ {\wedge} \psi (t) ^ {\wedge}. \\ \end{array}
$$
