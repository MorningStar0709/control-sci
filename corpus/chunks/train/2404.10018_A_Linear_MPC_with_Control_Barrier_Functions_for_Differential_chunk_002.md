# A. Motivation

AFETY, stability, and optimality of control systems are fundamental problems that have tight conflicting coupling [1]. The increased deployment of mobile robots in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and logistics, encouraged researchers to develop robust and comprehensive control architectures over the past decade [2]. Starting with the early work in [3] where a local Lyapunov-based control design has been proposed, followed by [4] a Dynamic Feedback Linearization (DFL) and thereafter backstepping tracking control approach [5] for the global trajectory tracking of unicycle robots. Addressing parametric uncertainties, sliding Mode Control (SMC) has been investigated by targeting robot stabilization and trajectory tracking [6]–[10].
