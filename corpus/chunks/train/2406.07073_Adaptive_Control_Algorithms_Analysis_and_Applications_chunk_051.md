Contributions by Gevers (1993), Van den Hof and Schrama (1995) have led to the observation that in indirect adaptive control the objective of the plant parameter estimation is to provide the best prediction for the behavior of the closed loop system, for given values of the controller parameters (in other words this allows to assess the performances of the controlled system). This can be achieved by either using appropriate data filters on plant input-output data or by using adaptive predictors for the closed-loop system parameterized in terms of the controller parameters and plant parameters. See Landau and Karimi (1997b), Chaps. 9 and 16.
