$$\boldsymbol {U} \delta \boldsymbol {a} - \boldsymbol {W} - \boldsymbol {X} \delta \boldsymbol {x} = \mathbf {0} \tag {26}$$

Then, we may derive a control law, i.e. 𝛿𝒂 as a function of 𝛿𝒙 by solving analytically the accordingly extended version of (24), i.e.

$$
