Substituting these expressions into the inequality (29) proposed by Loeb, one obtains the orbital stability condition for relay-type control systems,

$$\frac {\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} \omega} \operatorname{Im} \left\{\frac {1}{W (j \omega)} \right\} \bigg | _ {\omega = \omega^ {*}} < 0. \tag {30}$$
