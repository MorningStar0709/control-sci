Now combining (6.133a) and (6.133b) yields

$$(\partial \mathbf {r} / \partial b) _ {\hat {\mathbf {r}}} = \partial r / \partial b \tag {6.135a}$$

The four equations (6.130a), (6.130b), (6.135a), and (6.135b) form the basic equations for generating the 16 in-plane error coefficients.
