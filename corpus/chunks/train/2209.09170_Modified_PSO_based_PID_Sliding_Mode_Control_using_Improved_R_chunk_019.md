# 1. INVERTED PENDULUM SYSTEM

The system shown in Figure 1 consists of an inverted pendulum mounted to a motorized cart. The main aim is to balance the pendulum upwards to a position desirable for the experiment and to maintain the position without letting it fall. The cart is driven by the dc motor, controlled by a controller. A disturbance force is applied on the top of the position.
