$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| a ^ {*} x + b ^ {*} C _ {K} ^ {\theta} (x) - a ^ {*} y - b ^ {*} C _ {K} ^ {\theta} (y) \right| \\ = \left| \left(a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K\right) x - \frac {b ^ {*}}{b} D _ {U} - \left(a ^ {*} - \frac {a b ^ {*}}{b}\right) y \right| \\ \leq \left| \left(a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K\right) x - D _ {U} \right| + \left| 1 - \frac {b ^ {*}}{b} \right| D _ {U} + \left| a ^ {*} - \frac {a b ^ {*}}{b} \right| | y | \\ \leq \left| a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K \right| d + O _ {T} (\epsilon) (d + | x |) + \left| 1 - \frac {b ^ {*}}{b} \right| D _ {U} + \left| a ^ {*} - \frac {a b ^ {*}}{b} \right| | y | \tag {30} \\ \leq | a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K | d + O _ {T} (\epsilon) (d + | x |) + \left| 1 - \frac {b ^ {*}}{b} \right| D _ {U} + \left| a ^ {*} - \frac {a b ^ {*}}{b} \right| (| x | + d) \\ \leq \left(\left| a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K \right| + O _ {T} (\epsilon)\right) d + O _ {T} (\epsilon) \left(\left| x \right| + D _ {U}\right) \\ \leq \left(\left| a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K \right| + O _ {T} (\epsilon)\right) d + O _ {T} (\epsilon) \left(\left| x \right| + \| D \| _ {\infty}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

where in the last line we once again bounded $\begin{array} { r } { | 1 - \frac { b ^ { * } } { b } | = O _ { T } ( \epsilon ) } \end{array}$ and $\begin{array} { r } { | a ^ { * } - \frac { a b ^ { * } } { b } | = O _ { T } ( \epsilon ) } \end{array}$ Therefore, we have shown in this case that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| a ^ {*} x + b ^ {*} C _ {K} ^ {\theta} (x) - a ^ {*} y - b ^ {*} C _ {K} ^ {\theta} (y) \right| \\ \leq \min \left(2 (| a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K | + O _ {T} (\epsilon)) d, (| a ^ {*} - b ^ {*} K | + O _ {T} (\epsilon)) d + O _ {T} (\epsilon) (| x | + \| D \| _ {\infty})\right) \\ \end{array}
$$

Case 4 Lemma 4:

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| C _ {K} ^ {\theta} (x) - C _ {K} ^ {\theta} (y) \right| = \left| - K x - \frac {D _ {U} - a y}{b} \right| \\ \leq | K | | x - y | + \left| - K y - \frac {D _ {U} - a y}{b} \right| \\ \leq | K | | x - y | + \left| \frac {(a - b K) y - D _ {U}}{b} \right| \\ \leq | K | | x - y | + \frac {| a - b K |}{b} \left| y - \frac {D _ {U}}{a - b K} \right| \\ \leq | K | | x - y | + \frac {| a - b K |}{b} | y - x | \tag {29} \\ = | K | d + \frac {| a - b K |}{b} d \\ \leq \frac {\bar {a} + 1}{\underline {{b}}} d + \frac {1}{\underline {{b}}} d \\ = O _ {T} (d). \\ \end{array}
$$

Because these four cases cover all possible situations, we have shown the desired two lemmas. □
