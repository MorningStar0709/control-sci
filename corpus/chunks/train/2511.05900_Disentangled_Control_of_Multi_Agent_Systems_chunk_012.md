Theorem III.1 provides a systematic way toward convergence-guaranteed time-varying controller synthesis, and it can be directly leveraged by stacking all agents’ states and carrying out a centralized synthesis. However, the centralized approach, as discussed in Section I, is not an ideal architecture for multi-agent settings.
