We aim to design a controller (2) for the plant (1) by solving the control problem

$$\underset {A _ {c}, B _ {c}, C _ {c}, D _ {c}} {\text { minimize }} \quad v (A _ {c}, B _ {c}, C _ {c}, D _ {c}), \tag {3}$$

s.t. A is stable,

where the cost function is defined as

$$v (A _ {c}, B _ {c}, C _ {c}, D _ {c}) = \sup _ {w \in \mathscr {W}} \quad \operatorname * {l i m s u p} _ {T \to \infty} \frac {1}{T} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 1} \mathbb {E} \left\| z (t) \right\| ^ {2} \tag {4a}
\text { s.t. } \binom{\chi (t + 1)}{z (t)} = \left( \begin{array}{c c} \mathcal {A} & \mathcal {B} \\ \mathcal {C} & \mathcal {D} \end{array} \right) \binom{\chi (t)}{w (t)}, \tag {4b}
\chi (0) = 0. \tag {4c}$$
