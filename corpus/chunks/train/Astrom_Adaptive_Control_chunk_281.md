# Normalized Algorithms

It follows from Eq. (5.13) that the adaptive system will be unstable if the transfer function $G(s)$ has pole excess larger than 1 and parameter $\mu$ in Eq. (5.14) is sufficiently large. The parameter $\mu$ is large if the signals are large or if the adaptation gain is large. The behavior of the system depends strongly on the signal levels. This will be illustrated by a numerical experiment.
