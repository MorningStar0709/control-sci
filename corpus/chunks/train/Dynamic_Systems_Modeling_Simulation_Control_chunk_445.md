# Laplace Transform of the Input-Output Equation

The basic steps for using the Laplace transform method to obtain the solution of the time-domain I/O equation are

1. Take the Laplace transform of every term in the I/O equation and include the initial conditions. This step will convert an ODE into an algebraic equation in the Laplace variable s.   
2. Solve the algebraic expression from step 1 for the Laplace transform of the output Y(s).   
3. Take the inverse Laplace transform of Y(s) to obtain the time response of the output y(t).
