# D. Benchmark $H _ { \infty }$ control scheme

The $H _ { \infty }$ control scheme designed in [4] is used as the benchmark scheme in the present work. It uses the same nominal linear equivalent model of (2) and similarly aims to minimize single link displacement tracking error at low frequencies, and vertical body acceleration and tire deflection at higher frequencies. Moreover, the zero convergence of the SL angle is addressed via a free integrator. For more details refer to [4].
