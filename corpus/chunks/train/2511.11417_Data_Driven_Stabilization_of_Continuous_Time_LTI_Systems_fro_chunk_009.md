# Filtering

Filter signals: compute for all $t \in [ 0 , T ] \colon$

$$
\zeta (t) := \left[ \begin{array}{c} \chi (t) \\ \hat {z} (t) \end{array} \right] := \left[ \begin{array}{c} e ^ {\Lambda t} \Gamma \\ \int_ {0} ^ {t} e ^ {F (t - \tau)} (G u (\tau) + L y (\tau)) \mathrm{d} \tau \end{array} \right]. \tag {9}
$$
