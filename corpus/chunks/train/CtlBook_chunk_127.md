$$R e (a) = R e (b) \quad I m (a) = - I m (b)$$

$$\omega_ {n} = \sqrt {R e (a) ^ {2} + I m (a) ^ {2}} = | a | = | b |\theta = \tan^ {- 1} \left(\frac {\omega}{\sigma}\right) = \tan^ {- 1} \left(\frac {I m (a)}{- R e (a)}\right)\zeta = \cos (\theta)$$
