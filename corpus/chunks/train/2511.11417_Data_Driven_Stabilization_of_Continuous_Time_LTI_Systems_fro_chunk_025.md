# VII. CONCLUSION

We have proposed a data-driven approach to stabilize continuous-time LTI systems using solely the noisy input– output data collected from an experiment. The resulting algorithm involves a data-based LMI derived from the matrix S-lemma whose feasibility, under an interval excitation condition and a known noise energy bound, is both necessary and sufficient for quadratic stabilization. We have also related the LMI feasibility to the dataset’s signal-to-noise ratio and provided tuning guidelines based on the process and measurement noise finite-horizon $\mathcal { L } _ { 2 } [ 0 , T ]$ norms. Future work will extend the approach to broader classes of systems and consider datasets given by finite samples instead of a full continuous-time trajectory.
