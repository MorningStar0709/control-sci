The second term is evaluated in the same way:

$$\left. \right. - \left\{\int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} a _ {N} (x, y, \mu) \mathrm{d} y\right)\left(\mu (t, \mathrm{d} x) - \mu_ {N} (t, \mathrm{d} x)\right)\right\} \leq \left\| \mu (t, \cdot) - \mu_ {N} (t, \cdot) \right\|. \tag {109}$$

The third term is evaluated as

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left. - \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} \left(a _ {N} (x, y, \mu) - a _ {N} (x, y, \mu_ {N})\right) d y\right) \mu_ {N} (t, d x) \right. \\ \leq \int_ {A} \left(\int_ {\Omega} \left| a _ {N} (x, y, \mu) - a _ {N} (x, y, \mu_ {N}) \right| \mathrm{d} y\right) \mu_ {N} (t, \mathrm{d} x). \tag {110} \\ \leq \int_ {\Omega} \left(\int_ {\Omega} \left| a _ {N} (x, y, \mu) - a _ {N} (x, y, \mu_ {N}) \right| d y\right) \mu_ {N} (t, d x) \\ \end{array}
$$

As in (100), we have

$$\left| a _ {N} (x, y, \mu) - a _ {N} (x, y, \mu_ {N}) \right| \leq \frac {L _ {a}}{\eta} \left(\left| U _ {N} (x, \mu) - U _ {N} (x, \mu_ {N}) \right| + \left| U _ {N} (y, \mu) - U _ {N} (y, \mu_ {N}) \right|\right). \tag {111}$$

If $\boldsymbol { x } \in \Omega _ { i }$ with some $i \in \left\{ 1 , 2 , 3 , . . . N \right\}$ , then we have

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| U _ {N} (x, \mu) - U _ {N} (x, \mu_ {N}) \right| = \left| U _ {N} \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, \mu\right) - U _ {N} \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, \mu_ {N}\right) \right| \\ = \left| g \left(\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} f \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, x _ {j - 1 / 2}\right) \mu (t, \Omega_ {j})\right) - g \left(\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} f \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, x _ {j - 1 / 2}\right) \mu_ {N} (t, \Omega_ {j})\right) \right| \\ \leq L _ {g} \left| \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} f \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, x _ {j - 1 / 2}\right) \mu (t, \Omega_ {j}) - \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} f \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, x _ {j - 1 / 2}\right) \mu_ {N} (t, \Omega_ {j}) \right| \tag {112} \\ \leq L _ {g} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} f \left(x _ {i - 1 / 2}, x _ {j - 1 / 2}\right) \left| \mu (t, \Omega_ {j}) - \mu_ {N} (t, \Omega_ {j}) \right| \\ \leq L _ {g} \max _ {u, v \in \Omega} | f (u, v) | \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} \left| \mu (t, \Omega_ {j}) - \mu_ {N} (t, \Omega_ {j}) \right| \\ \leq L _ {g} \max _ {u, v \in \Omega} | f (u, v) | \| \mu (t, \cdot) - \mu_ {N} (t, \cdot) \| \\ \end{array}
$$

The same bound applies to the last term of (111), and hence,

$$\left| a _ {N} (x, y, \mu) - a _ {N} (x, y, \mu_ {N}) \right| \leq \frac {2 L _ {a} L _ {g}}{\eta} \max _ {u, v \in \Omega} | f (u, v) | \| \mu (t, \cdot) - \mu_ {N} (t, \cdot) \|. \tag {113}$$
