# II. SPARSE IDENTIFICATION OF NONLINEAR DYNAMICS

The Sparse Identification of Nonlinear Dynamics (SINDy) method provides a data-driven framework for identifying parsimonious models of dynamical systems [7]. The core assumption of SINDy is that most dynamical systems can be represented by only a few active terms, while the majority of potential candidate functions remain inactive. This approach leverages sparse regression techniques to select the most relevant terms in the governing equations of the system.
