# 7. Flight Experiments

In this section, the proposed FFTS-ESO is validated through flight experiments. Its hardware and software are custom-designed and developed based on the open-source autopilot PX4 by Meier et al. (2015). To demonstrate the capability of estimating and rejecting the disturbances, flight experiments are conducted under wind disturbances generated by a fan arrary wind tunnel (FAWT) from the Switzerland-based company WindShape. We first describe the hardware and software configurations of the UAV and the setup of the experiment. Afterwards, we present our experimental results including the characteristics of the wind disturbances and the control performance of the UAV when exposed to disturbances generated by the FAWT.
