$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \sum_ {i _ {s p} ^ {p}, \dots , i _ {1} ^ {p}} \langle h _ {0} \rangle u _ {i _ {1} ^ {p}} ^ {(q _ {1} ^ {p})} \dots u _ {i _ {s p} ^ {p}} ^ {(q _ {s p} ^ {p})} + \sum \left\langle \left\langle \sum_ {i _ {s p} ^ {p}, \dots , i _ {1} ^ {p}} \langle h _ {0} \rangle u _ {i _ {1} ^ {p}} ^ {(q _ {1} ^ {p})} \dots u _ {i _ {s p} ^ {p}} ^ {(q _ {s p} ^ {p})} \right\rangle \right\rangle \right| \\ \leqslant N _ {0 ^ {p}} ^ {s} \left(\left| \sum_ {i _ {s p} ^ {p}, \dots , i _ {1} ^ {p}} \langle h _ {0} \rangle + \sum \left\langle \left\langle \sum_ {i _ {s p} ^ {p}, \dots , i _ {1} ^ {p}} \langle h _ {0} \rangle \right\rangle \right\rangle \right|\right). \tag {8.5.31} \\ \end{array}
$$

令

$$\tilde {h} (0) = \sum_ {i _ {s p} ^ {p}, \dots , i _ {1} ^ {p}} \left\langle h _ {0} \right\rangle ,$$

以 $\tilde{h}(0)$ 代替 $h(0)$ , 重复对 $z(t)$ 一致绝对收敛性的讨论即知, 式 (8.5.31) 右端是一致绝对收敛的. 这样就证明了用逐项求导法求 $z^{(s)}(0)$ 的合法性.
