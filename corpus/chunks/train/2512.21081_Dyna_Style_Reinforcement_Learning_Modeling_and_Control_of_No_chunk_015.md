# 3.1.2 Sparsity Coefficient and Library Selection

For this system, the chosen value for the sparsity coefficient λ was 0.9 after iteratively testing values between 0 and 1.

For the library functions selection, the following candidate functions were selected as shown in the following equation which consists of polynomials, sines, cosines and combinations of them in order to accurately capture the system dynamics.

$$
\Theta (X, U) = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c c c} x _ {1} & \dots & \dots & x _ {6} & u \\ x _ {1} \cos x _ {2} & \dots & \dots & x _ {6} \cos x _ {2} & u \cos x _ {2} \\ x _ {1} ^ {2} \sin x _ {2} & \dots & \dots & x _ {6} ^ {2} \sin x _ {2} & u ^ {2} \sin x _ {2} \\ \sin x _ {1} & \dots & \dots & \sin x _ {6} & \sin u \\ \cos x _ {1} & \dots & \dots & \cos x _ {6} & \cos u \end{array} \right] \tag {8}
$$
