# 3.2 Sliding mode control design for the contraction constraint

This section presents our design of $h \left( \pmb { \xi } \right)$ in $( \mathrm { 8 e } )$ using the sliding mode control technique. Let us consider the sliding surface of the control system as follow:

$$s = \dot {\boldsymbol {z}} _ {1} + \lambda \boldsymbol {z} _ {1} = \boldsymbol {z} _ {2} + \lambda \boldsymbol {z} _ {1}, \tag {9}$$

where λ is a positive gain matrix.

Taking the first derivative of (9), we have

$$\dot {s} = \dot {\boldsymbol {z}} _ {2} + \lambda \dot {\boldsymbol {z}} _ {1} = \left(\dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {2} - \dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {2 d}\right) + \lambda \boldsymbol {z} _ {2}. \tag {10}$$

Consider the SMC Lyapunov candidate function

$$V _ {\mathrm{SMC}} = \frac {1}{2} s ^ {T} s. \tag {11}$$

Differentiating VSMC in (11) and noting (3), we get

$$\dot {V} _ {\mathrm{SMC}} = s ^ {T} \dot {s} = s ^ {T} \left(\boldsymbol {G} _ {1} g (\boldsymbol {\xi} _ {2}) + \boldsymbol {G} _ {2} \boldsymbol {U} - \dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {2 d} + \lambda \boldsymbol {z} _ {2}\right). \tag {12}$$

To ensure $\dot { V } _ { \mathrm { S M C } }$ negative, we choose the control signal of the form

$$\boldsymbol {U} _ {\mathrm{SMC}} = \boldsymbol {U} _ {e q} + \boldsymbol {U} _ {s w}, \tag {13}$$

where $U _ { e q }$ is the equivalent control signal that maintains the system state on the sliding manifold and $U _ { s w }$ is the signal that leads the system to the sliding surface. They are chosen as follows:

$$\boldsymbol {U} _ {e q} = \boldsymbol {G} _ {2} ^ {- 1} \left(\dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {2 d} - \lambda \boldsymbol {z} _ {2} - \boldsymbol {G} _ {1} g \left(\boldsymbol {\xi} _ {2}\right)\right) \tag {14}\pmb {U} _ {s w} = - \pmb {G} _ {2} ^ {- 1} \left(c _ {1} \mathrm{sign} (s) + c _ {2} s\right),$$

where $c _ { 1 }$ and $c _ { 2 }$ are positive diagonal matrices. Substituting (13) and (14) into (12) gives

$$\dot {V} _ {\mathrm{SMC}} = - s ^ {T} \left(c _ {1} \operatorname{sign} (s) + c _ {2} s\right) \leq 0. \tag {15}$$

Thus, the Lyapunov stability of the SMC is satisfied. From (15), the detailed expression of the contraction constraint (8e) for the LNMPC corresponding to (12) becomes

$$\dot {V} _ {\text { LNMPC }} \left(t _ {k}\right) = s \left(t _ {k}\right) ^ {T} \left(\boldsymbol {G} _ {1} g \left(\boldsymbol {\xi} _ {2} \left(t _ {k}\right)\right) + \boldsymbol {G} _ {2} \boldsymbol {U} \left(t _ {k}\right) - \dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} _ {2 d} \left(t _ {k}\right) + \lambda \boldsymbol {z} _ {2} \left(t _ {k}\right)\right) \tag {16}\leq - s \left(t _ {k}\right) ^ {T} \left(c _ {1} \mathrm{sign} \left(s \left(t _ {k}\right)\right) + c _ {2} s \left(t _ {k}\right)\right).$$
