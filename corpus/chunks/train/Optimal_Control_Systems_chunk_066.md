# Example 2.4

Find the increment of the functional

$$J = \int_ {t _ {0}} ^ {t _ {f}} [ 2 x ^ {2} (t) + 1 ] d t. \tag {2.1.8}$$

Solution: The increment of $J$ is given by

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Delta J \triangleq J (x (t) + \delta x (t)) - J (x (t)), \\ = \int_ {t _ {0}} ^ {t _ {f}} [ 2 (x (t) + \delta x (t)) ^ {2} + 1 ] d t - \int_ {t _ {0}} ^ {t _ {f}} [ 2 x ^ {2} (t) + 1 ] d t, \\ = \int_ {t _ {0}} ^ {t _ {f}} [ 4 x (t) \delta x (t) + 2 (\delta x (t)) ^ {2} ] d t. \tag {2.1.9} \\ \end{array}
$$
