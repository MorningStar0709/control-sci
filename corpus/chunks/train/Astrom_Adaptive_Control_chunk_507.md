# The Estimation Problem

To solve the dual control problem, it is necessary to be able to evaluate the influence of the control signal on the future outputs and to estimate and predict the behavior of the stochastic parameters. The estimation problem is defined so as to compute the conditional probability distribution of the parameters, given the measured data.
