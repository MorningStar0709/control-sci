# A. Corridor MPC

Now that $\bar { L } _ { \delta r } ^ { l } ( \Delta t ) , \bar { L } _ { \delta v } ^ { l } ( \Delta t ) , \bar { c } _ { \delta r } ^ { l } ( \Delta t ) , \bar { c } _ { \delta v } ^ { l } ( \Delta t )$ are defined, the CMPC scheme [11, Eqs. 17-18] applied to control each inspector along its trajectory is given as

$$
J _ {N} ^ {*} (\tilde {\boldsymbol {e}} _ {x} (k \Delta t)) = \min _ {\mathbf {u} _ {k} ^ {*}} J _ {N} (\boldsymbol {e} _ {x}, \boldsymbol {u}) \tag {16a}
$$

$$
\boldsymbol {x} ((m + 1) | k \Delta t) = \eta_ {d} (\boldsymbol {x} (m | k \Delta t), \boldsymbol {u} (m | k \Delta t), k \Delta t) \tag {16b}
$$

$$
\zeta_ {\delta \boldsymbol {v}} (\boldsymbol {x} (0 | k \Delta t), \boldsymbol {u} (0 | k \Delta t), 0) - \bar {L} _ {\delta \boldsymbol {v}} ^ {l} \Delta t - \bar {c} _ {\delta \boldsymbol {v}} ^ {l} \epsilon_ {d} \geq 0 \tag {16c}
$$

$$
\zeta_ {\delta \boldsymbol {r}} (\boldsymbol {x} (0 | k \Delta t), \boldsymbol {u} (0 | k \Delta t), 0) - \bar {L} _ {\delta \boldsymbol {r}} ^ {l} \Delta t - \bar {c} _ {\delta \boldsymbol {r}} ^ {l} \epsilon_ {d} \geq 0 \tag {16d}
$$

$$
\boldsymbol {u} (m | k \Delta t) \in \mathbb {U}, \quad \forall m \in \mathbb {N} _ {[ 0, N - 1 ]} \tag {16e}
$$

$$
\boldsymbol {e} _ {x} (n | k \Delta t) = \boldsymbol {x} (n | k \Delta t) - \boldsymbol {x} _ {r} (n | k \Delta t) \tag {16f}
$$

$$
\boldsymbol {x} (0 | k \Delta t) = \tilde {\boldsymbol {x}} (k \Delta t), \quad \forall n \in \mathbb {N} _ {[ 0, N ]} \tag {16g}
$$

where

$$
\begin{array}{l} J _ {N} (\boldsymbol {e} _ {x}, \boldsymbol {u}) = \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {N - 1} \| \boldsymbol {e} _ {x} (n | k \Delta t) \| _ {Q} + \| \boldsymbol {u} (n | k \Delta t) \| _ {R} \tag {17} \\ + V \left(\boldsymbol {e} _ {x} (N | k \Delta t)\right), \\ \end{array}
$$
