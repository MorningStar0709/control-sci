As opposed to our own previous work, this paper replaces the finite horizon problem with the infinite horizon problem, and employs approximate dynamic programming to obtain a control online as the density evolves.

Summary. What we do is as follows:

1. Formulate dynamics and robust differential game for finitely many agents;   
2. Formulate the corresponding robust mean-field game;   
3. Obtain DP equations for the robust mean-field game;   
4. Define an approximate value function using weighted basis functions;   
5. Use the DP equations to form dynamics for the weights and traffic density;   
6. Show these dynamics give unique weights and controlled traffic densities;   
7. Demonstrate our ADP using a numerical example, and interpret results.
