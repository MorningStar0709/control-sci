# 2.2 Rigid Contacts

Consider N fully actuated robotic agents rigidly grasping an object (see Fig. 2.1). We denote by $\{ E _ { i } \} , \{ O \}$ the end-effector and object’s center of mass frames, respectively; {I} corresponds to an inertial frame of reference. The rigidity assumption implies that the agents can exert both forces and torques along all directions to the object.
