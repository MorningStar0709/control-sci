# 1.3 Notation

The norm $\| \cdot \|$ denotes the Euclidean norm for vectors and the spectral norm for matrices, while $\lVert \cdot \rVert _ { F }$ is the Frobenius norm. The operators $\mathsf { d } \mathsf { l y a p } ( A , Q )$ and $\mathtt { D A R E } ( A , B , Q , R )$ denotes the solutions to the discrete Lyapunov and Riccati equations, respectively. We use $\mathcal { O } ( \cdot )$ to hide constant factors and $\widetilde { \mathcal { O } } ( \cdot )$ to hide logarithmic terms.
