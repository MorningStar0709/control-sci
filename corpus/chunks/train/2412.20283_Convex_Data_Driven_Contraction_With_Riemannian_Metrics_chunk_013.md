# B. Solution via Duality

In principle, Problem 2 can be reduced to sequence of SDPs by restating it as a polynomial optimization in the indeterminates x and $F _ { i j }$ , the elements of F , and exploiting Scherer’s Positivstellensatz [23]. However, this approach is practically limited to relatively small, low-order systems due to the very poor scaling properties of the resulting SDP with respect to the size of F . To avoid this difficulty, in this paper, we will pursue a duality-based approach to obtain an equivalent condition that does not involve F .
