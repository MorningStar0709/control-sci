Clearly, Corollary 2 also applies to the single-channel case. However, the alternative formulation in (11) leads to a simpler gradient expression in (18), further offering additional insight into global optimality. Substituting this into the Riccati equation (11b) results in a single Riccati equation, as summarized below.
