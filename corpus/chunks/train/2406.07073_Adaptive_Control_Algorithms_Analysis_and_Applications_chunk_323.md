# Predictor Interpretation of the Tracking and Regulation with Independent Objectives

We have a result similar to that for the pole placement. For the plant model of (7.1) with a delay d and $P ( q ^ { - 1 } ) = P _ { D } ( q ^ { - 1 } ) P _ { F } ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ the corresponding controller will be given by:

$$S _ {d} (q ^ {- 1}) u (t) = - R _ {d} (q ^ {- 1}) y (t) + P (q ^ {- 1}) y ^ {\star} (t + d + 1) \tag {7.122}$$

where

$$S _ {d} (q ^ {- 1}) = B ^ {\star} (q ^ {- 1}) S _ {d} ^ {\prime} (q ^ {- 1}) \tag {7.123}$$

and $S _ { D } ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ and $R _ { D } ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ ) are solutions of:

$$A (q ^ {- 1}) S _ {d} ^ {\prime} (q ^ {- 1}) + q ^ {- d - 1} R _ {d} (q ^ {- 1}) = P _ {D} (q ^ {- 1}) P _ {F} (q ^ {- 1}) \tag {7.124}$$

Theorem 7.4 (Landau 1993b) Controller (7.122) through (7.124) for the plant (7.1) is equivalent to the following one:

$$P _ {F} (q ^ {- 1}) S _ {0} (q ^ {- 1}) u (t) = - R _ {0} (q ^ {- 1}) P _ {F} (q ^ {- 1}) \hat {y} (t + d | t)+ P _ {F} (q ^ {- 1}) P _ {D} (q ^ {- 1}) y ^ {\star} (t + d + 1) \tag {7.125}P _ {F} (q ^ {- 1}) \hat {y} (t + d | t) = F (q ^ {- 1}) y (t) + B (q ^ {- 1}) E (q ^ {- 1}) u (t) \tag {7.126}$$

where

$$S _ {0} (q ^ {- 1}) = B ^ {\star} (q ^ {- 1}) S _ {0} ^ {\prime} (q ^ {- 1}) \tag {7.127}$$

$S _ { 0 } ^ { \prime } ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ and $R _ { 0 } ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ are solutions of

$$A (q ^ {- 1}) S _ {0} ^ {\prime} (q ^ {- 1}) + q ^ {- 1} R _ {0} (q ^ {- 1}) = P _ {D} (q ^ {- 1}) \tag {7.128}$$

and $F ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ and $E ( q ^ { - 1 } )$ are solutions of

$$A (q ^ {- 1}) E (q ^ {- 1}) + q ^ {- d} F (q ^ {- 1}) = P _ {F} (q ^ {- 1}) \tag {7.129}$$
