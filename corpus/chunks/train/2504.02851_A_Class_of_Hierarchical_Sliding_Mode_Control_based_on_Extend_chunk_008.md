Therefore, the element $- \Omega \wedge \mathbf { J } _ { M } \Omega$ can be calculated as:

$$
\begin{array}{l} - \boldsymbol {\Omega} \wedge \mathbf {J} _ {M} \boldsymbol {\Omega} = - \left[ \begin{array}{c} \dot {\phi} \\ \dot {\theta} \\ \dot {\psi} \end{array} \right] \wedge \left( \begin{array}{c c c} I _ {x x} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & I _ {y y} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & I _ {z z} \end{array} \right) \left[ \begin{array}{c} \dot {\phi} \\ \dot {\theta} \\ \dot {\psi} \end{array} \right] \\ = \left[ \begin{array}{l} \dot {\theta} \dot {\psi} (I _ {y y} - I _ {z z}) \\ \dot {\phi} \dot {\psi} (I _ {z z} - I _ {x x}) \\ \dot {\phi} \dot {\theta} (I _ {x x} - I _ {y y}) \end{array} \right]. \tag {4} \\ \end{array}
$$

The drag forces $\mathbf { F } _ { d }$ is defined as:

$$
\mathbf {F} _ {d} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c} - K _ {d x} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & - K _ {d y} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & - K _ {d z} \end{array} \right) \dot {\boldsymbol {\xi}} \tag {5}
$$

The gravity forces: $\mathbf { F } _ { g } = [ 0 , 0 , - m g ] ^ { T }$ . The forces generated by the propeller rotor $\mathbf { F } _ { b }$ can be calculated based on the total forces applied on the UAVs in the body frame by:

$$
\mathbf {F} _ {b} = \mathbf {R} _ {E} \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {4} F _ {i} \end{array} \right] \tag {6}
$$

where $\mathbf { R } _ { E }$ is the homogenous matrix transformation:

$$
\mathbf {R} _ {E} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} c _ {\psi} c _ {\theta} & - s _ {\psi} c _ {\phi} + c _ {\psi} s _ {\theta} s _ {\phi} & s _ {\psi} s _ {\phi} + c _ {\psi} s _ {\theta} c _ {\phi} \\ s _ {\psi} c _ {\theta} & c _ {\psi} c _ {\phi} + s _ {\psi} s _ {\theta} s _ {\phi} & - c _ {\psi} s _ {\phi} + s _ {\psi} s _ {\theta} c _ {\phi} \\ - s _ {\theta} & c _ {\theta} s _ {\phi} & c _ {\theta} c _ {\phi} \end{array} \right] \tag {7}
$$

where $c _ { \phi , \theta , \psi } = \cos { ( \phi , \theta , \psi ) } , s _ { \phi , \theta , \psi } = \sin { ( \phi , \theta , \psi ) }$ .

Moreover, $\Gamma _ { f }$ is the general moment generated by the propeller forces and torques:

$$
\boldsymbol {\Gamma} _ {f} = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {4} \boldsymbol {\tau} _ {F _ {i}} ^ {B} + \boldsymbol {\tau} _ {i} ^ {B} = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {4} \boldsymbol {p} _ {O _ {i}} ^ {B} \wedge \mathbf {F} _ {i} + \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {4} ^ {B} \mathbf {R} _ {O _ {i}} \boldsymbol {\tau} _ {i} = \left[ \begin{array}{l} C _ {1} \\ C _ {2} \\ C _ {3} \end{array} \right] \tag {8}
$$
