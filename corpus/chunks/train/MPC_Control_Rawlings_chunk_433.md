# Exercise 3.2: Maximizing a difference

Prove the claim used in the proof of Theorem 3.10 that

$$\max _ {w} \{a (w) \} - \max _ {w} \{b (w) \} \leq \max _ {w} \{a (w) - b (w) \}$$

Also show the following minimization version

$$\min _ {w} \{a (w) \} - \min _ {w} \{b (w) \} \geq \min _ {w} \{a (w) - b (w) \}$$
