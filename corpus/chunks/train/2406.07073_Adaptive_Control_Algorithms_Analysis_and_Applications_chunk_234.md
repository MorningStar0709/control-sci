# 5.3.2 Extended Least Squares (ELS)

This method has been developed in order to identify without bias plant + disturbance models of the form (ARMAX model):

$$A (q ^ {- 1}) y (t) = q ^ {- d} B (q ^ {- 1}) u (t) + C (q ^ {- 1}) e (t) \tag {5.11}$$

The idea is to simultaneously identify the plant model and the disturbance model, in order to obtain a prediction (adaptation) error which is asymptotically white.
