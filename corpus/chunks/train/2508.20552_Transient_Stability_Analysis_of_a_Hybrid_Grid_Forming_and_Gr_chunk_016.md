# A. Multiple Control Combination Region

The control combination determines the dynamics of the device and the overall characteristics of the system. Hence, the primary target is to derive the feasible operating range $R _ { n }$ and its boundary for the $n ^ { \mathrm { t h } }$ control combination, $n = 1 \sim 6$ . Since the bandwidths of the inner voltage and current loops are much higher than that of the synchronization loop, the dynamics of the inner control loop can be neglected when analyzing transient stability issues caused by the synchronization loop, which is a common practice in largedisturbance studies [7]-[11], [13], [17]. The subsequent derivations are based on this assumption.
