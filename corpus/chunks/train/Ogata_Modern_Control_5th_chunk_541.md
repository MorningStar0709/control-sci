The integral $\oint _ { T } d \ln \vert F \vert$ is zero since the magnitude ln∑ $| F |$ is the same at the initial point and the final point of the contour Thus we obtainG.D

$$\frac {\theta_ {2} - \theta_ {1}}{2 \pi} = P - Z$$

The angular difference between the final and initial values of u is equal to the total change in the phase angle of $F ^ { \prime } ( s ) / F ( s )$ as a representative point in the s plane moves along the closed contour. Noting that N is the number of clockwise encirclements of the origin of the $F ( s )$ plane and $\theta _ { 2 } - \theta _ { 1 }$ is zero or a multiple of 2p rad, we obtain

$$\frac {\theta_ {2} - \theta_ {1}}{2 \pi} = - N$$
