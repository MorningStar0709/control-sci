# DEFINITION 5.6 Extended space

If $X$ is a normed linear subspace of $Y$ , then the extended space $X_e$ is the set $\{x \in Y \mid x_T \in X \text{ for some fixed } T \geq 0\}$ .

The extended $L_{2}$ space is denoted $L_{2e}$ . There is now a simple way to introduce the notion of the gain of a system.
