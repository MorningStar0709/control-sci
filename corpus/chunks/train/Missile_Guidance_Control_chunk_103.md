# Translation Equations

(1) Longitudinal Acceleration

$$\frac {d u}{d t} = R v - Q w + (T _ {\text { boost }} - T _ {\text { sust }}) / m - g \sin \theta - (C _ {A} q S) / m. \tag {3.53a}$$

(2) Lateral Acceleration

$$\frac {d v}{d t} = P w - R u + g \cos \theta \sin \phi - (C _ {Y} q S) / m. \tag {3.53b}$$

(3) Vertical Acceleration

$$\frac {d w}{d t} = Q u - P v + g \cos \theta \cos \phi - (C _ {N} q S) / m. \tag {3.53c}$$
