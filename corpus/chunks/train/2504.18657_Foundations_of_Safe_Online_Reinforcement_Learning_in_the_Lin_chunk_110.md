# G.4 Proof of Lemma 22

proof. Let $x _ { T }$ be the state after starting at $x _ { 0 } ~ = ~ 0$ and using the controller $C _ { K } ^ { \theta }$ for $T$ steps under dynamics θ. Therefore, because $C _ { K } ^ { \theta }$ is safe for dynamics $\theta ,$ , we must have that $| x _ { T } | \leq \operatorname* { m a x } ( D _ { U } , | D _ { L } | ) + \bar { w } \leq 2 \log ^ { 2 } ( T )$ for sufficiently large $T$ . Therefore, there must exist an $L \leq 2 \log ^ { 2 } ( T )$ such that $\mathbb { P } ( | x | \ge L ) \mathbb { E } [ x ^ { 2 } \ | \ | x | \ge L ] = o _ { T } ( 1 / T ^ { 1 1 } )$ . Define $W ^ { \prime } = \{ w _ { i } \} _ { i = 0 } ^ { T }$ . We can apply Lemma 41 in the sixth line below to get that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, 2 T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T) \right| \\ = \left| \frac {T \cdot \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta} , T) + T \cdot \mathbb {E} [ \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta} , T , x _ {T}) ]}{2 T} - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T) \right| \\ = \left| \frac {\mathbb {E} \left[ \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta} , T , x _ {T}) \right]}{2} - \frac {1}{2} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T) \right| \\ = \frac {1}{2 T} \left| \mathbb {E} \left[ T \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T, x _ {T}) \right] - T \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T) \right| \\ = \frac {1}{2 T} \left| \mathbb {E} \left[ T J \left(\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T, x _ {T}, W ^ {\prime}\right) - T J \left(\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T, 0, W ^ {\prime}\right) \right] \right| \\ \leq \frac {1}{T} \tilde {O} _ {T} \left(\mathbb {E} [ | x _ {T} | ] + 0 + \frac {1}{T ^ {2}}\right) \tag {Lemma41} \\ \leq \tilde {O} _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T}\right). | x _ {T} | \leq \| D \| _ {\infty} + \bar {w} = \tilde {O} _ {T} (1) \\ \end{array}
$$

The last line follows from the fact that $C _ { K } ^ { \theta }$ is safe for dynamics θ. Finally, we have that

$$
\begin{array}{l} | \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}) | = \left| \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {\infty} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, 2 ^ {i} T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, 2 ^ {i + 1} T) \right| \\ \leq \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {\infty} \left| \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, 2 ^ {i} T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\theta}, 2 ^ {i + 1} T) \right| \\ = \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {\infty} \tilde {O} _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T 2 ^ {i}}\right) \\ = \tilde {O} _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$
