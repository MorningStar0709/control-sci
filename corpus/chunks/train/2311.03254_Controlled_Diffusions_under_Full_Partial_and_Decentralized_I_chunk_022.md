As in the partially observable case, let

$$G _ {t} := \prod_ {i = 1} ^ {N} e ^ {\int_ {0} ^ {t} g ^ {i} (X _ {s}) d Y _ {s} ^ {i} - \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {t} | g ^ {i} (X _ {s}) | ^ {2}} d s$$

$$\frac {d \mathbb {P}}{d Q} := G _ {T},$$
