$$\delta D R = (\partial D R / \partial \mathbf {V}) \delta \mathbf {V} + (\partial D R / \partial \mathbf {R}) \delta \mathbf {R}, \tag {6.117a}\delta C R = (\partial C R / \partial \mathbf {V}) \delta \mathbf {V} + (\partial C R / \partial \mathbf {R}) \delta \mathbf {R}. \tag {6.117b}$$
