Thus, we note that the optimal control (3.5.50) and optimal state (3.5.49) obtained from using the closed-loop optimal control are identical to those of (3.5.43) and (3.5.42), respectively. We can easily extend this analysis for the general case. Intuitively, this equivalence should exist as the optimal control being unique should be same by any method.
