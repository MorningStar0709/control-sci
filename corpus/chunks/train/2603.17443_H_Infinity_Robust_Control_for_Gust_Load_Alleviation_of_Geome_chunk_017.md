The controller commands an initial downward flap rotation, which generates a nose-down pitching moment that partially counteracts the gust-induced lift increment. The flap deflection profile exhibits a smooth bell-shaped response, consistent with the second-order actuator model in Equation (4). The closed-loop wing-tip response shows both reduced peak amplitude and enhanced damping of the post-gust oscillation.
