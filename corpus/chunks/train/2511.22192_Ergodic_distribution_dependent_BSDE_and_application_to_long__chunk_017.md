$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathrm{d} \Big | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \Big | ^ {p} \leq - \eta p \Big | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \Big | ^ {p} \mathrm{d} t + p \Big | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \Big | ^ {p - 1} | b (t, 0, \delta_ {0}) | \mathrm{d} t + p \Big | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \Big | ^ {p - 1} K _ {\mathcal {L}} ^ {b} \mathcal {W} _ {2} (\left[ X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \right], \delta_ {0}) + \frac {p (p - 1)}{2} \Big | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \Big | ^ {p - 2} \| \sigma \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} t + \mathrm{d} M _ {t} \\ \leq - \eta p \left| X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \right| ^ {p} \mathrm{d} t + p \left| X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \right| ^ {p - 1} | b (t, 0, \delta_ {0}) | \mathrm{d} t + p \left| X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \right| ^ {p - 1} K _ {\mathcal {L}} ^ {b} C (1 + \| \theta \| _ {2}) \mathrm{d} t + \frac {p (p - 1)}{2} \left| X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \right| ^ {p - 2} \| \sigma \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} t + \mathrm{d} M _ {t}. \\ \end{array}
$$

Now, using Young’s inequality with $0 < \varepsilon < p \eta / 3$ , gives

$$\mathrm{d} \Big | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \Big | ^ {p} \leq (- p \eta + 3 \varepsilon) \Big | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \Big | ^ {p} \mathrm{d} t + \left(\varepsilon^ {- (p - 1)} (p | b (t, 0, \delta_ {0}) |) ^ {p} + \varepsilon^ {- (p - 1)} (p K _ {\mathcal {L}} ^ {b} C (1 + \| \theta \| _ {2})) ^ {p} + \varepsilon^ {- \frac {p - 2}{2}} \left(\frac {p (p - 1)}{2} \| \sigma \| _ {\infty} ^ {2}\right) ^ {p / 2}\right) \mathrm{d} t + \mathrm{d} M _ {t}$$

If we set $\textstyle 0 < \lambda = \eta - { \frac { 3 \varepsilon } { p } }$ , then applying Itˆo’s formula to $\mathrm { d } e ^ { p \lambda t } \big \vert X ^ { [ \theta ] } \big \vert ^ { p }$ gives, by taking the expectation

$$e ^ {p \lambda t} \mathbb {E} \bigg [ \bigg | X _ {t} ^ {[ \theta ]} \bigg | ^ {p} \bigg ] \leq \| \theta \| _ {p} ^ {p} + \int_ {0} ^ {t} C (1 + \| \theta \| _ {p} ^ {p}) e ^ {\lambda s} \mathrm{d} s,$$

which gives the wanted result. We prove (2.5) by using same computations and (2.4).
