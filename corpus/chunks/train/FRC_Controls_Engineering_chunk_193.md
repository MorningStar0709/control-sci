# 7.3.3 Definition

The model can be discretized as follows

$$\mathbf {x} _ {k + 1} = \mathbf {A} _ {d} \mathbf {x} _ {k} + \mathbf {B} _ {d} \mathbf {u} _ {k} + \mathbf {w} _ {k}\mathbf {y} _ {k} = \mathbf {C} _ {d} \mathbf {x} _ {k} + \mathbf {D} _ {d} \mathbf {u} _ {k} + \mathbf {v} _ {k}$$

with covariances

$$\mathbf {w} _ {k} \sim N (0, \mathbf {Q} _ {d})\mathbf {v} _ {k} \sim N (0, \mathbf {R} _ {d})$$
