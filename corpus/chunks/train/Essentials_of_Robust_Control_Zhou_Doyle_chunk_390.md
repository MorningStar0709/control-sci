# 14.10 Notes and References

The first part of this chapter is based on Sampei, Mita, and Nakamichi [1990], Packard [1994], Doyle, Glover, Khargonekar, and Francis [1989], and Chen and Zhou [1996]. The proof of Theorem 14.4 comes from Ran and Vreugdenhil [1988]. The detailed derivation of the $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ solution for the general case is treated in Glover and Doyle [1988, 1989]. A fairly complete solution to the singular $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ problem is obtained in Stoorvogel [1992]. The $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ filtering and smoothing problems are considered in detail in Nagpal and Khargonekar [1991]. There is a rich body of literature on the LMI approach to $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ control and related problems. In particular, readers are referred to the monograph by Boyd, Ghaoui, Feron, and Balakrishnan [1994] for a comprehensive treatment of LMIs and their applications in control. See also the paper by Chilali and Gahinet [1996] for an application of LMIs in $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty }$ control with closed-loop pole constraints.
