# Process Model

Let the process be the harmonic oscillator with the transfer function

$$G (s) = \frac {\omega_ {0} ^ {2}}{s ^ {2} + \omega_ {0} ^ {2}} \quad \omega_ {0} = 1$$

See Example A.3. The sampled pulse-transfer function is

$$H (z) = \frac {(1 - \beta) (z + 1)}{z ^ {2} - 2 \beta z + 1} = \frac {B (z)}{A (z)} \quad \beta = \cos (\omega_ {0} h)$$
