# 6. Empirical evaluation

In this section, we present two case studies and a series of benchmarks that explore the applicability of our technique for systems more complex than the running example.2 More precisely, we describe the application of the strategy proposed in Section 5.1 to two case studies. In the first case study, we find causes for (systematically inserted) faults on a suspension system of a vehicle. In the second one, we explore an autonomous vehicle platoon to find causes behind communication issues. For the benchmarks, we explore 4 scalable systems.
