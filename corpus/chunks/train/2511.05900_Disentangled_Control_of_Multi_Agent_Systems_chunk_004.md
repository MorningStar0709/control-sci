This paper proceeds as follows. In Section II, we present necessary definitions used throughout this paper. In Section III, first, we identify a situation where the time-varying control barrier and control Lyapunov functions have the same functionality, which helps terminological consistency when we highlight new insights into [15], [22] in Appendix A. Then, we extend the nonsmooth control Lyapunov function framework in [23] to the time-varying case for the preparation of dealing with piecewise-constant graph topologies. In Section IV, we identify two types of Lyapunov functions that cause key challenges in decentralized multi-agent control and develop theorems to address them respectively, followed by a general optimization framework, whose effectiveness is demonstrated across three experiments in Section V. Finally, Section VI concludes the paper.
