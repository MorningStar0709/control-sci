# Solution

Identify the unobservable mode for the network of Example 3.2, with $y = x_{1} - x_{2}$ .

The eigenvectors are

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left[ \begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right] \quad \text { corresponding   to   the   mode } - 1 \\ \left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ - 1 \end{array} \right] \quad \text { corresponding   to   the   mode } - 2. \\ \end{array}
$$

For $y = x_{1} - x_{2}$ , the $C$ matrix is $[1 - 1]$ . Clearly,

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{l l} 1 & - 1 \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right] = 0
$$

so the mode -1 is not observable.
