# 4.3 Experimental Evaluation: STL verification with NN reachability

In this section, we experimentally evaluate the efficacy of our verification method and the TNN (and STL2NN) networks that we have formulated. In the case studies considered in this section, we assume access to the physics-based difference equations, which are used to generate data to train the ReLU-NN plant models. During training we use the difference between the next state predicted by the DNN-based plant model and the actual next state as the loss function.
