# 4 Regularization in direct data-driven control via convex relaxations

In this section, we apply Theorem 1 and derive three direct DDPC strategies via convex relaxations, including regularization using projection-based norm, causalitybased norm, and $l _ { 1 }$ norm. The projection-based norm and $l _ { 1 }$ norm are widely used in the literature (see [1] and its references), and the causality-based norm is new.
