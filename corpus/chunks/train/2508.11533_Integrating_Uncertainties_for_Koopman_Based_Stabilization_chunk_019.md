$$\mathcal {C} = \mathcal {E} := \left\{\left(\boldsymbol {\zeta} + \mathbf {A} ^ {- 1 / 2} \boldsymbol {\gamma} \mathbf {Q} ^ {1 / 2}\right) ^ {\top}: \| \boldsymbol {\gamma} \| \leq 1 \right\}. \tag {27}$$
