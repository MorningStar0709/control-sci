In this problem, we investigate the scenario where the system identification of $( A , B )$ is performed with quantized ,data and a least-square algorithm akin to the Dynamic Mode Decomposition method [11], [12]. While the least square problem—to be described soon—is able to accurately identify the system under unquantized data, it fails to attain such perfect accuracy under quantized data. The objective of this letter is to investigate the effect of the quantization on the system identification and, more generally, on the trajectory tracking MPC problem. Before proceeding further, let us assume the following on the original system (1).

Assumption 1: (A B) is a controllable pair.
