There are also related properties such as differential dissipativity (Forni et al., 2013; van der Schaft, 2013) and equilibriumindependent dissipativity (Simpson-Porco, 2018; Hines et al., 2011); however, these notions have not yet been explored as much in the context of system identification. Similarly, integral quadratic constraints (IQCs) establish an attractive framework to achieve tighter descriptions of input-output properties, with a number of uncertainties that can be characterized by IQCs in Megretski and Rantzer (1997). However, in the context of physics-constrained system identification, IQCs have not been explored much thus far.
