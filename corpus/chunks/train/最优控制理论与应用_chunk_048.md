$$x _ {1} (1) = 0 \qquad x _ {2} (1) \text {自由}$$

要求确定最优控制 $u^{*}(t)$ ，使指标泛函

$$J (u) = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} u ^ {2} (t) \mathrm{d} t$$

取极小值

解 这里 $x_{2}(1)$ 是自由的, 所以要用到横截条件式(3-24), 因终端指标 $\phi[X(t_{f}), t_{f}] = 0$ , 所以

$$\lambda_ {2} (1) = \frac {\partial \phi}{\partial x _ {2} (1)} = 0 \tag {3-30}$$

作哈密顿函数

$$H = \frac {1}{2} u ^ {2} + \lambda_ {1} x _ {2} + \lambda_ {2} u \tag {3-31}$$

由式 $(3-21)\sim(3-23)$ 可求得

$$\dot {\lambda} _ {1} = - \frac {\partial H}{\partial x _ {1}} = 0\dot {\lambda} _ {2} = - \frac {\partial H}{\partial x _ {2}} = - \lambda_ {1}\frac {\partial H}{\partial u} = 0$$

得

$$u + \lambda_ {2} = 0$$

即

$$u ^ {*} (t) = - \lambda_ {2} (t) \tag {3-32}$$

将 $u^{*}(t)$ 代入状态方程, 可得

$$\dot {x} _ {1} = x _ {2} (t) \tag {3-33}\dot {x} _ {2} = - \lambda_ {2} (t) \tag {3-34}\dot {\lambda} _ {1} = 0 \tag {3-35}\dot {\lambda} _ {2} = - \lambda_ {1} (t) \tag {3-36}$$

边界条件为

$$x _ {1} (0) = 1 \quad x _ {2} (0) = 1 \tag {3-37}x _ {1} (1) = 0 \quad \lambda_ {2} (1) = 0$$

可见这是两点边值问题,对正则方程式 $(3-33)\sim(3-36)$ 进行拉氏变换,可得

$$s X _ {1} (s) - x _ {1} (0) = X _ {2} (s) \tag {3-38}s X _ {2} (s) - x _ {2} (0) = - \lambda_ {2} (s) \tag {3-39}s \lambda_ {1} (s) - \lambda_ {1} (0) = 0 \tag {3-40}s \lambda_ {2} (s) - \lambda_ {2} (0) = - \lambda_ {1} (s) \tag {3-41}$$

由式 $(3-38)\sim(3-41)$ 可解出

$$s ^ {4} X _ {1} (s) = s ^ {3} x _ {1} (0) + s ^ {2} x _ {2} (0) - s \lambda_ {2} (0) + \lambda_ {1} (0)$$

代入初始条件 $x_{1}(0) = 1, x_{2}(0) = 1$ ，可得

$$X _ {1} (s) = \frac {1}{s} + \frac {1}{s ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{s ^ {3}} \lambda_ {2} (0) + \frac {1}{s ^ {4}} \lambda_ {1} (0)$$

故

$$x _ {1} (t) = 1 + t - \frac {1}{2} \lambda_ {2} (0) t ^ {2} + \frac {1}{6} \lambda_ {1} (0) t ^ {3} \tag {3-42}$$

同样可解得

$$\lambda_ {2} (s) = \frac {1}{s} \lambda_ {2} (0) - \frac {1}{s ^ {2}} \lambda_ {1} (0)\lambda_ {2} (t) = \lambda_ {2} (0) - \lambda_ {1} (0) t \tag {3-43}$$

利用终端条件 $x_{1}(1) = 0, \lambda_{2}(1) = 0$ ，由式(3-42)、(3-43)可得

$$2 - \frac {1}{2} \lambda_ {2} (0) + \frac {1}{6} \lambda_ {1} (0) = 0\lambda_ {2} (0) - \lambda_ {1} (0) = 0$$

由上两式可解出

$$\lambda_ {1} (0) = 6 \quad \lambda_ {2} (0) = 6$$

由式(3-42)可得最优状态轨迹

$$x _ {1} ^ {*} (t) = 1 + t - 3 t ^ {2} + t ^ {3}$$

由式(3-43)可得最优协态

$$\lambda_ {2} ^ {*} (t) = 6 (1 - t)$$

由式 $(3-32)$ 可得最优控制

$$u ^ {*} (t) = 6 (t - 1)$$
