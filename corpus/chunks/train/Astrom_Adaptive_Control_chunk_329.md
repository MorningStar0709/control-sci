# EXAMPLE 5.13 Second-order system

Consider the transfer function

$$G (s) = \frac {s ^ {2} + 6 s + 8}{s ^ {2} + 4 s + 3}$$

First notice that G has no poles in the right half-plane. Furthermore,

$$f (\omega) = \operatorname{Re} \left((- \omega^ {2} + 6 i \omega + 8) (- \omega^ {2} - 4 i \omega + 3)\right) = \omega^ {4} + 1 3 \omega^ {2} + 2 4$$

Hence

$$g (x) = x ^ {2} + 1 3 x + 2 4$$

We get

$$
\begin{array}{l} g _ {1} (x) = x ^ {2} + 1 3 x + 2 4 \\ g _ {2} (x) = 2 x + 1 3 \\ g _ {3} (x) = \frac {7 3}{4} \\ \end{array}
$$

Since $V(0) = 0$ , $V(\infty) = 0$ , $g(x)$ has no zeros on the positive real axis. The transfer function $G(s)$ is then SPR. ☐
