# G. Result Summary

In summary, the simulation confirmed that:

• The LQRL agent effectively tracked the lead vehicle with acceptable control smoothness;   
• The Lyapunov constraint partially succeeded in limiting instability growth under dynamic conditions;   
• Further gain adaptation or stochastic regularization could improve asymptotic convergence.

Overall, the integration of Lyapunov stability within a quantum policy network demonstrated strong feasibility and provides a reproducible basis for future quantum-safe control research.
