$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} y _ {1} = - x _ {2}, \\ y _ {2} = x _ {2} ^ {2} - 2 x _ {3}, \\ y _ {3} = \sin (x _ {2} - x _ {3}) + 2 x _ {1}. \end{array} \right.
$$

(ii) 设一个向量场 $X$ 在 $x$ 坐标中表达为 $X = [\sin (x_1 - x_2), \mathrm{e}^{x_3}, x_2^2]^{\mathrm{T}}$ . 试给出它在 $y$ 坐标下的表达;
