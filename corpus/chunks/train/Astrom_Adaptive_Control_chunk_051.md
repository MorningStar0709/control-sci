# EXAMPLE 1.10 A general linear controller

A general linear controller can be described by

$$R (s) U (s) = - S (s) Y (s) + T (s) U _ {c} (s)$$

where R, S, and T are polynomials and U, Y, and $U_{c}$ are the Laplace transform of the control signal, the process output, and the reference value, respectively. Several design methods are available to determine the parameters in the controller when the system is known.
