# Appendix F. Visual Tracking

The state estimator we used for ViZDoom and AI2-THOR is based on colored point-to-plane ICP (Chen and Medioni, 1992; Steinbrucker et al.¨ , 2011; Audras et al., 2011). Since MPC assumes some knowledge of the transition dynamics, it makes sense to merge this into the tracker as well. We modify the basic algorithm slightly, adding the squared distance from the transition model’s prediction as another loss term into the objective. This was previously done by Kayalibay et al. (2022) and section 4.2 of their paper summarises the approach. Note that we use the true simulator for emissions while their paper uses a world model.
