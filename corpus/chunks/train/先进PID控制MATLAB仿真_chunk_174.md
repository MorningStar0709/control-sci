$$K _ {\mathrm{g}} K _ {\mathrm{p}} \frac {1}{\omega_ {\mathrm{c}}} \frac {1}{\omega_ {\mathrm{c}}} \frac {1}{T _ {\mathrm{i}}} \frac {\sqrt {\left(T _ {\mathrm{i}} \omega_ {\mathrm{c}}\right) ^ {2} + 1}}{\sqrt {\left(T _ {\mathrm{g}} \omega_ {\mathrm{c}}\right) ^ {2} + 1}} = K _ {\mathrm{g}} K _ {\mathrm{p}} \frac {1}{\omega_ {\mathrm{c}}} \frac {1}{\omega_ {\mathrm{c}}} \omega_ {\mathrm{c}} = 1$$

即

$$K _ {\mathrm{g}} K _ {\mathrm{p}} \frac {1}{\omega_ {\mathrm{c}}} = 1K _ {\mathrm{p}} = \omega_ {\mathrm{c}} / K _ {\mathrm{g}} \tag {2.21}$$
