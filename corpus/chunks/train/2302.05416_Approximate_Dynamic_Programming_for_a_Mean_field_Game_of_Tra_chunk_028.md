$$D _ {\rho} \left[ D _ {A} f (A, B, \rho) h \right] h _ {\rho} = \left\langle D _ {\rho} f (A, B, \rho) h _ {\rho}, D _ {A} f (A, B, \rho) h \right\rangle_ {\mathrm{L} ^ {2} (Y; \mathbb {R})}$$
