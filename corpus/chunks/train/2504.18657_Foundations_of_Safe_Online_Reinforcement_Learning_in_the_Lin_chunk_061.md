# C.4 Proof of Lemma 7

proof. For sufficiently large T we have the following two results, using that $\| \theta - \theta ^ { * } \| _ { \infty } \leq$ $\frac { 1 } { \log ^ { 1 0 } ( T ) }$ :

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {a - 1}{b} \geq \frac {a ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)} - 1}{b ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*} - 1}{b ^ {*}} \cdot \frac {b ^ {*}}{b ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) (b ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)})} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*} - 1}{b ^ {*}} \cdot \left(1 - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) \left(b ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}\right)}\right) - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) (b ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)})} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*} - 1}{b ^ {*}} - \frac {a ^ {*} - 1}{b ^ {*} \log^ {1 0} (T) (b ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)})} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) (b ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)})} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*} - 1 - O _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}\right)}{b ^ {*}}. \\ \end{array}

\begin{array}{l} \frac {a}{b} \leq \frac {a ^ {*} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}}{b ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*}}{b ^ {*}} \cdot \frac {b ^ {*}}{b ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) (b ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)})} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*}}{b ^ {*}} \cdot \left(1 + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) \left(b ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}\right)}\right) + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) (b ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)})} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*}}{b ^ {*}} + \frac {a ^ {*}}{b ^ {*} (b ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)}) \log^ {1 0} (T)} + \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T) (b ^ {*} - \frac {1}{\log^ {1 0} (T)})} \\ = \frac {a ^ {*} + O _ {T} (1 / \log^ {1 0} (T))}{b ^ {*}}. \\ \end{array}
$$
