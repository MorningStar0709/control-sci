$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathcal {T} _ {i \theta} (v) = \mathcal {T} _ {(v + 1) \theta} (v) \\ = \Lambda_ {(v + 1) \theta} (\theta T + t _ {v}) - \Lambda_ {(v + 1) (\theta + 1)} ((\theta + 1) T + t _ {v}) \\ = \left\{\theta \Big [ \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}) t _ {i} + (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {1}}) T \Big ] \right. \\ \left. + \sum_ {l = 1} ^ {v} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {l}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {l + 1}}) (\theta T + t _ {l}) + \alpha_ {\lambda_ {v + 1}} (\theta T + t _ {v}) \right\} \\ - \left\{(\theta + 1) \left[ \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} \left(\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}\right) t _ {i} + \left(\alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {1}}\right) T \right] \right. \\ + \sum_ {l = 1} ^ {v} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {l}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {l + 1}}) \Big [ (\theta + 1) T + t _ {l} \Big ] \\ \left. + \alpha_ {\lambda_ {v + 1}} \left[ (\theta + 1) T + t _ {v} \right] \right\} \\ = - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}) t _ {i} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} T \\ = - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}) t _ {i} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} T + \alpha_ {\lambda_ {1}} t _ {0} \\ = - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m} \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} (t _ {i} - t _ {i - 1}). \tag {15} \\ \end{array}
$$

For $v = m - 1 , T _ { i \theta } ( v )$ is given by

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathcal {T} _ {i \theta} (v) = \mathcal {T} _ {m \theta} (m - 1) \\ = \Lambda_ {m \theta} (\theta T + t _ {m - 1}) - \Lambda_ {m (\theta + 1)} ((\theta + 1) T + t _ {m - 1}) \\ = \Big [ (\theta + 1) \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}) t _ {i} + \alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} (\theta T + t _ {m - 1}) \Big ] \\ - \left\{(\theta + 2) \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}) t _ {i} \right. \\ \left. + \alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} \Big [ (\theta + 1) T + t _ {m - 1} \Big ] \right\} \\ = - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}) t _ {i} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} T \\ = - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m - 1} (\alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i + 1}}) t _ {i} - \alpha_ {\lambda_ {m}} T + \alpha_ {\lambda_ {1}} t _ {0} \\ = - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m} \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} (t _ {i} - t _ {i - 1}). \tag {16} \\ \end{array}
$$

Therefore, for all $v \in \mathcal V$ and $\theta \in \mathbb { N } ,$ it holds that

$$\mathcal {T} _ {i \theta} (v) = - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m} \alpha_ {\lambda_ {i}} (t _ {i} - t _ {i - 1}). \tag {17}$$
