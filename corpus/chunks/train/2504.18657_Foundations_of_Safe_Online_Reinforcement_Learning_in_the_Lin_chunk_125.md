$$\frac {r K ^ {2} \mathbb {E} [ X _ {T} ^ {2} ]}{T} = \frac {r K ^ {2} \sigma_ {\mathcal {D}} ^ {2} \frac {1 - \rho^ {T}}{1 - \rho}}{T}.$$

Therefore,

$$
\begin{array}{l} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}) = \lim _ {T \to \infty} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T) \\ = \lim _ {T \to \infty} - \frac {r K ^ {2} \sigma_ {\mathcal {D}} ^ {2} \frac {1 - \rho^ {T}}{1 - \rho}}{T} + \frac {\sigma_ {\mathcal {D}} ^ {2} (q + r K ^ {2})}{1 - \rho} \Bigl (1 - \frac {1 - \rho^ {T}}{T (1 - \rho)} \Bigr) \\ = \frac {\sigma_ {\mathcal {D}} ^ {2} (q + r K ^ {2})}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

Now, we note the following derivatives:

$${\frac {d}{d K}} \left({\frac {1}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}}}\right) = {\frac {2 b (a - b K)}{(1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}) ^ {2}}}$$

and

$$\frac {d}{d K} \left(\frac {K ^ {2}}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}}\right) = \frac {2 a K (1 - (a - b K))}{(1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}) ^ {2}}.$$

For $\begin{array} { r } { K \in \left( \frac { a - 1 } { b } , \frac { a } { b } \right] , \mathrm { i f } 1 - ( a - b K ) = c > 0 } \end{array}$ , then $1 - ( a - b K ) ^ { 2 } > c > 0$ , and therefore these derivatives imply that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \frac {d}{d K} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}) \right| = \left| \frac {d}{d K} \frac {\sigma_ {\mathcal {D}} ^ {2} (q + r K ^ {2})}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}} \right| \\ = \left| \sigma_ {\mathcal {D}} ^ {2} \left(q \frac {2 b (a - b K)}{(1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}) ^ {2}} + r \frac {2 a K (1 - (a - b K))}{(1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}) ^ {2}}\right) \right| \\ \leq \sigma_ {\mathcal {D}} ^ {2} \left(q \frac {2 b (a - b K)}{c ^ {2}} + r \frac {2 a | K | (1 - (a - b K))}{c ^ {2}}\right) \\ <   \infty . \\ \end{array}
$$

For all $K \in ( \frac { a - 1 } { b } , \frac { a } { b } ]$ , we also have that

$${\frac {d ^ {2}}{d K ^ {2}}} \left({\frac {1}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}}}\right) = b ^ {2} \Big ({\frac {1}{(1 - (a - b K)) ^ {3}}} + {\frac {1}{(1 + (a - b K)) ^ {3}}} \Big) > 0$$

and

$$\frac {d ^ {2}}{d K ^ {2}} \left(\frac {K ^ {2}}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}}\right) = b ^ {2} \Bigl (\frac {(a - 1) ^ {2}}{(1 - (a - b K)) ^ {3}} + \frac {(a + 1) ^ {2}}{(1 + (a - b K)) ^ {3}} \Bigr) > 0$$

This implies that

$$\frac {d ^ {2}}{d K ^ {2}} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}) > 0.$$

If $a - b K = 1 - c < 1$ , we also have that

$$\frac {d ^ {2}}{d K ^ {2}} \left(\frac {1}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}}\right) = b ^ {2} \Bigl (\frac {1}{(1 - (a - b K)) ^ {3}} + \frac {1}{(1 + (a - b K)) ^ {3}} \Bigr) \leq b ^ {2} \left(\frac {1}{c ^ {3}} + 1\right) < \infty$$

and

$$\frac {d ^ {2}}{d K ^ {2}} \left(\frac {K ^ {2}}{1 - (a - b K) ^ {2}}\right) = b ^ {2} \Big (\frac {(a - 1) ^ {2}}{(1 - (a - b K)) ^ {3}} + \frac {(a + 1) ^ {2}}{(1 + (a - b K)) ^ {3}} \Big) \leq b ^ {2} \left(\frac {(a - 1) ^ {2}}{c ^ {3}} + (a + 1) ^ {2}\right) < \infty .$$

These two equations imply that for $K \in ( \frac { a - 1 } { b } , \frac { a } { b } ]$ ,

$$\frac {d ^ {2}}{d K ^ {2}} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}) < \infty .$$
