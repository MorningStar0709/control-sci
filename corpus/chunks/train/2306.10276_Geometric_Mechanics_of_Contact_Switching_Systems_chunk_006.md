# II. TWO-FOOT, CONTACT-SWITCHING LOCOMOTION

As the first step towards describing the geometric mechanics-based, stratified panel framework for quasistatic legged locomotion, we consider a two-footed toy robot model as the simplest instantiation of contact-switching walking (Fig. 1). We additionally note that our choice to focus on legged locomotion without slipping in this manuscript enables us to generate a minimal template-style [21] locomotion model which is sufficient to model the contact-switching mechanics of walking and explore geometric properties of periodic actuation in a hybrid shape-space.
