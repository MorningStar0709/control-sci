# Sinusoidal Driving Forces

A simple case of averaging is when the external driving force is sinusoidal, that is, $v(t) = u_{0} \sin \omega t$ . The signals $\varphi$ and e are then given by

$$\varphi (t) = G _ {\varphi \nu} (\vartheta , \omega) \nu (t)e (t) = G _ {e \nu} (\vartheta , \omega) \nu (t)$$
