# 3 Case Study

In order to assess the applicability of a Digital Twin(DT)-based approach for Physical Twin (PT) enhancement, we instantiate a generic infrastructure on the fortissimo rover platform. This robotic platform serves as the PT, and is an industry-oriented use case involving Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADASs) and Automated Driving (AD) functions. The case study will capture two different test scenarios. The first experiment demonstrates the ability of a DT to augment the capabilities of the PT, whereas in the second experiment, the DT increases the fault tolerance of the system and reduces the down-time by taking over the functionality of the PT’s faulty components.
