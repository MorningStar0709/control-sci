# 11.4 Notes and References

The conventional Youla parameterization can be found in Youla et al. [1976a, 1976b], Desoer et al. [1980], Doyle [1984], Vidyasagar [1985], and Francis [1987]. The statespace derivation of all stabilizing controllers was reported in Lu, Zhou, and Doyle [1996]. The paper by Moore et al. [1990] contains some other related interesting results. The parameterization of all two-degree-of-freedom stabilizing controllers is given in Youla and Bongiorno [1985] and Vidyasagar [1985].
