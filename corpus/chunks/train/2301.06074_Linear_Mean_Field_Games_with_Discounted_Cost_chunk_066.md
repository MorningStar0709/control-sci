$$J _ {i} (\mu^ {*}; \boldsymbol {\pi} ^ {*}) \leq \inf _ {\pi \in \Pi} J _ {i} (\mu^ {*}; \pi , \boldsymbol {\pi} _ {- i} ^ {*}) + \Theta_ {1} (N, \varepsilon) + \Theta_ {2} (N, \varepsilon) \tag {54}$$

for all $i = 1 , \ldots , N$ .

PROOF. Since the game model is symmetric, it is sufficient to prove (54) for agent 1. In this case, by Theorem 4.2 and Theorem 4.4, we have

$$
\begin{array}{l} J _ {1} (\mu^ {*}; \boldsymbol {\pi} ^ {*}) - \inf _ {\pi \in \Pi} J _ {1} (\mu^ {*}; \pi , \pi^ {*}, \dots , \pi^ {*}) = J _ {1} (\mu^ {*}; \boldsymbol {\pi} ^ {*}) - J _ {1} (\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}, \pi^ {*}, \dots , \pi^ {*}) \\ = J _ {1} \left(\mu^ {*}; \pi^ {*}\right) - J \left(\mu^ {*}; \pi^ {*}\right) + J \left(\mu^ {*}; \pi^ {*}\right) - J \left(\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}\right) + J \left(\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}\right) - J _ {1} \left(\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}, \pi^ {*}, \dots , \pi^ {*}\right) \\ \leq | J _ {1} (\mu^ {*}; \boldsymbol {\pi} ^ {*}) - J (\mu^ {*}; \pi^ {*}) | + | J (\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}) - J _ {1} (\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}, \pi^ {*}, \dots , \pi^ {*}) | \\ \end{array}
\text {(as} J (\mu^ {*}; \pi^ {*}) - J (\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}) = \inf _ {\pi \in \Pi_ {l}} J (\mu^ {*}; \pi) - J (\mu^ {*}; \gamma^ {*, N}) \leq 0)\leq \Theta_ {1} (N, \varepsilon) + \Theta_ {2} (N, \varepsilon)$$

This completes the proof.
