# A. Hybrid FES-Exoskeleton Setup

1) Functional Electrical Stimulation: A research-grade FES device (Tecnalia Research & Innovation, Spain), interfaced with multi-array electrodes (Fig. 4), provides real-time control over stimulation parameters, including pulse frequency, width, and amplitude. In this study, FES delivers a biphasic electrical pulse at a fixed frequency of 25 Hz and a fixed pulse width of 300 $\mu s .$ . The pulse amplitude is used as the control variable and is adjustable with a command resolution of 100 $\mu A$ .
