Remark 1. Since it is assumed that $| g ( x , y ) | \leq 1$ , for all $x , y \in [ 0 , 1 ]$ , the operator norm of $g$ satisfies

$$\| g \| _ {\mathrm{op}} := \sup _ {v \in L ^ {2} [ 0, 1 ]} \frac {\| g \circ v \|}{\| v \|} \leq \sqrt {\int_ {[ 0 , 1 ] ^ {2}} g (x , y) ^ {2} d x d y} \leq 1,$$
