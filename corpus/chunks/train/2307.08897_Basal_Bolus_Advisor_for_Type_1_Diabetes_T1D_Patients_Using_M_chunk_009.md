To develop a closed-loop insulin delivery system that accurately represents real-life scenarios and adapts insulin dosages based on real-time glucose readings, we leverage the SAC RL model introduced in [30] and integrate it into the testing platform. By training RL agents using real-time data from the BG simulator across three diverse scenarios, our aim is to enable the agents to dynamically optimize insulin dynamics and enhance the time in range (TIR) within the euglycemic range of 80-170 mg/dL. The utilization of multiple scenarios allows for the generalization and robustness of the trained agents to different real-life situations, ensuring their effectiveness in practical applications.
