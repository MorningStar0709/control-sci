The MPSO PID-SMC is proposed to be applied to the liquid level controller of a conical tank system. The objective is to control the liquid level of the tank by introducing a leakage (external disturbance) in the tank. The objective consists in minimizing the liquid level tracking error in presence of model uncertainties and leakage in the tank, limited only by the sensors capability to detect the level.
