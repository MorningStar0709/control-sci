# 5.5 Planning and Control for Multi-Robot-Object Systems under Temporal Logic Formulas

The final section of this chapter considers the general case of a multi-robotobject system, with $N > 1$ agents and $M > 1$ objects. Unlike the previous section, the objects are now not assumed to be grasped in the starting configuration. Moreover, temporal logic specifications are imposed both to the robotic agents and the objects, whose behavior depends on the agent actions.
