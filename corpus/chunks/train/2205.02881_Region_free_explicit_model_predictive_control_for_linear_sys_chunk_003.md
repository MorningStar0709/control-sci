MPC for infinite-dimensional continuous-time plants, usually without constraints, has been studied before; see in particular [18], but also, e.g., [19], [20], [21], [22]. The continuous-time setting in these studies makes the analysis rather technical, and hoping to make an impact in engineering we will prefer a more practically oriented and elementary approach in discrete time, which allows us to handle a finite number of general affine constraints, similar to [23], [24].
