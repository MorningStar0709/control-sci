# A. System Overview

The proposed system architecture (Fig. 2) consists of four main modules:

1) Longitudinal Dynamics Environment: Simulates vehicle states $x = [ z , v _ { r } , v _ { e } ] ^ { T }$ in continuous time.   
2) Quantum-Inspired Policy Network: Uses variational quantum circuits (VQC) to generate control inputs u.   
3) Lyapunov Stability Module: Evaluates and penalizes violations of stability conditions.   
4) Learning Loop: Updates policy parameters via gradient descent based on reward and stability feedback.
