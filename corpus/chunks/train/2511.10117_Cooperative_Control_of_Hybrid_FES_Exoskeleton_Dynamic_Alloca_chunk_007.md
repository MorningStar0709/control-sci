# B. Paper Organization

The article is organized as follows. Section II describes the hybrid control architecture addressing both high- and lowlevel control of the system. This section is further divided into three parts: Section II-A introduces the shared and cooperative control strategy that addresses actuator redundancy through dynamic allocation; Section II-B describes the FES torque model and control; and Section II-C presents the exoskeleton control strategy. Section III elaborates on the experimental evaluation, and the results are presented in Section IV. Finally, a conclusion is provided in Section V.
