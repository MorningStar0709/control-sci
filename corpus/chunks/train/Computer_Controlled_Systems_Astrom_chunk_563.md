# Selection of Resolution of A-D and D-A Converters

Today the resolution of the D-A converters are about 10 bits and the A-D converters have normally 14 bits. With double-precision arithmetic the computations are typically done with a resolution of 64 bits. This implies that it is the accuracy of the converters that have the largest influence on the performance. A couple of examples illustrate the influence of the quantization in the converters.
