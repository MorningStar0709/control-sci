式中， $K_{r}=\mathrm{diag}\left[k_{rii}\right]$ ， $k_{rii}\geqslant\left|E_{i}\right|$ ， $i=1,\cdots,n$ 。

由式（7.12）和式（7.13）的右边得

$$\boldsymbol {D} _ {0} (\boldsymbol {q}) \ddot {\boldsymbol {q}} _ {\mathrm{r}} + \boldsymbol {C} _ {0} (\boldsymbol {q}, \dot {\boldsymbol {q}}) \dot {\boldsymbol {q}} _ {\mathrm{r}} + \boldsymbol {G} _ {0} (\boldsymbol {q}) - \boldsymbol {D} (\boldsymbol {q}) \dot {\boldsymbol {r}} - \boldsymbol {C} (\boldsymbol {q}, \dot {\boldsymbol {q}}) \boldsymbol {r} + \boldsymbol {E} _ {\mathrm{M}} + \boldsymbol {d} (\dot {\boldsymbol {q}}) =\boldsymbol {D} _ {0} (\boldsymbol {q}) \ddot {\boldsymbol {q}} _ {\mathrm{r}} + \boldsymbol {C} _ {0} (\boldsymbol {q}, \dot {\boldsymbol {q}}) \dot {\boldsymbol {q}} _ {\mathrm{r}} + \boldsymbol {G} _ {0} (\boldsymbol {q}) + \boldsymbol {K} _ {\mathrm{p}} \boldsymbol {r} + \boldsymbol {K} _ {\mathrm{i}} \int_ {0} ^ {t} \boldsymbol {r} \mathrm{d} t + \boldsymbol {K} _ {\mathrm{r}} \operatorname{sgn} (\boldsymbol {r})$$

上式又可以写为

$$\boldsymbol {D} (\boldsymbol {q}) \dot {\boldsymbol {r}} + \boldsymbol {C} (\boldsymbol {q}, \dot {\boldsymbol {q}}) \boldsymbol {r} + \boldsymbol {K} _ {\mathrm{i}} \int_ {0} ^ {\mathrm{t}} \boldsymbol {r} \mathrm{d} t = - \boldsymbol {K} _ {\mathrm{p}} \boldsymbol {r} - \boldsymbol {K} _ {\mathrm{r}} \operatorname{sgn} (\boldsymbol {r}) + \boldsymbol {E} \tag {7.16}$$
