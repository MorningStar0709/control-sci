In order to generate a step, use $H_{r}(q) = q/(q - 1)$ ; to generate a ramp, use $H_{r}(q) = q/(q - 1)^{2}$ , and a sinusoid from a harmonic oscillator (compare Examples A.1 and A.3 in Appendix A). From an input-output viewpoint, disturbances may be described as impulse responses. Disturbances also may be regarded as the responses of dynamic systems with zero inputs but nonzero initial conditions. In both cases the major characteristics of the disturbances are described by the dynamic systems that generate them. The approach, of course, can be applied to continuous-time, as well as discrete-time, systems.
