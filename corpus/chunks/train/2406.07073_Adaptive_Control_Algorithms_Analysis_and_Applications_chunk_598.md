$$\operatorname{tr} F (t + 1) = \operatorname{tr} F (t) - \frac {\alpha (t) \bar {\phi} (t) F ^ {2} (t) \bar {\phi} (t)}{1 + \alpha (t) \bar {\phi} (t) F (t) \bar {\phi} (t)} \tag {12.134}$$

$$V (t + 1) = V (t) + \alpha (t) \bar {w} ^ {2} (t + 1) - \frac {\alpha (t) \varepsilon_ {a} ^ {2} (t + 1)}{1 + \alpha (t) \bar {\phi} (t) F (t) \bar {\phi} (t)} \tag {12.135}$$

with:

In view of (10.102), (12.115) and (12.117) one has:

$$1 + \alpha (t) \bar {\phi} (t) F (t) \bar {\phi} (t) \leq 1 + \alpha (t) \operatorname{tr} F (t) \| \bar {\phi} (t) \| ^ {2} \leq 1 + \operatorname{tr} F (0) \stackrel {\Delta} {=} \gamma \tag {12.137}$$

$( \| \bar { \phi } ( t ) \| \leq 1$ from (10.7)). Introducing (12.126) and (12.137) in (12.135) one obtains:

$$V (t + 1) \leq V (t) + \frac {\alpha (t)}{\gamma} [ \gamma \delta^ {2} (t + 1) - \varepsilon_ {a} ^ {2} (t + 1) ] \tag {12.138}$$

Using (12.117), (12.118) and (12.119) it follows:

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\alpha (t)}{\gamma} [ \gamma \delta^ {2} (t + 1) - \varepsilon_ {a} ^ {2} (t + 1) ] = \frac {\alpha (t)}{\gamma} [ \gamma \bar {\delta} ^ {2} (t + 1) - \gamma \sigma \delta (t + 1) - \varepsilon_ {a} ^ {2} (t + 1) ] \\ \leq - \alpha (t) \sigma \delta (t + 1) \tag {12.139} \\ \end{array}

$$0 \leq V (t + 1) \leq V (t) - \alpha (t) \sigma \delta (t + 1) \leq V (0) - \sigma \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {t + 1} \alpha (i) \delta (i + 1) \tag {12.140}$$
