# 2.1. Ergodic Control of the Cartpole System

Consider the Cartpole system which has, over the years, become a benchmark for testing control strategies (Khalil, 2015). This would typically produce a controller with tolerable performance fairly quickly, but would also suffer from nonidealities of parameter estimation.
