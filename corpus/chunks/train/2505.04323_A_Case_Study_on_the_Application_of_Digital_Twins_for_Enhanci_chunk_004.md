# 3.1 The fortissimo rover

The fortissimo rovers are customized scale 1:10 model cars equipped with redundant sensors, namely two ultrasonic sensors, one laser sensor and one camera, and a distributed E/E platform architecture. They serve as a teaching and evaluation platform for model-based systems engineering (e.g., [2]) at the fortiss Mobility Lab1. The rovers feature a highway pilot: an autonomous driving function capable of taking over both longitudinal and lateral control within a specific operational domain. Its functionality is modeled in AutoFOCUS32, a model-based tool supporting the design, development and validation of safety-critical embedded systems. The rover’s DT was co-developed as a co-simulation of its behavior within a customized CARLA simulation [3] of its environment.
