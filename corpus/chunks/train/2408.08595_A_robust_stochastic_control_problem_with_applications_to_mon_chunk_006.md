We now introduce the following scalar-valued linear stochastic differential equation (SDE):

$$d X _ {t} = (A _ {t} X _ {t} + u _ {t} ^ {\prime} B _ {t}) d t + (X _ {t} C _ {t} ^ {\prime} + u _ {t} ^ {\prime} D _ {t}) d W _ {t}, \quad X _ {0} = x \in \mathbb {R}. \tag {2.1}$$

$$\Pi := L _ {\mathbb {F}, \mathbb {P}} ^ {2} (0, T; \mathbb {R} ^ {n}).$$
