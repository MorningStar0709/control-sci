# Appendix B. Proof of Theorem 1

Proof. We define the velocity tracking signal as

$$\tilde {v} _ {i} = v _ {i} - \mathbb {1} _ {\Omega} v _ {d} \tag {B.1}$$

Consider the following Lyapunov function for some $\alpha >$ 0:

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \underbrace {\frac {1}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \tilde {v} _ {i} ^ {T} \tilde {v} _ {i}} _ {V _ {1}} + \underbrace {\frac {c _ {1}}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} \Phi_ {i j}} _ {V _ {2}} + \underbrace {c _ {3} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \mathbb {1} _ {i \in L} \mathbb {1} _ {\Omega^ {-}} \| p _ {i} - \mathcal {P} _ {\Omega} (p _ {i}) \|} _ {V _ {3}} \\ + \underbrace {\frac {c _ {4}}{c _ {5}} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \mathbb {1} _ {i \in L} \mathbb {1} _ {\Omega} \mathbf {1} _ {n} ^ {T} \log (\cos (c _ {5} (p _ {i} - \gamma_ {i})))} _ {V _ {4}} + \underbrace {\frac {\zeta}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} \dot {\phi} _ {i j} ^ {T} \dot {\phi} _ {i j}} _ {V _ {5}}. \tag {B.2} \\ \end{array}
$$

The time derivative V for the system dynamics given by (1) can be decomposed by

$$\dot {V} = \dot {V} _ {1} + \dot {V} _ {2} + \dot {V} _ {3} + \dot {V} _ {4} + \dot {V} _ {5} \tag {B.3}$$

The derivative of $V _ { 1 }$ can be obtained using (B.1) and control input (6) as

$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {V} _ {1} = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \tilde {v} _ {i} ^ {T} (u _ {i} - \mathbb {1} _ {\Omega} \dot {v} _ {d} - \dot {\phi} _ {i}) \\ = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \hat {v} _ {i} ^ {T} \Big (- c _ {1} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} \nabla_ {p _ {i}} \Phi_ {i j} - c _ {2} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} \hat {v} _ {i j} \\ \left. - c _ {3} \mathbb {1} _ {i \in L} \mathbb {1} _ {\Omega^ {-}} \frac {p _ {i} - \mathcal {P} _ {\Omega} (p _ {i})}{\| p _ {i} - \mathcal {P} _ {\Omega} (p _ {i}) \|} - c _ {4} \mathbb {1} _ {i \in L} \mathbb {1} _ {\Omega} \tanh (c _ {5} (p _ {i} - \gamma_ {i}))\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

Using the lemma 1, the derivative of $V _ { 2 }$ is

$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {V} _ {2} = c _ {1} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} v _ {i} ^ {T} \nabla_ {p _ {i}} \Phi_ {i j} \\ = c _ {1} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} \tilde {v} _ {i} ^ {T} \nabla_ {p _ {i}} \Phi_ {i j} (\mathrm{As} v _ {i} - v _ {j} = \tilde {v} _ {i} - \tilde {v} _ {j}). \\ \end{array}
$$
