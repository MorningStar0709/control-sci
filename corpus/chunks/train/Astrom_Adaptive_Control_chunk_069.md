There are normally sessions on adaptive control at the major control conferences.
The International Federation of Automatic Control is responsible for the Symposium on Adaptive Systems in Control and Signal Processing (ACASP), which is held every third year.
The first symposium was held in San Francisco in 1983.
These symposia provide up-to-date information about progress in the field.
