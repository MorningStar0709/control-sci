# D. Numerical Validation

To validate our approach numerically, we apply our controller synthesis on a normalized version of the two tank benchmark system [34]. We performed system identification using Neuromancer [14], fitting the plant to an FRNN with $n \ = \ 8$ neurons and tanh (·) activations. We apply Propositions 27 and 30 to design a controller and an observer respectively, and design a gain for the integral controller via Lemma 33.
