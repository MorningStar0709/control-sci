Both the PD-approximated and SINDy-identified models offer distinct advantages within the MPC framework. The PD model provides a simple, computationally efficient approximation for control inputs, making it suitable for realtime applications. Meanwhile, the SINDy model captures more of the system’s nonlinearities, improving trajectory tracking accuracy, especially in the presence of external disturbances [10]. The ability to combine these two approaches provides a flexible and powerful toolset for UAV control, enabling precise and robust performance across a wide range of operational scenarios.
