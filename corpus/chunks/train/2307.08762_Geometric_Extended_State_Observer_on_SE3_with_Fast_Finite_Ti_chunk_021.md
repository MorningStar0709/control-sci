# 5. Fast Finite-Time Stable Extended State Observer (FFTS-ESO) on SE(3)

In this section, we present the FFTS-ESO on SE(3). As mentioned in the previous section, the ESO design on $\operatorname { S E } ( 3 )$ can be represented as a translational ESO on the vector space $\mathbb { R } ^ { 3 }$ to estimate disturbance forces, and an rotational ESO on SO(3) to estimate disturbance torques. We present the two ESO designs along their stability proofs in this section.
