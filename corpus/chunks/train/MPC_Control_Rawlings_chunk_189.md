# 1.6 Exercises

(a) Choose a reasonable sample time, ∆, and disturbance model, and simulate an offset-free discrete time MPC controller for this setpoint change. List all of your chosen parameters.   
(b) Choose PID tuning parameters to achieve “good performance” for this system. List your PID tuning parameters. Compare the performances of the two controllers.
