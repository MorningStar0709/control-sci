# Example 4.6 Constant input disturbance

Consider the situation with a constant disturbance that acts on the process input. The matrix $\Phi_{w}$ then becomes the identity and we have $\Phi_{xw} = \Gamma$ . The system is described by

$$x (k + h) = (\Phi - \Gamma L) x (k) + \Gamma \left(1 - L _ {w}\right) w (k)w (k + h) = w (k)$$

The effect of the disturbance v on x is thus eliminated by choosing $L_{w}=1$ .
