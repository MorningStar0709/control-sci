Even though the algorithm took hours to close the gap between upper and lower bound to 0.01 for MultiCast instances, we would like to note that in hindsight, the lower bound arrives at a near optimal value very quickly as shown in Figure 2. The remainder of the algorithm mostly reduces the upper bound to provide an optimality guarantee. Recall that CHSVI is an anytime algorithm. This means that even if we terminate the algorithm early, we can still obtain a strategy with near optimal performance.
