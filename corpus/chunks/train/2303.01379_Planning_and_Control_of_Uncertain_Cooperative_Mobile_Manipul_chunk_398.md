Remark 5.2. Note that, according to the aforementioned analysis, we implicitly assume that the agents start executing their respective transitions at the same time (we do not take into account individual control jumps in the Lyapunov analysis, i.e., it is valid only for one transition). Intuition suggests that if the regions of interest are sufficiently far from each other, then the agents will be able to perform the sequence of their transitions independently. Detailed technical analysis of such cases is part of future research.
