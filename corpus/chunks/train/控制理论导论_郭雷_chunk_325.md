ii) 对 $\xi_t \in \mathcal{P}_T$ ,

$\int_0^t\xi_s\mathrm{d}w_s$ 对 $t\in [0,T)$ 连续； (4.3.14)

iii) 对 $\xi_t \in \mathcal{P}_T$ , $\int_0^t \xi_s \mathrm{d}w_s = \int_0^u \xi_s \mathrm{d}w_s + \int_u^t \xi_s \mathrm{d}w_s, \quad 0 \leqslant u \leqslant t$ ;

iv) 设 $\xi_t, \xi_t^1, \xi_t^2 \in \mathcal{M}_T$ , 那么

$$E \int_ {0} ^ {t} \xi_ {s} \mathrm{d} w _ {s} = 0, \quad \forall t \in [ 0, T ], \tag {4.3.15}\left(\int_ {0} ^ {t} \xi_ {s} \mathrm{d} w _ {s}, \mathcal {F} _ {t}\right) \text {是鞅}, \tag {4.3.16}$$

并且

$$E \left(\int_ {0} ^ {t} \xi_ {s} ^ {1} \mathrm{d} w _ {s}\right) \left(\int_ {0} ^ {t} \xi_ {s} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} w _ {s}\right) ^ {*} = E \int_ {0} ^ {t} \xi_ {s} ^ {1} (\xi_ {s} ^ {2}) ^ {*} \mathrm{d} s. \tag {4.3.17}$$

这些性质对简单过程是显见的事实，然后对一般的过程用简单过程逼近的办法证明.
