# APPENDIX A: DESIGN DISTRIBUTED SLIDING MODE CONTROL

The SMC can be derived as follows [16]:

First, the sliding surface is selected as

$$s = k _ {1} (L + B) \bar {\varepsilon} _ {1} + \bar {\varepsilon} _ {2} \tag {61}$$

where $k _ { 1 }$ is a positive constant.

The sliding mode control can be designed as

$$\tau = [ \bar {J} (\eta_ {2}) \bar {\Pi} ] ^ {- 1} \left(- \bar {\Phi} (v, \eta) v + \tau^ {\prime}\right) \tag {62}$$
