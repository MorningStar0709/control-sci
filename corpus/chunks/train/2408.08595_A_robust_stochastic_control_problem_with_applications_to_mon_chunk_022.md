# 4 Connection with the control problem with mean-variance cost functional

In this section, we study the following stochastic control problem:

$$\sup _ {u \in \Pi} \left[ \mathbb {E} [ X _ {T} ^ {u} ] - \frac {\theta}{2} \mathrm{Var} (X _ {T} ^ {u}) \right], \tag {4.1}$$

where the state process satisfies (2.1). We can show that the optimal control ˆu of the robust stochastic control problem (2.4) is exactly the optimal control of problem (4.1).

Theorem 4.1 Under assumption (A), the control uˆ defined by (3.17) is the optimal control of stochastic control problem (4.1). Moreover, the stochastic control problem (4.1) has the same optimal value with robust stochastic control problem (2.4),

$$\sup _ {u \in \Pi} \left[ \mathbb {E} [ X _ {T} ] - \frac {\theta}{2} \mathrm{Var} (X _ {T}) \right] = h _ {0} x + \frac {1}{2 \theta} (Y _ {0} - 1).$$

Proof. First, let us consider

$$F (K) := \inf _ {u \in \Pi^ {K}} \mathbb {E} \left[ (X _ {T} - K) ^ {2} \right], \tag {4.2}$$

where

$$\Pi^ {K} := \left\{u \in \Pi \mid \mathbb {E} [ X _ {T} ^ {u} ] = K \right\}.$$

It follows from [20], that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sup _ {u \in \Pi} \left[ \mathbb {E} [ X _ {T} ] - \frac {\theta}{2} \mathrm{Var} (X _ {T}) \right] = \sup _ {K \in \mathbb {R}} \sup _ {u \in \Pi^ {K}} \left[ K - \frac {\theta}{2} \mathbb {E} \left[ (X _ {T} - K) ^ {2} \right] \right] \\ = \sup _ {K \in \mathbb {R}} \left[ K - \frac {\theta}{2} \inf _ {u \in \Pi^ {K}} \mathbb {E} \left[ (X _ {T} - K) ^ {2} \right] \right] \tag {4.3} \\ = \sup _ {K \in \mathbb {R}} \left[ K - \frac {\theta}{2} F (K) \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
