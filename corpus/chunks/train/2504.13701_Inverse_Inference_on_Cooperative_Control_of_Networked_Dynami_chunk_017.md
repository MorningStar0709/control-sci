$$\Sigma_ {\Delta , 0} (T) = \frac {1}{T} \left(Y _ {\Delta , T} ^ {-}\right) \left(Y _ {\Delta , T} ^ {-}\right) ^ {\intercal}, \Sigma_ {\Delta , 1} = \frac {1}{T} \left(Y _ {\Delta , T} ^ {+}\right) \left(Y _ {\Delta , T} ^ {-}\right) ^ {\intercal}. \tag {24}$$

Corollary 1. For the linear growth case (22), define the estimator for $A _ { c }$ as $\widehat { A } _ { d } ^ { s } ( T ) = ( \Sigma _ { \Delta , 1 } ( T ) + I _ { N } \otimes \Gamma ) ( \Sigma _ { \Delta , 0 } ( T ) -$ $2 I _ { N } \otimes \Gamma ) ^ { - 1 }$ , then, with probability one, we have

$$\lim _ {T \to \infty} \| \widehat {A} _ {d} ^ {s} (T) - A _ {d} \| = 0. \tag {25}$$

Proof. First, when no confusion is caused, we will alternatively use $\Delta y _ { t }$ for $\Delta y ( t )$ , while the convergence stated in this proof refers to almost sure convergence. By the definition of $\Sigma _ { \Delta , 1 } ( T )$ , it can be expanded as

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Sigma_ {\Delta , 1} (T) = \frac {1}{T} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 1} \Delta y _ {t + 1} \Delta y _ {t} ^ {\intercal} \\ = \frac {1}{T} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 2} \left(A _ {d} \Delta y _ {t} - A _ {d} \Delta v _ {t} + \Delta v _ {t + 1}\right) \left(\Delta x _ {t} + \Delta v _ {t}\right) ^ {\intercal} \\ = A _ {d} \Sigma_ {\Delta , 0} (T + 1) + \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 2} \frac {\left(\Delta v _ {t + 1} - A _ {d} \Delta v _ {t}\right) \left(\Delta x _ {t} + \Delta v _ {t}\right) ^ {\intercal}}{T}, \tag {26} \\ \end{array}
$$

where $\Delta { v } _ { t } = { v } _ { t + 1 } - { v } _ { t }$ is the noise difference at instant t. Note that when the NDS is subject to the linear growth (22), the second term in (26) will converge as $T \to \infty$ , given by

$$
\begin{array}{l} \lim _ {T \to \infty} \frac {\sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 2} \left(\Delta v _ {t + 1} - A _ {d} \Delta v _ {t}\right) \left(\Delta x _ {t} + \Delta v _ {t}\right) ^ {\intercal}}{T} \\ = \lim _ {T \rightarrow \infty} \frac {\sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 2} \Delta v _ {t + 1} \Delta x _ {t} ^ {\intercal} + \Delta v _ {t + 1} \Delta v _ {t} ^ {\intercal} - A _ {d} \Delta v _ {t} \Delta x _ {t} ^ {\intercal} - A _ {d} \Delta v _ {t} \Delta v _ {t} ^ {\intercal}}{T} \\ = 0 + \lim _ {T \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 2} \frac {\left(v _ {t + 2} - v _ {t + 1}\right)\left(v _ {t + 1} - v _ {t}\right) ^ {\intercal}}{T} \\ - 0 - \lim _ {T \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 2} \frac {A _ {d} \left(v _ {t + 1} - v _ {t}\right)\left(v _ {t + 1} - v _ {t}\right) ^ {\intercal}}{T} \\ = 0 - I _ {N} \otimes \Gamma - 0 - A _ {d} \cdot 2 (I _ {N} \otimes \Gamma), \tag {27} \\ \end{array}
$$
