# Jump Law of Co-State in Optimal Control for State-Dependent Switched Systems and Applications

Mi Zhou1 Erik I. Verriest1 Yue Guan2 Chaouki Abdallah1

Abstract— This paper presents the jump law of co-states in optimal control for state-dependent switched systems. The number of switches and the switching modes are assumed to be known a priori. A proposed jump law is rigorously derived by theoretical analysis and illustrated by simulation results. An algorithm is then proposed to solve optimal control for statedependent hybrid systems. Through numerical simulations, we further show that the proposed approach is more efficient than existing methods in solving optimal control for state-dependent switched systems.
