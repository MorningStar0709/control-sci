# C. Attitude Control and Finite Time Convergence

In this subsection, the finite time $t _ { r }$ of the AFTSMC is computed for the proposed controller (37). It is assumed that the time interval between the initial error values $[ e ( 0 ) \neq$ $0 , \Omega _ { e } ( 0 ) \neq 0 ] \neq 0$ to reach $e = 0$ is $t _ { r }$ .

Lemma 2. The state errors reach the AFTSMC surface in (35) using the controller (37) and adaptive update law (38) in a finite time $t _ { r } .$ .

Proof: To obtain finite time convergence, consider the following real value function:

$$V _ {t r} = \frac {1}{2} s ^ {2} \tag {45}$$

The derivative of (45) is obtained as:

$$\dot {V} _ {t r} = s \left(\ddot {e} + \gamma_ {1} \dot {e} + \gamma_ {2} \frac {n}{l} e ^ {n / l - 1} \dot {e} + \dot {\Omega} _ {e}\right) \tag {46}$$

Using (37) and (1), the above expression (46) can be rewritten as follows:

$$\dot {V} _ {t r} = s (\frac {1}{2} ((\dot {e} _ {0} \mathbf {I} _ {3} + [ \dot {e} ] _ {\times}) \Omega_ {e} + (e _ {0} \mathbf {I} _ {3} + [ e ] _ {\times}) \dot {\Omega} _ {e}) +\gamma_ {1} (\frac {1}{2} (e _ {0} \Omega_ {e} + [ e ] _ {\times} \Omega_ {e})) + \gamma_ {2} \frac {n}{l} e ^ {n / l - 1} \dot {e} + J ^ {- 1} ([ J \Omega_ {B} ] _ {\times} \Omega_ {B} +\left. \mathcal {T} + J \left[ \Omega_ {e} \right] _ {\times} R _ {e} \Omega_ {d} - J R _ {e} \dot {\Omega} _ {d})\right) \tag {47}$$

Substituting for (37) in (47), one obtains:

$$\dot {V} _ {t r} \leqslant - \frac {1}{2} \mu_ {1} s ^ {2} - \mu_ {2} | s | \leqslant 0 \tag {48}$$

such that

$$\dot {V} _ {t r} = \frac {d V _ {t r}}{d t} \leq - \mu_ {1} V _ {t r} - \mu_ {2} \sqrt {2} V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2} \tag {49}$$

Let us introduce the variable $\varrho = \mu _ { 2 } \sqrt { 2 } .$ One finds:

$$d t \leq \frac {- d V _ {t r}}{\mu_ {1} V _ {t r} + \varrho V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2}} \tag {50}$$

such that

$$d t \leq \frac {- V _ {t r} ^ {- 1 / 2} d V _ {t r}}{\mu_ {1} V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2} + \varrho} = - 2 \frac {d V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2}}{\mu_ {1} V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2} + \varrho} \tag {51}$$

Integrating both sides of (51), the finite time can be derived as:

$$\int_ {0} ^ {t _ {r}} d t \leq \int_ {V _ {t r} (0)} ^ {V _ {t r} \left(t _ {r}\right)} \frac {- 2 d V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2}}{\mu_ {1} V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2} + \varrho} \tag {52}= \left[ \frac {- 2}{\mu_ {1}} \ln \left(\mu_ {1} V _ {t r} ^ {1 / 2} + \varrho\right) \right] _ {V _ {t r} (0)} ^ {V _ {t r} (t _ {r})}$$

Finally, based on (52), $t _ { r }$ is calculated as:

$$t _ {r} \leq \frac {2}{\mu_ {1}} \ln \left(\frac {\mu_ {1} V _ {t r} (0) ^ {1 / 2} + \varrho}{\varrho}\right) \tag {53}$$
