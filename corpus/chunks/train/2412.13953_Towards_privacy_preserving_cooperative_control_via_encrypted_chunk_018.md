# 4.2 Encrypted ADMM via key switches

We already prepared the encrypted implementation of Algorithm 1 by means of (12) and (13). It remains to specify the encrypted update of $z _ { i }$ in the first step of Algorithm 1 and secure data exchanges between the agents. In plaintext, updating $z _ { i }$ is carried out by solving (6) for $z _ { i } ^ { \tau + 1 }$ . More formally, given the (block) inverse

$$
\left( \begin{array}{c c} \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} & \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 2} \\ * & * \end{array} \right) := \left( \begin{array}{c c} H _ {i} + \rho I _ {v _ {i}} & G _ {i} ^ {\top} \\ G _ {i} & 0 \end{array} \right) ^ {- 1}, \tag {14}
$$

we obtain

$$
\begin{array}{l} z _ {i} ^ {\tau + 1} = \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} \left(\rho (\zeta^ {\tau}) _ {\mathcal {K} _ {i}} - F _ {i} p _ {i} - \lambda_ {i} ^ {\tau}\right) + \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 2} E _ {i} p _ {i} \\ = \rho \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} (\zeta^ {\tau}) _ {\mathcal {K} _ {i}} - \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} \lambda_ {i} ^ {\tau} + \left(\Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 2} E _ {i} - \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} F _ {i}\right) p _ {i}. \tag {15} \\ \end{array}
$$

Now, based on (15), it is tempting to realize the encrypted update of $z _ { i }$ via

$$
\begin{array}{l} \llbracket z _ {i} ^ {\tau + 1} \rrbracket_ {0} = \left(\rho \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} \odot \llbracket (\zeta^ {\tau}) _ {\mathcal {K} _ {i}} \rrbracket_ {0}\right) \ominus \left(\Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} \odot \llbracket \lambda_ {i} ^ {\tau} \rrbracket_ {0}\right) \\ \oplus \left(\left(\Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 2} E _ {i} - \Gamma_ {i} ^ {1 1} F _ {i}\right) \odot [ [ p _ {i} ] ] _ {0}\right). \tag {16} \\ \end{array}
$$
