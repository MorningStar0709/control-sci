$$
\begin{array}{l} \left(- r _ {b} F _ {l}\right) + \left(r _ {b} F _ {r}\right) = J \frac {\dot {v} _ {r} - \dot {v} _ {l}}{2 r _ {b}} \\ - r _ {b} F _ {l} + r _ {b} F _ {r} = \frac {J}{2 r _ {b}} (\dot {v} _ {r} - \dot {v} _ {l}) \\ - F _ {l} + F _ {r} = \frac {J}{2 r _ {b} ^ {2}} (\dot {v} _ {r} - \dot {v} _ {l}) \\ \end{array}
\frac {2 r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (- F _ {l} + F _ {r}) = \dot {v} _ {r} - \dot {v} _ {l}\dot {v} _ {r} = \dot {v} _ {l} + \frac {2 r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (- F _ {l} + F _ {r})
$$

Substitute in equation (12.31) for $\dot { v } _ { l }$ to obtain an expression for $\dot { v } _ { r }$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {v} _ {r} = \left(\frac {2}{m} (F _ {l} + F _ {r}) - \dot {v} _ {r}\right) + \frac {2 r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (- F _ {l} + F _ {r}) \\ 2 \dot {v} _ {r} = \frac {2}{m} (F _ {l} + F _ {r}) + \frac {2 r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (- F _ {l} + F _ {r}) \\ \dot {v} _ {r} = \frac {1}{m} (F _ {l} + F _ {r}) + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (- F _ {l} + F _ {r}) (12.32) \\ \dot {v} _ {r} = \frac {1}{m} F _ {l} + \frac {1}{m} F _ {r} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} F _ {l} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} F _ {r} \\ \dot {v} _ {r} = \left(\frac {1}{m} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {l} + \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {r} (12.33) \\ \end{array}
$$

Substitute equation (12.32) back into equation (12.31) to obtain an expression for $\dot { v } _ { l }$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {v} _ {l} = \frac {2}{m} (F _ {l} + F _ {r}) - \left(\frac {1}{m} (F _ {l} + F _ {r}) + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (- F _ {l} + F _ {r})\right) \\ \dot {v} _ {l} = \frac {1}{m} (F _ {l} + F _ {r}) - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (- F _ {l} + F _ {r}) \\ \dot {v} _ {l} = \frac {1}{m} (F _ {l} + F _ {r}) + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} (F _ {l} - F _ {r}) \\ \dot {v} _ {l} = \frac {1}{m} F _ {l} + \frac {1}{m} F _ {r} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} F _ {l} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J} F _ {r} \\ \end{array}
\dot {v} _ {l} = \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {l} + \left(\frac {1}{m} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {r} \tag {12.34}
$$

Now, plug the expressions for $F _ { l }$ and $F _ { r }$ into equation (12.33).

$$\dot {v} _ {r} = \left(\frac {1}{m} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {l} + \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {r}\dot {v} _ {r} = \left(\frac {1}{m} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) \left(C _ {1} v _ {l} + C _ {2} V _ {l}\right) + \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) \left(C _ {3} v _ {r} + C _ {4} V _ {r}\right) \tag {12.35}$$

Now, plug the expressions for $F _ { l }$ and $F _ { r }$ into equation (12.34).

$$\dot {v} _ {l} = \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {l} + \left(\frac {1}{m} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) F _ {r}\dot {v} _ {l} = \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) \left(C _ {1} v _ {l} + C _ {2} V _ {l}\right) + \left(\frac {1}{m} - \frac {r _ {b} ^ {2}}{J}\right) \left(C _ {3} v _ {r} + C _ {4} V _ {r}\right) \tag {12.36}$$

This model will be converted to state-space notation in section 8.7.
