On the other hand, to the best of our knowledge, Trybu la and Zawisza [20] first considered a continuous-time stochastic factor model, obtained the optimal strategy and value function for both the MV and MMV optimization problems in an explicit form, and demonstrated their equivalence through direct comparison. Following the same direction as [20], Shen and Zou [15] considered a deterministic coefficients model with cone trading constraints and solved the MMV and the MV problems successively by means of the HJBI equation approach and verified that the optimal strategies to both problems coincide. Then, Hu, Shi and Xu [4] generalized [15] to a diffusion model with random appreciation rate, volatility and deterministic interest rate. Recently, Shi and Xu [17] reach the same conclusion for the MMV and MV investment-reinsurance problems. In the conclusion of [4], they pointed out that how to solve the MMV problem when the interest rate is random even without any constraints constitutes an interesting question for future research.
