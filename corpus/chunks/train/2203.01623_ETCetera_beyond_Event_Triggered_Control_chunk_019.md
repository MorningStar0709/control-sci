# 3.2 Traffic abstractions of linear PETC systems

In the case of unperturbed linear systems under PETC, the construction of abstractions can be performed with simpler computations, provide exact simulations (thanks to the periodic nature of condition checking), and in general the abstractions are tighter than when employing the general scheme described in the previous section.
