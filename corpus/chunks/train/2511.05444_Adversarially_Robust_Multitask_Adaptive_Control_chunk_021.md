Proposition 3.1 (Intra-cluster heterogeneity). Consider a cluster $\mathcal { C } _ { j }$ consisting of Mj similar but non-identical systems with bounded intra-cluster heterogeneity as in Assumption 1. Suppose the initial epoch length $\tau _ { 1 }$ and the number of iterations N in RCSI are chosen as in Lemma 3.1. Then, for a small $\delta \in ( 0 , 1 )$ , the model estimate $\widehat { \Theta } ^ { ( i ) }$ at epoch k satisfies:

$$\left\| \widehat {\Theta} _ {k} ^ {(i)} - \Theta_ {\star} ^ {(i)} \right\| _ {F} ^ {2} \leq C _ {\mathrm{stat}} \frac {\sigma_ {w} ^ {2} (d _ {x} ^ {2} + d _ {x} d _ {u}) \log (M _ {j} / \delta)}{\sigma_ {k} ^ {2} M _ {j} \tau_ {k}} + C _ {\mathrm{het}} \epsilon_ {\mathrm{het}} ^ {2} + C _ {\mathrm{mis,1}} e ^ {- C _ {\mathrm{mis,2}} \sigma_ {k} ^ {2} \tau_ {k}},$$

with probability at least $1 - \delta$

Lemma 3.2 (Adversarial systems). Consider a cluster $\mathcal { C } _ { j }$ containing $M _ { j }$ similar but non-identical systems, among which at most $f _ { j } < M _ { j } / 2$ are adversarial and $m _ { j } = M _ { j } - f _ { j }$ are honest. Assume the aggregation rule is $( f _ { j } , \lambda )$ -resilient as defined in Definition 2.1, and that Assumption 1 holds. Suppose further that the initial epoch length $\tau _ { 1 }$ and the number of iterations N in RCSI are chosen as in Lemma 3.1. Then, for a small $\delta \in ( 0 , 1 )$ , the model estimate $\widehat { \Theta } ^ { ( i ) }$ at epoch k satisfies:

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left\| \widehat {\Theta} _ {k} ^ {(i)} - \Theta_ {\star} ^ {(i)} \right\| _ {F} ^ {2} \leq C _ {\mathrm{stat}} \frac {\sigma_ {w} ^ {2} (d _ {x} ^ {2} + d _ {x} d _ {u}) \log (M _ {j} / \delta)}{\sigma_ {k} ^ {2} \tau_ {k}} \left(\frac {1}{m _ {j}} + \lambda^ {2} d _ {x}\right) + C _ {\mathrm{het}} (1 + \lambda) ^ {2} \epsilon_ {\mathrm{het}} ^ {2} \\ + C _ {\mathrm{mis}, 1} e ^ {- C _ {\mathrm{mis}, 2} \sigma_ {k} ^ {2} \tau_ {k}}, w. p. 1 - \delta . \\ \end{array}
$$
