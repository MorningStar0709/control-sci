# A. Validation of the Physicist (Baseline)

Fig. 5 compares the theoretical predictions from Eq. (13) with simulation results. We observe good qualitative agreement between theory and simulations for both the BK and ABC flows. The agreement is slightly better in the BK case, where both the SOC formulation and the value function $V _ { \phi }$ are analytically tractable, compared to the ABC flow, where the estimation of $\bar { \lambda } _ { 1 }$

is approximate.
