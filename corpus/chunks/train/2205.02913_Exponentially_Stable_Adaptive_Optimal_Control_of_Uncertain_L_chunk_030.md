# 3.4 Tuning guidelines

The arbitrary parameters of the proposed ALQ control law (11), (32) can be divided into three main groups:

i) the parameters $\tau _ { \infty }$ and $p ,$ which determine the value of the augmented error $\varepsilon _ { \xi } ,$   
ii) the parameters $r , l , k _ { 0 } , k _ { 1 }$ and $\sigma ,$ , which ensure the propagation of the regressor excitation through parametrizations (23), (25), (31);   
iii) the coefficients $\gamma _ { 0 } , \gamma _ { 1 }$ , which adjust the convergence rate of the augmented error $\xi \left( t \right)$ , and $\rho ,$ which defines the initial time instant of the control law (11) adjustment.
