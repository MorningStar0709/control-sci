$$\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {T _ {i}} X _ {t _ {i, j}} (N ^ {\star}) \leq \sqrt {(3 2 l _ {y} ^ {2} \log \frac {3 h}{\delta}) \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {T _ {i}} \left\| N _ {t _ {i , j} - h} - N ^ {\star} \right\| ^ {2}}.$$

Applying a union bound, we further get that with probability at least $1 - \delta / 3$ ,

$$\sum_ {t = h} ^ {T - 1} X _ {t} (N ^ {\star}) = \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {h - 1} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {T _ {i}} X _ {t _ {i, j}} (N ^ {\star}) \leq \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {h - 1} \sqrt {(3 2 l _ {y} ^ {2} \log \frac {3 h}{\delta}) \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {T _ {i}} \left\| N _ {t _ {i , j} - h} - N ^ {\star} \right\| ^ {2}}\stackrel {(a)} {\leq} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {h - 1} \left(\frac {1 6 l _ {y} ^ {2} \log \frac {3 h}{\delta}}{\varrho} + \frac {\varrho}{2} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {T _ {i}} \left\| N _ {t _ {i, j} - h} - N ^ {\star} \right\| ^ {2}\right)\stackrel {(b)} {=} \frac {1 6 l _ {y} ^ {2} h \log \frac {3 h}{\delta}}{\varrho} + \frac {\varrho}{2} \sum_ {t = h} ^ {T - 1} \| N _ {t - h} - N ^ {\star} \| ^ {2}\leq \frac {1 6 l _ {y} ^ {2} h \log \frac {3 h}{\delta}}{\varrho} + \frac {\varrho}{2} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 1} \| N _ {t} - N ^ {\star} \| ^ {2},$$

where (a) follows from the inequality $\begin{array} { r } { \sqrt { a b } \leq \frac { a } { 2 \varrho } + \frac { \varrho } { 2 } b } \end{array}$ that holds for any $a , b , \varrho \in \mathbb { R } _ { > 0 }$ , and (b) follows from the way we set $t _ { i , j }$ . Setting $\varrho = ( \alpha _ { 0 } \log ^ { 2 } T ) / 2$ gives the result of the lemma.
