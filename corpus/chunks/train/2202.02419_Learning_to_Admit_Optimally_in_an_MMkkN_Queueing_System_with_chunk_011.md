# 1.2. Organization

The paper is organized as follows. In Section 2, we introduce the problem and the learning objective. Section 3.1 presents our learning-based dispatching policy. In Section 3, we demonstrate the asymptotic optimality of the proposed policy in a multi-server Erlang-B system and characterize its regret compared to a system with a known service rate. Section 4 extends the results of Section 3 to a queueing system with a finite buffer. In Section 5, we study the performance of our proposed policy through experiments and verify our theoretical analysis.
