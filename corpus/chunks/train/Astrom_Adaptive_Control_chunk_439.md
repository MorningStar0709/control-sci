For command signals containing several sinusoidals the equilibrium can still be stable, provided that the command signal is dominated by components with frequencies in the range in which the phase lag of the process is less than $90^{\circ}$ . Notice that it helps to filter the command signal so that the signals in the frequency range in which the plant has a phase shift of more than $90^{\circ}$ are attenuated.
