# III. LINEAR EQUIVALENCE MODEL

In this section, the linear equivalence model of the unicycle in (9) is discussed. In view of (2), the solvability of the full input to state feedback linearization of the unicycle model will render the input-output relation to be linear. The goal of this section is to find the feedback linearizing controller in the form of $u = \alpha ( x ) + \beta ( x ) \nu$ , where $\alpha ( x )$ and $\beta ( x )$ can be written as follows:

$$
\alpha (x) = A ^ {- 1} (x) \left[ \begin{array}{c} \mathcal {L} _ {f} ^ {r _ {1}} h _ {1} (x) \\ \vdots \\ \mathcal {L} _ {f} ^ {r _ {m}} h _ {m} (x) \end{array} \right], \quad \beta (x) = A ^ {- 1} (x). \tag {16}
$$
