One may worry that disclosing the residue signal $\bar { r } ( t )$ could leak some sensitive or private information. Moreover, residue signals are typically dominated by noise, disturbances, and model uncertainties in practice, so it will be difficult to recover meaningful information solely from ¯r(t); see (Jang et al., 2025, Remark 4) for related discussion.
