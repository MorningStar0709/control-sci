Theorem 1 demonstrates the stability of the closed-loop system under the NBS tracking controller when there are no disturbances. It is unconditionally stable for all compatible neural network parameters. That is, the controller can guarantee closed-loop stability as long as the neural networks Φ and D satisfy the conditions in Theorem 1, irrespective of the neural network parameters. In the following theorem, we will show that the NBS tracking controller can achieve a bounded tracking error in the presence of bounded disturbances.
