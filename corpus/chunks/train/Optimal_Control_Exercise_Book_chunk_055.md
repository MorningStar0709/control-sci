# Exercise 3.24

Consider the problem

$$u ^ {*} := \underset {u: [ 0, 1 ] \rightarrow \mathbb {R}} {\operatorname{argmin}} J (u) := \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} (x (t) ^ {2} + P u (t) ^ {2}) d t \tag {194}$$

subject to

$$\dot {x} (t) = u (t), \quad x (0) = 1, \quad x (1) = \text { free } \tag {195}$$

where $P > 0$ us a weighting parameter. Find an optimal solution $u ^ { * }$ and $x ^ { * }$ using the Euler-Lagrange condition.

Discuss how the solution changes as $P  \infty$ and $P  0$ . Provide an intuitive explanation of the phenomenon.
