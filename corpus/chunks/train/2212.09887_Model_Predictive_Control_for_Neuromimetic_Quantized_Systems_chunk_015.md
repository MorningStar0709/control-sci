# 6. CONCLUSION AND FUTURE WORK

The work described above uses concepts of model predictive control (MPC) to extend out previous research on neuromimetic emulation of finite dimensional linear systems (See Baillieul and Sun (2021) and Sun and Baillieul (2022)). The main results are shown to hold under assumptions that we expect to relax in the near future. Next research will also describe neuro-inspired machine learning approaches to these and other classes of neuromimetic emulation.
