# 5.3 Recursive Identification Methods Based on the Whitening of the Prediction Error (Type I)

The following recursive identification methods belonging to this category will be presented:2

• Recursive Least Squares (RLS);   
• Extended Least Squares (ELS);   
• Output Error with Extended Prediction Model (OEEPM);   
• Recursive Maximum Likelihood (RML) ;   
• Generalized Least Squares (GLS) .
