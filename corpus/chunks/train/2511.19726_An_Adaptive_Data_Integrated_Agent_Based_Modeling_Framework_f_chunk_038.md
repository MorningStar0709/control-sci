# 10.5 Policy Layer: Adaptive Regulation

Policy parameters are represented as:

$$P _ {t} = (\lambda_ {t}, \tau_ {t}, \sigma_ {t}),$$

where $\lambda _ { t }$ is a carbon tax or price, $\tau _ { t }$ a cap or emissions budget, and $\sigma _ { t }$ a subsidy or support parameter. The policymaker updates $P _ { t }$ via an optimization rule G using observed performance:

$$P _ {t + 1} = G (P _ {t}, \hat {J} _ {t}, s _ {t}).$$

This captures iterative adjustments common in climate policy, such as updating carbon prices or tightening emissions caps.
