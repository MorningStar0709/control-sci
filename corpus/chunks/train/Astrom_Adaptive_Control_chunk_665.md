# Covariance Resetting

In some situations the parameters are constant over long periods of time and change abruptly occasionally. Exponential forgetting, which is based on the assumption of a behavior that is homogeneous in time, is less suitable in this case. In such a situation it is more appropriate to reset the covariance matrix of the estimator to a large matrix when the changes occur. This is called covariance resetting.
