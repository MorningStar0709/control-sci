# 2.4.5 Augmenting the equivalent control

During sliding mode, equivalent control is derived using equivalent control method (Utkin, 1991). However, if the initial state of the system is not on the surface, then to force or drive the state trajectories onto the sliding surface ??, discontinuous or switched control part is appended to the original equation, i.e.

$$u = u _ {e q} + u _ {N}. \tag {2.26}$$

where, $u _ { N }$ is added to satisfy the reaching condition which may have different forms.
