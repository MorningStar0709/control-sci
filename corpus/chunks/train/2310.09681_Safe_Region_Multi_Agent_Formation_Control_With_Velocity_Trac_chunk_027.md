# Appendix A. Proof of Lemma 2

Proof. Let $\lambda _ { 1 } = ( r ^ { 2 } - \| p _ { i } - p _ { j } \| ^ { 2 } + \mu ) $ and $\lambda _ { 2 } = ( \parallel p _ { i } - p _ { j } -$ $d _ { i j } \Vert ^ { 2 } )$ . As $\| p _ { i } - p _ { j } \| < r$ always hold true, $\lambda _ { 1 }$ and $\lambda _ { 2 }$ are always non-negative. Then $\dot { f }$ is given by-

$$\dot {f} = \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} \dot {\Phi} _ {i j},= \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} (v _ {i} - v _ {j}) ^ {T} \left(\frac {2 \lambda_ {1} (p _ {i} - p _ {j} - d _ {i j}) + 2 \lambda_ {2} (p _ {i} - p _ {j})}{(\lambda_ {1}) ^ {2}}\right),= \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} v _ {i} ^ {T} \left(\frac {2 \lambda_ {1} (p _ {i} - p _ {j} - d _ {i j}) + 2 \lambda_ {2} (p _ {i} - p _ {j})}{(\lambda_ {1}) ^ {2}}\right)+ \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} v _ {j} \left(\frac {2 \lambda_ {1} (p _ {j} - p _ {i} - d _ {j i}) + 2 \lambda_ {2} (p _ {j} - p _ {i})}{(\lambda_ {1}) ^ {2}}\right).$$

As we have $d _ { i j } = - d _ { j i }$ , we get

$$= \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} v _ {i} ^ {T} \left(\frac {2 \lambda_ {1} (p _ {i} - p _ {j} - d _ {i j}) + 2 \lambda_ {2} (p _ {i} - p _ {j})}{(\lambda_ {1}) ^ {2}}\right),= \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} v _ {i} ^ {T} \nabla_ {p _ {i}} \Phi_ {i j}.$$

This completes the proof of the lemma.
