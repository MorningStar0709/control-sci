# 5.7 Selection of the Pseudo Random Binary Sequence

In Sect. 3.4, it was shown that the correct parameter estimation requires the use of a rich signal (persistently exciting signal). It was pointed out that pseudo-random binary sequences (PRBS) offer on the one hand a signal with a large frequency spectrum approaching the white noise and, on the other hand, they have a constant magnitude. This allows to define precisely the level of the instantaneous stress on the process (or actuator).

In this section, we will discuss how to select the PRBS for a good identification taking into account the various constraints which are encountered in practice.
