$$
\begin{array}{l} u _ {v x} = - E _ {p x} + \hat {k} _ {v 1} E _ {v x} + k _ {p 1} \dot {E} _ {p x} + \ddot {x} _ {d} - \mathcal {Q} _ {p} (1) \\ u _ {v y} = - E _ {p y} + \hat {k} _ {v 2} E _ {v y} + k _ {p 2} \dot {E} _ {p y} + \ddot {y} _ {d} - \mathcal {Q} _ {p} (2) \tag {27} \\ u _ {v z} = - E _ {p z} + \hat {k} _ {v 3} E _ {v z} + k _ {p 3} \dot {E} _ {p z} + \ddot {z} _ {d} - \mathcal {Q} _ {p} (3) \\ \end{array}
$$

Based on (21), the total thrust of the entire system $\mathcal { U } _ { t h }$ and the desired orientation angles $( \phi _ { d } , \theta _ { d } )$ as outputs of the (outer loop) position controller are given as follows:

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathcal {U} _ {t h} = m _ {s} \sqrt {u _ {v x} ^ {2} + u _ {v y} ^ {2} + (u _ {v x} + g) ^ {2}} \\ \theta_ {d} = \arctan \left(\frac {u _ {v x} \cos (\psi_ {d}) + u _ {v y} \sin (\psi_ {d})}{u _ {v z} + g}\right) \tag {28} \\ \phi_ {d} = \arctan \left(\frac {\cos (\theta_ {d}) (u _ {v x} \sin (\psi_ {d}) - u _ {v y} \cos (\psi_ {d}))}{u _ {v z} + g}\right) \\ \end{array}
