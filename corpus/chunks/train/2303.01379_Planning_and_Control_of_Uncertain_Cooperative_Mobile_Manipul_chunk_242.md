This specific choice yields the vector $G ^ { * } G h = G ^ { \dagger } G h \in \mathrm { r a n g e } ( G ^ { \top } )$ that is closest to $h .$ , i.e., $\| h - G ^ { \dagger } G h \| \leq \| h - y \|$ , $\forall y \in \operatorname { r a n g e } ( G ^ { \top } )$ . Theorem 3.3. Consider N robotic agents rigidly grasping an object with coupled dynamics (3.39). Let $h \in \mathbb { R } ^ { 6 N }$ be the stacked vector of agent forces exerted at the grasping points. Then the agent forces h and the internal forces $h _ { i n t }$ are related as:

$$h _ {\mathrm{int}} = (I _ {6 N} - M G ^ {\top} (G M G ^ {\top}) ^ {- 1} G) h.$$
