# 习题 8.5

8.5.1 考虑系统

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} {\left[ \begin{array}{c} \dot {x} _ {1} \\ \dot {x} _ {2} \\ \dot {x} _ {3} \\ \dot {x} _ {4} \\ \dot {x} _ {5} \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c} x _ {2} \\ x _ {5} \\ x _ {4} \\ x _ {5} + x _ {1} ^ {2} \\ x _ {4} \end{array} \right] + \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ x _ {1} ^ {2} \end{array} \right] u _ {1} + \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ x _ {2} \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ - 1 \end{array} \right] u _ {2},} \\ y _ {1} = x _ {1}, \\ y _ {3} = x _ {3}. \end{array} \right.
$$

(i) 计算系统的解耦矩阵;  
(ii) 解耦问题可解吗？如果可解，请解之.

8.5.2 考虑下列系统：

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \dot {x} _ {1} = x _ {3} ^ {3} + x _ {2}, \\ \dot {x} _ {2} = \cos (x _ {2}) - \mathrm{e} ^ {x _ {3}} u _ {1}, \\ \dot {x} _ {3} = \sin (x _ {2}) - x _ {4} \\ \dot {x} _ {4} = \ln (x _ {1} ^ {2} + 1) + \mathrm{e} ^ {x _ {4}} u _ {2}, \\ y _ {1} = 2 x _ {1}, \\ y _ {2} = x _ {3}. \end{array} \right.
$$

(i) 在 $0 \in \mathbb{R}^4$ 找出相对阶向量;  
(ii) 在 $0 \in \mathbb{R}^4$ 求出解耦矩阵；  
(iii) 在 $0 \in \mathbb{R}^4$ 解局部输入解耦问题.
