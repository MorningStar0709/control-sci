# G.1 Proof of Lemma 19

By Lemmas 1 and 2, the class of truncated linear controllers satisfy all of the assumptions of Lemma 26 in Schiffer and Janson [2024]. Therefore, the proof of Lemma 19 follows exactly as the proof of Lemma 26 in Schiffer and Janson [2024], except for Algorithm 2 from this paper instead of Algorithm 2 in Schiffer and Janson [2024] and with the analogous definition of event E.
