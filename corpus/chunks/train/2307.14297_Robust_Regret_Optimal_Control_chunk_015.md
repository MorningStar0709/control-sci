Proof. The optimality of the non-causal controller will be shown via completion of the squares. First, define $H : = R + B _ { u } ^ { \top } X B _ { u } \succ 0$ and express the DARE as:

$$0 = X - A ^ {\top} X A - Q + K _ {x} ^ {\top} H K _ {x}. \tag {9}$$

Substitute for Q using the DARE to show:

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} Q & S \\ S ^ {\top} & R \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c} K _ {x} ^ {\top} \\ I \end{array} \right] H \left[ \begin{array}{c c} K _ {x} & I \end{array} \right] + \left[ \begin{array}{c c} X & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right] - \left[ \begin{array}{c} A ^ {\top} \\ B _ {u} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] X \left[ \begin{array}{c c} A & B _ {u} \end{array} \right].
$$

Thus the per-step cost achieved by any controller K is:

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left[ \begin{array}{c} x _ {t} \\ u _ {t} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} Q & S \\ S ^ {\top} & R \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} x _ {t} \\ u _ {t} \end{array} \right] = (u _ {t} + K _ {x} x _ {t}) ^ {\top} H (u _ {t} + K _ {x} x _ {t}) \\ + x _ {t} ^ {\top} X x _ {t} - \left(A x _ {t} + B _ {u} u _ {t}\right) ^ {\top} X \left(A x _ {t} + B _ {u} u _ {t}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

The per-step cost can be rewritten in terms of the input $u ^ { o }$ generated by the non-causal controller $K ^ { o } { \mathrm { : } }$ :

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left[ \begin{array}{c} x _ {t} \\ u _ {t} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} Q & S \\ S ^ {\top} & R \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} x _ {t} \\ u _ {t} \end{array} \right] = (u _ {t} - u _ {t} ^ {o}) ^ {\top} H (u _ {t} - u _ {t} ^ {o}) \\ - \left(K _ {d} d _ {t} + K _ {v} v _ {t + 1}\right) ^ {\top} H (K _ {d} d _ {t} + K _ {v} v _ {t + 1}) \tag {10} \\ - 2 (K _ {d} d _ {t} + K _ {v} v _ {t + 1}) ^ {\top} H (u _ {t} + K _ {x} x _ {t}) \\ + x _ {t} ^ {\top} X x _ {t} - (A x _ {t} + B _ {u} u _ {t}) ^ {\top} X (A x _ {t} + B _ {u} u _ {t}). \\ \end{array}
$$

The terms on the last two lines can be combined and simplified after some algebra. There are two key steps in this simplification. First use the dynamics for $x _ { t + 1 }$ to show:

$$
\begin{array}{l} x _ {t + 1} ^ {\top} X x _ {t + 1} = \left(A x _ {t} + B _ {u} u _ {t}\right) ^ {\top} X \left(A x _ {t} + B _ {u} u _ {t}\right) \\ + 2 d _ {t} ^ {\top} B _ {d} ^ {\top} X (A x _ {t} + B _ {u} u _ {t}) + d _ {t} ^ {\top} B _ {d} ^ {\top} X B _ {d} d _ {t}. \\ \end{array}
$$
