\begin{array}{l} \left\{x _ {i}, y _ {i} \right\} _ {\Omega} = \min \left\{\left| x _ {i} - y _ {i} \right|, 2 \pi - \left| x _ {i} - y _ {i} \right| \right\} \operatorname{sign} \left(x _ {i} - y _ {i}\right) \tag {26} \\ \cdot \operatorname{sign} (\pi - | x _ {i} - y _ {i} |) \\ \end{array}
$$
