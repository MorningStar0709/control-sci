# Universal Stabilizers

An interesting class of adaptive algorithms was discovered during attempts to investigate whether Assumption A3 is necessary. The following question was posed. Consider the scalar system

$$\frac {d y}{d t} = a y + b u \tag {6.91}$$

where $a$ and $b$ are constants. Does there exist a feedback law of the form

$$
\begin{array}{l} u = f (\hat {\theta}, y) \\ \frac {d \hat {\theta}}{d t} = g (\hat {\theta}, y) \tag {6.92} \\ \end{array}
$$
