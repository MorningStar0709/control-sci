# 4.1 Optogenetically active neuron:

The individual neuron model with embedded CHR2 channels was excited with simulated light pulses of intensity $\mathbf { l m } \mathbf { W } / \mathbf { m m } ^ { 2 } .$ , 5mS pulse width and a period of 55mS. It produces single spikes with low rise time, comparable to spikes produced by electrical pulses. This precise strength and pulse width was utilized for training in all the training examples.
