Lemma D.1 (Kronecker Lemma): (Goodwin and Sin 1984) Assume that {x(t)} and $\{ b _ { t } \}$ are sequences of reals such that:

$$\text { (i) } \quad \lim _ {N \to \infty} \sum_ {t = 1} ^ {N} x (t) < \infty\text { (ii) } \quad \{b _ {N} \}: b _ {N} \geq b _ {N - 1}\text {(iii)} \quad \lim _ {N \to \infty} b _ {N} = \infty$$

Then:

$$\lim _ {N \to \infty} \frac {1}{b _ {N}} \sum_ {t = 1} ^ {N} b _ {t} x (t) = 0 \tag {D.50}$$
