$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {j = 0} ^ {n} \lambda_ {j} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} - \overline {{\mathbf {H}}} _ {\phi} (x, x _ {0}) + \overline {{\mathbf {H}}} _ {\phi} (x _ {j}, x _ {0}) & \mathbf {0} \\ \mathbf {0} & \mathbf {0} \end{array} \right] \\ + \mathrm{He} \Biggl (\left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {0} & (- \overline {{\mathbf {H}}} _ {\zeta} (x, x _ {0}) + \overline {{\mathbf {H}}} _ {\zeta} (x _ {j}, x _ {0})) ^ {\top} \\ \mathbf {0} & \mathbf {0} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {0} & \mathbf {0} \\ \mathbf {0} & \mathbf {I} \end{array} \right] \Biggr). \\ \end{array}
$$

From here, the proof follows as Theorem 10. ✷
