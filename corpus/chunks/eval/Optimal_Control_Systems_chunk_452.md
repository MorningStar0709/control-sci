# Multiplication of a Matrix by a Scalar

The scalar multiplication of two matrices of the same order, and the addition or subtraction is easily seen to be

$$\mathbf {C} = \alpha_ {1} \mathbf {A} + \alpha_ {2} \mathbf {B} \tag {A.2.5}$$

where, $\alpha_{1}$ and $\alpha_{2}$ are scalars, and

$$c _ {i j} + \alpha_ {1} a _ {i j} + \alpha_ {2} b _ {i j}. \tag {A.2.6}$$
