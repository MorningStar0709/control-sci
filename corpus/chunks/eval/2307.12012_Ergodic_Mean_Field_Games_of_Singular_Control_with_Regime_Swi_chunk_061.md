# APPENDIX B. SKOROKHOD REFLECTION PROBLEM WITH REGIME-SWITCHING AND PROOF OF THEOREM 3.2

In this section we focus on the existence and uniqueness of a solution to $\mathbf { S P } ( \alpha , \beta , x , i )$ as defined in Definition 3.1. Then we show a comparison result and finally we prove Theorem 3.2.

Recall I as in (3.16) and denote by $\Gamma : \mathcal { D } [ 0 , \infty )  \mathcal { D } [ 0 , \infty )$ the following operator

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Gamma (X) _ {t} := I (X) _ {t} - \max \left\{\left(x - \beta_ {i} (\theta)\right) ^ {+} \wedge \inf _ {s \leq t} \left(I (X) _ {s} - \alpha_ {Y _ {s}} (\theta)\right), \right. \\ \sup _ {s \leq t} \left(\left(I (X) _ {s} - \beta_ {Y _ {s}} (\theta)\right) \wedge \inf _ {u \in [ s, t ]} \left(I (X) _ {u} - \alpha_ {Y _ {u}} (\theta)\right)\right) \Bigg \} =: I (X) _ {t} - \Xi (X) _ {t}. \tag {B.1} \\ \end{array}
$$

We first prove that the operator has a fixed point.

Proposition B.1. There exists unique solution to Skorokhod problem $\mathbf { S P } ( \alpha , \beta , x , i )$ .

Proof. Let $T > 0$ and X, $X ^ { \prime } \in \mathcal { D } [ 0 , \infty )$ , then using the inequalities

$$
\begin{array}{l} \max \left\{\alpha_ {1}, \beta_ {1} \right\} - \max \left\{\alpha_ {2}, \beta_ {2} \right\} \leq \max \left\{\alpha_ {1} - \alpha_ {2}, \beta_ {1} - \beta_ {2} \right\} \\ \left| \sup _ {s \leq t} | f (s) | - \sup _ {s \leq t} | g (s) | \right| \leq \sup _ {s \leq t} \left| f (s) - g (s) \right| \\ \big | \inf _ {s \leq t} | f (s) | - \inf _ {s \leq t} | g (s) | \big | \leq \sup _ {s \leq t} \big | f (s) - g (s) \big |, \\ \end{array}
$$

we obtain that

$$\sup _ {s \leq t} \left| \Gamma (X) _ {s} - \Gamma (X ^ {\prime}) _ {s} \right| ^ {2} \leq 4 \sup _ {s \leq t} \left| I (X) _ {s} - I (X ^ {\prime}) _ {s} \right| ^ {2}, t \in [ 0, T ]. \tag {B.2}$$

We now derive an estimate for $\begin{array} { r } { \operatorname* { s u p } _ { s \leq t } \left| I ( X ) _ { s } - I ( X ^ { \prime } ) _ { s } \right| ^ { 2 } } \end{array}$ . In particular, we have that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {s \leq t} \left| I (X) _ {s} - I \left(X ^ {\prime}\right) _ {s} \right| ^ {2} \right] \leq 2 \mathbb {E} \left[ \sup _ {s \leq t} \left| \int_ {0} ^ {s} \left(b \left(X _ {s}, Y _ {s}\right) - b \left(X _ {s} ^ {\prime}, Y _ {s}\right)\right) d s \right| ^ {2} \right. \\ \left. + \sup _ {s \leq t} \left| \int_ {0} ^ {s} \left(\sigma (X _ {s}, Y _ {s}) - \sigma (X _ {s} ^ {\prime}, Y _ {s})\right) d W _ {s} \right| ^ {2} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
