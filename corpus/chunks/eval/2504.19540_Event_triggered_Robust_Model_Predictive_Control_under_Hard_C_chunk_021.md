# VI. CONCLUSION

We presented a novel event-triggered robust MPC, which takes strictly limited resources of central computer servers into account, by only computing a new control input for a subset of systems. Through a theoretical analysis, we showed that our approach leads to stable closed loop systems. In experiments, we demonstrated that our approach is able to control many systems with limited resources.
