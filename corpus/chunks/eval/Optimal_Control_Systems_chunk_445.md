# Multiplication of Vectors

The multiplication of two vectors is done by transposing one of the vectors and then multiplying this vector with the other vector. Thus,

$$\mathbf {x} ^ {\prime} \mathbf {y} = < \mathbf {x}, \mathbf {y} > = \sum_ {i} ^ {n} x _ {i} y _ {i} = x _ {1} y _ {1} + x _ {2} y _ {2} + \dots + x _ {n} y _ {n}. \tag {A.1.7}$$
