As a result, the ResNet weights are able to compensate for the system uncertainty as shown in Figure 3 which yields improved tracking and function approximation performance. Additionally, the ResNet requires approximately the same control effort as the fully-connected DNN. Therefore, the ResNet improves the tracking performance without requiring a higher control effort in comparison to the fully-connected DNN.
