Precision Position Fixes: Precision terrain referenced coordinates. Absolute errors in latitude and longitude do not degrade TERCOM accuracies. Accuracy does not degrade with total time or distance traveled.

Repeatable Precision: TERCOM accuracy remains the same independent of weather, ECM conditions, time of day, etc., because the Earth does not change. TERCOM accuracy is repeatable from one TERCOM system to another.

Operates Under ECM Conditions: The only element subject to ECM is the radar altimeter. The radar altimeter has a directional antenna pointed straight down and, with the exception of some missiles, will be traveling in a random path over the Earth. This condition makes it impractical to implement effective ECM against TERCOM. If one position fix should be interrupted by passing over an ECM system, the ECM will not affect subsequent fixes.
