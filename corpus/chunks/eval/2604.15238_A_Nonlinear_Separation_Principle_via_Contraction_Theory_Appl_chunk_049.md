# APPENDIX II PROOF FOR LEMMA 2

Proof: Given $x _ { 1 } , x _ { 2 } \in \mathbb { R } ^ { n }$ , adopt the shorthands $\Delta x = x _ { 1 } - x _ { 2 } \in \mathbb { R } ^ { n }$ , $\Delta y = y _ { 1 } - y _ { 2 } = H \Delta x \in \mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ , and $\Delta \Psi = \Psi ( y _ { 1 } ) - \Psi ( y _ { 2 } ) \in \mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ . Since

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c} \Delta y \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c} H \Delta x \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} H & \mathbb {0} _ {m \times m} \\ \mathbb {0} _ {m \times n} & I _ {m} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} \Delta x \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right],
$$

we rewrite the incremental multiplier matrix condition (1) as

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{l} \Delta y \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} M \left[ \begin{array}{l} \Delta y \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] \geq 0 \iff \left[ \begin{array}{l} \Delta x \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} M _ {H} \left[ \begin{array}{l} \Delta x \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] \geq 0. \tag {84}
$$

The continuous time result is a special case of the result in [10, Theorem 4.2]. In the discrete time case, right and left multiplying (6) by $\begin{array} { r } { \left[ \Delta x ^ { \top } \quad \Delta \Psi ^ { \top } \right] ^ { \top } } \end{array}$ and its transpose respectively, and using (84) we get,

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c} \Delta x \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} A ^ {\top} P A - \rho^ {2} P & A ^ {\top} P B \\ B ^ {\top} P A & B ^ {\top} P B \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} \Delta x \\ \Delta \Psi \end{array} \right] \leq 0 \tag {85}
\Longleftrightarrow \| A \Delta x + B \Delta \Psi \| _ {P} ^ {2} \leq \rho^ {2} \| \Delta x \| _ {P} ^ {2}. \tag {86}$$

This is precisely the contraction condition for discrete-time systems.
