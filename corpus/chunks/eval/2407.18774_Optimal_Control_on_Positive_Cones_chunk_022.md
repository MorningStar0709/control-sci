\left[ \begin{array}{c c} I & 0 \\ - Y _ {2} ^ {+} Y _ {1} ^ {\top} & 0 \end{array} \right] A \left(T \left[ \begin{array}{c c} X & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right] T ^ {\top}\right) \left[ \begin{array}{c c} I & 0 \\ - Y _ {2} ^ {+} Y _ {1} ^ {\top} & 0 \end{array} \right] ^ {\top}.
$$

It then follows that

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} X & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right] \in \mathcal {P} _ {Q} \implies \left[ \begin{array}{c} I \\ - M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] X \left[ \begin{array}{c} I \\ - M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} \in \mathcal {P} _ {Q}.
$$

Next observe that since $\left( I - M _ { 2 } ^ { + } M _ { 2 } \right) M _ { 1 } ^ { \top } = 0$ (else there would exist no Λ such that (12) holds),

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} M _ {1} M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {1} \\ M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {2} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} I \\ - M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] X \left[ \begin{array}{c} I \\ - M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right] ^ {\top} = 0. \tag {16}
$$

Finally for fixed X note that the minimizer for (13) equals the minimizer for

$$
\inf \Bigl \{\operatorname{tr} \biggl (\left[ \begin{array}{c c} M _ {1} M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {1} \\ M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {2} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c} X & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right] \bigr): \left[ \begin{array}{c c} X & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right] \in \mathcal {P} _ {Q} \Bigr \}.
$$

From the definition of a dual cone,

$$
\operatorname{tr} \left(\left[ \begin{array}{c c} M _ {1} M _ {2} ^ {+} M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {1} \\ M _ {1} ^ {\top} & M _ {2} \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c} X & U _ {1} \\ U _ {1} ^ {\top} & U _ {2} \end{array} \right]\right) \geq 0.
$$

Therefore by (16) the right hand side of (14) is a minimizer as required.
