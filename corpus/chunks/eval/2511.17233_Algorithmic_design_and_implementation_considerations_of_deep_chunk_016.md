# VI. CONCLUSIONS

We have discussed about algorithmic implementation details of deep MPC. We have demonstrated through a numerical experiment that the poor choice of learning authority leads to poor performance. Future work may involve further simplification of constraint tightening or different approaches to satisfy state constraints. Evaluation of deep MPC for more challenging problems with different neural network architectures and experience selection criterion can be an interesting direction. The deep MPC algorithm is not suitable for random uncertainties but its integration with stochastic MPC approaches [28], [29] can also be interesting.
