# V. CONCLUSION

This work presents a mixed $\mathcal { H } _ { \infty } \mathrm { - p a s s i v i t y }$ framework that coordinates DHS temperature regulation with EPS secondary frequency control. A forecast-free DHS regulator achieves the optimal energysharing equilibrium without heat-load prediction and provides tunable EPS–DHS performance trade-offs via LMI conditions. The resulting controller guarantees closed-loop stability and convergence to the optimal CHP operating point, and simulations across low-, mid-, and high-frequency disturbances confirm the expected performance gains.
