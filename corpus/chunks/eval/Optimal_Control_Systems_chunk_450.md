# Unity Matrix

A unity matrix $\mathbf{I}$ is defined as the matrix having values 1 for all diagonal elements and having the rest of the elements as zero as

$$
\mathbf {I} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c c c} 1 & 0 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ \dots & \dots & \dots & \dots & \dots \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & \dots & 1 \end{array} \right]. \tag {A.2.2}
$$
