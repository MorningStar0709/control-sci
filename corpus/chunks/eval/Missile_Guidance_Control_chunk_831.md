#

The QF-4 is a converted, remotely piloted F-4 Phantom fighter aircraft, used for full-scale training and/or testing purposes. The QF-4 replaces the QF-106 as a joint service full-scale aerial target, and uses an improved flight control system and has a greater payload. Guidance of the QF-4 consists of multifunction command-andcontrol multilateration system.
