$$
\begin{array}{l} J = E \left[ \boldsymbol {x} ^ {\mathrm{T}} (\infty) \boldsymbol {Q} \boldsymbol {x} (\infty) + \boldsymbol {u} ^ {\mathrm{T}} (\infty) \boldsymbol {R} \boldsymbol {u} (\infty) \right] \\ = E \left\{\left[ \left(\boldsymbol {Q} ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \boldsymbol {x} (\infty)\right) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \quad \left(\boldsymbol {R} ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \boldsymbol {u} (\infty)\right) ^ {\mathrm{T}} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{l} \boldsymbol {Q} ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \boldsymbol {x} (\infty) \\ \boldsymbol {R} ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \boldsymbol {u} (\infty) \end{array} \right] \right\} \\ = \left\| \mathbf {G} _ {\mathrm{cl}} \right\| _ {2} ^ {2} \\ \end{array}
$$

由于平方运算是单调的,使 LQG 代价函数最小等价于使闭环系统 2-范数最小。
