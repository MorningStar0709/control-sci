As already discussed in Section 2, in [23] the authors provided sufficient conditions which guarantee the global existence of the whole interval [0, T ] of the solutions $X ( \cdot ) , \hat { X } ( \cdot )$ of the RDEs (7) and (8) satisfying the sign conditions (12)-(13). One can see evidently that if (12)-(13) are satisfied then the sign conditions (14)-(15) are also verified. The problem considered in [23] can then be viewed as a particular case of the problem addressed in this paper. As a matter of fact, by specializing the result given in Theorem 3.15 one can obtain necessary and sufficient conditions which guarantee the global existence of the whole interval [0, T ] of the solutions $X ( \cdot ) , \hat { X } ( \cdot )$ of the RDEs (7) and (8) satisfying the sign conditions (12)-(13).
