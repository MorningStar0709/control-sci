$$\mu (x _ {k} ^ {*}) \approx \mu (\mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}) + \frac {\partial \mu (x ^ {*})}{\partial x ^ {*}} \bigg | _ {x ^ {*} = \mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}} ^ {\top} (x ^ {*} - \mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}) + \mathcal {O} \left(| | x ^ {*} - \mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}} | | ^ {2}\right). \tag {A.5}$$

Subsequently, the expectation $\mathbb { E } _ { x _ { k } ^ { * } } \left[ \mu ( x _ { k } ^ { * } ) \right]$ is approximated following the process outlined in [30, Eq. 15]:

$$\mathbb {E} _ {\boldsymbol {x} _ {k} ^ {*}} \left[ \mu (\boldsymbol {x} _ {k} ^ {*}) \right] \approx \mathbb {E} _ {\boldsymbol {x} _ {k} ^ {*}} \left[ \mu (\mu_ {\boldsymbol {x} _ {k} ^ {*}}) + \frac {\partial \mu (\boldsymbol {x} ^ {*})}{\partial \boldsymbol {x} ^ {*}} \bigg | _ {\boldsymbol {x} ^ {*} = \mu_ {\boldsymbol {x} _ {k} ^ {*}}} ^ {\top} (\boldsymbol {x} ^ {*} - \mu_ {\boldsymbol {x} _ {k} ^ {*}}) \right],= \mu (\mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}) . \tag {A.6}$$

The expectation $\mathbb { E } _ { x _ { k } ^ { * } } \left[ \Sigma ( x _ { k } ^ { * } ) \right]$ , following the derivations in [30, Eq. 18] and [19, Appendix I], is approximated as:

$$\mathbb {E} _ {x _ {k} ^ {*}} \left[ \Sigma (x _ {k} ^ {*}) \right] \approx \mathbb {E} _ {x _ {k} ^ {*}} \left[ \Sigma (\mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}) + \frac {\partial \Sigma (x ^ {*})}{\partial x ^ {*}} \bigg | _ {x ^ {*} = \mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}} ^ {\top} (x ^ {*} - \mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}) \right]= \Sigma (\mu_ {x _ {k} ^ {*}}). \tag {A.8}$$
