# Example A.38: Marginal normal density

Given that $\xi$ and η are jointly, normally distributed with mean

$$
m = \left[ \begin{array}{l} m _ {x} \\ m _ {y} \end{array} \right]
$$

and covariance matrix

$$
P = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} P _ {x} & P _ {x y} \\ P _ {y x} & P _ {y} \end{array} \right]
$$

show that the marginal density of $\xi$ is normal with the following parameters

$$\xi \sim N (m _ {x}, P _ {x}) \tag {A.25}$$
