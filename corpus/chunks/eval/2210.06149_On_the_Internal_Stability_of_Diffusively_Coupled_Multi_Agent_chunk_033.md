$$\operatorname{zdir} _ {\mathrm{i}} (G, \lambda) = \ker G (\lambda) \quad a n d \quad \operatorname{zdir} _ {\mathrm{o}} (G, \lambda) = \ker [ G (\lambda) ] ^ {\top}.$$

Proof. Follows from the relations

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} A - \lambda I & B \\ C & D \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} A - \lambda I & 0 \\ C & G (\lambda) \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c} I & (A - \lambda I) ^ {- 1} B \\ 0 & I \end{array} \right] \\ = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} I & 0 \\ C (A - \lambda I) ^ {- 1} & I \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c c} A - \lambda I & B \\ 0 & G (\lambda) \end{array} \right] \\ \end{array}
$$

and the assumed invertibility of $A - \lambda I$ .
