# IV. EXAMPLES

The software CVX [17] was used to solve the optimization problem (27) in all examples.

Example 4.1: [18] For the system with matrices

$$
A = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 3 & 0 \end{array} \right], B = \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{array} \right] \quad ,

C = \left[ \begin{array}{r r r} {0} & {5} & {- 1} \\ {- 1} & {- 1} & {0} \end{array} \right],
$$

using $Q = \mathrm { d i a g } [ 1 3 0 . 1 ] , R = 1 0 ^ { - 4 }$ , the solution of (27) is $F = [ 6 . 8 9 8 1 8 4 . 9 2 2 4 ]$ .

Example 4.2: [6], [8], [11] For the triplet

$$
A = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c c} - 0. 0 3 6 6 & 0. 0 2 7 1 & 0. 0 1 8 8 & - 0. 4 5 5 5 \\ 0. 0 4 8 2 & - 1. 0 1 0 0 & 0. 0 0 2 4 & - 4. 0 2 0 8 \\ 0. 1 0 0 2 & 0. 3 6 8 1 & - 0. 7 0 7 0 & 1. 4 2 0 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array} \right],

B = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} 0. 4 4 2 2 & 0. 1 7 6 1 \\ 3. 5 4 4 6 & - 7. 5 9 2 2 \\ - 5. 5 2 0 0 & 4. 4 9 0 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right], C = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l l} 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \end{array} \right] \quad ,
$$

using $Q = I _ { 4 } , R = I _ { 2 }$ , (27) yields $F = [ 2 . 8 3 3 4 8 . 8 6 1 8 ]$ .
