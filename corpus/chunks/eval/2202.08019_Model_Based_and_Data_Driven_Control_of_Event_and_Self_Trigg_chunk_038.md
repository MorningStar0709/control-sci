# VI. CONCLUDING REMARKS

In this paper, we proposed data-based event- and selftriggering transmission schemes for discrete-time systems leveraging a novel looped-functional approach. We also developed methods for co-designing the controller gain and the triggering matrix for the discrete-time ETS and STS systems. Finally, a numerical example was presented to corroborate the role of our triggering schemes in saving communication resources, as well as the merits and effectiveness of our co-designing methods.
