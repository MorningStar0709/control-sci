# Cofactor of a Matrix

A cofactor of an element in the ith row and jth column of a matrix A is $(-1)^{i+j}$ times the determinant of the matrix formed by deleting the ith row and jth column. Thus, the determinant given by (A.2.14) can be written in terms of cofactors as

$$
\begin{array}{l} | \mathbf {A} | = a _ {1 1} [ \text { cofactor   of } a _ {1 1} ] + a _ {1 2} [ \text { cofactor   of } a _ {1 2} ] \\ + + a _ {1 3} \left[ \text { cofactor   of } a _ {1 3} \right] \tag {A.2.17} \\ \end{array}
$$
