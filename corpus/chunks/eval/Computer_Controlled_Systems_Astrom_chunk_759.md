# Example 12.14 Integral action

A regulator has integral action if z - 1 divides $R(z)$ . It follows from Theorem 12.5, and the internal-model principle, that this will occur if z - 1 divides both A and B, which means that the model is of the form

$$A _ {1} (q) (q - 1) y (k) = B _ {1} (q) (q - 1) u (k) + C (q) e (k)$$

This means that there is a drifting disturbance.
