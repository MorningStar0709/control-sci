This explains why Algorithms I and II will work in practice in most of the cases. It is, however, important to remark that if the disturbance is a single sinusoid (which violates the hypothesis of broadband disturbance) located in the frequency region where $H ^ { \prime }$ is not SPR, the algorithm may diverge (see Anderson et al. 1986; Ljung and Söderström 1983).

Without doubt, the best approach for relaxing the SPR conditions, is to use Algorithm III (given in (15.53)) instead of Algorithm II. This is motivated by (15.71) and (15.72).
