Remark 6.6. Note that no boundedness assumptions or growth conditions are needed for the vector fields $F ( x , z , t )$ and $G ( x , z , t )$ . In particular, the effect of $\boldsymbol { F } ( \boldsymbol { x } , z , t )$ is canceled by the introduced adaptive signal $\hat { d } ,$ which increases according to (6.22).
