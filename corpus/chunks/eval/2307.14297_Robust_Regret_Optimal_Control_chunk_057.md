# B. Spectral Factorization for Regret Bound

This appendix gives a spectral factorization result tailored for the regret bound in Section II-D. The closed-loop dynamics with $K ^ { o }$ , given in (14), have the form:

$$\hat {x} _ {t + 1} = \hat {A} \hat {x} _ {t} + \hat {B} d _ {t} \tag {14,Revisited}\hat {e} _ {t} = \hat {C} \hat {x} _ {t} + \hat {D} d _ {t},$$

with the state matrices:

$$
\hat {A} := \left[ \begin{array}{c c} \hat {A} _ {1 1} & - B _ {u} K _ {v} \hat {A} _ {1 1} ^ {- \top} \\ 0 & \hat {A} _ {1 1} ^ {- \top} \end{array} \right], \qquad \qquad \hat {B} := \left[ \begin{array}{c} B _ {d} \\ - X B _ {d} \end{array} \right],

\hat {C} := \gamma_ {J} \left[ \begin{array}{c c} C _ {e} - D _ {e u} K _ {x} & - D _ {e u} K _ {v} \hat {A} _ {1 1} ^ {- \top} \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right], \qquad \qquad \hat {D} := \gamma_ {d} \left[ \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ I \end{array} \right].
$$

Here $X \succeq 0$ is the stabilizing solution of the DARE (7):

$$0 = X - A ^ {\top} X A - Q \tag {7,Revisited}+ \left(A ^ {\top} X B _ {u} + S\right) \left(R + B _ {u} ^ {\top} X B _ {u}\right) ^ {- 1} \left(A ^ {\top} X B _ {u} + S\right) ^ {\top}.$$

Let $\hat { P }$ denote these closed-loop dynamics with state, input, and output dimension $2 n _ { x } , n _ { d }$ , and $n _ { \hat { e } }$ . Recall that the regret bound in Equation 13 is $\| \hat { e } \| _ { 2 } ^ { 2 } = \| \hat { P } d \| _ { 2 }$ .
