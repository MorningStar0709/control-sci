$$\left| \beta_ {i 2} \right| \leq \left| c _ {i} \right| \bar {\tau} _ {\max} ^ {N} \left| \int_ {0} ^ {t} e ^ {- \int_ {s} ^ {t} b _ {i \sigma (\xi)} d \xi} b _ {i \sigma (s)} d s \right|, \tag {43}$$

$$\left(1 - e ^ {- \int_ {0} ^ {t} b _ {i \sigma (\xi)} d \xi}\right) \in [ 0, 1), \tag {45}$$

$$\left| \beta_ {i 2} \right| \leq \left| c _ {i} \right| \bar {\tau} _ {\max} ^ {N} \left(1 - e ^ {- \int_ {0} ^ {t} b _ {i \sigma (\xi)} d \xi}\right), \tag {46}$$
