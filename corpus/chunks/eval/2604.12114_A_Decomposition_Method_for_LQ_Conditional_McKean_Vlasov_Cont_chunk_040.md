# Appendix A.1. Proof of Property (iv)

We aim to show that ${ \mathbb E } [ ( x _ { t } - H \bar { x } _ { t } ) ^ { \top } S _ { t } u _ { t } ] = { \mathbb E } [ \check { x } _ { t } ^ { \top } S _ { t } \check { u } _ { t } ] + { \mathbb E } [ \bar { x } _ { t } ^ { \top } \bar { S } _ { t } \bar { u } _ { t } ]$ . To this end, we first express $\mathbb { E } [ \breve { x } _ { t } ^ { \top } S _ { t } \breve { u } _ { t } ]$ as the sum of three terms as in

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {E} [ \breve {x} _ {t} ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \breve {u} _ {t} ] = \mathbb {E} \left[ (x _ {t} - \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} (u _ {t} - \bar {u} _ {t}) \right] \\ = \mathbb {E} \left[ \left(x _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t} + H \bar {x} _ {t} - \bar {x} _ {t}\right) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \left(u _ {t} - \bar {u} _ {t}\right) \right] \tag {A.1} \\ = \mathbb {E} \left[ (x _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} u _ {t} \right] - \underbrace {\mathbb {E} \left[ (x _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t} \right]} _ {D _ {1}} - \underbrace {\mathbb {E} \left[ (\bar {x} _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} (u _ {t} - \bar {u} _ {t}) \right]} _ {D 2}, \\ \end{array}
$$

where

$$
\begin{array}{l} D _ {1} = \mathbb {E} \left[ (x _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t} \right] = \mathbb {E} \left(\mathbb {E} \left[ (x _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t} \mid \mathcal {F} _ {t} ^ {0} \right]\right) \\ \begin{array}{l} = \mathbb {E} \left(\mathbb {E} \left[ (x _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} \mid \mathcal {F} _ {t} ^ {0} \right] S _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t}\right) \\ = \mathbb {E} \left[ (\bar {x} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t} \right] \end{array} \tag {A.2} \\ = \mathbb {E} \left[ \bar {x} _ {t} ^ {\intercal} \left(I _ {n} - H\right) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t} \right] \\ = \mathbb {E} \left[ \bar {x} _ {t} ^ {\intercal} \bar {S} _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t} \right], \\ \end{array}
$$

and

$$
\begin{array}{l} D _ {2} = \mathbb {E} \left[ (\bar {x} _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} (u _ {t} - \bar {u} _ {t}) \right] = \mathbb {E} \left(\mathbb {E} \left[ (\bar {x} _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} (u _ {t} - \bar {u} _ {t}) \mid \mathcal {F} _ {t} ^ {0} \right]\right) \\ = \mathbb {E} \left(\left(\bar {x} _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}\right) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \mathbb {E} \left[ u _ {t} - \bar {u} _ {t} \mid \mathcal {F} _ {t} ^ {0} \right]\right) \tag {A.3} \\ = 0. \\ \end{array}
$$

Hence, (A.1) is expressed as

$$\mathbb {E} [ \check {x} _ {t} ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} \check {u} _ {t} ] = \mathbb {E} \left[ (x _ {t} - H \bar {x} _ {t}) ^ {\intercal} S _ {t} u _ {t} \right] - \mathbb {E} \left[ \bar {x} _ {t} ^ {\intercal} \bar {S} _ {t} \bar {u} _ {t} \right], \tag {A.4}$$

which implies the property (iv).
