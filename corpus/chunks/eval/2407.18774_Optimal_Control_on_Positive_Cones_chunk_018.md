# 4 Conclusions

We have proved a general result on optimal control on positive cones, covering standard linear quadratic control and corresponding results for positive systems as special cases. Of course, many other special cases can be derived by defining other relevant cones, or combinations of the ones above. Hopefully, this will in the future form the basis for a powerful toolbox combining the versatility of linear quadratic control with the rich scalability properties of positive systems and shortest path problems in networks. Preliminary steps in this direction have been taken in [12] and [13].
