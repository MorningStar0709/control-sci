# 6 Conclusion

In this work, we have introduced a new LNMPC designed to track UAVs’ attitude trajectories. By using a contraction condition and the design of a SMC, we proved the feasibility and closed-loop stability of the proposed controller. The results show that our controller outperforms other popular nonlinear controllers in response time, tracking accuracy, and control signal smoothness, especially in challenging situations where significant changes in control effort are required. In addition, the results of SIL tests with the Gazebo simulator show the ability to deploy and apply the proposed controller in the embedded control system of UAVs, which then can be used to replace their built-in PID controller. Our future work will extend the LNMPC for the formation control of multiple UAVs.
