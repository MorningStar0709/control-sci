$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {V (t + 1)}{r (t + 1)} \leq \frac {V (t)}{r (t)} - \frac {1}{r (t + 1)} \left\{\left[ x ^ {T} (t), u ^ {T} (t + 1) \right] M \left[ \begin{array}{c} x (t) \\ u (t + 1) \end{array} \right] + x ^ {T} (t) M _ {0} x (t) \right. \\ + [ \bar {x} ^ {T} (t), \bar {u} ^ {T} (t + 1) ] \bar {M} (t) \left[ \begin{array}{c} \bar {x} (t) \\ \bar {u} (t + 1) \end{array} \right] + \kappa \| u (t + 1) \| ^ {2} \Bigg \} \\ + 2 \frac {\bar {y} (t + 1) ^ {T} \omega (t + 1)}{r (t + 1)} \tag {D.43} \\ \end{array}
$$

Adding in both sides of (D.43):

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {t} \frac {\left[ x ^ {T} (i) , u ^ {T} (i + 1) \right] M \left[ \begin{array}{c} x (i) \\ u (i + 1) \end{array} \right]}{r (i + 1)} + \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {t} \frac {x ^ {T} (t) M _ {0} x (t)}{r (i + 1)} \\ + \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {t} \frac {[ \bar {x} ^ {T} (i) , \bar {u} ^ {T} (i + 1) ] \bar {M} ^ {T} (t) [ \bar {u} (i + 1) ]}{r (i + 1)} + \kappa \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {t} \frac {\| u (i + 1) \| ^ {2}}{r (i + 1)} \tag {D.44} \\ \end{array}
$$

and taking the conditional expectation one gets using (D.40):

$$\mathbf {E} \{T (t + 1) | \mathcal {F} _ {t} \} \leq T (t) + 2 \mathbf {E} \left\{\frac {\bar {y} ^ {T} (t + 1) \omega (t + 1)}{r (t + 1)} | \mathcal {F} _ {t} \right\} \tag {D.45}$$

Under the hypotheses (D.33) and (D.34), it results using Theorem D.1:

$$\operatorname{Prob} \left\{\lim _ {t \rightarrow \infty} T (t) < \infty \right\} = 1 \tag {D.46}$$

Taking into account the structure of T (t) given in (D.40), it results immediately:

$$\lim _ {t \to \infty} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {t} \frac {x ^ {T} (i) M _ {0} x (i)}{r (i + 1)} < \infty ; \quad M _ {0} > 0 \text { a.s. } \tag {D.47}$$

and:

$$\lim _ {t \to \infty} \kappa \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {t} \frac {\| u (i + 1) \| ^ {2}}{r (i + 1)} < \infty ; \quad \kappa > 0 \text { a.s. } \tag {D.48}$$
