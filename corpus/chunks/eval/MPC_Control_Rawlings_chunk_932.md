# Exercise A.49: Distance of an extended state to an extended set

Let $x \in \mathbb { R } ^ { n }$ and X a set of elements in $\mathbb { R } ^ { n }$ , and $u \in \mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ and U a set of elements in $\mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ Denote distances from elements to their respective sets as

$$| x | _ {\mathbb {X}} := \inf _ {y \in \mathbb {X}} | x - y | _ {\alpha} \quad | u | _ {\mathbb {U}} := \inf _ {v \in \mathbb {U}} | u - v | _ {\beta}\left| (x, u) \right| _ {\mathbb {X} \times \mathbb {U}} := \inf _ {(y, v) \in \mathbb {X} \times \mathbb {U}} \left| (x, u) - (y, v) \right| _ {\gamma}$$

Use the norm of the extended state defined in Exercise A.48 to show that

$$\left| (x, u) \right| _ {\mathbb {X} \times \mathbb {U}} = \left| x \right| _ {\mathbb {X}} + \left| u \right| _ {\mathbb {U}}$$
