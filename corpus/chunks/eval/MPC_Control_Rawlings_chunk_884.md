# Exercise A.1: Norms in $\mathbb { R } ^ { n }$

Show that the following three functions are all norms in $\mathbb { R } ^ { n }$

$$
\begin{array}{l} | x | _ {2} := \left(\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} (x ^ {i}) ^ {2}\right) ^ {1 / 2} \\ | x | _ {\infty} := \max \{| x ^ {1} |, | x ^ {2} |, \dots , | x ^ {n} | \} \\ | x | _ {1} := \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} | x ^ {j} | \\ \end{array}
$$

where $x ^ { j }$ denotes the jth component of the vector x.
