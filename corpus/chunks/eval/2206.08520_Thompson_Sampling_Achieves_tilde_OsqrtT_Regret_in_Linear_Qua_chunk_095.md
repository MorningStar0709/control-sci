# F.4. Bounding $R _ { T } ^ { \bf T S }$

Lemma 22 (Bounding $R _ { T } ^ { \bf T S }$ for TSAC) Let $R _ { T } ^ { T S }$ be as defined by (112). Under the event of $E _ { T }$ , we have that

$$\left| R _ {T} ^ {T S} \right| \leq \tilde {O} \left(\sqrt {n} T _ {w} + \mathrm{poly} (n, d, \log (1 / \delta)) \sqrt {T - T _ {w}}\right).$$

with probability at least $1 - 2 \delta$ if $T _ { w } = \omega ( \sqrt { T } \log T )$ for singular $A _ { c , * }$ and $T _ { w } = \omega ( \log T )$ for non-singular $A _ { c , * }$ .

Proof We decompose $R _ { T } ^ { \mathrm { T S } }$ into two pieces as

$$R _ {T} ^ {\mathrm{TS}} = \underbrace {\sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T _ {w}} \left\{J _ {*} (\tilde {\Theta} _ {t} , \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) - J _ {*} (\Theta_ {*} , \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) \right\} \mathbb {1} _ {E _ {t}}} _ {R _ {T w} ^ {\mathrm{TS,exp}}} + \underbrace {\sum_ {t = T _ {w} + 1} ^ {T} \left\{J _ {*} (\tilde {\Theta} _ {t} , \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) - J _ {*} (\Theta_ {*} , \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) \right\} \mathbb {1} _ {E _ {t}}} _ {R _ {T} ^ {\mathrm{TS,noexp}}}$$

Since every sampled system is in set $s ,$ , we have that $\| P ( \tilde { \Theta } _ { t } ) \| _ { F } \leq D$ and therefore

$$
\begin{array}{l} R _ {T _ {w}} ^ {\mathrm{TS}, \mathrm{exp}} \leq \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T _ {w}} \left| J _ {*} (\tilde {\Theta} _ {t}, \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) - J _ {*} (\Theta_ {*}, \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) \right| \mathbb {1} _ {E _ {t}} \\ \leq \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T _ {w}} \left(\left| J _ {*} (\tilde {\Theta} _ {t}, \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) \right| + \left| J _ {*} (\Theta_ {*}, \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} I) \right|\right) (127) \\ \leq \sqrt {n} \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T _ {w}} \left(\| P (\tilde {\Theta} _ {t}) \| _ {F} + \| P (\Theta_ {*}) \| _ {F}\right) \leq 2 \sqrt {n} \sigma_ {w} ^ {2} D T _ {w} (128) \\ \end{array}
$$
