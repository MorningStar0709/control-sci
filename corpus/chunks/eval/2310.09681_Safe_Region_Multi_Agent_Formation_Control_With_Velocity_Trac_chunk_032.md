# Appendix C. Proof of Corollary 1

Proof. Consider the following Lyapunov function

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \underbrace {\frac {1}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} v _ {i} ^ {T} v _ {i}} _ {V _ {1}} + \underbrace {\frac {c _ {1}}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \sum_ {j \in \mathcal {N} _ {i}} \Phi_ {i j}} _ {V _ {2}} + \tag {C.1} \\ c _ {3} \underbrace {\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \mathbb {1} _ {i \in L} \mathbb {1} _ {\Omega^ {-}} \left\| p _ {i} - \mathcal {P} _ {\Omega} (p _ {i}) \right\|} _ {V _ {3}} + \underbrace {\frac {\zeta}{2} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {N} \phi_ {i j} ^ {T} \phi_ {i j}} _ {V _ {4}} \\ \end{array}
$$

By employing analogous reasoning to that presented in Theorem 1, the corollary can be proven. □
