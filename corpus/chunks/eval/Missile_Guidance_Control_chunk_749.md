TERCOM is the only fix-taking system that can operate autonomously in a wartime environment, that has a permanent source database. In particular, the following characteristics should be noted:

• The system is self-contained and provides precision guidance/navigation for:

(a) aircraft   
(b) drones (or UAVs unmanned aerial vehicles)   
(c) cruise missiles   
(d) reentry vehicles.

• TERCOM is applicable to both tactical and strategic systems and operates:

(a) under ECM (electronic countermeasures) conditions   
(b) day/night   
(c) all weather   
(d) low altitude (terrain following)   
(e) high altitude.
