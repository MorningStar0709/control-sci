$$\left\| e _ {z} (t) \right\| = \left\| \bar {F} ^ {t} e _ {\mathrm{ini}} + \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t - 1} \bar {F} ^ {\tau} \bar {G} e _ {v} (t - 1 - \tau) \right\|\leq \left\| \bar {F} \right\| ^ {t} \cdot \left\| e _ {\text { ini }} \right\| + \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t - 1} \left\| \bar {F} ^ {\tau} \right\| \cdot \left\| \bar {G} \right\| \cdot \left\| e _ {v} (t - 1 - \tau) \right\|\leq \left(1 + l _ {\max} \| \bar {G} \|\right) \Delta , \quad \forall t \geq 0.$$

Also, since $\Phi ^ { \dagger }$ has l columns, $\lVert \overline { { \Phi ^ { \dag } } } \rVert = \lVert \lceil \Phi ^ { \dag } / \mathsf { s } _ { 1 } \rfloor \rVert \leq \kappa / \mathsf { s } _ { 1 } +$ $l / 2$ . This leads to

$$\left\| \overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} e _ {z} (t) \right\| \leq \left\| \overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} \right\| \cdot \| e _ {z} (t) \| < \frac {\mathsf {L}}{2}, \tag {F.1}$$

and consequently,

$$\left\| \mathsf {L} \cdot \bar {x} (t) + \overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} e _ {z} (t) \right\| \leq \| \mathsf {L} \cdot \bar {x} (t) \| + \left\| \overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} e _ {z} (t) \right\| < \frac {q}{2}.$$

Since a mod $q = a$ for all $a \in \mathbb { Z }$ such that $| a | < q / 2$ , it is finally obtained from (F.1) that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left\lceil \frac {\overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} \mathrm{Dec} ^ {\prime} (\mathbf {z} ^ {(j)} (t)) \bmod q}{L} \left. \right\rfloor = \left\lceil \frac {\overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} \mathrm{Dec} ^ {\prime} (\mathbf {z} ^ {(j)} (t))}{L} \left. \right\rfloor \\ = \left\lceil \bar {x} (t) + \frac {\overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} e _ {z} (t)}{\mathsf {L}} \left. \right\rfloor = \bar {x} (t) + \left\lceil \frac {\overline {{\Phi^ {\dagger}}} e _ {z} (t)}{\mathsf {L}} \left. \right\rfloor \\ = \bar {x} (t), \\ \end{array}
$$

and this concludes the proof.
