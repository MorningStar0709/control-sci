# B.3 Results on controlling 4-stage and 6-stage OPS environments

We present the training curves (training reward vs. iterations) and testing curves (final pulse energy $P _ { N }$ vs. testing iterations) for the 4-stage OPS environment in Fig. 12 and for the 6-stage OPS environment in Fig. 13. From the figures, it is evident that TD3 and SAC outperform PPO in terms of performance. Moreover, comparing Fig. 12 (4-stage) to Fig. 13 (6-stage), it can be observed that as the stage number increases, the training convergence becomes slower and the final return $P _ { N }$ becomes smaller, particularly in the medium and hard difficulty modes.
