This example has demonstrated the extraordinary power of developing approximate linear models of nonlinear systems and using these LTI models for control system analysis and design. Despite the extreme difference between the LTI and nonlinear models (e.g., compare the LTI transfer function (11.78) with the nonlinear block diagram in Fig. 11.60), the linearization method can produce good results as demonstrated by the closed-loop simulations presented here.
