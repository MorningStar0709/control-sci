# Appendix A: Functions and parameter values of the Hodgkin-Huxley-type model

The functions included to Eqs. (1) are as follows:

$$I _ {C a} (V) = g _ {C a} m _ {\infty} (V) \left(V - V _ {C a}\right), \tag {A1a}I _ {K} (V, n) = g _ {K} n \left(V - V _ {K}\right), \tag {A1b}I _ {S} (V, S) = g _ {S} S \left(V - V _ {K}\right), \tag {A1c}I _ {K 2} (V) = g _ {K 2} p _ {\infty} (V) \left(V - V _ {K}\right), \tag {A1d}\omega_ {\infty} (V) = \left(1 + \exp \frac {V _ {\infty} - V}{\theta_ {\omega}}\right) ^ {- 1}, \quad \omega = m, n, S, \tag {A1e}p _ {\infty} (V) = \left(\exp \frac {V - V _ {p}}{\theta_ {p}} + \exp \frac {V _ {p} - V}{\theta_ {p}}\right) ^ {- 1} \tag {A1f}$$
