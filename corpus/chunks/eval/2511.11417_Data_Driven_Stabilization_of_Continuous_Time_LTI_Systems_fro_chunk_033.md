# D. Proof of Proposition 1

In the following, we use $\Re ( a )$ and $\Im ( a )$ to denote the real and imaginary parts of a complex number $a \in \mathbb { C }$ . Consider the following factorization of $\mathcal { G } _ { v } ( s )$ :

$$\mathcal {G} _ {v} (s) = \frac {\prod_ {i = 1} ^ {n _ {r}} \left(s - \vartheta_ {i}\right) \prod_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {c}} \left(s ^ {2} - 2 \Re (\varphi_ {j}) s + | \varphi_ {j} | ^ {2}\right)}{\prod_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \left(s - \varsigma_ {k}\right)}, \tag {76}$$

where $n _ { r } + 2 n _ { c } = n$ and $\vartheta _ { i } \in \mathbb { R } , \Re ( \varphi _ { j } ) \pm \mathrm { i } \Im ( \varphi _ { j } ) \in \mathbb { C } .$ , and $\varsigma _ { k } \in \mathbb { R } , i \in \{ 1 , . . . , n _ { r } \} , j \in \{ 1 , . . . , n _ { c } \} , k \in \{ 1 , . . . , n \}$ denote, respectively, the real zeros, the complex conjugate zero pairs, and the poles of $\mathcal { G } _ { v } ( s )$ . Therefore, $\mathcal { G } _ { v } ( s )$ is the product of rational functions of the form

$$\mathcal {G} _ {\mathrm{r}} (s) := \frac {s - \vartheta}{s - \varsigma}, \quad \mathcal {G} _ {\mathrm{c}} (s) := \frac {s ^ {2} - 2 \Re (\varphi) s + | \varphi | ^ {2}}{(s - \varsigma_ {1}) (s - \varsigma_ {2})}, \tag {77}$$

where, due to condition (53), it holds that $| \varsigma | \geq | \vartheta |$ and min $\{ | \varsigma _ { 1 } | , | \varsigma _ { 2 } | \} \geq | \varphi |$ . Thus, for all $\omega \in \mathbb R$ , we obtain

$$\left| \mathcal {G} _ {\mathrm{r}} (\mathrm{i} \omega) \right| ^ {2} = \frac {\vartheta^ {2} + \omega^ {2}}{\varsigma^ {2} + \omega^ {2}} \leq 1, \tag {78}$$

and

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \mathcal {G} _ {\mathrm{c}} (\mathrm{i} \omega) \right| ^ {2} = \frac {\left(| \varphi | ^ {2} - \omega^ {2}\right) ^ {2} + \left(2 \Re (\varphi) \omega\right) ^ {2}}{\left(\varsigma_ {1} ^ {2} + \omega^ {2}\right) \left(\varsigma_ {2} ^ {2} + \omega^ {2}\right)} \\ = \frac {(| \varphi | ^ {2} + \omega^ {2}) ^ {2} - (2 \Im (\varphi) \omega) ^ {2}}{(\varsigma_ {1} ^ {2} + \omega^ {2}) (\varsigma_ {2} ^ {2} + \omega^ {2})} \tag {79} \\ \leq \frac {(| \varphi | ^ {2} + \omega^ {2}) ^ {2}}{(\varsigma_ {1} ^ {2} + \omega^ {2}) (\varsigma_ {2} ^ {2} + \omega^ {2})} \leq 1. \\ \end{array}
$$

We conclude the proof by noticing that both the numerator and the denominator of $\mathcal { G } _ { v } ( s )$ are monic and of the same degree, thus

$$\lim _ {\omega \to \infty} | \mathcal {G} _ {v} (\mathrm{i} \omega) | = 1, \tag {80}$$
