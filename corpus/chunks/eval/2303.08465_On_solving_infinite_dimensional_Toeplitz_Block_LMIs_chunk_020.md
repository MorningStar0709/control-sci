# VI. CONCLUSION

In this paper, we provided a novel approach that allows to solve, up to an arbitrarily small error, infinite dimensional TBLMIs and some related convex optimization problems encountered in the analysis and control of dynamical systems in the harmonic framework. The result is based on a well-defined finite dimensional truncated problem that allows to recover the original infinite-dimensional solution up to an arbitrarily small error. This framework is not only useful for robustness and multiobjective optimization issues of LTP systems but also for the analysis and control of more general periodic systems such as periodic polynomial systems.
