$$\operatorname * {l i m s u p} _ {T \to \infty} \frac {1}{T} \sum_ {t = 0} ^ {T - 1} \mathbb {E} w _ {t} ^ {\top} (C _ {i} ^ {\psi} x _ {t} ^ {\psi} + D _ {i} ^ {\psi} w _ {t}) \leq \gamma_ {i}, \quad i = 1, \ldots , N,$$

where $x ^ { \psi }$ is the stacked vector of delayed disturbance inputs

$$
x _ {t} ^ {\psi} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c c} w _ {t - 1} ^ {\top} & w _ {t - 2} ^ {\top} & \dots & w _ {t - l} ^ {\top} \end{array} \right) ^ {\top} \in \mathbb {R} ^ {(l + 1) n _ {w}}.
$$

and $C _ { i } ^ { \psi }$ and $D _ { i } ^ { \psi }$ are matrices that captures the terms $M _ { i } ^ { k }$ in the sense that

$$
C _ {i} ^ {\psi} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c} M _ {i} ^ {1} & \dots & M _ {i} ^ {l} \end{array} \right), \quad D _ {i} ^ {\psi} = M _ {i} ^ {0}.
$$

We emphasize that $x _ { t } ^ { \psi }$ can be recursively defined through an appropriate state-space representation of the form

$$x _ {t + 1} ^ {\psi} = A ^ {\psi} x _ {t} ^ {\psi} + B ^ {\psi} w _ {t}.$$
