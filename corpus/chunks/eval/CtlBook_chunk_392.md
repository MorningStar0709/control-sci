# A.8 Matrix-Vector Quiz

Carry out the following Matrix-Vector multiplications:

1.

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} 2 & 6 \\ 1 & 5 \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 3 \end{array} \right] = X _ {1}
$$

2.

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 3. 1 2 7 & 4. 6 8 & 2. 7 \\ - 7. 2 & 1. 2 6 & 0. 8 7 \\ 2. 6 & 3. 2 5 & - 4. 7 \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 6 \\ - 7 \\ 8 \end{array} \right] = X _ {2}
$$

(you may program this in Python)

3.

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c c} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{array} \right] \left[ \begin{array}{c} r \\ s \\ t \end{array} \right] = X _ {3}
$$

4. Convert the following set of linear equations to matrix form (note the $x _ { i }$ subscripts are not in order!):

$$1 4 x _ {1} - 3 x _ {2} + 1 7 x _ {3} = 52 x _ {2} + 4. 7 x _ {3} - 5. 3 x _ {1} = - 46 x _ {3} + 2. 6 x _ {1} + 6. 8 x _ {2} = 1 2$$
