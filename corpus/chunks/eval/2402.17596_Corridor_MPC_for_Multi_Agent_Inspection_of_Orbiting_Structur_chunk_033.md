The simulations were performed on a 2.3 GHz Dual-Core Intel Core i5 CPU with 8GB of RAM. The simulation considers zonal harmonic terms up to order six and an exponential atmospheric model. The inspectors are 6U-CubeSats with a mass of 10 kg and an omnidirectional propulsion system delivering a maximum thrust of 200 mN (and hence 0.02 ms $^ { - 2 }$ in acceleration). Similar specifications were applied in [6] giving a realistic scenario for the inspection mission. The ISS has a mass of 419400 kg and no actuation capabilities. The reference ISS orbit parameters are obtained from New Horizon Database on 2023-Feb-04 00h:00m:00s.
