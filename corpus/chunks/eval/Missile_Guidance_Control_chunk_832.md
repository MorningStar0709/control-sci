# QF-106:

The QF-106 is a converted, remotely piloted Convair F-106A Delta Dart fighter used for full-scale training or testing. With a service ceiling of 50–55,000 ft, the QF-106 has a range of 575 miles. Its power plant is a 24,500 lb thrust (with afterburning) Pratt & Whitney J75-P-17 turbojet.
