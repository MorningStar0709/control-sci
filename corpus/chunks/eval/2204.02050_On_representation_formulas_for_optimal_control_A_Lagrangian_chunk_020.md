A δ-net of U is constructed as the set of 100 points that are obtained uniformly discretizing U . The initial state $x ( 0 ) = x$ is chosen as the origin. All experiments were performed using a PC with 3.3GHz 14-core i9 CPU and 64 GB RAM. CVXPY on Python 3.8.8 was used to numerically solve the convex optimization problems.
