# Algorithm 5 (Optimal investment with stopping time under SLV models)

1: The algorithm is given by the following recursion for $n = 0 , 1 , \ldots , N - 1$ ,

$$
\begin{array}{l} \widetilde {V} _ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {n} \approx (1 - \gamma h) V _ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {n + 1} + \psi_ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {n + 1} \\ + h \sum_ {k _ {0} = 0} ^ {M _ {0}} \sum_ {k _ {1} = 0} ^ {M _ {1}} \sum_ {k _ {2} = 0} ^ {M _ {2}} V _ {\mathbf {k}} ^ {n + 1} \int_ {\Omega^ {3}} \prod_ {j = 0} ^ {2} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (y _ {j}) \mathcal {L} ^ {\pi_ {n + 1} ^ {*}} \left(\prod_ {i = 0} ^ {2} g _ {k _ {i}} ^ {(i)} (y _ {i})\right) d \mathbf {y}, \tag {3.38} \\ \end{array}
$$

where $\pi _ { n + 1 } ^ { * }$ is given at (3.36). Then we have

$$\widetilde {V} ^ {n} (\mathbf {v}) \approx \sum_ {k _ {0} = 0} ^ {M _ {0}} \sum_ {k _ {1} = 0} ^ {M _ {1}} \sum_ {k _ {2} = 0} ^ {M _ {2}} \widetilde {V} _ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {n} \prod_ {j = 0} ^ {\ell} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (\tilde {v} _ {j} (v _ {j})),
(\psi^ {n} (\mathbf {v}), V ^ {n} (\mathbf {v})) = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \Big (0, \widetilde {V} ^ {n} (\mathbf {v}) - h \psi^ {n} (\mathbf {v}) \Big), & \text { if } \widetilde {V} ^ {n} (\mathbf {v}) - h \psi^ {n + 1} (\mathbf {v}) \geq G, \\ \Big (\frac {G - \widetilde {V} ^ {n} (\mathbf {v})}{h} + \psi^ {n + 1} (\mathbf {v}), G \Big), & \text { otherwise }. \end{array} \right.
$$

Then we can calculate

$$V _ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {n} = \int_ {\Omega^ {3}} V ^ {n} (\hat {\mathbf {v}} (\mathbf {y})) \prod_ {j = 0} ^ {2} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (y _ {j}) d \mathbf {y},\psi_ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {n} = \int_ {\Omega^ {3}} \psi^ {n} (\hat {\mathbf {v}} (\mathbf {y})) \prod_ {j = 0} ^ {2} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (y _ {j}) d \mathbf {y}.$$

The terminal condition

$$V _ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {N} = \int_ {\Omega^ {3}} U (\hat {v} _ {0} (y _ {0})) \prod_ {j = 0} ^ {2} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (y _ {j}) d \mathbf {y}, \quad \psi_ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {N} = 0. \tag {3.39}$$

The integrals in (3.38) and (3.39) can be evaluated by Gauss-Legendre quadrature rules.

2: Finally we obtain the approximation of the value functions

$$V (t _ {n}, \mathbf {v}) \approx \sum_ {k _ {0} = 0} ^ {M _ {0}} \sum_ {k _ {1} = 0} ^ {M _ {1}} \sum_ {k _ {2} = 0} ^ {M _ {2}} V _ {\mathbf {m}} ^ {n} \prod_ {j = 0} ^ {\ell} g _ {m _ {j}} ^ {(j)} (\tilde {v _ {j}} (v _ {j})),$$

and the optimal strategies

$$
\pi^ {*} (t _ {n}, \mathbf {v}) \approx \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \pi_ {n} ^ {*} (\mathbf {v}), & \text { if } \psi^ {n} (\mathbf {v}) = 0, \\ 0, & \text { otherwise }, \end{array} \right.
$$

where $v _ { 0 } = x , v _ { 1 } = s , v _ { 2 } = v .$
