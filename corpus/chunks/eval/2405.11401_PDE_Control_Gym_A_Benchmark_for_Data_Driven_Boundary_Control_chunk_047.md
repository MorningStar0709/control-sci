$$\nabla^ {2} p = \frac {\partial^ {2} p}{\partial x ^ {2}} + \frac {\partial^ {2} p}{\partial y ^ {2}} = - \rho (\frac {\partial^ {2} u}{\partial x ^ {2}} + 2 \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} \frac {\partial v}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial^ {2} v}{\partial y ^ {2}}). \tag {28}$$

Then, we iteratively solve the Poisson equation for pressure:

$$
\begin{array}{l} p _ {i, j} ^ {i t e r + 1} \leftarrow \frac {1}{2 (\Delta x ^ {2} + \Delta y ^ {2})} \left(p _ {i + 1, j} ^ {i t e r} + p _ {i - 1, j} ^ {i t e r}\right) \Delta y ^ {2} + (p _ {i, j + 1} ^ {i t e r} + p _ {i, j - 1} ^ {i t e r}) \Delta x ^ {2}) \\ + \rho (\frac {u _ {i + 1 , j} ^ {*} - u _ {i - 1 , j} ^ {*}}{2 \Delta x} + \frac {v _ {i + 1 , j} ^ {*} - v _ {i - 1 , j} ^ {*}}{2 \Delta y}) \Delta x ^ {2} \Delta y ^ {2}, \tag {29} \\ \end{array}
$$

with iter denotes the iteration of the procedure for solving pressure. We denote the pressure solution as $p ^ { * }$ . Then we can perform the corrector step to compute the velocoty vector at next time step:

$$u _ {i, j} ^ {n + 1} = u _ {i, j} ^ {*} - \Delta t \cdot \frac {1}{\rho} \frac {p _ {i + 1 , j} ^ {*} - p _ {i - 1 , j} ^ {*}}{2 \Delta x}, \tag {30}v _ {i, j} ^ {n + 1} = v _ {i, j} ^ {*} - \Delta t \cdot \frac {1}{\rho} \frac {p _ {i , j + 1} ^ {*} - p _ {i , j - 1} ^ {*}}{\Delta y}. \tag {31}$$

We apply boundary conditions every time step after the predictor step and corrector step.
