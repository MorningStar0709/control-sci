# VI. CONCLUSION

In this paper, we derived the jump law of co-state in optimal control for state-dependent switching systems, based on which we developed an efficient algorithm for solving optimal control problems with hybrid systems. Future work will be extended to the following aspects:

• Investigate the generalized function aspects to derive the jump law of co-state directly from the Euler-Lagrange equation along with the ideas of [19] and [20].   
• Study the co-state in optimal control of state-dependent switched systems with time delay.
