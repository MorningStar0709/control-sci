$$\int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} y ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma \leqslant M _ {2} \int_ {\Omega} | \nabla y | ^ {2} \mathrm{d} x, \quad \forall y \in H _ {\Gamma_ {0}} ^ {1} (\Omega).$$

令 $M_{3} = \max_{\Gamma_1}\{|h|\ell \}, M_{4} = (N - 1)\max_{\Gamma_1}\{\ell \}, M_{5} = \max_{\Gamma_1}\{\langle h,\nu \rangle \}$ . 于是利用Cauchy不等式，对于任意 $\varepsilon >0$ ，有

$$- \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \langle h, \nabla y \rangle \ell \dot {y} d \sigma \leqslant \varepsilon \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} | \nabla y | ^ {2} d \sigma + \frac {M _ {3} ^ {2}}{\varepsilon} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \dot {y} ^ {2} d \sigma , \tag {10.5.12}- \frac {N - 1}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \ell y \dot {y} \mathrm{d} \sigma \leqslant \frac {\varepsilon}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} y ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma + \frac {M _ {4} ^ {2}}{2 \varepsilon} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \dot {y} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma\leqslant \frac {M _ {2} \varepsilon}{2} \int_ {\Omega} | \nabla y | ^ {2} \mathrm{d} x + \frac {M _ {4} ^ {2}}{2 \varepsilon} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \dot {y} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma , \tag {10.5.13}\frac {1}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \dot {y} ^ {2} \langle h, \nu \rangle \mathrm{d} \sigma \leqslant \frac {M _ {5}}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \dot {y} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma , \tag {10.5.14}- \frac {1}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} | \nabla y | ^ {2} \langle h, \nu \rangle \mathrm{d} \sigma \leqslant - \frac {C _ {1}}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} | \nabla y | ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma . \tag {10.5.15}$$

综合式 (10.5.9)\~(10.5.15)，得到

$$\dot {E} _ {\mu} (t) \leqslant - \frac {\mu}{2} \int_ {\Omega} (\dot {y} ^ {2} + | \nabla y | ^ {2}) \mathrm{d} x - (1 - \mu) t \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \ell \dot {y} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma- \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \langle h, \nabla y \rangle \ell \dot {y} d \sigma - \frac {N - 1}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \ell y \dot {y} d \sigma+ \frac {1}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \dot {y} ^ {2} \langle h, \nu \rangle \mathrm{d} \sigma - \frac {1}{2} \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} | \nabla y | ^ {2} \langle h, \nu \rangle \mathrm{d} \sigma\leqslant - \frac {\mu}{2} \int_ {\Omega} \dot {y} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} x - \left(\frac {\mu}{2} - \frac {M _ {2} \varepsilon}{2}\right) \int_ {\Omega} | \nabla y | ^ {2} \mathrm{d} x- \left((1 - \mu) M _ {0} t - \frac {M _ {3} ^ {2}}{\varepsilon} - \frac {M _ {4} ^ {2}}{2 \varepsilon} - \frac {M _ {5}}{2}\right) \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} \dot {y} ^ {2} \mathrm{d} \sigma- \left(\frac {C _ {1}}{2} - \varepsilon\right) \int_ {\Gamma_ {1}} | \nabla y | ^ {2} d \sigma ,$$
