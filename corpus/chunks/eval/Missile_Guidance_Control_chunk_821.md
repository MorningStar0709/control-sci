The air density $\rho$ decreases exponentially with altitude within the isothermal layers. That is,

Layers II, IV, VI:

$$\rho = C _ {1} e ^ {- p H}. \tag {D.10}$$

Layers I, III, V:

$$\rho = C _ {2} \tau^ {- k}, \tag {D.11}$$

where $C _ { 1 } , C _ { 2 } , p$ , and $k$ are constant within a given layer.
