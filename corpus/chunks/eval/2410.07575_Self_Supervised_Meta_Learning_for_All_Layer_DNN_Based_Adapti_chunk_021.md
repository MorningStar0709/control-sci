The PID controller struggles with handling unknown wind dynamics, while the INDI controller is affected by acceleration measurement noise and delays in disturbance prediction. Both SSML-AC and SSML-AC-LL perform well in trajectory tracking; however, SSML-AC achieves more accurate disturbance prediction and lower tracking error (Table I), highlighting the benefits of full-network adaptation.
