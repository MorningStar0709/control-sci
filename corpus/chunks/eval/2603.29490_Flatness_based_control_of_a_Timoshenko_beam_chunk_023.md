# VII. CONCLUDING REMARKS

This paper presents a flatness-based control design approach for hyperbolic systems that employs the HCF, a special state representation that greatly simplifies the design of stabilizing feedback laws. The concept is demonstrated for the classical Timoshenko beam, but can be extended to general hyperbolic systems. Since the HCF derived here constitutes a generalized state representation, a particular point of future research is whether a classical state representation free of input predictions can be obtained through an alternative choice of the HCF state.
