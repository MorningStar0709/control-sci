$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} f _ {q} ^ {(\ell + 1)} (x) - \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} \tilde {f} _ {q} ^ {(\ell + 1)} (x) \right| \leq R \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {\ell}} \left| \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} f _ {j} ^ {(\ell)} - \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} \tilde {f} _ {j} ^ {(\ell)} \right| + 4 (\ell + 1) \eta \pi_ {\ell} ^ {3} R ^ {3 \ell + 2} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {\boldsymbol {n} _ {\ell + 1}} \left| \theta_ {j} - \tilde {\theta} _ {j} \right| \\ \leq n _ {\ell} \cdot 2 \ell (\ell + 1) \eta \pi_ {\ell - 1} ^ {3} R ^ {3 \ell - 1} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {\boldsymbol {n} _ {\ell}} \left| \theta_ {j} - \tilde {\theta} _ {j} \right| + 4 (\ell + 1) \eta \pi_ {\ell} ^ {3} R ^ {3 \ell + 2} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {\boldsymbol {n} _ {\ell + 1}} \left| \theta_ {j} - \tilde {\theta} _ {j} \right| \\ \leq 2 (\ell + 1) (\ell + 2) \pi_ {\ell} ^ {3} R ^ {3 \ell + 2} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {\boldsymbol {n} _ {\ell + 1}} | \theta_ {j} - \tilde {\theta} _ {j} |, \\ \end{array}
$$

which by mathematical induction implies the desired claim (A.9).

(iv) Obtain the final estimate. Last, for $\ell = L$ , direct computation shows

$$\partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} f - \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} \tilde {f} = \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {L - 1}} a _ {1 j} ^ {(L)} \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} f _ {j} ^ {(L - 1)} - \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {L - 1}} \tilde {a} _ {1 j} ^ {(L)} \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} \tilde {f} _ {j} ^ {(L - 1)}$$

Thus, by the estimate (A.9) and Lemma A.3,

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} f - \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} \tilde {f} \right| \leq \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {L - 1}} | a _ {1 j} ^ {(L)} - \tilde {a} _ {1 j} ^ {(L)} | | \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} f _ {j} ^ {(L - 1)} | + \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {L - 1}} | \tilde {a} _ {1 j} ^ {(L)} | \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} f _ {j} ^ {(L - 1)} - \partial_ {x _ {p}} ^ {2} \tilde {f} _ {j} ^ {(L - 1)} | \\ \leq (L - 1) \pi_ {L - 2} R ^ {2 L - 2} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {L - 1}} | a _ {1 j} ^ {(L)} - \tilde {a} _ {1 j} ^ {(L)} | + \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n _ {L - 1}} R \cdot 2 (L - 1) L \eta \pi_ {L - 2} ^ {3} R ^ {3 L - 4} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {\boldsymbol {n} _ {L - 1}} \left| \theta_ {j} - \tilde {\theta} _ {j} \right| \\ \leq 2 (L - 1) L \eta \pi_ {L - 1} ^ {3} R ^ {3 L - 3} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {\boldsymbol {n} _ {L}} \left| \theta_ {j} - \tilde {\theta} _ {j} \right|. \\ \end{array}
$$

This completes the proof of the lemma.
