# VI. CONCLUSION AND FUTURE WORK

The UAV trajectory tracking controllers underwent both simulated and laboratory testing. The evaluation compared controller performance in both control quality and computational complexity. Generally, MPCs showed superior performance, especially in terms of optimality. Even so, ICs can provide comparable performance and serve as a viable alternative to MPCs due to their computational efficiency, especially on devices with limited computing power.

Our research suggests that the eIC is a promising controller for trajectory tracking, displaying faster computation times and enhanced accuracy in comparison to IC. Additionally, parallel versions of ICs have shown further reductions in computation time. Integrating the IC directly into the autopilot of the Crazyflie UAV in the future could potentially advance trajectory tracking. Although, careful consideration of the platform’s limitations would be necessary.
