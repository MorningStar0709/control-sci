# VII. CONCLUSION

In this article we propose a novel distributed adaptive controller to estimate bias in relative position measurements along with guaranteed exact consensus in a network of doubleintegrator systems. It is shown that joint (δ, T )-connectivity and joint non-Bipartite properties of the graph are necessary for bias estimation and consensus. In future work, we seek to explore more general measurement errors and nonlinear agent dynamics. We will also focus on consensus under erroneous relative measurements over directed and time varying communication graphs.
