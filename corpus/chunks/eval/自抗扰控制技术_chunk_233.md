$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \dot {x} _ {1} = - x _ {2} - x _ {3}, & x _ {1} (0) = - 1. 0 \\ \dot {x} _ {2} = x _ {1} + 0. 2 x _ {2} + u, & x _ {2} (0) = - 1. 0 \\ \dot {x} _ {3} = 0. 2 + x _ {3} (x _ {1} - 5. 7), & x _ {3} (0) = 0 \\ y (t) = x _ {1} (t) \end{array} \right.
$$

让此系统的输出 $y(t)$ 来跟踪上述 $v(t)$ 的仿真算法为

(1) 生成被跟踪信号

$$
x _ {1} (0) = - 4. 4 7, x _ {2} (0) = - 0. 5 0 5, x _ {3} (0) = 2 8. 0 2
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x _ {1} = x _ {1} + h ^ {*} 1 0 (x _ {2} - x _ {1}) \\ x _ {2} = x _ {2} + h (2 8 x _ {1} - x _ {2} - x _ {1} x _ {3}) \\ x _ {3} = x _ {3} + h \left(- \frac {8}{3} x _ {3} + x _ {1} x _ {2}\right) \\ v = x _ {1} \end{array} \right.
$$

(2) 生成被跟踪信号的微分信号对象状态扰动的估计

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \mathrm{fh} = \operatorname{fhan} (v _ {1} - v (t), v _ {2}, v _ {0}, h) \\ v _ {1} = v _ {1} + h ^ {*} v _ {2} \\ v _ {2} = v _ {2} + h ^ {*} \mathrm{fh} \\ e = z _ {1} - y, \mathrm{fe} = \operatorname{fal} (e, 0. 5, h), \mathrm{fe} _ {1} = \operatorname{fal} (e, 0. 2 5, h) \\ z _ {1} = z _ {1} + h ^ {*} (z _ {2} - \beta_ {0 1} e) \\ z _ {2} = z _ {2} + h ^ {*} (z _ {3} - \beta_ {0 2} \mathrm{fe} + u) \\ z _ {3} = z _ {3} + h ^ {*} (- \beta_ {0 3} \mathrm{fe} _ {1}) \end{array} \right.
$$

(3) 生成控制信号

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} e _ {1} = v _ {1} - z _ {1}, e _ {2} = v _ {2} - z _ {2} \\ u = - \operatorname{fhan} \left(e _ {1}, c e _ {2}, r, h _ {1}\right) - z _ {3} \end{array} \right.
$$

(4) 驱动被控对象生成新的输出信号

$$
x _ {1} (0) = - 1. 0, x _ {2} (0) = - 1. 0, x _ {3} (0) = 0
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x _ {1} = x _ {1} + h (- x _ {2} - x _ {3}) \\ x _ {2} = x _ {2} + h (x _ {1} + 0. 2 x _ {2} + u) \\ x _ {3} = x _ {3} + h (0. 2 + x _ {3} (x _ {1} - 5. 7)) \\ y = x _ {1} \end{array} \right.
$$

用这个算法,并取参数

$$h = 0. 0 0 1, r _ {0} = 1 0 0 0 0, \beta_ {0 1} = 1 0 0 0, \beta_ {0 2} = 3 0 0 0 0, \beta_ {0 3} = 8 0 0 0 0 0r = 3 0 0 0, c = 3, h _ {1} = 0. 0 8$$
