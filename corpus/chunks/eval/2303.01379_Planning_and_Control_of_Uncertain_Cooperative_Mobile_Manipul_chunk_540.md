Definition A.5. ([12, 319]) The equilibrium point $x = 0$ of (A.1) is

• uniformly stable, if there exists a class  function $\gamma ( \cdot )$ and a positive constant c independent of $t _ { 0 }$ , such that

$$\| x (t) \| \leq \gamma (\| x (t _ {0}) \|), \forall t \geq t _ {0}, \| x (t _ {0}) \| < c, \tag {A.2}$$
