# A.1 Lipschitz Continuous Systems

We start with defining standard results on the existence of solutions of ODEs.

Consider the initial value problem:

$$\dot {x} = h (x, t), x \left(t _ {0}\right) \in \Omega , \tag {A.1}$$

Definition A.1. [375] A solution $x ( t )$ of the initial value problem (A.1) is maximal if it has no proper right extension that is also a solution of (A.1).

$$\| h (x, t) \| \leq C _ {K},\left\| h (x, t) - h (y, t) \right\| \leq L _ {K} \| x - y \|,\forall (x, t), (y, t) \in K.$$
