# Operating Modes

An adaptive controller has at least three operating modes: manual, constant-parameter control, and adaptation. The controllers that are used in constant-parameter mode may be of several types: PID, relay, or a general linear controller. In this mode the controller parameters must also be loaded and stored. Parameter estimation may also be initiated in the constant-parameter mode. Estimation can also be enhanced by introducing extra perturbations. The nature of these perturbations must also be specified.
