# APPENDIX

The details of the linearized matrix $\mathbf { A } ( \mathbf { X } _ { k } , \mathbf { U } _ { k } ) , \mathbf { B } ( \mathbf { X } _ { k } , \mathbf { U } _ { k } )$ are indentifed as:

$$
\mathbf {A} (\mathbf {X} _ {k}, \mathbf {U} _ {k}) = \left( \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {0} _ {6 \times 6} & \mathbf {I} _ {6 \times 6} \\ \mathbf {0} _ {6 \times 6} & \mathbf {A} _ {\mathbf {1}} (\mathbf {X} _ {k}, \mathbf {U} _ {k}) \end{array} \right) \tag {102}
\mathbf {B} (\mathbf {X} _ {k}, \mathbf {U} _ {k}) = \binom {\mathbf {0} _ {6 \times 6}} {\mathbf {B} _ {1} (\mathbf {X} _ {k}, \mathbf {U} _ {k})} \tag {103}$$

where:

$$
\mathbf {A} _ {\mathbf {1}} (\mathbf {X} _ {k}, \mathbf {U} _ {k}) = \left( \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {A} _ {\mathbf {1 1}} & \mathbf {0} _ {3 \times 3} \\ \mathbf {0} _ {3 \times 3} & \mathbf {A} _ {\mathbf {2 2}} \end{array} \right) \tag {104}

\mathbf {B} _ {\mathbf {1}} (\mathbf {X} _ {k}, \mathbf {U} _ {k}) = \left( \begin{array}{c c} \mathbf {B} _ {\mathbf {1 1}} & \mathbf {0} _ {3 \times 3} \\ \mathbf {0} _ {3 \times 1} & \mathbf {B} _ {\mathbf {2 2}} \end{array} \right) \tag {105}
$$

with

$$
\mathbf {A} _ {\mathbf {1 1}} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c} - \frac {K _ {d x}}{m} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & - \frac {K _ {d y}}{m} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & - \frac {K _ {d z}}{m} \end{array} \right) \tag {106}

\mathbf {A} _ {\mathbf {2 2}} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c} 0 & \frac {\dot {\psi} \left(I _ {y y} - I _ {z z}\right)}{I _ {x x}} & \frac {\dot {\theta} \left(I _ {y y} - I _ {z z}\right)}{I _ {x x}} \\ \frac {\dot {\psi} \left(I _ {z z} - I _ {x x}\right)}{I _ {y y}} & 0 & \frac {\dot {\phi} \left(I _ {z z} - I _ {x x}\right)}{I _ {y y}} \\ \frac {\dot {\theta} \left(I _ {x x} - I _ {y y}\right)}{I _ {z z}} & \frac {\dot {\phi} \left(I _ {x x} - I _ {y y}\right)}{I _ {z z}} & 0 \end{array} \right) \tag {107}

\mathbf {B} _ {\mathbf {1 1}} = \left( \begin{array}{c} \frac {\sin (\phi) \sin (\psi) + \cos (\phi) \cos (\psi) \sin (\theta)}{m} \\ \frac {\cos (\phi) \sin (\psi) \sin (\theta) - \cos (\psi) \sin (\phi)}{m} \\ \frac {\cos (\phi) \cos (\theta)}{m} \end{array} \right) \tag {108}

\mathbf {B} _ {\mathbf {2 2}} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c} \frac {1}{I _ {x x}} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac {1}{I _ {y y}} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \frac {1}{I _ {z z}} \end{array} \right). \tag {109}
$$
