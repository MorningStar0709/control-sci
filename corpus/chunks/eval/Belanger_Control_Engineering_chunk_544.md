# ◆ ◆ ◆ REMARK

The conversion from a transfer function to discrete time may be done by MATLAB c2dm.

Once the conversion to discrete time has been carried out, we have a system consisting of interconnected discrete-time blocks. The algebra of block diagrams is exactly the same as in the $s$ -domain, and the closed-loop pulse transfer function is given by the same expression as in continuous time.
