# D.3 Proof of Lemma 5.2

Consider the function $f _ { t ; h } ( \cdot )$ defined in Definition 5.1 for the state estimation problem. Note that the update rule in line 9 of Algorithm 2 is based on $\nabla \tilde { f } _ { t } ( M _ { t } )$ (rather than the true gradient $\nabla f _ { t } ( M _ { t } ) )$ . Now, following similar arguments in the proof of Lemma 4.2 leading up to (C.3) (replacing N with

M and replacing $\nabla f _ { t } ( \cdot )$ with $\nabla \tilde { f } _ { t } ( \cdot ) )$ , one can get that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \left(f _ {t _ {j}; h} (M _ {t _ {j}}) - f _ {t _ {j}; h} (M ^ {\star})\right) \\ \leq \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \left(\nabla f _ {t _ {j}; h} (M _ {t _ {j}}) ^ {\top} (M _ {t _ {j}} - M ^ {\star}) - \frac {\alpha_ {0}}{2} \left\| M _ {t _ {j}} - M ^ {\star} \right\|\right) \\ \leq \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \left(\frac {1}{\eta_ {j + 1}} - \frac {1}{\eta_ {j}} - \alpha_ {0}\right) \left\| M _ {t _ {j}} - M ^ {\star} \right\| + \frac {\left\| M _ {t _ {1}} - M ^ {\star} \right\| ^ {2}}{2 \eta_ {1}} \\ + \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \frac {\eta_ {j}}{2} \left\| \nabla \tilde {f} _ {t _ {j}} (M _ {t _ {j}}) \right\| ^ {2} - \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \langle \nabla \tilde {f} _ {t _ {j}} (M _ {t _ {j}}) - \nabla f _ {t _ {j}; h} (M _ {t _ {j}}), M _ {t _ {j}} - M ^ {\star} \rangle \\ = - \frac {\alpha_ {0}}{4} \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \left\| M _ {t _ {j}} - M ^ {\star} \right\| ^ {2} + \frac {R _ {M} ^ {2} \alpha_ {0}}{2} + \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \frac {\eta_ {j}}{2} \left\| \nabla \tilde {f} _ {t _ {j}} (M _ {t _ {j}}) \right\| ^ {2} \\ - \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \left\langle \varepsilon_ {t _ {j}} ^ {s}, M _ {t _ {j}} - M ^ {\star} \right\rangle - \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \left\langle \tilde {\varepsilon} _ {t _ {j}} ^ {s}, M _ {t _ {j}} - M ^ {\star} \right\rangle , \\ \end{array}
