$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \mathrm{fh} = \text { fhan } (v _ {1} - v (t), v _ {2}, r _ {0}, h _ {0}) \\ v _ {1} = v _ {1} + h v _ {2} \\ v _ {2} = v _ {2} + h \mathrm{fh} \\ e = z _ {1} - y \\ z _ {1} = z _ {1} + h (z _ {2} + b _ {0} u) - \beta_ {0 1} e \\ z _ {2} = z _ {2} - \beta_ {0 2} e \\ e _ {1} = v _ {1} - z _ {1} \\ u = (\beta_ {1} e _ {1} - z _ {2}) / b _ {0} \end{array} \right. \tag {6.3.10}
$$

在时滞对象(6.3.2)中,取采样步长取 h = 0.1s, 对象时间常数取 T = 0.1, 控制算法(6.3.10)中的参数取 $r_{0} = 0.02, \beta_{01} = 1$ , $\beta_{02} = \frac{1}{3h}$ ; 然后根据不同时滞调节 $\beta_{1}, b_{0}$ 所作的仿真结果如图
