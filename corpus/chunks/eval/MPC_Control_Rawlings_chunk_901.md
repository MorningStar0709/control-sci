# Exercise A.18: Sum and max of convex functions are convex

Suppose that $f _ { i } : \mathbb { R } ^ { n }  \mathbb { R } , i = 1 , 2 , \ldots ,$ m are convex. Show that

$$\psi^ {1} (x) := \max _ {i} \{f _ {i} (x) \mid i \in \{1, 2, \dots , m \} \},\psi^ {2} (x) := \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {m} f _ {i} (x)$$

are both convex.
