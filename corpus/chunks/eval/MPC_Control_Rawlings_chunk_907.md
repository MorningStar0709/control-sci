# Exercise A.24: Partitioned positive definite matrices

Consider the partitioned positive definite, symmetric matrix

$$
H = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} H _ {1 1} & H _ {1 2} \\ H _ {2 1} & H _ {2 2} \end{array} \right]
$$

Prove that the following matrices are also positive definite

1. $H _ { 1 1 }$

2. $H _ { 2 2 }$

3. H in which

$$
\overline {{H}} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} H _ {1 1} & - H _ {1 2} \\ - H _ {2 1} & H _ {2 2} \end{array} \right]
$$

4. $H _ { 1 1 } - H _ { 1 2 } H _ { 2 2 } ^ { - 1 } H _ { 2 1 }$ and $H _ { 2 2 } - H _ { 2 1 } H _ { 1 1 } ^ { - 1 } H _ { 1 2 }$
