# H.5 Proof of Lemma 37

proof. By Lemma 39, when starting at $x _ { 0 } = 0$ and using controller $C _ { K } ^ { \mathrm { u n c } }$ we have that

$$\left| x _ {T} \right| \leq \frac {\bar {w}}{\epsilon}. \tag {150}$$

Therefore, we can conclude that (for $W ^ { \prime } = \{ w _ { t } \} _ { t = 0 } ^ { T - 1 } )$

$$\left| \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, 2 T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T) \right|= \left| \frac {T \cdot \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}} , T) + T \cdot \mathbb {E} \left[ \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}} , T , x _ {T}) \right]}{2 T} - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T) \right|= \left| \frac {\mathbb {E} \left[ \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}} , T , x _ {T}) \right]}{2} - \frac {1}{2} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T) \right|= \frac {1}{2 T} \left| \mathbb {E} \left[ T \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T, x _ {T}) \right] - T \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T) \right|= \frac {1}{2 T} \left| \mathbb {E} \left[ \mathbb {E} \left[ T J (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T, x _ {T}, W ^ {\prime}) - T J (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T, 0, W ^ {\prime}) \right. \mid x _ {T} \right] \right|= \frac {1}{2 T} \left| \mathbb {E} \left[ O _ {T} \left(x _ {T} ^ {2}\right) \right] \right| \tag {Lemma39}= O _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T} \mathbb {E} [ x _ {T} ^ {2} ]\right)= O _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T} \mathbb {E} \left[ \frac {\bar {w} ^ {2}}{\epsilon^ {2}} \right]\right) \tag {Equation(150)}= O _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T}\right).$$

Furthermore, we have that

$$
\begin{array}{l} | \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}) | = \left| \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {\infty} \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, 2 ^ {i} T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, 2 ^ {i + 1} T) \right| \\ \leq \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {\infty} \left| \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\text { unc }}, 2 ^ {i} T) - \bar {J} (\theta , C _ {K} ^ {\text { unc }}, 2 ^ {i + 1} T) \right| \\ = \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {\infty} O _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T 2 ^ {i}}\right) \\ = O _ {T} \left(\frac {1}{T}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$
