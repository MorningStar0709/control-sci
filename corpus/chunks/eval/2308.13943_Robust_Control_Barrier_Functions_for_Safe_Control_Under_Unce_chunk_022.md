# V. CONCLUSIONS

This paper studies a novel controller design, called ESOR-QP, combining an ESO with a CBF for provably safe control of uncertain dynamics without access to full state. Compared with state-of-the-art observer-based CBF-QP, we succeeds in obtaining a tighter estimation error bound to mitigate overconservatism. The comparisons in a cruise control system and a Segway robot validate the efficacy of our approach. Our future work includes the improvement of the nominal control law to minimize the interventions of QP-CBF and the testing in more complex robotic systems.
