# G Regret Analysis

With the estimation error bounds and the probability of the success event in place, we are now ready to synthesize the regret bounds for the three settings under consideration: (i) no intra-cluster heterogeneity, (ii) intra-cluster heterogeneity, and (iii) the presence of adversarial systems. In this section, we leverage the adapted regret bounds from Lee et al. [2023], revisited in Lemmas D.2, D.3, and D.4, and derive conditions on the exploration sequence $\{ \sigma _ { k } \} _ { k }$ and the epoch length $\tau _ { k }$ (i.e., exploitation phase) that ensure a reduction of the leading term in the regret proportional to the number of honest systems participating in the collaboration.
