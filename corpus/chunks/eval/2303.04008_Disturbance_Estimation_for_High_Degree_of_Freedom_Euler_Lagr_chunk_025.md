# 3.2 Experiment 2: Physical Contacts on the Robot

The experimental results are shown in Fig. 5. To avoid redundancy, we only display the results for $4 5 \leq t \leq 7 5$ .

Fig. 5 reveals an accurate estimation result of the proposed method for the physical-contact torques, which is comparable to the measured values. It is also noticed that the estimated torques show a lower noise level than the sensory measurement, due to the filtering property of the observer. Therefore, from the results of the two experiments, we summarize that the proposed observer-based method provides accurate estimation results for external disturbances and applies to practical problems, such as contact-force estimation.
