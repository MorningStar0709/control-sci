# VIII. CONCLUSION AND FUTURE WORK

We proposed k-hop Prescribed Performance Observers that enable each agent to estimate the state and input of agents up to k hops away while ensuring predefined transient and steady-state performance. The proposed solution is robust to bounded external disturbance and do not require global knowledge of the network. Furthermore, we proved that under set-ISS condition of the feedback control law, the state estimates can be used in the controller to drive the agents toward the team objective.

Future work will address observer design for time-varying and directed graphs.
