# Vector

A vector x, generally considered as a column vector, is an arrangement of n elements in a column as

$$
\mathbf {x} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} x _ {1} \\ x _ {2} \\ \cdot \\ \cdot \\ \cdot \\ x _ {n} \end{array} \right], \tag {A.1.1}
$$

. The number $n$ is also referred to as order, size or dimensions of the vector. We can also write the vector $\mathbf{x}$ as

$$\mathbf {x} = [ x _ {1} x _ {2} \dots . x _ {n} ] ^ {\prime} \tag {A.1.2}$$

where, ' denotes the transpose as defined below.
