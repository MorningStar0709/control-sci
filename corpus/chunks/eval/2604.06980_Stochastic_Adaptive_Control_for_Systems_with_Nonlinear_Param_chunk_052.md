$$
\begin{array}{l} V _ {t} \leq V _ {0} - \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} d _ {\tau} ^ {2} t r [ (\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}) ^ {\top} \phi_ {\tau} \phi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} (\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}) ] \\ + 2 \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} a _ {\tau} d _ {\tau} ^ {2} \phi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} P _ {\tau} \phi_ {\tau} t r [ \psi_ {\tau} w _ {\tau + 1} ^ {\top} ] \\ - 2 \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} d _ {\tau} t r [ (\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}) ^ {\top} \phi_ {\tau} w _ {\tau + 1} ^ {\top} ] \\ + \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 2} d _ {\tau} ^ {2} \phi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} P _ {\tau + 1} \phi_ {\tau} t r [ w _ {\tau + 1} w _ {\tau + 1} ^ {\top} ]. \tag {F.9} \\ \end{array}
$$

We now analyze the last three terms on the right-hand side (RHS) of (F.9). For the third term on the RHS of (F.9), by the martingale convergence theorem (Theorem 2.8 in Chen and Guo (1991)) and Assumption 2, we have

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} d _ {\tau} t r [ (\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}) ^ {\top} \phi_ {\tau} w _ {\tau + 1} ^ {\top} ] \\ = o \left(\sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} d _ {\tau} ^ {2} t r \left[ \left(\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}\right) ^ {\top} \phi_ {\tau} \phi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} \left(\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}\right) \right]\right), a. s. \tag {F.10} \\ \end{array}
$$

For the fourth term, note that $a _ { \tau } d _ { \tau } ^ { 2 } \phi _ { \tau } ^ { \top } P _ { \tau } \phi _ { \tau } \leq \mathrm { ~ \dot ~ { 1 } ~ }$ and $t r [ \psi _ { \tau } \psi _ { \tau } ^ { \top } ] \leq \overline { { g } } _ { \tau } ^ { 2 } t r [ ( \theta ^ { * } - \hat { \theta } _ { \tau } ) ^ { \top } \phi _ { \tau } \phi _ { \tau } ^ { \top } ( \theta ^ { * } - \hat { \theta } _ { \tau } ) ]$ ]. Then, again applying Theorem 2.8 in Chen and Guo (1991) together with (A.6) and (F.6), we obtain

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} a _ {\tau} d _ {\tau} ^ {2} \phi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} P _ {\tau} \phi_ {\tau} t r [ \psi_ {\tau} w _ {\tau + 1} ^ {\top} ] \\ = o \left(\sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} (\mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} a _ {\tau} d _ {\tau} ^ {2} \phi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} P _ {\tau} \phi_ {\tau}) ^ {2} t r [ \psi_ {\tau} \psi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} ]\right) \\ = o \left(\sum_ {\tau = 0} ^ {t} \mu_ {\tau} ^ {- 1} d _ {\tau} ^ {2} t r [ (\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}) ^ {\top} \phi_ {\tau} \phi_ {\tau} ^ {\top} (\theta^ {*} - \hat {\theta} _ {\tau}) ]\right), a. s. \\ \end{array}
