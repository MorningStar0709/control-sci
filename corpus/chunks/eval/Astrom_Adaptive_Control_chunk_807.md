# 13.6 FUTURE TRENDS

In this section we speculate on open research issues and the future of adaptive control. One interesting aspect of adaptive control is that it may be viewed as an automation of the modeling and design of control systems. To apply a technique automatically, it is necessary to have a very clear understanding of the conditions under which it can be applied. Ideally, this understanding should be formalized. Research on adaptive control will thus sharpen the understanding of control and parameter estimation.
