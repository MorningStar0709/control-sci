Supplementary Figure S1. Selecting the highest performing policy parameters during training. During training of the three policies, the average return from the policy was continually monitored with a rolling average of the last 100 episodes. Whenever the average return during the last 100 episodes was higher than at any point previously recorded during training, the parameters (i.e., the weights and biases) of the actor network, ????, were saved. For evaluating the performance of the policy after learning, the highest performing parameters saved during the training session were used, rather than the policy parameters of the network at the end of the training session. This was done because we sometimes observed a decrease in the performance of the policy at the end of training.
