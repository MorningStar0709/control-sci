One can remark a strong attenuation of the disturbances (larger than 45 dB). Note that there exists a significant measurement noise at 50 Hz (power network) which is not amplified in closed loop. Harmonics of the disturbances are present in open-loop operation but they disappear in closed-loop operation since the fundamental frequencies are strongly attenuated.

Time-domain results obtained with direct adaptation scheme in “adaptive” operation regime for step changes of the frequency of the disturbances are shown in Fig. 14.8. The disturbances are applied at 1 s (the loop has already been closed) and step changes of their frequencies occur every 3 s. Figure 14.9 shows the corresponding evolution of the parameters of the polynomial Q. The convergence of the output requires less than 0.4 s in the worst case.
