# Exercise A.37: Maximum of n normally distributed random variables

Given n independent, identically distributed normal random variables, $\xi _ { 1 } , \xi _ { 2 } , \ldots , \xi _ { n }$ and the random variable defined by the maximum operator

$$\eta = \max (\xi_ {1}, \xi_ {2}, \dots \xi_ {n})$$

(a) Derive a formula for $\eta ^ { \prime } s$ density.

(b) Plot $p _ { \eta }$ for $\xi _ { i } \sim N ( 0 , 1 )$ and $n = 1 , 2 , \ldots 5$ . Describe the trend in $p _ { \eta }$ as n increases.
