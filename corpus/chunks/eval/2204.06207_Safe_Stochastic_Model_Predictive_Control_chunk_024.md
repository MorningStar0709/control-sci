# VI. CONCLUSION

The proposed safe SMPC algorithm offers the possibility to combine optimistic SMPC planning with a safety guarantee. In addition, recursive feasibility and stability is guaranteed, without the need of an SMPC terminal constraint.

The proposed algorithm is not limited to SMPC. Instead of using SMPC, other controllers, e.g., learning-based methods, can be used. This would allow ensuring safety and stability for learning-based controllers.
