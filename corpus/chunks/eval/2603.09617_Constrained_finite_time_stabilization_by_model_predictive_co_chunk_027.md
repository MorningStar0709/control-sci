# APPENDIX

In this appendix, the solution to the optimization (24) in Step 7 of the proposed finite-time MPC (Algorithm 1) is explained.

At each time step k, update $x ( 0 | k ) = x ( k )$ .

The predictive state sequence can be expressed by

$$X (k) = F x (0 | k) + \Phi U (k),$$

where X(k) and U(k) are predictive state and control defined in (1) and (2), respectively. The matrices F and Φ can be calculated by

$$
F = \left[ \begin{array}{c} A \\ A ^ {2} \\ \vdots \\ A ^ {N} \end{array} \right],   \Phi = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c c} B & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ A B & B & \dots & \vdots \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & 0 \\ A ^ {N - 1} B & A ^ {N - 2} B & \dots & B \end{array} \right].
$$

The cost function can be rewritten by

$$
\begin{array}{l} J = \sum_ {i = n} ^ {N - 1} \| x (i | k) \| _ {Q} ^ {2} + \| u (i | k) \| _ {R} ^ {2} + \| x (N | k) \| _ {P} ^ {2} \\ = X (k) ^ {T} \mathscr {Q} X (k) + U (k) ^ {T} \mathscr {R} U (k) \\ = x (0 | k) ^ {T} F ^ {T} \mathscr {Q} F x (0 | k) + 2 x (0 | k) ^ {T} F ^ {T} \mathscr {Q} \Phi U (k) \\ + U (k) ^ {T} (\Phi^ {T} \mathscr {Q} \Phi + \mathscr {R}) U (k), \\ \end{array}
$$

where

$$\mathcal {Q} = \operatorname{diag} [ \underbrace {0 , \cdots , 0} _ {n - 1}, Q, \dots , Q, P ]$$

and

$$\mathcal {R} = \operatorname{diag} [ \underbrace {0 , \cdots , 0} _ {n - 1}, R, \dots , R ],$$

implying that the cost function is quadratic with respect to U (k).

It then follows that the constrained optimization (24) can then be solved by using MATLAB function “quadprog”.
