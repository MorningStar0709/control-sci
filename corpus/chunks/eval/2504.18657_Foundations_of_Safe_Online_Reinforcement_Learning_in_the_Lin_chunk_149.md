$$\mathbb {E} [ (T - T _ {0}) \cdot J (\theta^ {*}, C _ {F _ {\mathrm{opt}} (\hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}})} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T - T _ {0}, 0, \{w _ {t} \} _ {t = T _ {0}} ^ {T - 1}) ] = (T - T _ {0}) \cdot \bar {J} (\theta^ {*}, C _ {F _ {\mathrm{opt}} (\hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}})} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T - T _ {0}),$$

taking $\epsilon = \sqrt { T } c \log ( T )$ gives conditional on $E _ { 2 } ^ { 0 }$ ,

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {P} \left(\left| (T - T _ {0}) \cdot J (\theta^ {*}, C _ {F _ {\mathrm{opt}} (\hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}})} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T - T _ {0}, 0, \{w _ {t} \} _ {t = T _ {0}} ^ {T - 1}) - T - T _ {0}\right) \cdot \bar {J} (\theta^ {*}, C _ {F _ {\mathrm{opt}} (\hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}})} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T - T _ {0}) \right| \geq \sqrt {T} c \log (T) \left| \hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}}\right) \\ = o _ {T} (1 / T). \tag {145} \\ \end{array}
$$

Define

$$E _ {\mathrm{P12}} := \left\{| (T - T _ {0}) \cdot J (\theta^ {*}, C _ {F _ {\mathrm{opt}} (\hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}})} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T - T _ {0}, 0, \{w _ {t} \} _ {t = T _ {0}} ^ {T - 1}) - T - T _ {0}) \cdot \bar {J} (\theta^ {*}, C _ {F _ {\mathrm{opt}} (\hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}})} ^ {\mathrm{unc}}, T - T _ {0}) | < \sqrt {T} c \log (T) \right\}.$$

By the law of total expectation, Equation (145) implies that

$$\mathbb {P} (E _ {\mathrm{P12}} \mid E _ {2} ^ {0}) = \mathbb {E} [ \mathbb {P} (E _ {\mathrm{P12}} \mid \hat {\theta} _ {\mathrm{wu}}, E _ {2} ^ {0}) \mid E _ {2} ^ {0} ] \geq 1 - o _ {T} (1 / T).$$

Because $\mathbb { P } ( E _ { 2 } ^ { 0 } ) \geq \mathbb { P } ( E ) = 1 - o _ { T } ( 1 / T )$ , we therefore have that

$$\mathbb {P} (E _ {\mathrm{P12}}) \geq \mathbb {P} (E _ {\mathrm{P12}} \mid E _ {2} ^ {0}) \mathbb {P} (E _ {2} ^ {0}) = 1 - o _ {T} (1 / T)$$

as desired.
