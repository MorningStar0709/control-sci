$$\partial_ {\alpha} f _ {\ell} (\alpha^ {*}) = 0, \quad \forall \ell \{1,..., L \}. \tag {57}$$

Then by (57) and (A4) together with (50), it follows that (54) and (55) hold. ✷

Remark 2. Throughout this paper, whenever $\alpha ^ { * } \in ( 0 , 1 )$ is a critical (i.e. locally maximal or minimal) interior point of $J ( u ^ { \alpha ^ { * } } , z )$ it is assumed that

$$\frac {\partial J (u ^ {\alpha^ {*}} , z)}{\partial \alpha} = 0, \tag {58}$$

which requires that differentiation with respect to $\alpha \in$ (0, 1) is meaningful within the LQG-GMFG framework introduced in Foguen Tchuendom et al. (2021). To justify this, differential calculus for GMFGs with respect to node parameterization is made rigorous via the formulation of embedded vertexon graphons in compact subsets of $R ^ { d }$ , for some $d \geq 1$ , in Caines (2022). The further development of this topic is a future direction of the work in the current paper.
