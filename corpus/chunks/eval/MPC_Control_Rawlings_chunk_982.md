# C.2 Optimality Conditions

In this section we obtain optimality conditions for problems of the form

$$f ^ {0} = \inf _ {u} \{f (u) \mid u \in U \}$$

In these problems, $u \in \mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ is the decision variable, $f ( u )$ the cost to be minimized by appropriate choice of uand $U \subset \mathbb { R } ^ { m }$ the constraint set. The value of the problem is $f ^ { 0 }$ . Some readers may wish to read only Section C.2.2, which deals with convex optimization problems and Section C.2.3 which deals with convex optimization problems in which the constraint set U is polyhedral. These sections require some knowledge of tangent and normal cones discussed in Section C.2.1; Proposition C.7 in particular derives the normal cone for the case when U is convex.
