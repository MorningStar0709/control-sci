# 18.3 Notes and References

The model validation problems are discussed in Smith and Doyle [1992] in the frequency domain; in Poolla, Khargonekar, Tikku, Krause, Nagpal [1994 in the discrete time domain (on which Section 18.1 is based); and in Rangan and Poolla [1996] and Smith and Dullerud [1996] in the continuous time domain. See also Davis [1995] and Chen and Wang [1996]. The mixed µ problems are discussed in detail in Young [1993] (on which Section 18.2 is based), Fan, Tits, and Doyle [1991], Packard and Pandey [1993], and references therein.
