# A. Proof of Proposition 27

Proof: Under the full state feedback control law $u = K x ,$ , the closed-loop dynamics are given by

$$\dot {x} = - x + \Psi (W x + B K x) = - x + \Psi (W _ {c l} x), \tag {87}$$

where $W _ { c l } = W + B K$ . For a given contraction rate $c \in ( 0 , 1 ] .$ , the contraction condition (25) for $W _ { c l }$ is

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} - 2 (1 - c) P & P + (Q W _ {c l}) ^ {\top} \\ P + Q W _ {c l} & - 2 Q \end{array} \right] \preceq 0, \tag {88}
$$

where P ≻ 0 is a symmetric matrix and $Q \ \succ \ 0$ is a diagonal matrix. To convert this expression into an LMI, we pre- and post-multiply (88) by the block diagonal matrix diag $( P ^ { - 1 } , Q ^ { \bar { - } 1 } )$ . This yields the equivalent inequality:

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} - 2 (1 - c) P ^ {- 1} & Q ^ {- 1} + P ^ {- 1} W _ {c l} ^ {\top} \\ Q ^ {- 1} + W _ {c l} P ^ {- 1} & - 2 Q ^ {- 1} \end{array} \right] \preceq 0. \tag {89}
$$

Applying the change of variables $X = P ^ { - 1 }$ and $D = Q ^ { - 1 }$ , and substituting $W _ { c l } = W + B K$ , the (2, 1) block becomes

$$D + W _ {c l} X = D + W X + B K X. \tag {90}$$

Defining the variable Y = KX, the transformed inequality is rendered into an LMI in the variables (X, D, Y ), given by,

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} - 2 (1 - c) X & D + X W ^ {\top} + Y ^ {\top} B ^ {\top} \\ D + W X + B Y & - 2 D \end{array} \right] \preceq 0. \tag {91}
$$

The original P and Q matrices may be recovered via $P = X ^ { - 1 }$ and $Q = D ^ { - 1 }$ , and the stabilizing state feedback gain is uniquely recovered via K = Y X−1. $K = Y X ^ { - 1 }$
