# B. Proof of Proposition 30

Proof: We begin by noting that substituting ${ \hat { y } } = C { \hat { x } }$ into the observer dynamics (55) yields an FRNN with synaptic matrix $W _ { o b s } = W - L C$ . Writing (25) for $W _ { o b s }$ , substituting $M = Q L$ , we obtain,

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c} - 2 (1 - c) P & P + W ^ {\top} Q - C ^ {\top} M ^ {\top} \\ P + Q W - M C & - 2 Q \end{array} \right] \preceq 0. \tag {92}
$$

This is an LMI jointly in $P , Q , M$ , and the gain matrix L may be recovered by computing $Q ^ { - 1 } M$ .
