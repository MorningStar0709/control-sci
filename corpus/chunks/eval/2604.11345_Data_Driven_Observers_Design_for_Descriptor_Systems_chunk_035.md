$$
\bar {\Sigma} _ {X _ {p}} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c c} 0. 1 5 0 1 & - 0. 0 3 3 4 & - 0. 3 3 0 0 \\ 0. 0 7 4 3 & 0. 1 3 4 3 & - 0. 0 9 6 1 \\ - 0. 1 5 0 1 & 0. 0 3 3 4 & 0. 3 3 0 0 \end{array} \right],
$$

For validation, the known input is chosen as $u ( k ) = 4 \sin ( k )$ , while the unknown input is generated randomly from $( - 1 , 1 )$ .
