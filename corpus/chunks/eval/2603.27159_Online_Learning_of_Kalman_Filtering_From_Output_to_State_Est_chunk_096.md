$$\sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \big (f _ {t _ {j}} (M _ {t _ {j}}) - f _ {t _ {j}} (M ^ {\star}) \big) = \sum_ {t = \tau} ^ {T - 1} \big (f _ {t} (M _ {t}) - f _ {t} (M ^ {\star}) \big) \theta_ {t}.$$

Taking the expectation $\mathbb { E } _ { b } [ \cdot ]$ with respect to $\{ b _ { i } \} _ { i \ge 0 }$ sampled in line 3 of Algorithm 2, we obtain that

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {E} _ {b} \left[ \sum_ {t _ {j} \in S ^ {\prime}} \left(f _ {t _ {j}} \left(M _ {t _ {j}}\right) - f _ {t _ {j}} \left(M ^ {\star}\right)\right) \right] = \mathbb {E} _ {b} \left[ \sum_ {t = \tau} ^ {T - 1} \left(f _ {t} \left(M _ {t}\right) - f _ {t} \left(M ^ {\star}\right)\right) \theta_ {t} \right] \\ \stackrel {(a)} {=} \sum_ {t = \tau} ^ {T - 1} \mathbb {E} _ {b} \left[ f _ {t} (M _ {t}) - f _ {t} (M ^ {\star}) \right] \mathbb {E} _ {b} [ \theta_ {t} ] \\ \stackrel {(b)} {=} \mathbb {E} _ {b} \left[ \sum_ {t = \tau} ^ {T - 1} \left(f _ {t} (M _ {t}) - f _ {t} (M ^ {\star})\right) \right] \frac {1}{\tau}. \tag {D.15} \\ \end{array}
