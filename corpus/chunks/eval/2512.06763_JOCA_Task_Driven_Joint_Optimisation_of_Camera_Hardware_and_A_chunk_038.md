# B.1. Camera Hardware

We use a population size of 10 over 35 generations, with 50% of the population generating offspring through crossover and mutation. A uniform crossover process is applied, where each parameter of the offspring is randomly selected from one of the parents. The mutation process multiplies each parameter by a random factor between 0.8 and 1.2. Initial camera parameters are randomly sampled from their respective feasible ranges. These hyperparameters and implementation choices follow the recommendations of [53], which optimises a similar number of parameters in its experiments.
