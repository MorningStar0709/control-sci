Proposition 7 (Concentration independent of system dimension n). Under the same hypothesis, we have

$$\mathbb {P} \left[ | \widehat {J} _ {T} (L) - J _ {T} (L) | \leq s \right] \geq 1 - 2 T \exp \left[ \frac {- M s ^ {2} / 2}{\bar {\mu} _ {L} ^ {2} T ^ {2} + 2 \bar {\mu} _ {L} T s / 3} \right],$$

and

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {P} \left[ \| \nabla \widehat {J} _ {T} (L) - \nabla J _ {T} (L) \| \leq s \right] \geq 1 - 2 T \exp \left[ \frac {- M s ^ {2} / 2}{\bar {\nu} _ {L} ^ {2} T ^ {2} + 2 \bar {\nu} _ {L} T s / 3} \right] \\ - 2 T \exp \left[ \frac {- M s ^ {2} / 2}{\kappa_ {\omega} ^ {2} \bar {\mu} _ {L} ^ {2} T ^ {2} + 2 \kappa_ {\omega} \bar {\mu} _ {L} T s / 3} \right] \\ \end{array}
$$

where

$$
\begin{array}{l} \bar {\mu} _ {L} := \frac {C _ {L} ^ {2} \| H ^ {\intercal} H \| _ {*}}{1 - \rho (A _ {L})} \kappa_ {L} ^ {2} \\ \bar {\nu} _ {L} := \frac {\left[ 2 C _ {L} + 4 C _ {L} ^ {3} \rho (A _ {L}) ^ {3 / 2} \right] \| H \| \| H ^ {\intercal} H \| _ {*} [ 1 - \rho (A _ {L}) ] ^ {2}}{\kappa_ {L} ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

Proof of Proposition 7. Similar to the previous proof, we have

$$\widehat {J} _ {T} (L) - J _ {T} (L) = \mathrm{tr} \left[ \left(\mathcal {Z} _ {L} - \mathbb {E} \left[ \mathcal {Z} _ {L} \right]\right) \mathcal {A} _ {L} ^ {\intercal} H ^ {\intercal} H \mathcal {A} _ {L} \right],$$

and thus, by (12) we obtain

$$\left| \widehat {J} _ {T} (L) - J _ {T} (L) \right| \leq \| \left(\mathcal {Z} _ {L} - \mathbb {E} [ \mathcal {Z} _ {L} ]\right) \| \| \mathcal {A} _ {L} ^ {\intercal} H ^ {\intercal} H \mathcal {A} _ {L} \| _ {*}. \tag {36}$$

Next, we consider the symmetric random matrix $\left( \mathcal { Z } _ { L } - \mathbb { E } \left[ \mathcal { Z } _ { L } \right] \right)$ and recall that $\| \xi ( t ) - L \omega ( t ) \| \le \kappa _ { L }$ almost surely; thus

$$\| \mathcal {X} _ {L} \| = \| \vec {\eta} _ {L} \| ^ {2} \leq \kappa_ {L} ^ {2} T ^ {2}.$$

It then follows that

$$\left\| \mathbb {E} \left[ \mathcal {X} _ {L} ^ {2} \right] \right\| \leq \mathbb {E} \left[ \| \mathcal {X} _ {L} \| ^ {2} \right] \leq \kappa_ {L} ^ {4} T ^ {4}.$$

$$\mathbb {P} \left[ \| \left(\mathcal {Z} _ {L} - \mathbb {E} \left[ \mathcal {Z} _ {L} \right]\right) \| \geq t \right] \leq 2 T \exp \left[ \frac {- M t ^ {2} / 2}{\kappa_ {L} ^ {4} T ^ {4} + 2 \kappa_ {L} ^ {2} T ^ {2} t / 3} \right] \tag {37}$$
