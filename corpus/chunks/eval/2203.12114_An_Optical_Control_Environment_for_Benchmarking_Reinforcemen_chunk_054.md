# B.4 Rendering the controlling results on OPS environment

Figure 14 depicts the pulse trains on a 5-stage hard mode OPS system controlled by TD3, starting from a random initial state. It is evident from the figure that the TD3 algorithm is capable of attaining a maximum power within 40 iterations. For a more comprehensive visualization, please refer to supplemental video $2 ~ ^ { 3 }$ .
