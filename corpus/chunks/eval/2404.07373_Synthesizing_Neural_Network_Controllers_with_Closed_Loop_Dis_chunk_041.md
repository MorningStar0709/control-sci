# B Expansion of Closed-Loop System

This section includes the expansion of the feedback system of plant and controller, described in Section 4.

$$
A = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} A _ {p} + B _ {p u} D _ {k u y} C _ {p y} & B _ {p u} C _ {k u} \\ B _ {k y} C _ {p y} & A _ {k} \end{array} \right]

B _ {w} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} B _ {p w} + B _ {p u} D _ {k u y} D _ {p y w} & B _ {p u} D _ {k u w} \\ B _ {k y} D _ {p y w} & B _ {k w} \end{array} \right]

B _ {d} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} B _ {p d} + B _ {p u} D _ {k u y} D _ {p y d} \\ B _ {k y} D _ {p y d} \end{array} \right]

C _ {v} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} C _ {p v} + D _ {p v u} D _ {k u y} C _ {p y} & D _ {p v u} C _ {k u} \\ D _ {k v y} C _ {p y} & C _ {k v} \end{array} \right]

D _ {v w} = \left[ \begin{array}{c c} D _ {p v w} + D _ {p v u} D _ {k u y} D _ {p y w} & D _ {p v u} D _ {k u w} \\ D _ {k v y} D _ {p y w} & D _ {k v w} \end{array} \right]

D _ {v d} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} D _ {p v d} + D _ {p v u} D _ {k u y} D _ {p y d} \\ D _ {k v y} D _ {p y d} \end{array} \right]
C _ {e} = \left[ C _ {p e} + D _ {p e u} D _ {k u y} C _ {p y} D _ {p e u} C _ {k u} \right]D _ {e w} = \left[ D _ {p e w} + D _ {p e u} D _ {k u y} D _ {p y w} D _ {p e u} D _ {k u w} \right]D _ {e d} = D _ {p e d} + D _ {p e u} D _ {k u y} D _ {p y d}$$
