# 内容简介

本书全面介绍作者研究发展了的自抗扰控制技术。自抗扰控制技术是分析经典PID技术优缺点的基础上，为了克服PID的先天不足，相继提出了安排过渡过程，跟踪微分器，扩张状态观测器，开发利用特殊非线性效应等思想来发展的新的数字控制技术。介绍了根据这些新技术研发出的不依赖于对象数学模型的新型实用数字控制器结构——非线性PID控制器结构与自抗扰控制器结构，并介绍这些新技术在各种典型控制问题上的应用问题。

第一章分析了经典PID控制技术的优缺点和事先安排过渡过程的好处；第二章介绍从给定信号合理提取其微分信号的问题；第三章介绍各种非线性反馈形式的功效和效率；第四章介绍如何估计作用于系统的各种总合扰动作用的问题；第五章介绍综合安排过渡过程、合理提取微分信号、总合扰动的估计与补偿及非线性反馈等技术提出的非线性PID结构和自抗扰控制器结构，并介绍相应的仿真研究；第六章介绍自抗扰控制技术在几类典型控制问题上的应用范例。

本书的内容将会对控制理论研究者和控制工程实践者会有很好的参考价值和实践指导作用.

A comprehensive introduction for ADRC technique, which is proposed by the author, is presented in this book. On the base of analyzing the advantages and shortcomings of the classical PID technique, the ideas of arranging the transient process, the Tracking-Differentiator, the Extended State Observer and utilizing some special nonlinear effects are proposed to overcome the shortcomings of PID. These ideas finally expanded into some novel practical digital control techniques: the nonlinear PID control and ADRC.

The text is organized in 6 chapters. Chapter 1 analyzes the advantages and shortcomings of the classical PID control and the advantages of arranging transient process. Chapter 2 treats the problem of how to get the differential signal from the given signal. Chapter 3 deals with the functions and the effects of some classes of nonlinear feedback. Chapter 4 treats the problem of how to estimate the effects of the total disturbances on the system. In Chapter 5, the nonlinear PID and ADRC, developed by combining the ideas of arranging the transient process, properly extracting the differential signal, estimating and compensating the total disturbance, and utilizing the nonlinear feedback, are introduce. Some simulation research is also proposed. Chapter 6 presents the applications of ADRC in some typical control problems.

The book can be used as a reference book for both control theory researchers and control engineers.
