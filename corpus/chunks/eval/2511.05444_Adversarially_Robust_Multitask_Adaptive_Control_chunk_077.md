We note that in the heterogeneous setting, the only additional contribution arises from the deviation between the true system parameters within each cluster $( { \mathrm { i . e . } }$ , intra-cluster heterogeneity). This effect appears in the first term through the heterogeneity bound, which introduces an additional bias term proportional to $\epsilon _ { \mathrm { h e t } } ^ { 2 }$ . Consequently, the regret bound becomes

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbb {E} \big [ \mathcal {R} _ {T} ^ {(i)} \big ] \leq \Omega_ {1} \sqrt {\frac {d _ {u} ^ {2} d _ {x} T}{M _ {j}}} + \Omega_ {2} (\log T) ^ {2} + \Omega_ {3} T \exp \left(- \frac {C _ {\mathrm{mis,2}} \sqrt {\tau_ {1}}}{\sqrt {M _ {j}}}\right) \\ + \sum_ {k = 2} ^ {k _ {\mathrm{fin}}} 1 4 2 (\tau_ {k} - \tau_ {k - 1}) \| P _ {\star} ^ {(i)} \| ^ {8} C _ {\mathrm{het}} \epsilon_ {\mathrm{het}} ^ {2}. \\ \end{array}
$$

By evaluating the summation over epochs and applying the doubling schedule $\tau _ { k } = 2 ^ { k - 1 } \tau _ { 1 }$ , we obtain the final bound

$$\mathbb {E} \big [ \mathcal {R} _ {T} ^ {(i)} \big ] \leq \Omega_ {1} \sqrt {\frac {d _ {u} ^ {2} d _ {x} T}{M _ {j}}} + \Omega_ {2} (\log T) ^ {2} + \Omega_ {3} T \exp \left(- \frac {C _ {\mathrm{mis,2}} \sqrt {\tau_ {1}}}{\sqrt {M _ {j}}}\right) + 1 4 2 \| P _ {\star} ^ {(i)} \| ^ {8} C _ {\mathrm{het}} T \epsilon_ {\mathrm{het}} ^ {2},$$

which completes the proof.
