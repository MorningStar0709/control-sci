Obfuscated Encodings: We now consider the case when the attacker exfiltrates a meaningful byte of data from the drone.

A second, more challenging encoding scheme is now used:

Encoded Bit 1 Attacker yaws drone approximately 5 degrees counter-clockwise. Attacker holds this orientation for 0.75 seconds to transmit another encoded bit 1.   
Encoded Bit 0 Attacker yaws drone approximately 5 degrees clockwise. Attacker holds this orientation for 0.75 seconds to transmit another encoded bit 0.
