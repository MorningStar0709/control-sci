Figure 12: A “cycle'' refers to the robot making its way from the warehouse to the shipping hub and back to the warehouse. The graphed data is an average of 10 trials.

Without using landmark detection localization, the accuracy of odometry will drift over time, leading to error accumulation in the long run as shown in Figure 12. The accuracy of odometry may not be realistic due to the 20-inch error accumulation in just 5 cycles, but error accumulation is still a serious issue to physical robots.
