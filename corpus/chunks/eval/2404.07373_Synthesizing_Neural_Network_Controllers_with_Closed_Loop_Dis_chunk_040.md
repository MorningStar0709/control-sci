# A Expansion of Extended and Transformed System

This section includes the expansion of the system that is the result of augmenting an uncertain LTI system with the filters corresponding to the dynamic IQC as described in Section 3.2. The definitions of the tilde variables are as follows.

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{c c c} \tilde {A} & \tilde {B} _ {w} & \tilde {B} _ {d} \\ \tilde {C} _ {v} & \tilde {D} _ {v w} & \tilde {D} _ {v d} \\ \tilde {C} _ {e} & \tilde {D} _ {e w} & \tilde {D} _ {e d} \end{array} \right] =

\left[ \begin{array}{c c c c c} A & 0 & 0 & B _ {w} & B _ {d} \\ B _ {\psi_ {1}} C _ {v} & A _ {\psi_ {1}} & 0 & B _ {\psi_ {1}} D _ {v w} & B _ {\psi_ {1}} D _ {v d} \\ 0 & 0 & A _ {\psi_ {2}} & B _ {\psi_ {2}} & 0 \\ \hline D _ {\psi_ {1}} C _ {v} & C _ {\psi_ {1}} & 0 & D _ {\psi_ {1}} D _ {v w} & D _ {\psi_ {1}} D _ {v d} \\ \hline C _ {e} & 0 & 0 & D _ {e w} & D _ {e d} \end{array} \right]
$$
