# Trace of a Matrix

For a square matrix $\mathbf{A}$ of $n$ dimension, the trace of $\mathbf{A}$ is defined as

$$\operatorname{tr} [ \mathbf {A} ] = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} a _ {i i}. \tag {A.2.44}$$

Thus, the trace is the sum of the diagonal elements of a matrix. Also,

$$\operatorname{tr} [ \mathbf {A} + \mathbf {B} ] = \operatorname{tr} [ \mathbf {A} ] + \operatorname{tr} [ \mathbf {B} ]\operatorname{tr} [ \mathbf {A B} ] = \operatorname{tr} \left[ \mathbf {A} ^ {\prime} \mathbf {B} ^ {\prime} \right] = \operatorname{tr} \left[ \mathbf {B} ^ {\prime} \mathbf {A} ^ {\prime} \right] = \operatorname{tr} [ \mathbf {B A} ]. \tag {A.2.45}$$
