\begin{array}{l} \boldsymbol {J} (\boldsymbol {q}) = \left[ \begin{array}{l l} - l _ {1} \cos (q _ {1}) - l _ {2} \cos (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) & - l _ {2} \cos (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) \\ - l _ {1} \sin (q _ {1}) - l _ {2} \sin (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) & - l _ {2} \sin (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) \end{array} \right] \dot {q} _ {1} \\ + \left[ \begin{array}{l l} - l _ {2} \cos (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) & - l _ {2} \cos (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) \\ - l _ {2} \sin (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) & - l _ {2} \sin (q _ {1} + q _ {2}) \end{array} \right] \dot {q} _ {2} \\ \end{array}
$$

可见， $J(q)$ 是由结构决定的，假定它在有界的工作空间 $\Omega$ 中是非奇异的。
