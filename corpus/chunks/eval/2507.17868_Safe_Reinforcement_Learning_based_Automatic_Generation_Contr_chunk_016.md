# V. CONCLUSION AND FUTURE WORK

In this paper, we developed a (safety) control barrier function-based framework for safe reinforcement learning (RL) in power system automatic generation control (AGC). We applied the framework to AGC in a multi-source, two-area power system. The results demonstrated that our framework ensures safety during online training, preventing and containing erroneous RL agent control decisions while allowing effective learning.

Future work will focus on practical implementation, addressing system non-linearity, deployment constraints, scalability for larger power systems, and further comparison with existing AGC methods.
