# Rank of a Matrix

The rank or full rank of a matrix $\mathbf{A}$ of order $nxn$ is defined as

1. the number of linearly independent columns or rows of A, or   
2. the greatest order of nonzero determinant of submatrices of A.

If the rank of $\mathbf{A}$ is $n$ , it means that the matrix $\mathbf{A}$ is nonsingular.
