# Norm of a Matrix

For matrices, the various norms are defined as

1. $||\mathbf{A}\mathbf{x}|| \leq ||\mathbf{A}||..||\mathbf{x}||,$   
2. $||\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B}||\leq ||\mathbf{A}|| + ||\mathbf{B}||,$ called the Schwartz inequality,   
3. $||\mathbf{A}\mathbf{B}|| \leq ||\mathbf{A}||..||\mathbf{B}||$

where. denotes multiplication.
