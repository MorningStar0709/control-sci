$$\nabla f (u) + \sum_ {i \in \mathcal {I} ^ {0} (u)} \mu_ {i} \nabla g _ {i} (u) = 0 \tag {C.25}$$

$$\mathcal {F} _ {U} ^ {*} (u) = \operatorname{cone} \left\{\nabla g _ {i} (u) \mid i \in | I ^ {0} (u) \right\}$$

$$- \nabla f (u) \in \operatorname{cone} \left\{\nabla g _ {i} (u) \mid i \in \mathcal {I} ^ {0} (u) \right\}$$
