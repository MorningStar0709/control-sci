$$
\begin{array}{l} d _ {t + 1} = \left| \left(a - b K ^ {\prime}\right) x _ {t} - \left(a y _ {t} - b C \left(y _ {t _ {1}}\right) \right. \right| \\ = \left| \left(a - b K ^ {\prime}\right) x _ {t} - \left(a - b K ^ {\prime}\right) y _ {t} + b K ^ {\prime} y _ {t} - b C \left(y _ {t}\right) \right| \\ \leq \left| \left(a - b K ^ {\prime}\right) x _ {t} - \left(a - b K ^ {\prime}\right) y _ {t} \right| + \left| b K ^ {\prime} y _ {t} - b C \left(y _ {t}\right) \right| \\ \leq (a - b K ^ {\prime}) d _ {t} + b O _ {T} (\upsilon) \quad \text { Equations   (137)   and   (139) } \\ \leq \left(a - b K ^ {\prime}\right) d _ {t} + O _ {T} (v). \tag {141} \\ \end{array}
$$

Equations (140) and (141) give the second half of both desired piecewise equations.
