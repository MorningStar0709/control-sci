This particular occasion was when the ship was off the Dutch coast, bound for Helder, with seas coming in from astern on the port quarter. I was resting in my cabin. The time was 04.00 hrs. Suddenly I sensed that the ship had started to roll perceptibly, and I wondered what was going on. At once, I went up on deck and asked the officer of the watch what on earth was happening, and what the reason was for this sudden increase in the ship's rolling motion. I was surprised to receive the reply, "We have just switched off the Roll-Nix. We need to have some data without Roll-Nix working, to see how much damping can be achieved." I think that that is the most illustrative experience I have had of the Roll-Nix system to date.
