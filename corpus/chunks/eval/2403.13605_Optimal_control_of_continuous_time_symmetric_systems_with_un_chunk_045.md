# A.3 Proof of Theorem 5

The proof of identity (70) is similar to the proof of (41) in Theorem 2. To prove the noise $\nu _ { k }$ has a bounded variance, we plug-in (32) in the definitions (A.5) and use the triangular inequality in (A.6) to obtain

$$\| \operatorname{Cov} (\nu_ {k}; t) \| _ {\infty} \leq\sum_ {i = 0} ^ {k - 1} \left\| \operatorname{Cov} \left(\alpha ((1 - \alpha) \mathcal {I} + \alpha \mathcal {S}) ^ {i} R ^ {- 1} \Sigma_ {e} \mathcal {J} \eta_ {2, k - 1 - i}; t\right) \right\| _ {\infty} +\left\| \operatorname{Cov} \bigl (\alpha \bigl ((1 - \alpha) \mathcal {I} + \alpha \mathcal {S} \bigr) ^ {i} R ^ {- 1} \Sigma_ {e} \mathcal {J G} \Sigma_ {e} Q \mathcal {J} \eta_ {1, k - 1 - i}; t \bigr) \right\| _ {\infty},$$

where using Lemma 5 gives

$$\| \operatorname{Cov} (\nu_ {k}; t) \| _ {\infty} \leqm ^ {3 / 2} e ^ {2} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {k - 1} \left(\left\| \alpha ((1 - \alpha) \mathcal {I} + \alpha \mathcal {S}) ^ {i} R ^ {- 1} \Sigma_ {e} \mathcal {J G} \Sigma_ {e} Q \mathcal {J} \right\| _ {\infty , t _ {f}} ^ {2} \right.+ \left. \left\| \alpha ((1 - \alpha) \mathcal {I} + \alpha \mathcal {S}) ^ {i} R ^ {- 1} \Sigma_ {e} \mathcal {J} \right\| _ {\infty , t _ {f}} ^ {2}\right) \leqm ^ {3 / 2} e ^ {2} \alpha^ {2} \| R ^ {- 1} \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} \left(\| \mathcal {G} \| _ {\infty , t _ {f}} ^ {2} \| Q \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} + 1\right) \times\sum_ {i = 0} ^ {k - 1} \| (1 - \alpha) \mathcal {I} + \alpha \mathcal {S} \| _ {\infty , t _ {f}} ^ {2 i} \leqm ^ {3 / 2} e ^ {2} \alpha^ {2} \| R ^ {- 1} \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} \left(\| \mathcal {G} \| _ {\infty , t _ {f}} ^ {2} \| Q \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} + 1\right) \times\sum_ {i = 0} ^ {k - 1} \left(1 - \alpha + \alpha \| R ^ {- 1} \| _ {\infty} \| G \| _ {\mathrm{pk}} ^ {2} \| Q \| _ {\infty}\right) ^ {2 i}, \tag {A.10}$$

in which the last inequality follows from (49). Condition (48) ensures that the series in (A.10) is convergent. Therefore, we obtain

$$\| \operatorname{Cov} (\nu_ {k}; t) \| _ {\infty} \leq \frac {m ^ {3 / 2} e ^ {2} \alpha^ {2} \| R ^ {- 1} \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} \left(\| G \| _ {\mathrm{pk}} ^ {2} \| Q \| _ {\infty} ^ {2} + 1\right)}{1 - \left(1 - \alpha + \alpha \| R ^ {- 1} \| _ {\infty} \| G \| _ {\mathrm{pk}} ^ {2} \| Q \| _ {\infty}\right) ^ {2}}$$

using (50). The above variance bound is independent of the horizon length and hence, it is bounded, as $t _ { f } \to \infty$ .
