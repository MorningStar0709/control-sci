# 6 Conclusions

This paper discussed various D-MPC approaches, with a particular focus on their numerical implementation. Specifically, it gives a detailed explanation of approaches such as subspace predictive control, PEM-based D-LMPC, RNN, and SSNN-based D-NMPC, etc, with numerical examples. Additionally, the paper highlights current trends in D-MPC research, offering insights into the recent advancements and challenges in the field. The paper also discusses potential future directions for research in the field of D-MPC, considering emerging developments on model-free control algorithms such as reinforcement learning.
