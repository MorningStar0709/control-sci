# Example 12.7 Influence of the delay

$$A ^ {*} (q ^ {- 1}) = 1 - 1. 5 q ^ {- 1} + 0. 7 q ^ {- 2}\boldsymbol {B} ^ {*} (q ^ {- 1}) = q ^ {- d} (1 + 0. 5 q ^ {- 1})C ^ {*} (q ^ {- 1}) = 1 - 0. 2 q ^ {- 1} + 0. 5 q ^ {- 2}$$

Compute the minimum-variance controller when d = 1, 3, or 5. The controller is given by (12.27), where the F-polynomial is given in Example 12.3. Figure 12.4 shows the output and input when the minimum-variance controller is used for different delays in the process.
