Satadru Dey (Senior Member, IEEE) received the master’s degree in control systems from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, Kharagpur, India, in 2010, and the Ph.D. degree in automotive engineering from Clemson University, Clemson, SC, USA, in 2015. He is an Assistant Professor with the Department of Mechanical Engineering, The Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA, USA. From August 2017 to August 2020, he was an Assistant Professor with the University of Colorado Denver, Denver, CO, USA. He was a Postdoctoral

Researcher with the University of California at Berkeley, Berkeley, CA, USA, from 2015 to 2017. His technical background is in the area of controls and his research interest lies in smart cities, energy, and transportation systems.
