According to the law of conservation of mass,

Inflow rate – outflow rate = Accumulation.

$$F _ {i n} - F _ {o u t} = A \frac {d h}{d t} \tag {5.4}F _ {o u t} = k \sqrt {h} \quad \text { where, k = discharge coefficient } \tag {5.5}$$

Therefore,

$$F _ {i n} - k \sqrt {h} = A \frac {d h}{d t} \quad \text { where, } A = \pi * R ^ {2} * h ^ {2} / H ^ {2} \tag {5.6}$$

Rate of change of height,

$$\frac {d h}{d t} = \left. F _ {i n} - k \sqrt {h} \right/ (\pi * R ^ {2} * h ^ {2} / H ^ {2}) \tag {5.7}$$
