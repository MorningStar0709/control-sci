$$
\begin{array}{l} c _ {n} = \frac {a _ {(- n)} + j b _ {(- n)}}{2} = \frac {1}{2} \left(\frac {2}{T} \int_ {- \frac {T}{2}} ^ {\frac {T}{2}} f _ {T} (t) \cos (- n) \omega_ {T} t d t + j \frac {2}{T} \int_ {- \frac {T}{2}} ^ {\frac {T}{2}} f _ {T} (t) \sin (- n) \omega_ {T} t d x\right) \\ = \frac {1}{T} \left(\int_ {- \frac {T}{2}} ^ {\frac {T}{2}} f _ {T} (t) (\cos n \omega_ {T} t - j \sin n \omega_ {T} t) d x \right. \\ = \frac {1}{T} \int_ {- \frac {T}{2}} ^ {\frac {T}{2}} f _ {T} (t) \mathrm{e} ^ {- \mathrm{j} n \omega_ {T} t} \mathrm{d} t \tag {B.4.8c} \\ \end{array}
$$

此时归纳式(8.4.8)，发现 $c_{n}$ 可以写成一个统一的形式，即

$$c _ {n} = \frac {1}{T} \int_ {- \frac {T}{2}} ^ {\frac {T}{2}} f _ {T} (t) \mathrm{e} ^ {- \mathrm{j} n \omega_ {T} t} \mathrm{d} t \quad n = 0, \pm 1, \pm 2, \pm 3, \dots \tag {B.4.9}$$

式(8.4.6)和式(8.4.9)说明，当使用复数表达傅里叶级数时会非常简单。
