# 13.9 Notes and References

There are many books and papers dealing with identification methods. Some basic references in book form are Jenkins and Watts (1968), Eykhoff (1974), Goodwin and Payne (1977), Ljung and Söderström (1983), Norton (1986), Ljung (1987), Söderström and Stoica (1989), and Johansson (1993). An early survey of system identification is given in Åström and Eykhoff (1971).

Good sources for further references are Biermann (1977), Eykhoff (1981), Isermann (1981), Lawson and Hansson (1974), and the special issue on "Identification and system parameter estimation," Automatica, 17, no. 1 (January 1981).

For someone interested in historical notes, see Gauss (1809) and Sorensen (1970).
