# Code of Conduct

ControlMind welcomes technical discussion, reproducibility reports, and constructive criticism. The project involves scientific, medical, and evaluation materials, so discussions should stay careful, evidence-based, and respectful.

中文说明：欢迎围绕技术、复现和证据链提出问题与改进。项目涉及科学文献、医疗文献和评测材料，讨论应保持尊重、严谨和基于证据。

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Discuss evidence, commands, data files, and observed behavior.
- Separate reproducibility limitations from personal judgments.
- Avoid sharing private documents, patient-level information, or credentials.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Posting live secrets, private medical data, or unauthorized documents.
- Misrepresenting generated or preliminary outputs as audited evidence.
- Pressuring contributors to disclose private credentials or local-only data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate these expectations. Repeated abuse may lead to blocked participation in the project spaces.
